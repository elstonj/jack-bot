## Notes & Context
This 24-month SBIR Phase II contract focused on developing volcano monitoring capabilities using Black Swift Technologies' S2 UAS platform. Key accomplishments include:

- **Contract Structure**: Quarterly demonstration reports with progressive invoicing tied to milestones ($44,212 - $129,799 per quarter)
- **Hardware Deliveries**: Multiple payload integrations (ADS-B, heated pitot, long-range communications, photogrammetry, trace gas, Iridium satellite backup) culminating in 2 S0 VTOL systems delivered to NOAA
- **Technical Focus**: Advanced autonomous capabilities for challenging volcanic environments, with emphasis on turbulence measurement, intelligent flight control in adverse conditions, and mission planning for constrained communication/terrain scenarios
- **Team Execution**: Strong project execution with consistent on-time delivery and only minor delays (most tasks completed within 1-5 days of due date)
- **NOAA Coordination**: Direct collaboration with NOAA/AOML Hurricane Research Division; Joe Cione served as primary contact; equipment delivery included matching funds from NOAA
- **Documentation**: Quarterly technical reports maintained via Overleaf collaborative platform

Project represents successful completion of government SBIR Phase II contract with full technical objectives met and all deliverables executed on schedule.
