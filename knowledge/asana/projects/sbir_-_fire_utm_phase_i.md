## Notes & Context
This was a pilot study demonstrating UAS observations to improve wildfire wind/turbulence modeling. The project involved significant regulatory coordination with CALFIRE and fire agencies to establish operational procedures for UAS in wildfire environments. Key technical achievements included developing sampling strategies, data ingestion methods, and demonstrating end-to-end operational workflows. The project successfully positioned BST for Phase II funding and established partnerships for future wildfire UAS operations.
