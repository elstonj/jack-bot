This was a **commercialization effort** for BST's Aeropod technology, not just improvements. Key technical focus areas included:

- **Fin Design**: Reducing thickness for drag/stability, changing aspect ratio from 3:1 to 2:1, exploring alternatives to foam materials
- **Particle Sensor**: Transitioning from Atmotube Pro to Sensirion SEN55 sensor with Arduino/Raspberry Pi logging

**Business Development**: Significant focus on licensing and commercialization strategy, including discussions with subcontractor Ted Miles about his independent commercialization plans and ensuring BST's interests are protected.
