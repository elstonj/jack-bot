## Notes & Context
This was a Phase II SBIR developing a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity, specifically for Air Force landing area assessment. The team collaborated with Orbital Micro Systems (OMS) as a subcontractor ($605,000 subcontract). Key contacts were Jason Foley (Primary TPOC) and Thomas Shea (CO). The project successfully transitioned from NASA soil moisture mapping technology to DoD soil integrity applications. Final deliverable (#5 for $100,000) was submitted December 2024 and approved, demonstrating successful project completion.
