## Project Description
Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture as a key variable to understanding soil integrity—as soil moisture increases, soil becomes more slippery and sticky while decreasing in strength. The team (BST, OMS, and CET) adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case: mapping landing area suitability. Soil integrity is quantified as cone index and compared against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements.
