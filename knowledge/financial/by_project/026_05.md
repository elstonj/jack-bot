   - **Payment Receipt:** Unknown if By Light has paid all three invoices. Net 15 terms mean first payment due by 2026-04-23 at latest; Scope 2 due (per Asana) by 2026-06-13.

4. **No Cost Category Visibility**
   - **Blind Spot:** Budget documents and QuickBooks expense detail were not provided. Cannot determine if spending is within budget by labor, materials, equipment, or other categories.
   - **Impact:** Unknown whether actual costs (labor hours, subcontractor fees, materials) align with $126,629 budget. Potential for cost overruns hidden by invoice aggregation.

5. **Technical Scope Complexity & Testing Dependencies**
   - **Flight Testing:** 2026-04-22 milestone includes maneuver testing and 6g tolerance validation—if failures occur, rework required.
   - **CAD Refinement Post-Flight:** If flight testing reveals design issues, CAD work (2026-04-23–24) is compressed into 1–2 days.

   - **Risk:** Technical failures or integration issues in April will compress final build timeline in May, increasing error risk.
