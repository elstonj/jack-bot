### Key Financial Risks

#### 1. **Critical Delivery Blocker — S0 VTOL Crash Bug (Week of 2026-04-20)**
- **Impact:** S0 VTOL platform has an active crash bug under investigation; flight test restart in progress.
- **Cost Risk:** If crash bug repair extends beyond May 1, 2026 handoff date, BST will incur:
  - Additional engineering labor (debug, redesign, re-test) not covered by $22,337 fixed-price contract
  - Delayed customer satisfaction and potential warranty/remediation costs
  - Reputational risk with client (Barbados Meteorological Services)
  - Possible impact on other S0 VTOL deliveries (e.g., ERAU platform — S0-VTOL is ranked #2 BST company priority)
- **Status:** **UNBUDGETED COST EXPOSURE** — Fixed-price contract does not reflect current troubleshooting labor and materials.

#### 2. **Bench Test Work Overdue (Visual Observation as of 2026-04-24)**
- **Deliverable:** "Visual Observation Bench Test" due by 2026-04-24 per Maciej Stachura.
- **Status:** Overdue as of April 24, 2026.
- **Impact:** Delay in test completion directly jeopardizes May 1 delivery date; additional labor hours required.
- **Cost Risk:** Unbudgeted overtime or schedule compression labor.

#### 3. **Imminent Instrumented Bench Test Deadline**
- **Due:** By Thursday following 2026-04-24 (i.e., ~2026-04-25 to -04-26).
- **Status:** Open with very short lead time.
- **Impact:** If not completed on time, further delays to delivery and handoff.

#### 4. **Ground Station Asset Scarcity Flagged**
- **Risk Signal:** Jack Elston (2026-04-20) flagged potential hardware repriorization: *"depending on timeline we might be able to shift some older ground stations from the S0-VTOL kits. (I hate to do that, but might be best)."*
- **Financial Impact:** If older ground stations are substituted, potential impact on system performance, customer satisfaction, and possible warranty obligations. Cost to upgrade/remediate is unbudgeted.
- **Context:** S0-VTOL is ranked #2 among top 5 BST priorities; higher-priority S3 IRAD (ranked #1) may be consuming ground station inventory.

#### 5. **Operator Training Unassigned & Unfunded for Curriculum Development**
- **Status:** Training delivery scheduled July 1–4, 2026 but **no assignee and no curriculum completion due date.**
- **Cost Risk:** Training materials and supplies development costs may not be reflected in original $22,337 contract estimate. Sabu Best available week of May 5, 2025 for preliminary materials review (note: this is in the past relative to April 2026 status date), suggesting curriculum work is **already behind schedule**.
- **Impact:** If curriculum development is incomplete or requires revision post-delivery, additional unbudgeted labor and materials.

#### 6. **NDAA Compliance Not Finalized**
- **Risk Signal:** Alex Lomis (2026-04-17) noted S0 VTOL "can be compliant but not 'fully' compliant yet."
- **Cost Impact:** Potential rework or additional testing required to achieve full NDAA compliance; cost is unbudgeted.
- **Severity:** Low-to-medium (not explicitly blocking but noted as outstanding).

#### 7. **ISM Band Interference Risk (902–928 MHz)**
- **Technical Issue:** S0 VTOL uses Microhard P900 radio (FCC ID: NS913P900, PN: MHS185000) operating in unregulated ISM band. Frequency-hopping may mitigate but **requires verification before operational deployment** flagged by Barbados Prime Minister's Office.
- **Cost Impact:** 
  - If interference verification fails, rework required (antenna redesign, software patch, or radio swap).
  - Deployment delay and customer dissatisfaction.
  - Possible hardware replacement cost if radio is incompatible.
- **Status:** **Pre-delivery verification required.** Unbudgeted testing/rework if verification fails.
