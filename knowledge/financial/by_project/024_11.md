## Key Deliverables & Milestones

**Source: Proposals & Reports**

### NOAA Hurricane Testbed (S0 Wind Data Integration)
**Project:** Enhancing Tropical Cyclone Forecasts through High-Frequency UAS Observations  
**Partner:** Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University / NOAA National Hurricane Center  
**Duration:** 3 years  
**Key Deliverables:**
- **Year 1:** Reduction factor derivation to standardize S0 wind measurements to 10-meter/10-minute meteorological standard (from 2024 S0 flights + dropsonde data)
- **Year 2:** Validation on historical storms; testbed evaluation; demonstration of forecast improvement to NHC forecasters
- **Year 3:** Operational implementation; integration into HDOBS or real-time data stream; delivery to NHC forecasters

**Status:** Year 1 in progress (19 S0 flights completed as of proposal date 2024-11). High activity in QuickBooks data (S0 Hurricane allocated $1.3M+ through 2026-01).

### DOE FECM Methane Leak Detection (S2 UAS with Quanta3 Spectrometer)
**Project:** DOE FECM RFP – Orphaned Well Emissions Monitoring  
**Proposal Date:** 2024-11-06  
**Key Deliverables:**
- S2 fixed-wing UAS with Quanta3 methane spectrometer (TRL 5 → validation)
- Detection capability: 3 ppb sensitivity; autonomous BVLOS flight; 50 wells per flight day
- Cost target: <$100 per well vs. $750 baseline (ground technician)

**Status:** Proposal stage. "Methane Emission" allocated $3,087 in 2025-01 QuickBooks entry (appears to be early-stage or planning expense).
