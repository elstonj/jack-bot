## Scope Summary

Black Swift Technologies will:
1. Develop a reduction factor to standardize S0 UAS wind measurements to standard 10-minute/10-meter wind speeds
2. Conduct field validation using 2024 S0 flight data combined with dropsonde and radar observations
3. Demonstrate forecast improvements through historical storm testbed evaluation
4. Operationalize real-time data delivery to NHC forecasters via HDOBS or equivalent data stream
