### Category-Level Analysis:

**CRITICAL OVERRUNS:**

1. **Direct Labor: +$7,553 (37% over budget)**
   - **Root Cause:** Field deployment required more intensive engineering support than estimated. Two key engineers exceeded budgeted hours significantly:
     - J. Elston (PI): +43.63 hours (+43.6% over estimate)
     - D. Prendergast (Travel Engineer): +59.5 hours (+59.5% over estimate)
   - **Documented Issues:** Budget Doc references avionics repair (5/13) and flap servo damage (5/19), indicating field challenges requiring extended troubleshooting.
   - **Recommendation:** This is the primary cost driver. Labor intensity of field operations was underestimated in proposal phase.

2. **Travel - Airfare: +$1,333 (71% over budget)**
   - **Root Cause:** International airfare to Costa Rica (Denver/San Jose routing) exceeded estimate.
   - **Actual:** $3,205 vs. Budgeted $1,872 (2 travelers initially budgeted; actual deployment may have involved more travelers or higher fares)
   - **Partial Offset:** Meals & Incidentals came in $2,005 under budget, partially offsetting airfare overrun.

3. **Travel - Car Rental: +$306 (69% over budget)**
   - **Actual:** $750 vs. Budgeted $444
   - **Root Cause:** Costa Rica field operations required extended vehicle rental; local market rates higher than estimate.

4. **Other Direct Costs: +$727 (unbudgeted)**
   - Computer Services: $499 (equipment/software support)
   - Shipping/Insurance: $228.05 (UPS shipping + Generali Global Assistance insurance)
   - These appear to be legitimate field support costs but were not anticipated in original budget.

**PARTIAL OFFSETS:**

5. **Travel - Meals & Incidentals: -$2,005 (60% under budget)**
   - Budgeted $3,336; Actual $1,331
   - Team controlled meal spending effectively in Costa Rica.
   - **Impact:** Saved ~$2,005, offsetting ~27% of labor overrun.

6. **Travel - Lodging: -$589 (18% under budget)**
   - Budgeted $3,234; Actual $2,645 (11 nights)
   - Negotiated competitive lodging rates (Rinconcito Lodge, Hotel Hacienda Guachipeli per Budget Doc).
   - **Impact:** Saved ~$589, minor offset.

**ON BUDGET CATEGORIES:**

7. **In-Country Consulting (Universidad de Costa Rica Foundation):** On budget at $25,000.00
   - No variance; critical subcontract for local coordination executed as planned.

8. **Indirect Costs (OH + G&A):** On budget at $21,218.00
   - Fixed allocation based on direct costs; no variance.

9. **Fee (7%):** On budget at $5,297.00
