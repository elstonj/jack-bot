# Project 200-13 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** CRATER (Costa Rica Airborne research on foresT Ecosystem Response to volcanic gas emissions)
- **Client/Agency:** NASA Ames Research Center
- **Contract Type:** NASA Phase III R&D
- **Contract Number:** 80NSSC25CA052
- **Period of Performance:** February 20, 2025 to August 20, 2025 (6 months)
- **Brief Description:** Deployment of Black Swift Technologies S2 unmanned aerial system to Costa Rica for airborne research on forest ecosystem response to volcanic gas emissions. Project includes field operations, training of personnel on SwiftCore flight management system and S2 UAS, real-time aircraft troubleshooting (avionics repair, flap servo repair), and delivery of demonstration reports.
