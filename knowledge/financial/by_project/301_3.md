# Project 301-3 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** S0 Hurricane Phase II - 2025
- **Client/Agency:** NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) / University of Miami
- **Contract Number:** 1305M225P0034 (with Modification P25002 for optional items)
- **Contract Type:** NOAA Small Uncrewed Aircraft Systems (sUAS) procurement and training contract
- **Period of Performance:** February 2025 – August 25, 2026 (6-month extension granted)
- **Brief Description of Scope:**
  - Delivery of 33 Black Swift S0 uncrewed aircraft systems for hurricane observation operations
  - Development and delivery of operator, observer, and instructor training courses
  - Mission support and operational testing (30 mission days)
  - Technical enhancements including 10Hz meteorological data, increased endurance, dual GPS, GUI improvements, and airops updates
  - Integration of advanced avionics (RTK positioning, radar altimeter, IAS systems)
