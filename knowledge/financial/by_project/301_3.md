**Notes on Budget Categories:**
- **Data Source Mismatch:** The "Proposed Budget" figures above represent *actual spend tracked in QuickBooks* as of February 2026 (from Drive Budget Docs, "Budget Summary" section). These are not pre-project budget projections but rather cumulative actual costs.
- **Reconciliation Issue:** QuickBooks shows $445,105.40 total project spend (as of February 2026 report), while Drive Budget Docs show cumulative labor + equipment + travel + overhead of $684,996.89. This discrepancy suggests either:
  1. Drive Budget Docs include projected costs beyond actual QBO records through the report date, OR
  2. Additional spending occurred after February 2026 not captured in the Drive Budget Docs snapshot.
- **QBO vs. Invoice Mismatch:** QBO project total ($935,473.80) is substantially higher than invoices issued to NOAA ($707,850 paid + $54,000 pending = $761,850). This suggests QBO may include internal cost allocations, overhead, and/or expenses not directly invoiced to the customer.
