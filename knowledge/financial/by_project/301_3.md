# Project 301-3 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** [301-3] S0 Hurricane Phase II - 2025
- **Client/Agency:** NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) / University of Miami
- **Primary Contacts:** Joe Cione (NOAA), Jun Zhang (jun.zhang@noaa.gov)
- **Contract Type:** NOAA Small Uncrewed Aircraft Systems (sUAS) contract with base and optional modifications
- **Contract Number:** 1305M225P0034 (Base); Modification P25002 (Optional)
- **Period of Performance:** February 2025 – August 25, 2026 (6-month extension granted)
- **Brief Description of Scope:** Design, manufacture, and deliver Black Swift S0 UAS platforms (26 base units + 7 optional additional units = 33 total) for hurricane observation research with NOAA. Includes operator training, instructor training, mission support, and technical enhancements (10Hz met data, increased endurance, dual GPS, GUI improvements, airops updates).
