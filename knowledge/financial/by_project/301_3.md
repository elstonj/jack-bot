# Project 301-3 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview

**Project Name:** S0 Hurricane Phase II - 2025

**Client/Agency:** NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) / University of Miami

**Contract Number:** 1305M225P0034 (with Modification P25002 for optional items)

**Contract Type:** NOAA Small Uncrewed Aircraft Systems (sUAS) contract — Base + Optional

**Period of Performance:** February 2025 – August 25, 2026 (6-month extension granted)

**Project Owner:** Josh Fromm

**Brief Description:**
Design, build, and deliver Black Swift S0 UAS platforms for hurricane observation operations. Includes 26 systems in base contract + 7 additional systems under optional modification, operator/observer training courses, instructor training, and 30 days of mission operational support. 34 systems have been delivered to AOC as of December 2025; hardware/software freeze completed November 2025. Project is active and progressing with key training and delivery deadlines through August 25, 2026.
