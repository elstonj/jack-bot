# Project 301-3 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** S0 Hurricane Phase II - 2025
- **Client/Agency:** NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) / University of Miami
- **Contract Type:** NOAA Small Uncrewed Aircraft Systems (sUAS) contract with optional items
- **Contract Number:** 1305M225P0034 (Base + Modification P25002)
- **Primary Contacts:** Joe Cione (NOAA), Jun Zhang (jun.zhang@noaa.gov)
- **Period of Performance:** February 2025 – August 25, 2026 (6-month extension granted)
- **Brief Description of Scope:** Design, manufacture, and delivery of Black Swift S0 UAS platforms for hurricane observation operations. Includes 26 base systems plus 7 optional systems, operator/instructor training course development and delivery, mission days operational support, and travel for testing/training. Critical deliverables include 10Hz meteorological data, increased endurance, dual GPS, GUI/usability improvements, Airops updates, and web-based controller.
