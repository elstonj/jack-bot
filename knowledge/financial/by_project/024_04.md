# Project 024-04 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (Department of Air Force)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Program/Solicitation:** AFWERX SBIR Phase II – Soil Moisture Mapping (Project 400-5, X22.4)
- **Period of Performance:** 2024-04-05 to 2026-04-17 (24 months, 12 days)
- **Brief Description:** Black Swift Technologies is developing modifications to its L-band soil moisture sensing system and E2 small UAS to enable runway integrity validation for the U.S. Air Force through real-time soil moisture and cone index measurements. The project involves upgrading the radiometer with improved RFI mitigation and calibration capabilities, integrating auxiliary thermal and NDVI sensors, and developing algorithms to correlate soil moisture with soil integrity metrics.

## Key Deliverables & Milestones

**Source: Proposals & Reports (Milestone 2 Report, dated 2024-04-11 to 2025-07-08)**

### Completed Milestones:
1. **Milestone 2 – Radiometer Modifications (Completed)**
   - Designed new revision of L-band Dual-Channel Correlator Radiometer (LDCR) with:
     - Real-time in-flight calibration using interior references
     - Enhanced RFI (radio frequency interference) mitigation
     - Digital correlator for improved RFI detection
     - Lumped element 0°/180° hybrid at L-band with gain imbalance <0.15 dB, phase imbalance <0.5°
     - 2x2 microstrip patch antenna array providing ~40° beamwidth and ~35 MHz bandwidth
   - RF chain design with three amplifier stages optimally driving down converter/digital back end

### Upcoming Deliverables (from document scope):
- **Milestone 3 & beyond:** UAS integration testing, algorithm development for soil moisture to cone index correlation, operational validation at Air Force bases
- **Final Deliverable:** Runway integrity validation system ready for Air Force operational deployment

**Financial Implications:** Milestones 1–2 are complete as of the report date (2025-07-08). Remaining work (integration, testing, validation) spans remainder of Period of Performance (through 2026-04-17). No specific milestone payment schedules are provided in available documents.
