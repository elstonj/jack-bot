# Project 024-04 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS (Soil Moisture Mapping Phase II)
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (Department of Air Force) / AFWERX
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Program:** AFWERX SBIR Phase II — Soil Moisture Mapping (Project 400-5, X22.4D)
- **Period of Performance:** 2024-04-05 to 2026-04-17 (24 months, 12 days)
- **Brief Description:** Black Swift Technologies is developing modifications to its L-band soil moisture sensing system and E2 small UAS to enable runway integrity validation for the U.S. Air Force through real-time soil moisture and cone index measurements. Deliverables include upgraded radiometer with improved RFI mitigation, integrated thermal and NDVI sensors, and algorithms correlating soil moisture to soil integrity metrics.
