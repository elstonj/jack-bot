# Project 024-04 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DAF — Department of Air Force)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Program/Solicitation:** AFWERX SBIR Phase II — Soil Moisture Mapping (Project 400-5, X22.4)
- **Period of Performance:** 2024-04-07 to 2026-09-28 (approximately 29 months)
- **Brief Description of Scope:** Black Swift Technologies is developing modifications to its L-band soil moisture sensing system and E2 small UAS to enable runway integrity validation for the U.S. Air Force through real-time soil moisture and cone index measurements. Work includes upgrading the radiometer with improved RFI mitigation and calibration capabilities, integrating auxiliary thermal and NDVI sensors, and developing algorithms to correlate soil moisture with soil integrity metrics for operational use at Air Force bases.
