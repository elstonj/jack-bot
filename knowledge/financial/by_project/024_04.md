# Project 024-04 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DAF — Department of Air Force)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Program:** AFWERX SBIR Phase II — Soil Moisture Mapping (Project 400-5, X22.4)
- **Period of Performance:** 2024-04-05 to 2026-04-17 (24 months, 12 days)
- **Brief Description:** Development of modifications to Black Swift Technologies' L-band soil moisture sensing system and E2 small UAS to enable runway integrity validation for the U.S. Air Force through real-time soil moisture and cone index measurements. Includes radiometer upgrades with improved RFI mitigation and calibration, integration of auxiliary thermal and NDVI sensors, and algorithm development to correlate soil moisture with soil integrity metrics.
