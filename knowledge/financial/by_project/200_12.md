- **Full Project Name:** AEROKATS and ROVER Education Network (AREN) 2025
- **Client/Agency:** NASA Science Mission Directorate
- **Contract Number:** 80NSSC25PA610
- **Contract Type:** Science Activation cooperative agreement (part of multi-year AREN project running 2021–present)
- **Period of Performance:** 2025 (Original contract due 2026-03-02; **project archived/shut down August 2025**)
- **Project Owner:** Meredith O'hara Needham
- **Key Personnel:** Kay Rufty (Aerospace Engineer, Co-Investigator); Ted Miles (Senior Engineering Technician); Jack Elston & Maciej Stachura (Technical Consultants)

The project was shut down ahead of schedule in August 2025 following the retirement of primary NASA POC Geoffrey Bland (NASA Ames).
