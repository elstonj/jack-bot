# Project 200-12 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** AEROKATS and ROVER Education Network (AREN) 2025
- **Client/Agency:** NASA Science Mission Directorate (Science Activation program)
- **Contract Number:** 80NSSC25PA610
- **Contract Type:** Cooperative Agreement (part of multi-year AREN project running 2021–present; primary agreement NNX16AB95A)
- **Period of Performance:** 2025 (project archived August 2025 ahead of original schedule; original contract due date was March 2, 2026)
- **Status:** **ARCHIVED — Project shut down August 2025 ahead of schedule**
- **Project Owner:** Meredith O'hara Needham
- **Primary Contact:** Geoffrey Bland, NASA Ames (retired August 2025)

**Brief Description:**  
BST participated in the AREN Science Activation project, a NASA-funded education and outreach initiative promoting learning in aeronautics and rover robotics. BST's contribution included labor and technical support via Aerospace Engineer Kay Rufty (named Co-Investigator), Senior Engineering Technician Ted Miles, and consulting services. Work centered on hardware integration, TerraROVER production and testing, MiniCam assembly, and national workshop delivery. Project was shut down in August 2025 following Geoffrey Bland's retirement and ahead of the original March 2026 completion date.
