BST participated in the AEROKATS and ROVER Education Network, a NASA Science Mission Directorate "Science Activation" project promoting learning and outreach. BST provided technical support via Aerospace Engineer Kay Rufty (named Co-Investigator). The project concluded ahead of schedule in August 2025 following Geoffrey Bland's retirement.
