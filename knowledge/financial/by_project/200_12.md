- **Client/Agency:** NASA Science Mission Directorate

- **Period of Performance:** 2025 (Original contract due 2026-03-02; **project archived August 2025**)
- **Project Owner:** Meredith O'Hara Needham
- **Key Personnel:** Kay Rufty (Aerospace Engineer, Co-Investigator); Ted Miles (Senior Engineering Technician); Jack Elston & Maciej Stachura (Technical Consultants)

**Description of Scope:**  
BST participated in the NASA AREN Science Activation project promoting STEM learning and outreach through the Aeropods platform and TerraROVER education systems. Kay Rufty served as a named Co-Investigator providing aerospace engineering support. The project involved hardware integration, assembly, testing, and distribution of educational robotics systems to regional partners. **Project was shut down in August 2025 ahead of schedule following the retirement of primary NASA POC Geoffrey Bland (NASA Ames).**
