# Project 200-12 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** NASA AREN '25 (Aerokats and Rover Education Network)
- **Client/Agency:** NASA Science Mission Directorate (Goddard Space Flight Center, Wallops Flight Facility)
- **Contract Type:** Science Activation cooperative agreement
- **Contract Number:** 80NSSC25PA610
- **Period of Performance:** 2025 (part of multi-year AREN project running 2021–present)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED (shut down 2025-08-26)
- **Brief Description:** BST provided aerospace engineering support and technical consulting to NASA's Science Activation initiative promoting learning and outreach through the AEROKATS and ROVER Education Network. Kay Rufty (Aerospace Engineer, named Co-Investigator) and Ted Miles (Senior Engineering Technician) led hardware integration, assembly, testing, and distribution of TerraROVER systems and Aeropods platform. Project concluded ahead of schedule due to key NASA POC Geoffrey Bland's retirement in August 2025.
