Work included airframe design finalization, COTS procurement, prototype assembly, comprehensive ground and flight testing, performance analysis, and delivery of validated design recommendations and flight test data. Project delivered multiple generations of prototype aircraft and extensive flight performance documentation to support 400–500 km range objectives at ~35 lb GTOW.
