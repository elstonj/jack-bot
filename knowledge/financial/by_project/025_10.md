### Detailed Cost Variance Analysis

#### Data Reconciliation Issue ⚠️
**QuickBooks unclassified.md reports total costs allocated to project 025-10 (By Light Mustang) as:**
- 2025-10: $242,310
- 2025-11: $530,759
- 2025-12: $254,579.29
- 2026-01: $251,407
- **Total (4 months): $1,279,055.29**

**vs. Invoiced to Customer: $450,000**

**Possible Explanations:**
1. **Multi-project cost allocation:** The QB unclassified.md summary may include costs allocated to OTHER projects (e.g., S0 Hurricane, Navy SBIR, NASA Persistence, Adonis) that are listed alongside By Light Mustang in monthly summaries. The monthly line shows "By Light Mustang ($100K), S0 Hurricane ($185,850), Navy SBIR ($49K)..." — these are separate project allocations within the same month, not all attributable to 025-10.

2. **Actual costs vs. invoiced amounts:** BST may have incurred $554,888 in actual project costs (labor, materials, subcontractors) but only invoiced the contractual $450,000 due to:
   - Milestone-based invoicing (capped at contract value)
   - Absorption of scope changes or overruns within fixed-price contract terms
   - Timing differences in expense recognition vs. invoice issuance

3. **2026-01 costs:** The $251,407 in January 2026 likely represents invoice timing or carryover expenses from the December final phase, as the project was marked complete 12/31/2025.

**Recommended Action:** Obtain detailed QB account-level reports (GL export) and cost-center allocation schedules to definitively separate By Light Mustang project costs from other concurrent projects. **This summary cannot validate true project profitability without that detail.**
