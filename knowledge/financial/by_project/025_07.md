# Project 025-07 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** [400-5] SBIR - SMM DoD 22.4D Phase II
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Period of Performance:** Late 2024 – July 11, 2025
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed with all deliverables accepted
- **Brief Description:** Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture as a key variable to understanding soil integrity and maps landing area suitability for the Air Force. The team (BST, Orbital Micro Systems, and CET) adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case, with soil integrity quantified as cone index and compared against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements.
