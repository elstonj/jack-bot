# Project 025-07 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** [400-5] SBIR - SMM DoD 22.4D Phase II
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Period of Performance:** Late 2024 – July 11, 2025
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed with all deliverables accepted

**Description (from Asana):** Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture to understand soil integrity—as soil moisture increases, soil becomes more slippery and sticky while decreasing in strength. The team (BST, Orbital Micro Systems, and CET) adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case: mapping landing area suitability at military installations. Soil integrity is quantified as cone index and compared against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements. Testing conducted at local sites and Tyndall Air Force Base.
