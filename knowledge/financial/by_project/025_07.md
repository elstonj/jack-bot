# Project 025-07 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** [400-5] SBIR - SMM DoD 22.4D Phase II
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II)
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Period of Performance:** Late 2024 – July 11, 2025
- **Status:** ARCHIVED — Successfully completed (all deliverables accepted)
- **Brief Description:** Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture to assess landing area suitability by mapping the relationship between cone index and volumetric water content (VWC). Technology adapted from NASA soil moisture mapping for Air Force runway/landing area assessment use case. Testing conducted at local sites and Tyndall Air Force Base.
