## Project Overview

- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Period of Performance:** Late 2024 – July 2025 (Contract due date: 2025-07-11)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed
- **Primary Technical Lead:** Dan Prendergast
- **Key Government Contacts:** Jason Foley (TPOC), Thomas Shea (CO)

**Scope Description (from Asana):** Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture to assess landing area suitability for the Air Force. The team (BST, Orbital Micro Systems, and CET) adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for DoD use. Soil integrity is quantified as cone index and compared against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements. All 85 tasks completed; all deliverables accepted by TPOC.
