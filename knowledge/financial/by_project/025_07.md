## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** [400-5] SBIR - SMM DoD 22.4D Phase II
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Period of Performance:** Late 2024 – July 11, 2025
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed (all deliverables accepted)

**Brief Description of Scope:**
Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer system for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture as a key variable to assess landing area suitability for Air Force operations. The team (Black Swift Technologies, Orbital Micro Systems, and CET) adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for DoD use. Soil integrity is quantified as cone index and compared against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements. Testing included local field validation and campaign testing at Tyndall Air Force Base.
