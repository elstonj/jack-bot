# Project 025-07 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** [400-5] SBIR - SMM DoD 22.4D Phase II
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Period of Performance:** Late 2024 – July 11, 2025 (completion date July 21, 2025)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed

**Description:** Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture to assess landing area suitability for military aircraft. Team adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case, measuring cone index against volumetric water content. Key partners: Orbital Micro Systems (OMS, subcontractor), Coastal Engineering Technologies (CET). Deliverables included soil integrity algorithms, field testing, Tyndall AFB validation, and final technical report. All milestones completed and accepted by TPOC.
