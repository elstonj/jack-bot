# Project 025-07 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** [400-5] SBIR - SMM DoD 22.4D Phase II
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Period of Performance:** Late 2024 – July 11, 2025 (Due date: 2025-07-11)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed with all deliverables accepted
- **Brief Description:** Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture to assess landing area suitability by quantifying cone index and comparing against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements. This project adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case in partnership with Orbital Micro Systems (OMS) and CET.
