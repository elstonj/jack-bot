# Project 025-07 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** [400-5] SBIR - SMM DoD 22.4D Phase II
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Period of Performance:** Late 2024 – July 11, 2025
- **Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed (all deliverables accepted)

**Scope Description:**
Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture to assess landing area suitability by quantifying soil integrity (cone index) against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements. Adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case. Testing conducted locally and at Tyndall Air Force Base. Team included Black Swift Technologies, Orbital Micro Systems (OMS), and CET.
