# Project 025-07 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** [400-5] SBIR - SMM DoD 22.4D Phase II
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003 (from Asana)
- **Period of Performance:** Late 2024 – July 11, 2025
- **Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed
- **Brief Description:** Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture to assess landing area suitability by comparing cone index against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements. Adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case. Team included Black Swift Technologies, Orbital Micro Systems (OMS), and CET.
