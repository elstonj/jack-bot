# Project 025-07 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** [400-5] SBIR - SMM DoD 22.4D Phase II
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Period of Performance:** Late 2024 – July 2025 (Due Date: 2025-07-11)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed with all deliverables accepted
- **Brief Description of Scope:** Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture to assess landing area suitability by mapping the relationship between volumetric water content (VWC) and cone index (soil strength). Technology adapted from NASA soil moisture mapping for Air Force use case. Team included Black Swift Technologies (prime), Orbital Micro Systems (OMS), and CET. Testing conducted at local field sites and Tyndall Air Force Base.
