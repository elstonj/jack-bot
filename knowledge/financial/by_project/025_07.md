# Project 025-07 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** [400-5] SBIR - SMM DoD 22.4D Phase II
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Period of Performance:** Late 2024 – July 11, 2025
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed (all deliverables accepted)

**Description:** Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture to assess landing area suitability by quantifying soil integrity as cone index and comparing against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements. Adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case in collaboration with subcontractors Orbital Micro Systems (OMS) and CET.
