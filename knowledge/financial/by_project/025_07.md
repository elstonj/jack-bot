# Project 025-07 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** [400-5] SBIR - SMM DoD 22.4D Phase II
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II
- **Period of Performance:** Late 2024 – July 11, 2025
- **Brief Description:** Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture to determine landing area suitability for the Air Force. The team (BST, Orbital Micro Systems, and CET) adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case, quantifying soil integrity as cone index compared against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements.
- **Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed with all deliverables accepted
