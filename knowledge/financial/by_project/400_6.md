### Category-by-Category Analysis:

#### ✅ **Direct Labor: SIGNIFICANTLY UNDERSPENT**
- **Budgeted:** $14,156.00 (73 hours at $150/hr + 29.28% fringe)
- **Actual (Jan–Aug 2025):** $969.59 (5 hours charged)
- **Variance:** $(13,186.41) underspent / **6.9% utilization**
- **Assessment:** Dr. Elston budgeted at 73 hours across 3-month PoP (roughly 25 hours/month), but only 5 hours logged through August 2025. This suggests either:
  - Work was performed in April/May but labor hours not yet recorded in the monthly tracking sheet
  - Project had a compressed schedule with concentrated effort on final report (April delivery)
  - Tracking lag between actual hours worked and monthly budget spreadsheet updates
  - **Financial impact:** Favorable variance if no additional labor charges occur post-tracking period; unfavorable if backdated labor hours inflate actual costs later

#### ✅ **Indirect Costs (Overhead + G&A): ON BUDGET**
- **Budgeted:** $10,400.00 (46.61% OH + 18.32% G&A on direct costs)

- **Assessment:** Indirect costs are baked into the $24,556 invoice. Since only $12,500 has been paid, indirect costs are proportionally also partially outstanding (estimated ~$4,200 of $10,400 still owed).

#### ✅ **Equipment, Travel, Materials, Other: ZERO SPEND**
- **Budgeted:** $0.00 across all categories
- **Actual:** $0.00
