## Financial Health Assessment

### Overall Budget Status: **MAJOR CONCERNS**

#### 1. **Significant Discrepancy Between Budgeted and Actual Labor**
- **Budgeted:** $14,156.00 (73 hours at $150/hr + 29.28% fringe)
- **Actual Charged (Jan–Aug 2025):** $969.59 (only 5 hours logged)
- **Variance:** $(13,186.41) underspent, or only 6.8% of budgeted labor utilized

**Assessment:** This dramatic underspend suggests one or more of the following:
- Labor hours are being charged under a different cost code or project
- Work is being performed but not accurately logged in the tracking system
- Schedule was significantly compressed, with bulk of work completed outside the tracked period (e.g., front-loaded in January or back-loaded in September after final payment)
- Reporting lag: Hours may be recorded after the August tracking cutoff

#### 2. **Invoicing vs. Payment Mismatch**
- **Full Invoice Amount:** $24,556.00 (issued 04/14/2025)
- **Payment Received (Sept 2025):** $12,500.00 (50.9% of invoice)
- **Outstanding Balance:** $12,056.00 (49.1% of invoice) — **past due as of October 3, 2025**

**Assessment:** 
- Invoice was due 05/14/2025 (Net 30); payment received 4+ months later (September)
- Remaining balance is now overdue and represents a cash flow risk
- Milestone-based payment structure (tied to CLIN0002 Final Report submission) suggests payments may be staggered; full payment terms unclear

#### 3. **Burn Rate Analysis**
