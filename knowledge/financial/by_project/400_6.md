### Key Financial Observations

1. **Fixed-Price vs. Time-and-Materials Billing:**
   - Contract is **fixed-price deliverable-based** (CLIN0002 Final Report), not T&M
   - Invoice 1664 ($24,556) issued upon completion and submission of final report (04/16/2025), not based on actual hours worked
   - Only 5 labor hours logged Jan–Aug 2025 ($969.59), yet full invoice ($24,556) was submitted
   - This is normal for SBIR Phase I contracts with milestone-based invoicing

2. **Schedule/Burn Rate Anomaly:**
   - **Budgeted Labor Hours:** 73 hours over 3 months (Feb 3–May 2, 2025)
   - **Actual Hours Charged:** 5 hours (1 hour Jan + 4 hours April)
   - **Interpretation:** Work was likely backend-loaded (concentrated Apr–May), with administrative/report-writing effort charged in April. Most effort may be non-billable under-the-hood work, or hours were reallocated to other cost structures not visible in this tracking.
   - **Risk:** If budgeted 73 hours were not completed, there may be unrecovered labor costs absorbed by BST or offset by overhead rates.

3. **Payment Reconciliation:**
   - Invoice issued: 04/22/2025 ($24,556; Net 30 terms, due 05/14/2025)
   - First payment received: 09/2025 ($12,500; 50.9% of invoice)
   - **Outstanding balance:** $12,056 (49.1% of invoice still unpaid as of Oct 2025)
   - **Payment Status:** 4+ months late against Net 30 terms; payment is in arrears
   - **Interim payment pattern:** Suggests milestone-based or progress payment schedule; final tranche may be pending Phase II award decision or final reconciliation

4. **Cost Control & Spending Discipline:**
   - **Zero overruns** in any budget category through project completion
   - **Zero unbudgeted expenses** (travel, equipment, materials all planned at $0 and spent $0)
   - **Conservative scope execution:** No scope creep or unplanned costs
   - **Indirect cost structure sustainable:** Overhead (46.61%) and G&A (18.32%) rates applied to base are locked into fixed-price; no cost overrun risk
