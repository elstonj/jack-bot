## Financial Health Assessment

### Overall Budget Status: **SIGNIFICANTLY UNDER-SPENT (3.9% utilization as of Aug 2025)**

**Positive Indicators:**
- ✅ **Well under budget:** Only $969.59 of $24,556.00 approved budget expended through August 2025 (96.1% remaining)
- ✅ **Deliverable completed on schedule:** CLIN0002 final report submitted 04/16/2025, within PoP window (02/03–05/02/2025)
- ✅ **Payment received:** $12,500.00 interim payment received September 2025; demonstrates client confidence in deliverable quality
- ✅ **No cost overruns:** No budget category exceeded; no CLINs overrun

**Concerns & Observations:**

1. **Dramatic Labor Underutilization:**
   - Budgeted: 73 hours @ $150/hr = $10,950 (base labor)
   - Actual: 5 hours = $969.59 through August 2025 (6.8% of budgeted labor)
   - **Gap:** 68 hours unaccounted for; only ~7% of budgeted effort delivered
   - **Possible causes:** 
     - Schedule compression (work concentrated outside tracked period)
     - Reporting lag or milestone-based billing model (payment in Sept 2025 suggests work may have occurred but invoiced as lump sum)
     - Staffing/availability constraints

2. **Payment Reconciliation Gap:**
   - Invoice amount: $24,556.00 (full contract value)
   - Actual labor spend: $969.59
   - Indirect costs applied at invoice: Embedded in $24,556 (should include $10,400 OH + G&A per approved budget)
   - **Outstanding:** $12,056.00 as of October 2025 (50.9% of contract value still unpaid)
   - **Interpretation:** September payment of $12,500 appears to be first installment; second payment of ~$12,056 likely pending final deliverable acceptance or contract closeout

3. **Indirect Cost Recovery Lag:**
   - Approved indirect costs: $10,400 (42.4% of total contract value)
   - These appear embedded in the full invoice ($24,556) but are not separately tracked in the monthly spend spreadsheet
   - **Risk:** If actual labor is only $969.59, indirect costs cannot be legitimately recovered at the full 46.61% OH + 18.32% G&A rates on such minimal labor base
   - **Clarification needed:** Whether invoice amount ($24,556) represents actual billable costs or is a fixed-price deliverable contract

4. **Burn Rate vs. Period of Performance:**
   - PoP: 3 months (02/03–05/02/2025)
   - Tracked actual spending: 5 hours over Jan–Aug 2025 (~$970)
   - **Monthly burn rate:** ~$323/month (if amortized); far below monthly budget of ~$8,185 ($24,556 ÷ 3)
   - **Assessment:** Either work is **backend-loaded** (concentrated at end of PoP and invoiced in lump sum April 2025) or **significantly behind schedule**

5. **Outstanding Balance Risk:**
   - Remaining to be paid: $12,056 (49.1% of contract)
   - No documented payment schedule for remaining balance
   - **Risk:** Late payment beyond Net 30 terms (due 05/14/2025, but first payment not until 09/2025 = 4+ months delay)
