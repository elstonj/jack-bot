# Project 042-1 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** S2 UAS Platform Components for Stanford Structural Health Monitoring Research (Fly-by-Feel Technology Integration)
- **Client/Agency:** Stanford University, Department of Aeronautics and Astronautics (Prof. Fu-Kuo Chang)
- **Contract Type:** Commercial equipment purchase / collaborative research arrangement
- **Period of Performance:** May 2, 2025 – June 2025 (invoice period); delivery requested by June 17, 2025
- **Brief Description:** Provision of S2 UAS platform components (right wing, fuselage with avionics, nose cone, payload development kit) as spare inventory from Black Swift Technologies operational fleet to support Stanford's structural health monitoring research initiative. Components will be integrated with "fly-by-feel" sensor technology and demonstrated at the International Workshop on Structural Health Monitoring (IWSHM) in September 2025.
