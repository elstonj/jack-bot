**Negotiated Price Reduction:**
- **Component List Value (retail pricing):** $18,283.26
- **Final Invoiced Amount:** $5,000.00
- **Discount Applied:** $13,283.26 (~73% reduction)
- **Source:** Drive Budget Doc; email chain (04/21–05/05/2025)

**Rationale for Discount:**
1. Components sourced from BST operational spare inventory (not new components)
2. Expedited fulfillment to meet Stanford's delivery timeline
3. Strategic partnership opportunity:
   - BST positioned as collaborator in future Stanford funding proposals
   - S2 platform showcase at IWSHM conference (Sept 2025) with ~600 participants including DoD and aviation industry
   - Joint "fly-by-feel" technology development initiative

**Negotiation Timeline:**
- **04/21–04/30/2025:** Stanford requested full S2 aircraft loan; BST offered components as alternative
- **05/02/2025:** Invoice issued; Stanford delivery budget ceiling ~$5,000.00
- **05/05/2025:** Negotiation finalized
- **06/12/2025:** PO 63769697 formally issued by Stanford (after invoice date)

**Original BST Fleet Replacement Context:** Per Jack Elston (BST CEO) email (04/30/2025), BST requested $6,000 compensation to replace these components in operational fleet; Stanford negotiated to $5,000.00.
