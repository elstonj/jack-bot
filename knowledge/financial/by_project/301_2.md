# Project 301-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2020 WPO Hurricane Phase II (NOAA Tropical Cyclone Research Advancement)
- **Client/Agency:** University of Miami (CIMAS) on behalf of NOAA/AOML Hurricane Research Division
- **Contract Type:** Services Agreement (Research & Development)
- **Contract/PO Number:** PO-0000470189 (dated 02/02/2022)
- **Principal Contacts:** Jun Zhang (University of Miami/CIMAS); Dr. Joseph Cione (NOAA/AOML/HRD)
- **Period of Performance:** 10/31/2021 – 06/30/2025 (extended via Addendum 4, 10/24/2024)
- **Brief Description:** Development, integration, and deployment of small unmanned aerial systems (sUAS) platforms (S0 designation) with integrated meteorological and oceanographic (METOC) payload for NOAA hurricane reconnaissance missions. Scope includes hardware procurement (16–20 fully assembled UAS units with Vaisala RD41 PTH sensors, IR camera, laser altimeter), non-recurring engineering (payload integration, flight automation, real-time data formatting for AirOPS/WMO HDOBS), in-field hurricane deployment support, and travel for calibration flights and storm intercepts.
