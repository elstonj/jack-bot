# Project 301-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2020 WPO Hurricane Phase II (NOAA Tropical Cyclone Research Advancement)
- **Client/Agency:** University of Miami (CIMAS) on behalf of NOAA/AOML Hurricane Research Division
- **Contract Type:** Services Agreement (Research & Development); Government contract
- **PO Number:** PO-0000470189 (dated 02/02/2022)
- **Period of Performance:** 10/31/2021 – 06/30/2025 (extended via Addendum 4, dated 10/24/2024)
- **Principal Contacts:** Jun Zhang (University of Miami/CIMAS); Dr. Joseph Cione (NOAA/AOML/HRD)

**Brief Description of Scope:**
Development, integration, and deployment of small unmanned aircraft systems (sUAS) platforms equipped with integrated meteorological (METOC) payload for real-time tropical cyclone observation and data collection. Deliverables include 16–20 fully assembled S0 UAS units with Vaisala RD41 PTH sensors, IR sensors, laser altimeter, and video/image capture; non-recurring engineering (NRE) for payload integration and NOAA real-time data requirements (AirOPS, WMO HDOBS formatting, VDM generation); in-field support and hurricane deployment operations; and project documentation.
