# Project 301-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2020 WPO Hurricane Phase II (NOAA Tropical Cyclone Research Advancement)
- **Client/Agency:** University of Miami (on behalf of NOAA/AOML Hurricane Research Division)
- **Contract Type:** Services Agreement (Research & Development)
- **PO Number:** PO-0000470189 (dated 02/02/2022)
- **Period of Performance:** 10/31/2021 – 06/30/2025
- **Principal Contacts:** Jun Zhang (University of Miami/CIMAS); Dr. Joseph Cione (NOAA/AOML/HRD)

**Scope:** Design, development, and deployment of S0 small uncrewed aircraft systems (sUAS) with integrated METOC (meteorological and oceanographic) payload for in-storm hurricane research. Deliverables include 16–20 fully assembled units with Vaisala RD41 PTH sensors, IR sensors, laser altimeter, and video/image capture capability; non-recurring engineering (NRE) for payload integration and real-time NOAA data formatting (AirOPS, WMO HDOBS, VDM); and field support for hurricane deployments (Ernesto, Francine, Helene, Milton in 2024).
