# Project 301-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2020 WPO Hurricane Phase II
- **Client/Agency:** University of Miami (on behalf of NOAA/AOML Hurricane Research Division)
- **Contract Type:** Services Agreement (Research & Development); NOAA tropical cyclone research advancement
- **PO Number:** PO-0000470189 (dated 02/02/2022)
- **Period of Performance:** 10/31/2021 – 06/30/2025 (extended via Addendum 4, October 2024)
- **Principal Contacts:** Jun Zhang (University of Miami/CIMAS); Dr. Joseph Cione (NOAA/AOML/HRD)
- **Brief Description:** Development and deployment of unmanned aerial systems (UAS) platforms with integrated meteorological payload (Vaisala RD41 PTH sensors, IR sensors, laser altimeter, video/imaging) for real-time hurricane research data collection. Includes hardware procurement, engineering support, payload integration, real-time data formatting (AirOPS, WMO HDOBS, VDM), field deployments, and documentation.
