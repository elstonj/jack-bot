# Project 301-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2020 WPO Hurricane Phase II (NOAA Tropical Cyclone Research Advancement)
- **Client/Agency:** University of Miami (on behalf of NOAA/AOML Hurricane Research Division)
- **Contract Type:** Services Agreement (Research & Development)
- **Contract/PO Number:** PO-0000470189 (issued 02/02/2022)
- **Period of Performance:** 10/31/2021 – 06/30/2025 (extended via Addendum 4, 10/24/2024)
- **Principal Contacts:** Jun Zhang (University of Miami/CIMAS); Dr. Joseph Cione (NOAA/AOML/HRD)

**Scope Summary:** Development and deployment of small unmanned aircraft systems (sUAS) platforms with integrated METOC (meteorological/oceanographic) payload for hurricane research. Deliverables include 16–20 fully assembled S0 UAS units with Vaisala RD41 sensors, IR imaging, laser altimeter, and video capability; non-recurring engineering for payload integration and NOAA real-time data formatting; in-field deployment support for hurricane operations (Ernesto, Francine, Helene, Milton, 2024); and data processing/algorithm development.
