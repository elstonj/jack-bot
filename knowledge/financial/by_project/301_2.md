# Project 301-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2020 WPO Hurricane Phase II (NOAA Tropical Cyclone Research Advancement)
- **Client/Agency:** University of Miami (on behalf of NOAA/AOML Hurricane Research Division)
- **Contract Type:** Services Agreement (Research & Development); NOAA tropical cyclone research
- **PO Number:** PO-0000470189 (dated 02/02/2022)
- **Period of Performance:** 10/31/2021 – 06/30/2025 (extended via Addendum 4, dated 10/24/2024)
- **Principal Contacts:** Jun Zhang (University of Miami/CIMAS); Dr. Joseph Cione (NOAA/AOML/HRD)
- **Brief Description:** Development, integration, and deployment of small uncrewed aircraft systems (sUAS) platforms (S0) equipped with METOC (meteorological/oceanographic) payload for real-time hurricane research data collection. Includes full automation, NOAA real-time data requirements compliance, and in-storm deployment support.
