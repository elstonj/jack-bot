# Project 301-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name**: 2020 WPO Hurricane Phase II (NOAA Tropical Cyclone Research Advancement)
- **Client/Agency**: University of Miami (on behalf of NOAA/AOML Hurricane Research Division)
- **Contract Type**: Services Agreement (Research & Development)
- **Period of Performance**: 10/31/2021 – 06/30/2025 (extended via Addendum 4, 10/24/2024)
- **PO Number**: PO-0000470189 (dated 02/02/2022)
- **Principal Contacts**: Jun Zhang (University of Miami/CIMAS); Dr. Joseph Cione (NOAA/AOML/HRD)

**Scope**: Development and deployment of small uncrewed aircraft systems (sUAS) with integrated meteorological (METOC) payload for hurricane research, including Vaisala RD41 PTH sensors, infrared imaging, laser altimetry, and real-time data transmission to NOAA standards. Includes hardware procurement (16–20 S0 platforms + Area-I MHTP integration), non-recurring engineering, field deployments (Hurricanes Ernesto, Francine, Helene, Milton), and data processing.
