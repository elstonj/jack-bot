# Project 301-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2020 WPO Hurricane Phase II (NOAA Tropical Cyclone Research Advancement)
- **Client/Agency:** University of Miami (on behalf of NOAA/AOML Hurricane Research Division)
- **Contract Type:** Services Agreement (Research & Development)
- **PO Number:** PO-0000470189 (dated 02/02/2022)
- **Period of Performance:** 10/31/2021 – 06/30/2025 (extended via Addendum 4, dated 10/24/2024)
- **Principal Contacts:** Jun Zhang (University of Miami/CIMAS); Dr. Joseph Cione (NOAA/AOML/HRD)

**Scope Summary:** Development, integration, and deployment of S0 unmanned aerial systems (UAS) platforms with integrated METOC payload (Vaisala RD41 PTH sensors, downward-looking IR sensor, laser altimeter, video/image capture) for hurricane research. Includes Area-I MHTP integration support, non-recurring engineering for full automation and NOAA real-time data requirements (AirOPS, WMO HDOBS formatting, VDM generation), in-field hurricane deployments, and project documentation.
