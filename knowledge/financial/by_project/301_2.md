# Project 301-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2020 WPO Hurricane Phase II (NOAA Tropical Cyclone Research Advancement)
- **Client/Agency:** University of Miami (CIMAS) on behalf of NOAA/AOML Hurricane Research Division
- **Contract Type:** Services Agreement (Research & Development)
- **Contract/PO Number:** PO-0000470189 (dated 02/02/2022)
- **Period of Performance:** 10/31/2021 – 06/30/2025 (extended via Addendum 4, dated 10/24/2024)
- **Principal Contacts:** Jun Zhang (University of Miami/CIMAS); Dr. Joseph Cione (NOAA/AOML/HRD)

**Brief Description:** Black Swift Technologies is developing and deploying small unmanned aircraft systems (sUAS) platforms integrated with meteorological sensors (Vaisala RD41 PTH, IR sensors, laser altimeter) for real-time tropical cyclone research. Scope includes hardware delivery (16–20 fully assembled S0 UAS units), non-recurring engineering (NRE), payload integration, NOAA real-time data requirements (AirOPS, WMO HDOBS formatting), and field deployment support for hurricane missions (Ernesto, Francine, Helene, Milton in 2024).
