# Project 301-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2020 WPO Hurricane Phase II (NOAA Tropical Cyclone Research Advancement)
- **Client/Agency:** University of Miami (CIMAS), on behalf of NOAA/AOML Hurricane Research Division
- **Contract Type:** Services Agreement (Research & Development)
- **PO Number:** PO-0000470189 (dated 02/02/2022)
- **Period of Performance:** 10/31/2021 – 06/30/2025 (extended via Addendum 4, dated 10/24/2024)
- **Principal Contacts:** Jun Zhang (University of Miami/CIMAS); Dr. Joseph Cione (NOAA/AOML/HRD)

**Scope:** Development, integration, and deployment of S0 sUAS (small unmanned aircraft systems) platforms with integrated METOC payload (Vaisala RD41 PTH sensors, downward-looking IR sensor, laser altimeter, video/image capture) for real-time hurricane and tropical cyclone data collection. Includes 16–20 fully assembled units, non-recurring engineering for payload integration and NOAA real-time data requirements (AirOPS, WMO HDOBS formatting, VDM generation), field support, hurricane deployments (Ernesto, Francine, Helene, Milton), and documentation.
