# Project 301-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2020 WPO Hurricane Phase II (NOAA Tropical Cyclone Research Advancement)
- **Client/Agency:** University of Miami (CIMAS) on behalf of NOAA/AOML Hurricane Research Division
- **Contract Type:** Services Agreement (Research & Development)
- **Contract/PO Number:** PO-0000470189 (issued 02/02/2022)
- **Principal Contacts:** Jun Zhang (University of Miami/CIMAS); Dr. Joseph Cione (NOAA/AOML/HRD)
- **Period of Performance:** 10/31/2021 – 06/30/2025 (extended via Addendum 4, 10/24/2024)
- **Brief Description:** Development and deployment of small unmanned aircraft systems (sUAS, designated "S0") integrated with NOAA meteorological observing (METOC) payload for hurricane research. Includes Vaisala RD41 PTH sensors, infrared imaging, laser altimeter, and real-time data transmission (AirOPS, WMO HDOBS, VDM formats). Platforms deployed operationally in hurricanes Ernesto, Francine, Helene, and Milton (2024).
