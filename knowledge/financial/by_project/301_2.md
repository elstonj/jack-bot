# Project 301-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2020 WPO Hurricane Phase II (NOAA Tropical Cyclone Research Advancement)
- **Client/Agency:** University of Miami (on behalf of NOAA/AOML Hurricane Research Division)
- **Contract Type:** Services Agreement (Research & Development)
- **PO Number:** PO-0000470189 (dated 02/02/2022)
- **Period of Performance:** 10/31/2021 – 06/30/2025 (extended via Addendum 4, dated 10/24/2024)
- **Principal Contacts:** Jun Zhang (University of Miami/CIMAS); Dr. Joseph Cione (NOAA/AOML/HRD)

**Scope:** Development, integration, and deployment of S0 sUAS (small Unmanned Aircraft Systems) platforms with integrated METOC payload (Vaisala RD41 PTH sensors, IR sensor, laser altimeter, video/image capability) for real-time tropical cyclone data collection. Deliverables include 16–20 fully assembled units, non-recurring engineering for payload integration and automation, real-time data formatting (AirOPS, WMO HDOBS, VDM), and in-field hurricane deployment support.
