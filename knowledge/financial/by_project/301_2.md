# Project 301-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2020 WPO Hurricane Phase II
- **Client/Agency:** University of Miami (CIMAS) on behalf of NOAA/AOML Hurricane Research Division
- **Contract Type:** Services Agreement (Research & Development); NOAA tropical cyclone research advancement
- **PO Number:** PO-0000470189 (dated 02/02/2022)
- **Period of Performance:** 10/31/2021 – 06/30/2025 (extended via addendum)
- **Principal Contacts:** Jun Zhang (University of Miami/CIMAS); Dr. Joseph Cione (NOAA/AOML/HRD)

**Brief Description of Scope:**
Development, integration, and deployment of small uncrewed aircraft systems (sUAS) platforms (S0 variants, 16–20 units) equipped with integrated meteorological and oceanographic (METOC) payloads including Vaisala RD41 PTH sensors, downward-looking IR sensors, laser altimeters, and video/image capture. Includes non-recurring engineering (NRE) for payload integration, real-time data requirements (AirOPS, WMO HDOBS formatting, VDM generation), automation enhancements, and in-field hurricane deployment support. Integration of Area-I MHTP (Multi-Hole Turbulence Probe) on Altius 600 platform.
