# Project 301-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2020 WPO Hurricane Phase II
- **Client/Agency:** University of Miami (CIMAS) on behalf of NOAA/AOML Hurricane Research Division
- **Contract Type:** Services Agreement (Research & Development); tropical cyclone research advancement
- **PO Number:** PO-0000470189 (issued 02/02/2022)
- **Period of Performance:** 10/31/2021 – 06/30/2025 (extended via Addendum 4, dated 10/24/2024)
- **Principal Contacts:** Jun Zhang (University of Miami/CIMAS); Dr. Joseph Cione (NOAA/AOML/HRD)

**Project Scope:** Development, integration, and deployment of S0 unmanned aerial systems (UAS) platforms equipped with integrated METOC payload (Vaisala RD41 sensors, IR sensors, laser altimeter, video/image capture) for real-time hurricane research data collection. Includes non-recurring engineering for payload integration, NOAA data formatting compliance, in-field hurricane deployments, and Area-I MHTP integration support.
