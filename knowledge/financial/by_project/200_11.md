- **Full Project Name:** Demonstrating Persistent IR Measurements Over Wildfires Using Small, Fixed-Wing Uncrewed Aircraft
- **Client/Agency:** NASA Ames Research Center
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase III (Research & Development Demonstration)
- **Contract Number:** 80NSSC25CA043
- **Period of Performance:** January 2025 – September 2025 (9 months)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED (contract completed November 2025)
