# Project 200-11 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Persistence Demo (NASA) — Demonstrating persistent IR measurements over wildfires using small, fixed-wing uncrewed aircraft
- **Client/Agency:** NASA Ames Research Center
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase III, Fixed Price
- **Contract Number:** 80NSSC25CA043
- **Period of Performance:** January 2025 – September 2025 (9 months)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED (contract completed and closed 11/24/2025)
- **Brief Description:** Demonstrated 24-hour persistent infrared (IR) wildfire monitoring capability using Black Swift's S2 small uncrewed aircraft platform integrated with NightFOX payload and Remote ID detection systems. Live demonstration conducted August 27–28, 2025 at Pawnee National Grassland with attendees from USFS, NASA, and USDA.
