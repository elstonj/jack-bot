### Cost Category Analysis

**Direct Labor:** 72.1% of budgeted amount tracked in QBO. All named personnel (PI, Engineer, Technician, Pilot, Consultant) show 100% allocation of their estimated labor rates, but actual hours logged differ from estimates. Asana task data shows **234:04:26 total hours** logged across the project vs. **280 estimated hours**, resulting in net underutilization of ~46 hours (16.4% under estimate).

**Materials & Supplies:** 57.3% of budgeted amount spent. S2 rental estimate was $6,000 (2 days @ $3,000/day), but actual SkyGuard Technologies invoice (#13260, 6/30/2025) was $3,120.00 for equipment (radio spectrum analyzer, screen, signal generator). Remaining $2,559.87 of materials budget not fully expended in tracked data.

**Indirect Costs (Overhead & G&A):** Proposed overhead (46.610% of labor base) = $16,270.00; G&A (18.320% of total direct costs) = $10,841.00. **Data not available from QuickBooks**—these costs are allocated into the fixed deliverable payments and not broken out separately in the actual invoices or tracking spreadsheets provided.

**Fee/Profit:** Proposed fee of $4,411.00 (6.30% per MilestonePlanBudget.pdf; alternative 7.00% = $4,901.00). Fee is issued as part of the final invoice (Invoice #1700, $4,428.00) and is **not separately tracked in QBO cost categories**.

**Overall Assessment:** The project was **fixed-price deliverable-based**, so the traditional cost-category tracking does not align perfectly with how payment was invoiced. The $74,428 contract was fully invoiced and paid in three milestone payments ($35,000 + $35,000 + $4,428), but only $25,186.18 of direct costs are itemized in the provided QBO tracking, suggesting $49,241.82 ($74,428 - $25,186.18) is absorbed in overhead, G&A, fee, and administrative costs not shown in line-item tracking.
