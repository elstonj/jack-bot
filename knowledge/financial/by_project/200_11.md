# Project 200-11 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Persistence Demo (NASA) — Demonstrating persistent IR measurements over wildfires using small, fixed-wing uncrewed aircraft
- **Client/Agency:** NASA Ames Research Center
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase III (Fixed Price)
- **Contract Number:** 80NSSC25CA043
- **Period of Performance:** January 2025 – September 2025 (9 months)
- **Project Status:** Archived/Completed (contract closed November 2025)
- **Primary Contact:** Matthew Fladeland, NASA Ames Technology Transfer Office, 650.604.3325

**Scope Summary:** Demonstrate persistent IR measurements over wildfires using the S2 small uncrewed aircraft platform and establish a scalable operational CONOPS for 24-hour wildfire monitoring. Project included development of Remote ID detection systems, NightFOX IR payload integration, and a live 24-hour persistence demonstration at Pawnee National Grassland (August 27–28, 2025).
