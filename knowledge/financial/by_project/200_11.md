# Project 200-11 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Demonstrating Persistent IR Measurements Over Wildfires Using Small, Fixed-Wing Uncrewed Aircraft
- **Project Code:** 200-11
- **Client/Agency:** NASA Ames Research Center
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase III (Fixed Price)
- **Contract Number:** 80NSSC25CA043
- **Period of Performance:** January 2025 – September 2025 (9 months)
- **Project Status:** Archived/Completed (contract closed November 24, 2025)
- **Brief Description:** Demonstration of persistent infrared (IR) measurements over wildfires using the S2 small, fixed-wing uncrewed aircraft platform, with a live 24-hour persistence demonstration event at Pawnee National Grassland (August 27–28, 2025). Project established a scalable operational concept of operations (CONOPS) for continuous wildfire monitoring integration with Remote ID detection systems.
