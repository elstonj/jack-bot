# Project 200-11 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Persistence Demo (NASA) — Demonstrating persistent IR measurements over wildfires using small, fixed-wing uncrewed aircraft
- **Client/Agency:** NASA Ames Research Center
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase III (Research & Development demonstration)
- **Contract Number:** 80NSSC25CA043
- **Period of Performance:** January 2025 – September 2025 (9 months)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED — Contract completed and officially closed November 24, 2025
- **Brief Scope:** Demonstrate 24-hour persistent infrared (IR) wildfire monitoring using the S2 uncrewed aircraft platform with integrated NightFOX payload and Remote ID detection system. Live demonstration conducted August 27–28, 2025 at Pawnee National Grassland with attendees from USFS, NASA, and USDA.
