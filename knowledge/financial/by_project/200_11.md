# Project 200-11 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Persistence Demo (Demonstrating persistent IR measurements over wildfires using small, fixed-wing uncrewed aircraft)
- **Client/Agency:** NASA Ames Research Center
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase III
- **Contract Number:** 80NSSC25CA043
- **Period of Performance:** January 2025 – September 2025 (9 months)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED (contract completed November 2025)
- **Brief Scope:** Demonstrate persistent infrared (IR) measurements over wildfires using the S2 platform (small, fixed-wing uncrewed aircraft) and establish a scalable, operational concept of operations (CONOPS) for 24-hour wildfire monitoring. Key deliverable: 24-hour persistence demonstration conducted August 27–28, 2025 at Pawnee National Grassland.
