# Project 200-11 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Persistence Demo (NASA) — Demonstrating persistent IR measurements over wildfires using small, fixed-wing uncrewed aircraft
- **Client/Agency:** NASA Ames Research Center
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase III (Research & Development demonstration)
- **Contract Number:** 80NSSC25CA043
- **Period of Performance:** January 2025 – September 2025 (9 months)
- **Project Status:** Archived/Completed (contract closeout approved November 2025)
- **Brief Scope:** Demonstrate 24-hour persistent infrared (IR) wildfire monitoring using the S2 uncrewed aircraft platform with integrated Remote ID detection and TAK (Team Awareness Kit) data downlink systems. Live demonstration conducted August 27–28, 2025 at Pawnee National Grassland with USFS, USDA, and NASA stakeholders.
