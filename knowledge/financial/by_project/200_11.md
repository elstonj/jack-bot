# Project 200-11 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Demonstrating Persistent IR Measurements Over Wildfires Using Small, Fixed-Wing Uncrewed Aircraft
- **Client/Agency:** NASA Ames Research Center
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase III (Research & Development)
- **Contract Number:** 80NSSC25CA043
- **Period of Performance:** January 2025 – September 2025 (9 months)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED (contract completed November 2025)
- **Brief Description of Scope:** Demonstration of persistent infrared (IR) measurements over wildfires using the S2 small uncrewed aircraft platform with NightFOX IR payload. Project included development of Remote ID detection systems, integration of Team Awareness Kit (TAK) data downlink, and execution of a 24-hour persistence flight demonstration at Pawnee National Grassland in August 2025.
