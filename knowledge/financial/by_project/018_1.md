# Project 018-1 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Murphy's Pond CH4 Monitoring
- **Client/Agency:** Murray State University (Dr. Bassil El Masri, Principal Investigator)
- **Contract Type:** Service contract for drone-based greenhouse gas measurement collection
- **Period of Performance:** Field campaigns scheduled for 2026 (original completion 2025-01-31; flights extended through 2026; S2 rental due 2026-06-01)
- **Brief Description of Scope:** Collection of CH4, CO2, and H2O measurements over a 5 km × 5 km area above Murphy's Pond, Hickman County, KY using Black Swift S2 fixed-wing UAS. Research focus: methane emissions from wetlands and bottomland hardwood forests; remote sensing to upscale site measurements to larger geographic areas.
