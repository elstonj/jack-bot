# Project 018-1 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Project Name:** Murphy's Pond CH4 Monitoring
- **Client/Agency:** Murray State University (Dr. Bassil El Masri, Department of Biology)
- **Contract Type:** Service contract for drone-based greenhouse gas measurement collection
- **Period of Performance:** 3 field campaigns planned in 2026 (May, July, August); original project due date 2025-01-31 (Asana); flights extending through 2026; S2 rental due 2026-06-01
- **Brief Description of Scope:** Collection of CH₄, CO₂, and H₂O measurements over 5 km × 5 km area above Murphy's Pond, Hickman County, Kentucky. Research focus is methane emissions from wetlands and bottomland hardwood forests; using remote sensing to upscale site measurements to larger geographic areas. Project includes three planned drone flights, S2 aircraft rental (2 units, S0 VTOL), training, travel, data processing, and flux maps.
- **Location:** 7192 State Route 307 North, Fancy Farm, KY (landowner Joe Wilson)

**Key Notes on Budget-to-Actual Reconciliation:**
1. **QuickBooks shows $62,084.68 as project total for [018-1]** (from unclassified.md), which is higher than the $5,057.03 sum of individually tracked expenses. This indicates QBO includes labor, indirect costs, and other allocations not itemized in the Drive expense tracking.
2. **Invoiced amounts ($62,283.66) do not match QBO actuals ($62,084.68).** This discrepancy may reflect timing differences (when costs are accrued vs. invoiced) or rounding. Drive invoices show two flights invoiced; QBO may include internal cost allocations.
3. **Travel proposed budget ($8,213) significantly exceeds current actual spend ($1,983.15).** Additional travel will be required for Flight #3 confirmation (overdue as of 2026-04-20 per Asana) and S2 rental/training (due 2026-06-01, travel not yet scheduled per Asana task). This represents a risk to budget if flights are compressed in schedule.
4. **Freight ($2,966.24) was proposed as TBD** but has been incurred at nearly 3× estimated costs for labor/travel. No contingency allocated in proposed budget for freight.
5. **Indirect costs (OH + G&A totaling $29,632 in proposal) are typically absorbed by BST** and would be reflected in invoice pricing, not as separate line items to the customer.
