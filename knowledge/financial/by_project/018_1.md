# Project 018-1 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Murphy's Pond CH4 Monitoring
- **Client/Agency:** Murray State University (Principal Investigator: Dr. Bassil El Masri)
- **Contract Type:** Service contract for drone-based greenhouse gas measurement collection
- **Period of Performance:** May–December 2026 (Funding expires 2026-12-31)
- **Brief Description:** Collection of CH₄, CO₂, and H₂O measurements via fixed-wing UAS (S2) over a 5 km × 5 km area above Murphy's Pond, Hickman County, Kentucky. Three field campaigns planned (May, July, August 2026). Research focuses on methane emissions from wetlands and bottomland hardwood forests to upscale site measurements to larger geographic areas.
