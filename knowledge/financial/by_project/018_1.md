# Project 018-1 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Murphy's Pond CH4 Monitoring
- **Client/Agency:** Murray State University, Department of Biology & Environmental Sciences (Dr. Bassil El Masri, Principal Investigator)
- **Contract Type:** Commercial service contract for drone-based greenhouse gas measurement collection
- **Period of Performance:** Project initiation May 2024; field campaigns conducted November 2025, December 2025; additional flights planned Spring 2026 through June 2026
- **Brief Description of Scope:** Collection of CH4, CO2, and H2O measurements over a 5 km × 5 km area above Murphy's Pond, Hickman County, KY using Black Swift S2 VTOL aircraft. Three field campaigns planned (May, July, August 2026) with data processing, flux map generation via Atmofacts, and archival. Research focus: upscaling methane emissions data from wetlands and bottomland hardwood forests.
