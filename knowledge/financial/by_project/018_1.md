## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Murphy's Pond CH4 Monitoring
- **Client/Agency:** Murray State University (Dr. Bassil El Masri, Principal Investigator)
- **Contract Type:** Service contract for drone-based greenhouse gas measurement collection
- **Period of Performance:** Original scope: 3 field campaigns planned for 2026 (May, July, August); **actual flights executed:** November 2025, December 2025; **Flight #3 confirmation deadline (2026-04-10) now overdue as of 2026-04-20**

Research focus: methane emissions from wetlands and bottomland hardwood forests; remote sensing to upscale site measurements to larger geographic areas.
