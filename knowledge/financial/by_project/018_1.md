# Project 018-1 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Murphy's Pond CH4 Monitoring
- **Client/Agency:** Murray State University (Dr. Bassil El Masri, Department of Geosciences)
- **Contract Type:** Service contract for drone-based greenhouse gas measurement collection
- **Period of Performance:** 3 field campaigns in 2026 (May, July, August); flights completed November 2025, December 2025; final reporting due 2026-01-31; aircraft rental flights extending through 2026-04-10
- **Brief Description:** Remote sensing research project to collect CH₄, CO₂, and H₂O measurements over a 5 km × 5 km area above Murphy's Pond in Hickman County, Kentucky, using Black Swift Technologies' S2 VTOL aircraft. Project supports Dr. El Masri's research on methane emissions from wetlands and bottomland hardwood forests, enabling upscaling of site measurements to larger geographic areas.
