# Project 018-1 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Murphy's Pond CH₄ Monitoring
- **Client/Agency:** Murray State University (Dr. Bassil El Masri, Principal Investigator)
- **Contract Type:** Commercial service contract for drone-based greenhouse gas measurement collection
- **Period of Performance:** Original timeline through January 31, 2025; flights extending through 2026; S2 rental due June 1, 2026
- **Scope:** Collection of CH₄, CO₂, and H₂O measurements using S2 VTOL aircraft over a 5 km × 5 km area above Murphy's Pond, Hickman County, KY (7192 State Route 307 North, Fancy Farm, KY). Three field campaigns planned for May, July, and August 2026. Research focus: methane emissions from wetlands and bottomland hardwood forests using remote sensing to upscale site measurements to larger geographic areas.
