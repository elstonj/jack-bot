# Project 018-1 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Murphy's Pond CH4 Monitoring
- **Client/Agency:** Murray State University (Dr. Bassil El Masri, Department of Geosciences)
- **Contract Type:** Service contract for drone-based greenhouse gas measurement collection
- **Period of Performance:** Project original due date 2025-01-31; flights extending through 2026-04-10; S2 rental due 2026-06-01
- **Brief Description:** Collection of CH4, CO2, and H2O measurements over a 5 km × 5 km area above Murphy's Pond in Hickman County, Kentucky using Black Swift's S2 VTOL aircraft. Three field campaigns planned for 2026 (May, July, August), with two flights completed to date (November and December 2025). Research focus on methane emissions from wetlands and bottomland hardwood forests using remote sensing to upscale site measurements to larger geographic areas.
