# Project 018-1 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Project Name:** Murphy's Pond CH4 Monitoring
- **Client/Agency:** Murray State University (Dr. Bassil El Masri, Principal Investigator)
- **Contract Type:** Service contract for drone-based greenhouse gas measurement collection
- **Period of Performance:** 3 field campaigns planned for 2026 (May, July, August); original project due date 2025-01-31 (per Asana, appears to have been extended; flights executing into 2026)
- **Brief Description:** Collection of CH4, CO2, and H2O measurements over a 5 km × 5 km area above Murphy's Pond, Hickman County, KY to measure methane emissions from wetlands and bottomland hardwood forests using remote sensing to upscale site measurements to larger geographic areas. Black Swift Technologies providing drone operations (S2 UAS), data collection, processing, and flux analysis.
