# Project 018-1 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Murphy's Pond CH4 Monitoring
- **Client/Agency:** Murray State University, Department of Biology (Dr. Bassil El Masri)
- **Contract Type:** Service contract for drone-based greenhouse gas measurement collection
- **Period of Performance:** 3 field campaigns planned for 2026 (May, July, August); original project completion target 2025-01-31, extended through 2026 (S2 rental due 2026-06-01)
- **Brief Description of Scope:** Collection of CH₄, CO₂, and H₂O measurements via Black Swift S2 unmanned aircraft over a 5 km × 5 km area above Murphy's Pond, Hickman County, Kentucky. Research focus: methane emissions from wetlands and bottomland hardwood forests using remote sensing to upscale site measurements to larger geographic areas.
