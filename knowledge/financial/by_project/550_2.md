# Project 550-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** N25A-T025 Expendable Air-sea Profiling Observations in Hazardous Weather Conditions
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Department of the Navy (Office of Naval Research)
- **Contract Type:** Navy STTR (Small Business Technology Transfer) Phase I with Option Period
- **Contract Number:** N6833525C0270
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies, LLC
- **Period of Performance:**
  - Base Period: July 22, 2025 – January 7, 2026
  - Option Period: January 7, 2026 – July 6, 2026
  - Final Deliverable Due: September 1, 2026
- **Status:** Active (Option Period currently in progress as of June 2026)
- **Brief Description of Scope:** Development and operational testing of the S0 platform (expendable unmanned aircraft system) for Navy use in collecting hazardous weather observations in air-sea environments. Work includes refinement of the S0 platform for operational deployment, collaboration with academic partners (ERAU, Old Dominion University, University of Miami) on aerodynamic and observational capabilities, and delivery of technical reports and operational readiness assessments.
