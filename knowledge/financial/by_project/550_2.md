## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** N25A-T025 Expendable Air-sea Profiling Observations in Hazardous Weather Conditions
- **Client/Agency:** United States Department of the Navy, Office of Naval Research
- **Contract Type:** Navy STTR (Small Business Technology Transfer) Phase I with Option Period
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies, LLC
- **Contract Number:** N6833525C0270
- **Period of Performance:**
  - Base Period: July 22, 2025 – January 7, 2026 ✓ **COMPLETED**
  - Option Period: January 7, 2026 – July 6, 2026 (in progress; technical work 100% complete as of April 17, 2026)
  - Final Deliverable Due: September 1, 2026
- **Brief Description of Scope:** Development of expendable uncrewed aircraft systems for profiling hazardous weather conditions in air-sea environments. Project includes technical research, subcontractor coordination with Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University (ERAU), University of Miami, and Old Dominion University, and delivery of progress reports, final reports, and TABA (Technical Accomplishment & Barriers Analysis) documentation.
