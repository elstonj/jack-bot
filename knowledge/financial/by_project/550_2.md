# Project 550-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** N25A-T025 Expendable Air-sea Profiling Observations in Hazardous Weather Conditions
- **Client/Agency:** United States Navy, Department of Naval Research (Office of Naval Research)
- **Contract Number:** N6833525C0270
- **Contract Type:** Navy STTR (Small Business Technology Transfer) Phase I with Option Period
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies, LLC
- **Period of Performance:**
  - Base Period: July 7, 2025 – January 6, 2026
  - Option Period: January 7, 2026 – July 6, 2026
  - Final Deliverable Due: September 1, 2026
- **Project Status:** Active (option period in progress)

**Brief Description:** Development and field validation of expendable air-sea profiling observations platforms for operational Navy use in hazardous weather conditions. Technical work includes refinement of the S0 platform for Navy operational deployment, collaboration with subcontractors (Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University, Old Dominion University, University of Miami) on aeronautical engineering and atmospheric sampling, and delivery of systematic progress and final reports tied to fixed-price deliverables (CLINs).
