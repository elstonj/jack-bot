# Project 550-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** N25A-T025 Expendable Air-sea Profiling Observations in Hazardous Weather Conditions
- **Client/Agency:** United States Navy, Department of Naval Research (Office of Naval Research)
- **Contract Type:** Navy STTR (Small Business Technology Transfer) Phase I with Option Period
- **Contract Number:** N6833525C0270
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies, LLC
- **Period of Performance:** 
  - Base Period: July 7, 2025 – January 6, 2026 (6 months)
  - Option Period: January 7, 2026 – July 6, 2026 (6 months)
  - Final deliverable due: September 1, 2026
- **Project Status:** Active (option period currently in progress)
- **Brief Description:** Development and refinement of the S0 platform (expendable UAS) for operational Navy use in hazardous weather conditions, including air-sea profiling observations. Technical work includes JSB simulation development, ERAU and ODU subcontractor research, and University of Miami data analysis support (funded through ERAU).
