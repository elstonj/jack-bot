# Project 550-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** N25A-T025 Expendable Air-sea Profiling Observations in Hazardous Weather Conditions
- **Client/Agency:** United States Navy, Department of Naval Research (Office of Naval Research)
- **Contract Number:** N6833525C0270
- **Contract Type:** Navy STTR (Small Business Technology Transfer) Phase I with Option Period
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies, LLC
- **Period of Performance:**
  - Base Period: July 22, 2025 – January 7, 2026
  - Option Period: January 7, 2026 – July 6, 2026
  - Final deliverable due: September 1, 2026
- **Project Status:** Active (Option period currently in progress as of April 2026)
- **Brief Description:** Development and operational validation of expendable air-sea profiling observations platforms for hazardous weather operations using Black Swift Technologies' S0 unmanned aircraft system (UAS). Research involves collaboration with subcontractors at Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University, Old Dominion University, and University of Miami to refine S0 platform for operational Navy use.
