# Project 550-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** N25A-T025 Expendable Air-sea Profiling Observations in Hazardous Weather Conditions
- **Client/Agency:** United States Navy, Department of Naval Research (Office of Naval Research)
- **Contract Type:** Navy Small Business Technology Transfer (STTR) Phase I with Option Period
- **Contract Number:** N6833525C0270
- **Contract Award Date:** July 22, 2025
- **Period of Performance:**
  - Base Period: July 7, 2025 – January 6, 2026 ✓ **COMPLETED**
  - Option Period: January 7, 2026 – July 6, 2026 (in progress)
  - Final deliverable due: September 1, 2026
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies, LLC
- **Brief Description of Scope:** Development and testing of expendable air-sea profiling observations systems for use in hazardous weather conditions. Technical work includes design, prototyping, and validation of UAS-based sensor platforms with planned NASA Glenn Ice Research Tunnel testing (Phase II). Phase I focuses on feasibility and technical approach; Phase II expands to full development with subcontractor support from Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University, University of Miami, and Old Dominion University.
