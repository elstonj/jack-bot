**Brief Description of Scope:**
Three main task areas supporting Unmanned Experts' autonomous sUAS swarming technology:
1. Autonomous sUAS integration — design, iteration, and build of autonomous swarming capability with multiple aircraft
2. Large aircraft integration — design and integration of swarm release mechanism for mothership deployment
3. Transition & Commercialization Preparations — support for licensing, regulatory comprehension, and commercialization planning

Key technical elements included integration of UMEX Air Commons swarming hardware with BST autopilot systems (FMS, PNT, V2V communication), V2V swarming capabilities on S0 platforms, air-dropped deployment from mothership aircraft, and custom component mounts/housing designs for S0 VTOL and S0 AD platforms.
