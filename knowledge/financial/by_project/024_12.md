# Project 024-12 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
**Project Code:** 024-12  
**Primary Project:** Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS (SMM Phase II / SBIR Phase II 2022.4D)  
**Client/Agency:** Department of Air Force (DAF), Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL)  
**Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II  
**Period of Performance:** Data available 2024-12 through 2026-01  
**Scope:** Development and validation of a small UAS platform with L-band radiometer sensor suite for runway integrity assessment through soil moisture measurements. System includes auxiliary sensors for surface temperature and NDVI, with onboard and post-processing algorithms to compute soil integrity metrics.
