# Project 024-12 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview

**Project Name:** Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS (SMM Phase II 22.4D)

**Client/Agency:** Department of Air Force (DAF), Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL)

**Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II

**Program/Solicitation:** AFWERX SBIR Phase II - Soil Moisture Mapping (2022.4)

**Period of Performance:** Data available 2024-12 through 2026-01 (from QuickBooks actuals)

**Brief Description of Scope:**  
Validation of runway integrity through soil moisture measurements using a small UAS equipped with an L-band radiometer sensor suite. Key technical work includes RF noise interference resolution, flight test approval, and field testing at Sunny Slope Sod Farm to collect soil moisture and soil integrity ground truth data. Milestone #5 report dated 2024-12-11 documents progress on RF shielding solutions and preparation for local field testing.
