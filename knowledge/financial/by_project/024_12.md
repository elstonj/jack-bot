# Project 024-12 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview

**Project Code:** 024-12

**Full Project Name:** Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a small UAS (SMM Phase II / SBIR Phase II)

**Client/Agency:** Department of Air Force (DAF), Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL)

**Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II

**Program/Solicitation:** AFWERX SBIR Phase II — Soil Moisture Mapping (2022.4)

**Period of Performance:** Not explicitly stated in provided documents. Milestone #5 report dated 2024-12-11 indicates ongoing Phase II work.

**Brief Description of Scope:**
Development and validation of a soil moisture measurement system using a small UAS platform equipped with an L-band radiometer sensor suite. Primary objective is to validate runway integrity through soil moisture measurements. The system includes auxiliary sensors for surface temperature and NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index), with onboard and post-processing algorithms to compute soil integrity (cone index) from L-band soil moisture data. Technical work has focused on resolving RF noise interference issues and completing flight test approval processes, with field testing planned at Sunny Slope Sod Farm in Longmont, CO.
