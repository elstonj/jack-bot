# Project 024-12 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview

**Project Name:** Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS (SMM Phase II)

**Client/Agency:** Department of Air Force (DAF), Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL)

**Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II

**Program/Solicitation:** AFWERX SBIR Phase II - Soil Moisture Mapping (2022.4)

**Period of Performance:** Data available 2024-12 through 2026-01; specific contract end date not provided in source documents

**Brief Description of Scope:**  
Validation of runway integrity through soil moisture measurements using a small UAS platform equipped with an L-band radiometer sensor suite. Primary technical focus: RF noise interference resolution, flight test approval, and field testing at Sunny Slope Sod Farm in Longmont, CO to collect soil moisture and soil integrity ground truth data. System includes real-time soil moisture calculation and algorithms to compute soil integrity (cone index) from L-band soil moisture data and soil type classification.
