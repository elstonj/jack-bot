# Project 001-00 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
**Project Name:** Coordinated UAS-Based Plume Tracking (DTRA PLUMES)  
**Client/Agency:** Defense Threat Reduction Agency (DTRA)  
**Contract Type:** SBIR Phase I Proposal  
**Solicitation:** DTRA 2025 (DTRA252-001 PLUMES; Proposal Number T252-001-0052)  
**Submission Date:** May 21, 2025  
**Period of Performance:** 7 months (proposed)  

**Scope Description:**  
Development of an AI-integrated, autonomous UAS system for real-time detection, tracking, and 3D volumetric reconstruction of airborne plumes produced during explosive tests. The system integrates Neural Radiance Fields (NeRFs), dynamic scene reconstruction algorithms, and BST's proven S2 UAS platforms with SwiftCore™ Flight Management System avionics. Phase I establishes technical feasibility through controlled laboratory experiments and field demonstrations, culminating in performance requirements and software infrastructure for Phase II prototyping. Collaboration includes Brookhaven National Laboratory (BNL) for software development.
