# Project 001-00 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
**Project Name:** Coordinated UAS-Based Plume Tracking (DTRA PLUMES)  
**Client/Agency:** Defense Threat Reduction Agency (DTRA)  
**Contract Type:** SBIR Phase I  
**Solicitation:** DTRA252-001 PLUMES; Proposal Number T252-001-0052  
**Submission Date:** May 21, 2025  
**Period of Performance:** 7 months (proposed)  

**Scope:** Development of an AI-integrated, autonomous UAS system for real-time detection, tracking, and 3D volumetric reconstruction of airborne plumes using Neural Radiance Fields (NeRFs) and dynamic scene reconstruction algorithms. Integration with BST's S2 UAS, SwiftCore™ Flight Management System, and multi-sensor data fusion platform (EO/IR cameras, GPS/IMU, environmental sensors). Collaboration with Brookhaven National Laboratory on neural volumetric capture software development.

**Source:** Proposals & Reports (Technical Volume, Budget Justification, Data Assertion Volume 5)
