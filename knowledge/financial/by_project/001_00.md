# Project 001-00 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Coordinated UAS-Based Plume Tracking (Neural Volumetric Reconstruction for DTRA)
- **Client/Agency:** Defense Threat Reduction Agency (DTRA)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase I Proposal
- **Solicitation:** DTRA252-001 PLUMES; Proposal Number T252-001-0052
- **Period of Performance:** 7 months (proposed)
- **Submission Date:** May 21, 2025
- **Brief Description:** AI-integrated autonomous UAS system enabling real-time detection, tracking, and 3D volumetric reconstruction of airborne plumes during explosive tests using Neural Radiance Fields (NeRFs) and dynamic scene reconstruction algorithms. Combines BST's S2 UAS platform with SwiftCore™ Flight Management System, advanced multi-sensor data fusion (EO/IR cameras, GPS/IMU, environmental sensors), and closed-loop autonomous flight control. Phase I focuses on technical feasibility through controlled lab experiments and field demonstrations in collaboration with Brookhaven National Laboratory.
