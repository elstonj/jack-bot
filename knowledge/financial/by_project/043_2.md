1. **Fixed-Price Discipline:** Black Swift maintained the $450,000 fixed-price commitment despite a 26-day project extension (10/6–12/31 vs. 10/6–12/5). This indicates strong project management and cost control.

2. **Milestone Invoicing On Schedule:** Despite the 26-day extension in overall project completion, all five milestone invoices were issued on or ahead of the original milestone dates, suggesting invoicing was tied to contractual milestones rather than actual task completion dates.

3. **Reimbursable Costs Underreported:** The contract structure explicitly budgets materials, supplies, and travel at actual cost. Only $542.79 in reimbursables are documented in the provided Drive budget files. This suggests either:
   - Additional reimbursable invoices exist but were not included in the submitted document set, or

4. **No Cost Overruns or Payment Issues:** All invoices marked "Paid" in QuickBooks; no disputes, payment delays, or cost modifications documented.

5. **Burn Rate vs. Period of Performance:**
   - Planned PoP: 9 weeks (10/6–12/5/2025)

   - **Result:** Extension actually improved the per-week burn rate while maintaining the fixed-price ceiling.
