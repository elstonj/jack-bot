### Navy SBIR Magnetometer Program (N251-016)
**Status Update:** November 2025 meeting  
**Milestone:** Ongoing electromagnetic compatibility (EMC) testing on S0 UAS platform
- **Objective:** Integrate magnetometer for sonobuoy-launched ASW vehicle
- **Current Phase:** Phase 1 — isolation testing, MuMetal shielding optimization, filter characterization
- **Key Activities:**
  - Motor & battery EMI isolation testing (test rig, not full UAS initially)
  - Placement validation (nose or <1 ft forward of aircraft)
  - Data validation completed at "Sod Farm" location
- **Technical Risks:** Uncertainty on motor vs. battery EMI source; ongoing frequency domain analysis
- **Financial Implication:** Appears to be under SBIR Phase I contract (Navy); invoiced amounts in Nov–Jan period ($49K Navy SBIR noted in monthly actuals)

### ByLight Mustang UAS Program (BYL-LRFE)
**Statement of Work:** Dated 2025-11-25 / Modified 2025-12-05  
**Client:** ByLight (Bryan Sparling)  
**Duration:** 9-week, two-effort program
