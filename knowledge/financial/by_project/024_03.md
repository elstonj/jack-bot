# Project 024-03 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name**: "On-demand wildfire susceptibility from fuel moisture and vegetative index maps"
- **Client/Funding Agency**: NASA (ROSES 2024, Opportunity A.59)
- **Contract Type**: NASA ROSES research grant (Step 2 submission)
- **Period of Performance**: April 1, 2025 – April 1, 2028 (3 years)
- **Key Personnel**: Jack Elston (PI/PD #1), Maciej Stachura, Margaret Boeger (Pepperwood), Ryan Ferrell (Co-I)
- **Project Scope**: Development and deployment of small UAS platform for on-demand wildfire susceptibility mapping using fuel moisture and vegetative index data integrated with NISAR satellite and UAVSAR data. Includes 9 field validation campaigns annually and 192 UAS flights per year.

## Key Deliverables & Milestones

**Source: Open Science and Data Management Plan (OSDMP) and Proposals**

### Technical Deliverables by Year

**Year 1 (FY2025–2026)**
- Small UAS operations: 192 flights generating 2m-resolution soil moisture maps (10GB data annually in HDF format)
- UAVSAR data acquisition: 9 flights planned, 6m-resolution soil moisture maps
- Field validation campaigns: 9 campaigns (Pepperwood) with 120 personnel-hours from Ryan Ferrell per campaign cycle
- Data processing codes (IDL and Python) for soil moisture and live fuel moisture products
- Initial NISAR data integration planning (NISAR operational from October 2025)

**Year 2–3 (FY2026–2028)**
- Continued UAS operations: 192 flights/year
- NISAR satellite integration: 200m-resolution soil moisture maps at 6-day intervals (6GB data annually)
- Ongoing field validation and seasonal campaigns
- Software refinement and operational code release (projected at project completion)
- Data archival and public release upon validation

### Financial Milestones
- **Project Start**: April 1, 2025 (Invoicing begins upon Notice of Award)
- **3-Year Period**: April 1, 2025 – April 1, 2028
- **Annual Payment Schedule**: Both partners (Pepperwood and Weather Stream) budget assumes annual invoicing aligned with effort and expense realization

### Data & Science Milestones
- **October 2025**: NISAR satellite becomes operational; transition from UAVSAR to NISAR for large-scale coverage
- **Project Completion (April 1, 2028)**: All operational codes to be released publicly; all data archived to NASA repositories
- **Product Availability**: Real-time and on-demand soil moisture and live fuel moisture maps available to fire management agencies
