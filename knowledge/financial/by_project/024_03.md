# Project 024-03 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name**: "On-demand wildfire susceptibility from fuel moisture and vegetative index maps"
- **Client/Funding Agency**: NASA (ROSES 2024, A.59 funding opportunity)
- **Contract Type**: NASA ROSES research grant (Step 2 submission)
- **Period of Performance**: April 1, 2025 – April 1, 2028 (3 years)
- **Project Description**: Research project to develop soil moisture and live fuel moisture mapping capabilities using small UAS platforms, NISAR satellite data, and UAVSAR, integrated with field validation campaigns at Pepperwood Preserve. Deliverables include operational codes for fuel moisture prediction and publicly archived datasets.
