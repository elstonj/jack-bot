# Project 024-03 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview

**Full Project Name:** "On-demand wildfire susceptibility from fuel moisture and vegetative index maps"

**Client/Agency:** NASA (National Aeronautics and Space Administration)

**Funding Program:** NASA ROSES 2024, Solicitation A.59 (Research Opportunities in Space and Earth Sciences)

**Contract Type:** NASA ROSES research grant (Step 2 submission)

**Period of Performance:** April 1, 2025 – April 1, 2028 (3 years)

**Brief Description of Scope:**
Multi-institutional research project to develop on-demand wildfire susceptibility mapping using fuel moisture and vegetative index data. Project integrates soil moisture and live fuel moisture mapping from a small UAS platform, NISAR satellite, and UAVSAR data. Key deliverables include high-resolution soil moisture maps (2m from UAS; 200m from NISAR; 6m from UAVSAR), live fuel moisture products, and open-science data management with public archival upon validation. Primary field validation campaigns (9 per year) conducted at Pepperwood Preserve.
