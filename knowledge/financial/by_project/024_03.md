# Project 024-03 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview

**Project Title:** "On-demand wildfire susceptibility from fuel moisture and vegetative index maps"

**Client/Funding Agency:** NASA (ROSES 2024, A.59 funding opportunity)

**Contract Type:** NASA ROSES research grant (Step 2 proposal submission)

**Period of Performance:** April 1, 2025 – April 1, 2028 (3 years)

**Project Scope:** Multi-institutional collaborative research integrating soil moisture and live fuel moisture (LFM) mapping via small UAS, NISAR satellite, and UAVSAR platforms to support wildfire susceptibility assessment. Includes field validation campaigns at Pepperwood Preserve and integration of remote sensing data with fire management decision support systems.

## Budget Narrative & Key Assumptions

### Pepperwood Preserve (Lead Institution)

**Primary Activities:**
1. **Field Validation Campaigns (9 per year):** Direct field measurements of live fuel moisture (LFM) and soil moisture at Pepperwood Preserve. Ryan Ferrell (Research Scientist) leads campaigns with support from Makayla Freed (Field Technician). Bulk of direct labor (~1,000 hrs/year) allocated to field work.

2. **Data Synthesis & Integration:** Post-campaign data processing, validation, and preparation for integration with satellite/remote sensing data.

3. **Project & Administrative Oversight:** CEO, COO, and Senior Accountant provide contract management, invoicing, and financial oversight (~360 hrs/year combined).

4. **Outreach:** Communications Coordinator engages fire management agencies and stakeholders (~120 hrs/year).

5. **Facilities & Logistics:** Onsite lodging at Bechtel House for field teams; vehicle fuel for Pepperwood access; field equipment and consumables.

**Assumption on Duration:** Budget documents do not explicitly state whether the $198,426 is:
- Single-year amount (to be escalated annually), OR
- 3-year aggregate

Given that Weather Stream budget is explicitly extended to 3 years ($79,966.80 × 3), **it is likely Pepperwood is also a 3-year commitment, requiring clarification from NASA award notice.**

### Weather Stream (Partner Institution)

**Primary Activities:**
1. **Flight Campaign Support:** 3 remote sensing flights per year generating soil moisture data at 2m resolution (192 flights total over 3 years).
2. **Data Processing & Firmware:** Processing acquired imagery, validating outputs, supporting UAS autopilot firmware updates.
3. **Programmatic Coordination:** Annual visit to Pepperwood Preserve for stakeholder coordination and field validation support (airfare, lodging, meals).

**Duration:** Explicitly 3-year extended period (April 1, 2025 – April 1, 2028) with annual costs replicated across 3 years.
