# Project 024-03 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Project Title**: "On-demand wildfire susceptibility from fuel moisture and vegetative index maps"
- **Client/Agency**: NASA ROSES 2024 (A.59 funding opportunity)
- **Contract Type**: NASA ROSES research grant (Step 2 submission)
- **Period of Performance**: April 1, 2025 – April 1, 2028 (3-year period)
- **Brief Description**: Multi-partner research project integrating small UAS, NISAR satellite, and UAVSAR platforms to generate high-resolution soil moisture and live fuel moisture maps for wildfire susceptibility assessment. Partners include Pepperwood (field validation), Weather Stream (flight operations & data processing), and Black Swift Technologies (UAS platform & integration).
