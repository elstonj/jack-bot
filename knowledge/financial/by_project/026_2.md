# Project 026-2 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
**Project Code:** 026-2  
**Project Name:** Black Swift S0 Platform — NOAA Tropical Cyclone Operations  
**Client/Agency:** National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA), Department of Commerce, Office of Marine and Aviation Operations (OMAO), Aircraft Operations Center (AOC)  
**Contract Type:** IDIQ (Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity) 2026-2030; builds on prior SBIR Phase I (1305M218CNRMW0059) and Phase II (1305M219CNRMW0030)  
**Period of Performance:** Calendar years 2026-2030 (5 years)  
**Brief Description:** Procurement and operational deployment of Black Swift S0 small uncrewed aircraft systems (sUAS) for tropical cyclone (TC) operations. The S0 is the only air-launched sUAS platform compatible with NOAA's WP-3D Hurricane Hunter aircraft, capable of collecting critical atmospheric measurements in extreme TC environments. System includes SwiftCore Flight Management System, SwiftPilot autopilot, and SwiftTab user interface development.
