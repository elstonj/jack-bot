## Project Overview

**Project Code:** 024-06

**Project Name:** Soil Moisture Mapping (SMM) Phase II – Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS

**Client/Agency:** Department of Air Force (DAF)

**Program/Solicitation:** AFWERX SBIR Phase II – Project X22.4D

**Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II

**Period of Performance:** Data available from 2024-06 through 2026-01 (per QuickBooks actuals); Milestone 3 report dated 2024-06-11

**Scope Description:** Development and integration of L-band radiometer with auxiliary thermal and NDVI sensors onto BST's E2 UAS platform for soil moisture and runway integrity validation. Work includes sensor integration, design optimization, field testing, and algorithm development for soil integrity calculation. Milestone 3 (sensor payload design and integration) completed as of June 2024; Milestone 4 (algorithm development) in progress.
