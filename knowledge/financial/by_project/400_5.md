| Field | Value |

| **Period of Performance** | November 2023 – July 2025 (21 months) |
| **Project Status** | ARCHIVED – Successfully completed (as of July 2025) |

**Scope Summary** *(from Proposals & Requirements)*: Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures volumetric water content (VWC) to assess soil moisture as a proxy for runway structural integrity, enabling safe C-130 landing area assessment. The team (BST, OMS, and CET) adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case. Technology successfully transitioned to DIU Blue List (E2 and Soil Moisture Sensor added 2025-07-24).
