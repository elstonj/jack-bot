# Project 400-5 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** X22.4 AFWERX SBIR Phase II - Soil Moisture Mapping (Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS)
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II), AFWERX
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II (Small Business Innovation Research)
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies LLC
- **Subcontractor:** Orbital Micro Systems, Inc. (OMS)
- **Period of Performance:** November 2023 – July 2025 (21 months)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED — Successfully completed with all deliverables accepted

**Brief Description of Scope:**
Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture as a key variable for understanding soil integrity and runway suitability for C-130 aircraft operations. BST adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case in partnership with OMS and CET. System was validated through local field testing and operational testing at Tyndall Air Force Base.
