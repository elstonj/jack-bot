# Project 400-5 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS (AFWERX SBIR Phase II - Soil Moisture Mapping, Topic X22.4)
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (USAF), AFWERX
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II (Small Business Innovation Research)
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies LLC
- **Key Subcontractor:** Orbital Micro Systems, Inc. (OMS)
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Period of Performance:** November 2023 – July 2025 (21 months)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed with all deliverables accepted (Asana)

**Project Description (from Asana & Proposals):**
Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture (volumetric water content) as a key variable to understanding soil integrity for Air Force runway/landing area suitability assessment. The team adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case: mapping landing area suitability and quantifying soil integrity as cone index compared against VWC measurements. Testing conducted at local field sites and Tyndall Air Force Base.
