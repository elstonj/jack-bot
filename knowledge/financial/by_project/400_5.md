# Project 400-5 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** SBIR Phase II - SMM DoD 22.4D: Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Program), AFWERX
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II (Small Business Innovation Research)
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies LLC
- **Period of Performance:** November 2023 – July 2025 (21 months)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed with all deliverables accepted

**Brief Description of Scope:**
Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer system for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture to assess landing area suitability for military aircraft operations (C-130 Hercules). BST led the prime contract with Orbital Micro Systems (OMS) as subcontractor. The team adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case: quantifying soil integrity as cone index compared against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements. Testing conducted at local field sites and Tyndall Air Force Base.
