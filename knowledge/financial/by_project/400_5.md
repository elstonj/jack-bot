# Project 400-5 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** X22.4 AFWERX SBIR Phase II - Soil Moisture Mapping (Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS)
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (USAF), AFWERX
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II (Small Business Innovation Research)
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies LLC
- **Subcontractor:** Orbital Micro Systems, Inc. (OMS)
- **Period of Performance:** November 2023 – July 2025 (21 months)
- **Status:** ARCHIVED — Successfully completed July 2025

**Brief Description of Scope:**
Development of a UAS-based L-band radiometer system for mapping soil integrity, specifically for Air Force landing area assessment. The project involved collaboration between BST (prime) and OMS (subcontractor) to develop sensing hardware, integrate onto a UAS platform, conduct field testing, and validate at Tyndall AFB. Final deliverables and reports completed with TPOC approval.
