# Project 400-5 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** X22.4 AFWERX SBIR Phase II - Soil Moisture Mapping (Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS)
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II), AFWERX
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II (Small Business Innovation Research)
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies LLC
- **Subcontractor:** Orbital Micro Systems, Inc. (OMS)
- **Period of Performance:** November 2023 – July 2025 (21 months)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED — Successfully completed with all deliverables accepted

**Brief Description of Scope (from Asana & Proposals):**
Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture as a proxy for runway structural integrity assessment, quantifying soil integrity as cone index and comparing against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements. The team (BST, OMS, and CET) adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case: mapping landing area suitability for C-130 Hercules aircraft operations. Key technical outputs include UAS-based L-band radiometer hardware integration, soil integrity algorithms, RevD data processing web interface, and sensor protective cover design.
