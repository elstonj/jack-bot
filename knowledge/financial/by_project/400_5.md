# Project 400-5 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** X22.4 AFWERX SBIR Phase II - Soil Moisture Mapping (Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS)
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (USAF), AFWERX
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II (Small Business Innovation Research)
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies LLC
- **Subcontractor:** Orbital Micro Systems, Inc. (OMS)
- **Period of Performance:** November 2023 – July 2025 (21 months)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed with all deliverables accepted

**Project Description (from Asana & Proposals):**
Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture as a key variable for assessing runway structural integrity—mapping soil moisture as a proxy for cone index and comparing against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements. The interdisciplinary team (BST, OMS, and CET) adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case: determining C-130 Hercules landing area suitability. All 85 project tasks completed with 100% completion rate.
