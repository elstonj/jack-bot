# Project 400-5 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** AFWERX SBIR Phase II – Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS (X22.4 SBIR DoD 22.4D)
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (USAF), AFWERX
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II (Small Business Innovation Research)
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies LLC
- **Subcontractor:** Orbital Micro Systems, Inc. (OMS)
- **Period of Performance:** November 2023 – July 2025 (21 months)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed with all deliverables accepted

**Brief Scope Description:** Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity and runway landing area suitability. The system measures soil moisture as a proxy for soil strength/cone index. Technology adapted from NASA soil moisture mapping for Air Force runway assessment use case. Testing conducted at local sites and Tyndall Air Force Base. All 8 milestones completed successfully.

**Critical Discrepancy:** The proposed budget ($1,249,725) and actual QBO invoicing ($949,725 for BST + estimated $605K for OMS ≈ $1,554K) do not reconcile cleanly. Possible explanations:

1. **QBO $949,725 figure includes multiplier effect:** If this is the total contract value invoiced (including mark-ups, overhead recovery, and profit), it may exceed the base labor + materials budget.
2. **OMS payment tracking:** Asana indicates ~$605K total OMS subcontract. Asana recent activity (2025-11-19) shows final OMS payment of $24,200. Drive Invoice 1078 ($72,600, dated 5/15/2025) is one of several OMS invoices. Full OMS payment schedule not provided.
3. **Monthly tracker undercount:** Drive Budget Documents monthly tracker totals $669,364.49 spent against $1,249,725 budget. This is *internal cost accrual only* and does not represent billed amounts.

**Recommendation:** Full reconciliation requires:
