# Project 400-5 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** X22.4 AFWERX SBIR Phase II - Soil Moisture Mapping (Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS)
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (USAF), AFWERX
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II (Small Business Innovation Research)
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Period of Performance:** November 2023 – July 2025 (21 months)
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies LLC
- **Subcontractor:** Orbital Micro Systems, Inc. (OMS)

**Scope Description:** Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer system for mapping soil integrity and runway suitability. The system measures soil moisture as a key variable for assessing soil strength. BST, OMS, and CET adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case, comparing soil integrity (cone index) against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements. Project includes local field testing and validation at Tyndall Air Force Base.

**Status:** ARCHIVED – Successfully completed with all deliverables accepted (Asana)
