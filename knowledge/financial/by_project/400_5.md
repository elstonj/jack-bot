# Project 400-5 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** AFWERX SBIR Phase II - Soil Moisture Mapping (Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS)
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II (Small Business Innovation Research)
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies LLC
- **Subcontractor:** Orbital Micro Systems, Inc. (OMS)
- **Period of Performance:** November 2023 – July 2025 (21 months)
- **Project Status:** ARCHIVED — Successfully completed with all deliverables accepted

**Scope Description (from Proposals/Reports & Asana):**
Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture as a key variable for understanding soil integrity—as soil moisture increases, soil becomes more slippery and sticky while decreasing in strength. The team (BST, OMS, and CET) adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case: mapping landing area suitability. Soil integrity is quantified as cone index and compared against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements. Testing conducted at local field sites and Tyndall Air Force Base.
