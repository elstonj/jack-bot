# Project 400-5 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** X22.4 AFWERX SBIR Phase II - Soil Moisture Mapping (Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS)
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II (Small Business Innovation Research)
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Period of Performance:** November 2023 – July 2025 (21 months)
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies LLC
- **Subcontractor:** Orbital Micro Systems, Inc. (OMS)

**Project Description (from Asana & Proposals):** Development and validation of a UAS-based L-band radiometer for mapping soil integrity. The system measures soil moisture as a key variable for understanding soil integrity and maps landing area suitability for Air Force runways. The team (BST, OMS, and CET) adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use case, with soil integrity quantified as cone index and compared against volumetric water content (VWC) measurements. Project **ARCHIVED** — Successfully completed as of July 2025 with all deliverables accepted.
