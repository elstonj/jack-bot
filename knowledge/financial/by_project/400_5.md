# Project 400-5 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** X22.4 AFWERX SBIR Phase II - Soil Moisture Mapping (Runway Integrity Validation through Soil Moisture Measurements from a Small UAS)
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Air Force (DoD SBIR Phase II)
- **Contract Number:** FA864924P0003
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase II (Small Business Innovation Research)
- **Period of Performance:** November 2023 – July 2025 (21 months)
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies LLC
- **Subcontractor:** Orbital Micro Systems, Inc. (OMS)

**Brief Description of Scope:**
BST developed and validated a UAS-based L-band radiometer system for mapping soil integrity and assessing runway suitability. The system measures volumetric water content (VWC) as a proxy for soil moisture, which correlates to cone index (soil strength). The project adapted NASA soil moisture mapping technology for Air Force use cases, integrating hardware from OMS with BST's algorithm development, testing, and validation. Key milestones included algorithm development, local field testing, model refinement, and testing at Tyndall Air Force Base. Project was **successfully completed** with all deliverables accepted and archived as of July 2025.
