## Financial Health Assessment

### Overall Project Health: ✅ **CLOSED – ON CONTRACT**

**Summary**:
- Project expended **$148,711.63 of $148,729.00** contracted budget (100.0% utilization, $17.37 unspent)
- **No cost overruns** at project level; minor variances by category remained within acceptable tolerance
- All 7 planned deployments (4 Year One + 3 Year Two) completed as scoped
- Project met final deadline of November 29, 2023

### Category-Level Analysis

**1. Materials/Equipment Rental — ⚠️ OVERSPENT**
- **Budgeted**: $35,000.00
- **Actual**: $39,000.00
- **Variance**: –$4,000.00 (111.4% of budget)
- **Analysis**: Aircraft rental costs exceeded budget by $4,000. This likely reflects either additional flight days beyond the 5-day baseline per deployment, or rate increases from the rental provider. However, total project variance absorbed this overrun through travel underspend.

**2. Direct Labor — ⚠️ SLIGHTLY OVERSPENT**
- **Budgeted**: $88,116.00
- **Actual**: $90,288.00
- **Variance**: –$2,172.00 (102.5% of budget)
- **Analysis**: Labor costs exceeded budget by $2,172 (2.5%). This modest overspend reflects actual crew hours and management time exceeding initial estimates. Likely due to additional data processing hours (Ben Busby team) or extended project management (Jack Elston).

**3. Travel — ✅ UNDERSPENT**
- **Budgeted**: $25,613.00
- **Actual**: $19,423.63
- **Variance**: +$6,189.37 (75.8% of budget)
- **Analysis**: Travel costs came in **24.2% under budget**. This significant underspend offset the equipment and labor overruns. Likely drivers: fewer actual meal days than budgeted, fewer lodging nights required, or lower vehicle rental utilization.
