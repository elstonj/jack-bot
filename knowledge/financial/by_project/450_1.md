### Category-Level Analysis:

#### **1. Materials/Equipment Rental (S2 Aircraft): 111.4% of Budget — OVERSPENT**
- Approved: $35,000.00
- Actual: $39,000.00
- **Overrun: $4,000.00**

**Assessment**: 
- Year One: $1,000 over (5.0% variance)
- Year Two: $3,000 over (20% variance)
- **Concern**: Year Two aircraft costs escalated significantly. Possible causes:
  - Extended flight operations beyond 5 days/deployment forecast
  - Higher daily rental rates (inflation, market demand)
  - Additional survey days required for data completeness
  - No formal justification provided in documents

**Recommendation**: Verify with project team whether Year Two deployments required extended aircraft operations or if rates increased.

#### **2. Labor (PM, Flight Crew, Data Processing): 102.5% of Budget — MODEST OVERRUN**
- Approved: $88,116.00
- Actual: $90,288.00
- **Overrun: $2,172.00**

**Assessment**:
- Year One: $1,508 over (3.0% variance)
- Year Two: $664 over (1.8% variance)
- **Trend**: Overruns declining from Year One to Year Two, suggesting efficiency improvements.
- **Labor Categories**:
  - Project Management (Jack Elston): $1,392 (Y1) + $1,044 (Y2) = $2,436 approved; actual not itemized
  - Flight Crew Operations (Pilot, Observer, Technician): $42,000 (Y1) + $31,500 (Y2) = $73,500 approved; actual ~$74,164 (modest overspend)
  - Data Processing (NRE + Per-Flight): $5,568 + $1,392 (Y1) + $4,176 + $1,044 (Y2) = $12,180 approved; actual ~$12,124 (in budget)

**Recommendation**: Overruns are contained and within typical variance thresholds (2–3%). No corrective action required.

#### **3. Travel (Meals, Lodging, Vehicle, Overhead): 75.8% of Budget — UNDERSPENT**
- Approved: $25,613.00
- Actual: $19,423.63
- **Savings: $6,189.37**

**Assessment**:
- Year One: $3,167 under (21.6% variance)
- Year Two: $3,022 under (27.5% variance)
- **Positive Finding**: Consistent travel cost discipline across both years.
- **Likely Factors**:
  - More efficient lodging (possibly negotiated group rates)
  - Reduced per diem meal costs
  - Lower vehicle rental duration than planned
  - Improved deployment logistics/planning

**Recommendation**: Travel efficiency demonstrates good cost management; consider adopting actual per diem rates in future proposals.
