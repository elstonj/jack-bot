## Project Overview
**Project Code:** 025-04  
**Project Name:** Next-Generation Methane Monitoring for Orphaned Wells: Leveraging Drone Technology and Continuous Ground-Based Solution  
**Client/Agency:** U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) Fossil Energy and Carbon Management (FECM)  
**Contract Type:** DOE FECM Program (specific solicitation number not provided in available documents)  
**Period of Performance:** Data available April 2025 – January 2026 (ongoing)  
**Brief Scope:** Integrated methane detection and monitoring system for orphaned well sites, combining Black Swift S2 UAS with flux towers, continuous ground monitoring, and FluxMapper™ software. Project aims to advance FluxMapper™ from TRL-4/5 to TRL-6 with 0.2 kg/h methane detection sensitivity.
