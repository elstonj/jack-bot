## Proposals & Reported Technical Scope
*Source: Proposals & Reports provided*

### 1. **DOE FECM – Methane Monitoring for Orphaned Wells** (Proposal dated 2025-04-09)
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Department of Energy (DOE) Fossil Energy and Carbon Management)
- **Scope:** Integrated drone + ground-based methane detection system (S2 UAS + FluxMapper™ software + eddy covariance towers)
- **Objective:** Advance FluxMapper™ TRL-4/5 → TRL-6; achieve 0.2 kg/h methane detection sensitivity
- **Products:** S2 UAS, Quanta3 methane sensor, FluxMapper™, HOO-nah device (HPWT)
- **Budget from Proposal:** *Not provided in excerpt*
- **Status:** Proposal submitted; execution status unknown

### 2. **NASA SBIR Phase I – Adaptive & Secure Autonomy for UAS** (Proposal dated 2025-04-08)
- **NASA Program:** SBIR Subtopic A2.02 (Enabling Aircraft Autonomy)
- **ProSAMS #:** A2.02-1017
- **Scope:** SwiftCore modular flight control system; hierarchical avionics platform for UAS with ML integration and multi-vehicle coordination
- **Key Innovation:** Safe sandbox, hot-swappable components, layered architecture (5 functional layers)
- **Products:** SwiftCore Flight Management System, S2 UAS integration
- **Key Personnel:** Dr. Jack Elston (PI), Dr. Maciej Stachura, Ben Busby
- **Budget from Proposal:** *Not provided in excerpt*
- **Status:** Phase I proposal; execution status unknown
- **QuickBooks Allocation:** NASA Persistence spending visible ($109,428 total Apr–Jan); may be related

### 3. **U.S. Air Force AFWERX SBIR Phase II – Runway Soil Moisture (Contract 400-5)** (Milestone #7 Report dated 2025-04-11)
- **Program:** AFWERX SBIR Phase II – Soil Moisture Mapping (2022.4)
- **Scope:** L-band radiometer on small UAS for soil moisture → runway soil integrity (cone index); final field testing at Pond's Field
- **Products:** Small UAS + L-band radiometer + E2 sensor application architecture
- **Key Personnel:** Daniel Prendergast, Stachura, Elston
- **Status:** Active; Milestone #7 (final testing phase) in progress as of Apr 2025
- **Technical Achievement to Date:** Collected soil moisture data (wet & moderately wet conditions); requires final dry soil data (<10% moisture) to complete algorithm coefficients
- **QuickBooks Allocation:** "SMM Phase II 22.4D" — $249,725 total (May–Aug 2025 primary spend)
