# Project 550-1 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Navy SBIR: Magnetometer
- **Client/Agency:** Department of the Navy (NAVAIR, NAWCAD — Naval Air Warfare Center Aircraft Division)
  - Technical Point of Contact: Angel Ruiz-Reyes, Physicist, Advanced Technology Development Department
- **Contract Number:** N6833525C0492
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase I (Base) with Phase II Option proposal
- **Period of Performance:** 
  - **Phase I:** January 2026 – June 2026
  - **Option Period (Phase I continuation):** April 14, 2026 – September 28, 2026
  - **Phase II (Proposed):** Base: January 2027 – June 2029 (30 months); Option I: July 2029 – June 2030 (12 months)

- **Project Description:** Development of a magnetometer payload system for Black Swift S0 unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) to conduct aerial magnetic field mapping and characterization for Naval applications. Phase I option period includes kick-off certification, progress reporting, and final technical reporting. Technical scope includes airframe integration, onboard data logging system design, ground testing, hand-launched flight testing, and demonstration at Camp Pendleton (August 17, 2026).
