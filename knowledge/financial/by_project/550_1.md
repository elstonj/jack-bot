## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Navy SBIR: Magnetometer
- **Client/Agency:** Department of the Navy (Naval Air Warfare Center Aircraft Division – NAWCAD, NAVAIR)
  - TPOC: Angel Ruiz-Reyes, Physicist, Advanced Technology Development Department
  - Secondary Contact: Anthony Brescia, Avionics Engineering S&T Domain Lead
  - Address: NAWCAD, 22347 Cedar Point Road, Bldg. 2185, Patuxent River, MD 20670
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase I (Completed Jan 2026) + Phase I Option Period (Apr–Sep 2026); Phase II Proposal under review
- **Contract Number:** N6833525C0492
- **Period of Performance:**
  - Phase I: January 2026 (completed)
  - Phase I Option Period: April 14, 2026 – September 28, 2026 (active/completed)
  - Phase II Base (Proposed): January 2027 – June 2029 (30 months)
  - Phase II Option I (Proposed): July 2029 – June 2030 (12 months)
- **Brief Description of Scope:**
  Integrating magnetic anomaly detection (MAD) and passive acoustic sensing into Black Swift Technologies' S0 small UAS platform for Navy applications. Phase I demonstrated technical feasibility through laboratory testing and design analysis. Phase I Option Period focused on detailed design refinement and certification. Phase II proposes full development, field testing, and technology demonstration with Navy operational stakeholder engagement (Camp LeJeune, Camp Pendleton).
