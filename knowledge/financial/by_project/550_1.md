# Project 550-1 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Navy SBIR: Magnetometer (Integration of Magnetic Anomaly Detection and Passive Acoustic Sensing into Small UAS)
- **Client/Agency:** Department of the Navy (Contact: Angel Ruiz-Reyes)
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase I (Base) with Phase II Option
- **Contract Number:** N6833525C0492
- **Prime Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies, LLC
- **Subcontractor:** Ultra Maritime (acoustic sensors, CRT collaboration noted)
- **Period of Performance:**
  - **Phase I Base:** January 2026 – June 2026 (6 months)
  - **Phase I Option Period:** April 14, 2026 – September 28, 2026 (6 months, overlaps base)
  - **Phase II Base (Proposed):** January 2027 – June 2029 (30 months)
  - **Phase II Option I (Proposed):** July 2029 – June 2030 (12 months)
- **Brief Description:** Integration of magnetic anomaly detection (MAD) and passive acoustic sensing into Black Swift's S0 unmanned aircraft system (UAS) for Navy applications. Phase I successfully demonstrated feasibility through laboratory testing and validation of magnetometer integration, acoustic sensor integration, and motor interference characterization. Phase II option was awarded by the Navy, confirming Phase I performance.
