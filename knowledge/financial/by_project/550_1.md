## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Navy SBIR: Magnetometer
- **Client/Agency:** Department of the Navy (NAVAIR, NAWCAD)
  - Technical Point of Contact: Angel Ruiz-Reyes, Physicist, Advanced Technology Development Department
  - Secondary Contact: Anthony Brescia, Avionics Engineering S&T Domain Lead
  - Address: NAWCAD, 22347 Cedar Point Road, Bldg. 2185, Patuxent River, MD 20670
- **Contract Number:** N6833525C0492
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase I (Base completed Jan 2026) + Phase II Option with proposed Phase II base contract
- **Period of Performance:**
  - **Phase I:** Completed January 2026
  - **Phase I Option Period (Current):** April 14, 2026 – September 28, 2026
  - **Phase II Proposed (if exercised):** January 2027 – June 2029 (Base, 30 months) + July 2029 – June 2030 (Option I, 12 months)
- **Brief Description of Scope:** Integration of magnetic anomaly detection (MAD) and passive acoustic sensing into the S0 small unmanned aircraft system (UAS) platform for naval applications. Phase I demonstrated technical feasibility through laboratory testing and validation. Phase I Option Period focuses on continued development with progress and final reporting. Phase II proposal (pending award) would include field testing, platform integration refinement, subcontract support from Ultra Maritime, and procurement of magnetometer sensors (Bartington, QuSpin) and additional S0 systems.
