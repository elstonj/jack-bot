# Project 550-1 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Navy SBIR: Magnetometer
- **Client/Agency:** Department of the Navy (NAVAIR, NAWCAD — Naval Air Warfare Center Aircraft Division)
  - TPOC: Angel Ruiz-Reyes, Physicist, Advanced Technology Development Department
  - Secondary Contact: Anthony Brescia, Avionics Engineering S&T Domain Lead
  - Location: NAWCAD, 22347 Cedar Point Road, Bldg. 2185, Patuxent River, MD 20670
- **Contract Number:** N6833525C0492
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase I (completed January 2026) + Phase II Option (April–September 2026)
- **Period of Performance:**
  - Phase I: Completed (January 2026)
  - **Option Period (Active):** April 14, 2026 – September 28, 2026
  - Phase II (Proposed): Base period January 2027 – June 2029 (30 months) + Option I July 2029 – June 2030 (12 months)
- **Brief Description of Scope:**
  Development of a high-performance magnetometer sensor system integrated onto the BST S0 unmanned aircraft system (UAS) for Navy aerial survey and sensing applications. Phase I involved magnetometer and acoustic sensor design, analysis, and testing. The current Option Period focuses on detailed design, ground testing, hand-launched flight validation, and live demonstration at Camp Pendleton (August 2026). Phase II (proposed) extends development with subcontractor Ultra Maritime.
