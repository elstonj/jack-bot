# Project 550-1 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** Navy SBIR: Magnetometer
- **Client/Agency:** Department of the Navy (Contact: Angel Ruiz-Reyes)
- **Contract Number:** N6833525C0492
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase I (completed January 2026) with Phase II Option Period (April–September 2026)
- **Period of Performance:** 
  - Phase I: Completed January 2026
  - Phase II Option Period: April 14, 2026 – September 28, 2026
- **Brief Description:** Integration of magnetic anomaly detection (MAD) and passive acoustic sensing into small UAS platform. Phase I successfully demonstrated feasibility through laboratory testing and validation including S0 platform modifications, motor interference characterization, acoustic sensor integration with Cetacean Research Technology (CRT), and QuSpin magnetometer integration.
