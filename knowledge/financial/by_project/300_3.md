# Project 300-3 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2026 IDIQ (Hurricane) — NOAA Uncrewed Systems Operations Center (UxSOC)
- **Client/Agency:** NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) — EAD-OMAO, Norfolk, VA
- **Contract Type:** IDIQ (Indefinite Delivery / Indefinite Quantity); firm fixed price with T&M extensions
- **Contract Numbers:** 1305M226D0012 (base IDIQ); 1305M226F0084 (delivery order)
- **Period of Performance:** 2026–2030 (five-year contract); **current delivery deadline: July 31, 2026**
- **Brief Description:** Supply 20 BST S0 small UAS platforms with critical atmospheric measurement capabilities (pressure, humidity, temperature) for NOAA hurricane operations. Includes platform delivery, operational support, training, and mission-day operations. Builds on prior SBIR Phase I (2018) and Phase II (2019–2020) collaboration.
