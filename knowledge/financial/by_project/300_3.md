# Project 300-3 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2026 IDIQ (Hurricane) – Black Swift S0 sUAS Platform Delivery to NOAA
- **Client/Agency:** NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration), Uncrewed Systems Operations Center (UxSOC), EAD-OMAO, Norfolk, VA
- **Contract Type:** IDIQ (Indefinite Delivery / Indefinite Quantity), Firm Fixed Price with T&M extensions
- **Contract Numbers:** 
  - Base IDIQ: 1305M226D0012
  - Delivery Order: 1305M226F0084 (primary invoicing vehicle)
  - RFQ: 1305M226Q0003
- **Period of Performance:** 2026–2030 (five-year IDIQ); current delivery phase July 2026
- **Brief Scope:** Delivery of 20 Black Swift S0 small Uncrewed Aerial Systems (sUAS) platforms with advanced atmospheric measurement capabilities (pressure, temperature, humidity sensors) for NOAA hurricane research operations. Builds on prior SBIR Phase I (2018) and Phase II (2019–2020) partnership. Includes operational support, training, and mission days.
