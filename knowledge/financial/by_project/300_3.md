## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2026 IDIQ (Hurricane) – NOAA UAS Platform Delivery
- **Client/Agency:** NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration), EAD-OMAO, Norfolk, VA
- **Contract Type:** IDIQ (Indefinite Delivery / Indefinite Quantity); firm fixed price with T&M extensions for select activities
- **Contract Number:** 1305M226D0012 (base IDIQ); 1305M226F0084 (delivery order)
- **Vendor:** Black Swift Technologies LLC, Boulder, CO
- **Period of Performance:** 2026–2030 (five-year contract)
- **Brief Description:** Delivery of 20 Black Swift S0 sUAS platforms for NOAA hurricane research operations, building on previous SBIR Phase I (2018) and Phase II (2019–2020) partnerships. Platforms will support critical atmospheric measurements during hurricane season (June–November). Contract includes operational support, training, and travel for deployments on WP-3D aircraft.
