## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** 2026 IDIQ (Hurricane) — BST S0 sUAS Platforms for NOAA
- **Client/Agency:** NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration), Uncrewed Systems Operations Center (UxSOC), EAD-OMAO, Norfolk, VA
- **Contract Type:** IDIQ (Indefinite Delivery / Indefinite Quantity), Firm Fixed Price with T&M extensions
- **Contract Number:** 1305M226D0012 (base IDIQ); Delivery Order 1305M226F0084

Includes operational support, mission days, testing, training, and travel for WP-3D onboard deployments during hurricane season.
