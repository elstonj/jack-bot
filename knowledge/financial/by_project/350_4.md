## Budget Narrative & Reconciliation

### Key Observation
The project was proposed with two deployment scenarios:
- **Option 1 (2-Week):** $99,080 deployment + $10,150 NRE = $109,230 total
- **Option 2 (1-Week):** $50,740 deployment + $8,400 NRE = $59,140 total

**Actual Contract Value:** $124,230.00 — **significantly higher than either base option**.

### Cost Composition
Per Drive Budget Documents (Invoice 1655, dated 03/13/2025), the invoiced amount of $124,230.00 comprises:

| Line Item | Amount | Notes |
|-----------|--------|-------|
| **Equipment & Services** | $89,155 | S2 rental, flight operations support, aircraft update & testing (>15,000 ft), shipping |
| **Travel & Subsistence** | $16,175 | Transportation, lodging, car rental, meals & per diem |
| **Data Processing & Support** | $18,900 | Flight planning (45 hrs), permissions/documentation (30 hrs), QA/archiving (25 hrs), meetings (8 hrs) |
| **TOTAL** | **$124,230.00** | Source: USGS Invoice 1655 (03.13.2025) |

### Why Contract Exceeded Base Proposal
1. **Aircraft Testing & Upgrades:** $19,555 additional charge for high-altitude operations (>15,000 ft) validation — not in original 1-week proposal
2. **Enhanced Flight Operations Support:** $47,600 (14 days @ $3,400/day) for operational support — higher than base travel team estimate
3. **Expanded NRE Hours:** Combined flight planning (45 hrs) and support hours far exceed Option 2 proposal (20 hrs flight planning)
4. **Result:** Original 1-week deployment estimate of $59,140 expanded to full contract value of $124,230.00 ($65,090 additional) to account for enhanced pre-deployment preparation, international logistics, and aircraft certification for volcano observation at high altitude
