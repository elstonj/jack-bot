### Key Findings

**1. Budget Overrun:**
- Project actual spend ($156,500) exceeds awarded contract value ($150,000) by $6,500.
- **Source of overrun not clearly identified** in provided documents. Possible explanations:
  - Internal labor/overhead cost allocations to project P&L exceeding billable CLIN amounts

- Total invoiced to NASA: $106,211.00
- Total recorded in QuickBooks: $156,500.00
- **Unexplained $50,289 gap** — represents ~32% of project costs not invoiced to customer
