- **Client/Agency:** NASA Shared Services Center (Stennis Space Center, Mississippi)
- **Contract Number:** 80NSSC25C0155
- **Contract Type:** SBIR Phase I

- **Period of Performance:** September 29, 2025 – March 27, 2026 (6 months)
- **Project Status:** **COMPLETED** — All technical work finished; final reports submitted March 27, 2026; administrative closure pending (due April 8, 2026)

**Brief Description of Scope:**
Develop modular, layered architecture for SwiftCore avionics system enabling aircraft autonomy with multi-vehicle coordination, machine learning and machine vision-based sensing and control. Deliverables included architecture definition, messaging protocol specification, safe sandbox development, ML-enabled control loop tuning, flight/bench testing, and supervisory controller with safety thresholds.
