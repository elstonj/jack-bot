## Key Deliverables & Milestones

**Source: Proposals & Reports**

### Task B.2 – S0 Measurement Evaluation (Navy STTR Phase I)
- **Status:** Phase I Deliverable; Document last modified 2026-01-02
- **Key Tasks:**
  - Error analysis of S0 wind, turbulence, and height measurements
  - Data validation against tower data, dropsondes, radiosondes, tail Doppler radar
  - De-icing strategy development (PTC heating, Ice-X compounds)
  - Platform compatibility verification (P-8A Poseidon, MH-60 helicopter, sonobuoy systems)
  - Testing at multiple data frequencies (100 Hz, 50 Hz, 25 Hz, 10 Hz)
  - Potential NASA wind tunnel testing for de-icing validation
- **Key Personnel:** Daniel Prendergast, Josh Fromm, Maciej Stachura, James Elston
- **Financial Implications:** De-icing strategy identified as critical (propeller de-icing noted as "show stopper"); scope may require NASA wind tunnel testing (scope impact TBD).

### Task B.5 – S0 Weather Collection Concept of Operations (Navy STTR Phase I)
- **Status:** CONOPS deliverable; Document created 2025-12-16, modified 2026-01-05
- **Key Deliverable:** Complete Concept of Operations for S0 Air-Deployed (S0-AD) weather collection system for Navy naval aircraft deployment (P-8, C-130, MH-60)
- **Systems Referenced:** S0-AD aircraft, SwiftTab control tablet, SwiftCore mission planning/data download system
- **Phase II Implications:** Mission Planning Computer to be developed in Phase II; indicates ongoing scope expansion
- **Financial Implications:** Phase II will require development of laptop-based mission planning system and integrated datalink/data recorder; phase transition scheduled.
