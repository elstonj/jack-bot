## Key Deliverables & Milestones

**Source: Proposals & Reports (Navy STTR Phase I deliverables)**

### Task B.2: S0 Measurement Evaluation
- **Deliverable Type:** Phase I Technical Task Plan
- **Status:** Document modified 2026-01-02
- **Key Components:**
  - S0 data validation with target uncertainties for atmospheric parameters
  - Error analysis of wind, turbulence, and height calculations at multiple data frequencies (100 Hz, 50 Hz, 25 Hz, 10 Hz)
  - De-icing strategy development (PTC heating of Multi-Hole Pitot Probe; Ice-X or similar compounds on wings/propellers; propeller de-icing identified as critical "show stopper" issue)
  - Navy platform integration studies (sonobuoy launch, P-8A Poseidon external pod mounts, MH-60 helicopter mounts)
  - **Financial Implication:** Detailed error analysis and platform compatibility testing support contract deliverable requirements

### Task B.5: S0 Weather Collection Concept of Operations (CONOPS)
- **Deliverable Type:** Phase I Concept of Operations
