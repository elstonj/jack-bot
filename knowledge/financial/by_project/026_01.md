# Project 026-01 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Project Name:** S0 Measurement Evaluation & Weather Collection (Navy STTR Phase I – Hazardous Weather)
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Navy
- **Contract Type:** STTR (Small Business Technology Transfer) Phase I
- **Period of Performance:** 2024–2026 (Phase I active; Phase II proposal in preparation as of 2026-01)
- **Brief Description:** Development and validation of S0 air-deployed uncrewed aircraft system for meteorological measurement and weather data collection in hazardous environments. Includes error analysis, platform compatibility testing, de-icing strategy development, and concept of operations for Navy deployment from P-8 Poseidon, C-130, and MH-60 aircraft.
