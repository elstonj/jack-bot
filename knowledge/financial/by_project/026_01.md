## Key Deliverables & Milestones

**From Proposals & Reports (Navy STTR Phase I):**

### Task B.2: S0 Measurement Evaluation
- **Status:** In Progress (document last modified 2026-01-02)
- **Key Deliverables:**
  - Comprehensive error analysis of S0 meteorological measurements (wind, turbulence, height calculations)
  - Data validation plan with target uncertainties for atmospheric parameters
  - De-icing strategy development (PTC heating for Multi-Hole Pitot Probe; Ice-X or equivalent for wings/propellers)
  - Platform compatibility verification for Navy launch systems (P-8A Poseidon, MH-60, sonobuoy systems)
  - Testing at multiple data frequencies (100 Hz, 50 Hz, 25 Hz, 10 Hz)
  - *Critical Issue Identified:* Propeller de-icing noted as "show stopper" requiring resolution

- **Scope:** Describes autonomous deployment from P-8, C-130, MH-60; high-resolution atmospheric and ocean-surface data collection; fleet operational planning and hurricane tracking support
- **System Components Defined:** S0-AD aircraft, deployment sleeve, mission planning computer, SwiftTab control tablet, VHF datalink
