# Project 026-01 — Financial Summary

## Project Overview
- **Full Project Name:** S0 Measurement Evaluation & Weather Collection Concept of Operations
- **Client/Agency:** United States Navy
- **Contract Type:** Navy STTR (Small Business Technology Transfer) Phase I & Phase II; Hazardous Weather Topic
- **Period of Performance:** Phase I active through 2026; Phase II preparation underway (DCAA preaward survey dated 2026-01-26 to 2026-02-02)
- **Brief Description of Scope:** Development and validation of S0 Air-Deployed (S0-AD) uncrewed aircraft system for high-resolution atmospheric and ocean-surface weather data collection in extreme environments. Tasks include measurement validation, error analysis, de-icing strategy development, platform compatibility testing, and operational concept of operations documentation for Navy deployment from P-8, C-130, and MH-60 aircraft.
