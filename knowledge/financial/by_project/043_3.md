### Scope Description
Design, engineering, manufacture, and flight testing of two (2) Halo-platform aircraft with integrated avionics, laser altimeter (LW20/C), gimbal, car launch compatibility, and delivery of tested airframe CAD files to By Light Integration Center (Fayetteville, NC). Work includes:
- Avionics integration and mounting (BST avionics, ADS-B Ping200x)
- Airframe assembly (Halo carbon fuselage with boom/tail/surfaces)
- Chilli wingsets and tailsets
- Car launch integration and fastener replacement
- Flight testing and validation (system tests, max endurance, 6g maneuver validation)
- Sensor integration (LW20/C Laser Altimeter, SA03 Pouch Cell Battery design/build)
- Design iterations and CAD documentation
