### S2 Aircraft Rental (Primary Equipment Cost)

**Budgeted**: $35,000.00
• Year One: $1,000/day × 5 days × 4 deployments = $20,000.00
• Year Two: $1,000/day × 5 days × 3 deployments = $15,000.00

**Actual**: $39,000.00 (per Budget Tracking)
• **Variance**: $4,000.00 over budget (111.4% of budgeted)

**Analysis**: Overrun likely due to:
- Additional flight days beyond 5 per deployment in one or more cycles
- Extended aircraft rental periods during operations
- Potential weather delays requiring retention of aircraft

This was the largest single cost category and represents ~26% of total project spend.

**No other materials or equipment purchases** are itemized in the QuickBooks or budget documents for this project. Primary spend was UAS aircraft rental, not consumables.
