**Standard Indirect Rates**: 
- Fringe Benefits: 29.28%
- Overhead: 46.67%
- **Combined Indirect Rate**: 76.1% of direct labor

**Indirect Cost Calculation** (if applied):
- Direct Labor: $90,288.00
- Indirect @ 76.1%: $90,288.00 × 0.761 = **$68,709.07**

**Critical Finding**: Budget documents do **NOT** show indirect costs as separate line items. The $88,116–$90,288 labor figures appear to be **all-inclusive rates** (possibly loaded rates per the $174/hr project management and $1,500/day flight crew rates, which are higher than raw salaries). 

**Conclusion**: Indirects are **already embedded in hourly/daily rates quoted in the contract**. No separate indirect application is needed.
