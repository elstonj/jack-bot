## INDIRECT COSTS (FRINGE + OVERHEAD)

**Government Contract (Navy SBIR) — Indirects Apply:**

| Rate Type | Rate | Applied Against | Calculation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Fringe Benefits | 29.28% | Direct Labor | Labor × 0.2928 |
| Labor Overhead | 46.67% | Direct Labor + Fringe | (Labor + Fringe) × 0.4667 |
| G&A | 18.32% | Total Direct Costs | Direct Costs × 0.1832 |

**Fully-Loaded Labor = Direct Labor + Fringe + Overhead**

**Example (Stachura's 5.6 tracked hours):**
- Direct: 5.6 × $89.25 = $499.80
- Fringe (29.28%): $499.80 × 0.2928 = $146.33
- Overhead (46.67% on direct + fringe): ($499.80 + $146.33) × 0.4667 = $302.33
- **Fully Loaded Cost: $948.46** (vs. $499.80 direct)
