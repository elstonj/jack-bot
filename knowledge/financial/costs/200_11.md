### Labor Cost Assessment

✅ **Significantly under budgeted hours:**
- **Budgeted:** 280 hours (60 PI + 60 Engineer + 80 Technician + 60 Pilot)
- **Actual (Toggl + Sept entry):** ~92.5 hours (~33% of estimate)
- **Reason:** Deliverable-based contract structure. Work was frontloaded into demonstration report milestones (Invoice 1660: $35K on 3/28/25; Invoice 1699: $35K on 9/15/25) rather than hourly tracking. August peak ($21.5K) reflects intensive demonstration prep but still far below 280-hour estimate.

⚠️ **Key observation:** Labor hours significantly lower than anticipated, suggesting either:
1. Scope completed more efficiently than planned, or
2. Much of the contracted deliverable value is absorbed into fixed milestone payments rather than labor hours
