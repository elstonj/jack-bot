# Federal Projects — Shared Drive

## Overview
- **Total files:** 90,871 | **Total folders:** 3,629
- **Date range:** January 1, 1980 – January 24, 2099 (Created: November 16, 2011)
- **Primary purpose:** Central repository for all Black Swift Technologies federal government contracts, proposals, flight testing data, and project deliverables across NASA, NOAA, DoD, USGS, and other federal agencies.

---

## Folder Structure

### Top-Level Organization
The drive is organized by **project number prefix** and **agency**:

1. **Active Projects/** — Current contract work organized by project code
   - [035] Unmanned Experts (Adonis) — Navy/DoD program
   - [200] NASA — Multiple NASA subcontracts (PersistenceDemo, S2 Crater, Autonomy, AREN, USDA)
   - [300] NOAA — Hurricane research, autonomous systems, ocean missions
   - [350] USGS — Volcanic and geological surveys
   - [400] DoD Air Force/AFWERX — SBIR programs (Soil Moisture, Swarming)
   - [450] University of Colorado — Closed academic collaborations
   - [500-550] USDA, Navy SBIR/STTR — Military small business programs

2. **Proposals - In Development/** — Nascent opportunities and templates
3. **Proposals - Submitted/** — Recently submitted proposals awaiting award
4. **Completed/Inactive/Unsubmitted Projects/** — Historical archive by agency

### Typical Project Subfolder Pattern
Each active project contains:
- **Contract Documents/** — Awards, mods, signed agreements
- **Budget & Invoices/** — Cost tracking and billing
- **Deliverables/** — Technical reports, demos, certifications (often CLIN-organized)
- **Flight Testing/** — Raw sensor data, flight logs, processed imagery
- **Proposals/** — Original solicitation responses with volumes (Vol 1-6)
- **Meetings/** — Notes, recordings, presentations
- **Flight Planning/** — Area overlays, waypoints, safety plans
