## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies LLC completed the required SBA SBIR/STTR Funding Agreement Certification to establish eligibility for a Navy STTR Phase I award in the Hazardous Weather topic area. The certification confirms BST meets all applicable small business eligibility requirements and compliance obligations under SBA regulations (13 CFR Part 121).

## Eligibility & Compliance Certifications Confirmed

**Business Structure & Ownership:**
- BST is a Limited Liability Company (LLC)
- Meets ownership and control requirements (13 CFR 121.702)
- Not majority-owned by venture capital operating companies, hedge funds, or private equity firms
- Not a Covered Small Business Concern

**Personnel & Citizenship:**
- All individuals BST relies upon for eligibility are U.S. citizens or permanent resident aliens
- Principal Investigator/Project Manager will spend >50% of time (based on 40-hour workweek) as BST employee

**Size Standards:**
- Employee count: ≤500 employees (including affiliates)
- No SBA size determination currently in effect finding BST exceeds 500-employee threshold

**Work Performance Requirements:**
- STTR Phase I commitment: BST will perform ≥40% of research/R&D
- Work will be performed in United States (waiver has been granted for R&D location)
- Work will be performed at BST's facilities with BST employees (per approved application)
- No essentially equivalent work has been submitted for funding to this or another Federal agency

**Program-Specific (STTR):**
- BST (Small Business Concern) exercises management direction and control—not the partnering Research Institution
- Will immediately notify Federal agency if any portion of this award is subsequently funded by another Federal agency
