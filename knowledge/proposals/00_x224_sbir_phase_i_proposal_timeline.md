## Evaluation Criteria
Proposals evaluated on three equally-weighted factors:

1. **Commercialization Potential** - Government or private sector commercialization potential, company's SBIR commercialization track record (pre-sales, pilots, sales, revenue, active users, subscriptions, downloads), Phase II follow-on commitments

2. **Defense Need** - Appropriateness, relevance, specificity of identified Defense Need; letters of support from AF customers/end-users valued; adequacy of proposed effort; evidence of previous investment in supporting research

3. **Technical Merit & Team** - Soundness and innovation of proposed approach; differentiation from alternatives; incremental progress toward Defense Need; PI/PM qualifications and team capability
