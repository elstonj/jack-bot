## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies develops purpose-built Group 1 Unmanned Aircraft Systems designed for specialized missions in austere conditions. The S2 is a compact, modular UAS featuring vertical take-off and landing capability, deployable as airline checked baggage, with a full proprietary software stack and significant endurance for Earth observation and atmospheric sampling missions.

## Technical Approach
BST distinguishes itself as one of few US domestic companies developing complete aircraft, control systems, payloads, and ground stations in-house. The S2 leverages modular, field-swappable payload architecture and common flight management systems across platforms. The aircraft features a vertical take-off and landing (VTOL) variant for enhanced operational flexibility. BST maintains expertise in complete aircraft design, testing, and a company-developed full software stack.

## Products & Capabilities Described

### S2 UAS
- **What it is**: A purpose-built Group 1 Unmanned Aircraft System designed for specialized missions in austere conditions
- **Key characteristics**: 
  - Modular, field-swappable payload architecture
  - VTOL variant available
  - Deployable as airline checked baggage
  - Full proprietary software stack
- **Specifications**:
  - Wingspan: 3.0 m (10 ft)
  - Maximum Gross Takeoff Weight (MGTOW): 9.3 kg (20.5 lb)
  - Max Payload Capacity: 2.3 kg (5.1 lb)
  - Wind Limit: 15 m/s (34 mph)
  - Service Ceiling: 6,000 m (20,000 ft)
  - Endurance: 90 minutes
  - Cruise Speed: 19 m/s (42 mph)
  - Maximum Range: 103 km (64 miles)
  - Operating Temperature Range: -30°C to +45°C
