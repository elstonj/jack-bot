## Executive Summary
This is a biographical sketch for Stefan Metzger, Founder and Surface-Atmosphere Steward at AtmoFacts in Longmont, Colorado. Metzger is an environmental scientist with expertise in surface-atmosphere exchange, micrometeorology, and measurement systems, holding a PhD in Micrometeorology from the University of Bayreuth and extensive experience in atmospheric and ecological research.
