## Executive Summary
BST met with SOCOM to discuss integration of the S0 hand-launched UAS for operational use. Key discussion focused on getting S0 operational from a ramp with ground station battery operation and addressing certification/airworthiness requirements for SOCOM use.

## Technical Approach
- **S0 Launch**: Hand-launched from ramp (sleeve and aircraft configuration) - Priority #1
- **Ground Station (GS)**: Battery-powered operation
- **Communications**: 415 MHz VHF antenna connection confirmed available
- **GPS Usage**: Limited to range determination between operator and aircraft only
- **Repeaters**: SOCOM can equip aircraft with OTA (Over-The-Air) repeaters for extended communications
- **Testing**: Over-land testing planned to determine recoverability (noted: S0 not designed for recovery)
