## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies is a subcontractor to Curable Composites on an Air Force AFWERX Phase I proposal for field-deployable composite manufacturing enabling rapid, automated production of modular unmanned aircraft systems (UAS). BST's subcontract value is $24,750 for a 90-day performance period (February 3 – May 2).
