# Prior, Current, or Pending Support of Similar Proposals or Awards

## Executive Summary
This document is a compliance guidance statement outlining DoD requirements for disclosing prior, current, or pending federal funding for substantially similar proposals. It serves as a reminder to BST that failure to disclose such support or re-using previous proposal material can result in disqualification.

## Content Summary
This is a boilerplate instructional document, not a substantive technical or capabilities proposal. It provides:

- **Disclosure Requirement**: Applicants must reveal on the Proposal Cover Sheet if a submitted proposal is substantially similar to another proposal that is funded, being funded, or pending with another Federal Agency or DoD Component.

- **Compliance Consequence**: Failure to disclose similar proposals or awards results in **proposal disqualification**.

- **Re-use Warning**: Re-using parts or whole proposals from previously awarded proposals will likely result in the submission being deemed "essentially equivalent" (and therefore ineligible).

- **Alternative Statement**: If no similar support exists, the proposal must explicitly state: "No prior, current, or pending support for proposed work."
