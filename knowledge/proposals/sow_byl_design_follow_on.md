## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies agreed to conduct an 8-week design and build follow-on for ByLight's Mustang aircraft platform. The work includes completing an updated current airframe for demonstration and building a second carbon fiber airframe variant, with both systems to undergo flight testing. All deliverables and intellectual property transfer to the client upon completion.

## Technical Approach
Two sequential scopes of work:

**Scope 1 (2/16/26 - 3/13/26): Current Airframe Update**
- Build out battery pack using pouch cells to achieve 400+ km range
- Integrate Trillium mass model into fuselage
- Paint red tube
- Flight test to 3.7 hours duration at 30 m/s airspeed

**Scope 2 (3/16/26 - 4/10/26): Carbon Mustang Build**
- Revise mechanical designs for slightly different diameter carbon fuselage
- Construct second wing set and new carbon fuselage
- Implement removable BST avionics with provisions for EchoPilot integration (ByLight responsibility)
- Flight test to 3.7 hours duration

## Products & Capabilities Described
- **Mustang Airframe**: Lightweight aircraft platform with configurable battery/power options; demonstrates 400+ km endurance capability
- **BST Avionics**: Modular flight control system designed for removal/integration compatibility; architected to work alongside third-party systems like EchoPilot
- **Battery System**: Pouch cell configuration achieving extended endurance (400+ km)

## Use Cases & Applications
Extended-endurance aircraft platform development and testing; compatibility testing for avionics integration with external flight management systems.
