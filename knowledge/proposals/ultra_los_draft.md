## Executive Summary
This is a draft letter of support from Ultra Maritime endorsing Black Swift Technologies' Navy Phase II SBIR proposal for development of an expendable sonobuoy-launched unmanned aerial vehicle (SL UAS) for anti-submarine warfare (ASW) applications. Ultra Maritime commits to serving as technology partner, providing expertise in hydrophone and electric-field sensor design and integration.

## Technical Approach
- **Primary System**: Expendable Sonobuoy-Launched UAS designed for ASW operations
- **Sensor Integration**: Ultra Maritime will design and integrate hydrophone and e-field (electric field) sensors into the SL UAS platform
- **Partner Responsibilities**: 
  - Finalize acoustic and e-field sensor specifications (frequency, range, sensitivity, deployment requirements)
  - Manufacture prototype sensors
  - Conduct required testing

## Products & Capabilities Described

### Sonobuoy-Launched UAS (SL UAS)
- **What it is**: An expendable unmanned aerial vehicle designed to be launched from sonobuoys
- **Proposed Use**: Anti-submarine warfare (ASW) maritime surveillance
- **Integration**: Will carry hydrophone and e-field sensors for acoustic and electromagnetic detection

### Hydrophone & E-Field Sensor Package (Ultra Maritime)
- **Components**: Acoustic hydrophone and electric-field sensor
- **Configurable Specifications**: Frequency, range, sensitivity, deployment requirements (to be finalized)

## Use Cases & Applications
- **Primary Application**: Persistent maritime surveillance for anti-submarine warfare
- **Cost Benefit**: Positioned as cost-effective solution compared to existing ASW platforms

## Notable Details
- This is a **draft** letter (not final) dated for Phase II proposal submission (due Jan 12)
- Ultra Maritime is positioned as a key technology partner rather than subcontractor
- Focus on affordability/cost-effectiveness as competitive advantage
- Sonobuoy launch mechanism suggests expendable/one-way mission profile
- Letter emphasizes technology maturation for operational Navy deployment
