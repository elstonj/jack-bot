## Professional Summary
Maciej Stachura is Co-Founder and Chief Technology Officer of Black Swift Technologies. He holds a PhD in Aerospace Engineering from University of Colorado (2013), an MS in Aerospace Engineering from University of Colorado (2009), and a BS in Aerospace Engineering from University of Toronto (2006). He has been with BST since 2011 and has 14+ years of experience in UAS systems, controls, and cooperative navigation.
