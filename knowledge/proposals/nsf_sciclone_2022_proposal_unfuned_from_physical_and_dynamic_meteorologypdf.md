## Executive Summary (for reference only)
This NSF proposal develops SCICLONE (Sequential Control of Intelligent agents considering Correlation of meteoroLogical Observations and Non-Linear Entropy), a framework for autonomous multi-agent deployment of small unmanned aerial systems to improve hurricane intensity forecasting. The research integrates information-theoretic path planning, dynamic optimization, and real-world hurricane data collection to address gaps in existing hurricane observation systems.
