## Executive Summary
This is a binding IP rights allocation agreement between Black Swift Technologies and Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University governing their Navy STTR Phase I project on "Expendable Air-Sea Profiling Observations in Hazardous Weather Conditions via Small Uncrewed Aircraft System." The agreement clarifies ownership of background IP, project IP, commercialization rights, and confidentiality obligations between the small business prime contractor and academic research institution.

## Project Intellectual Property

### Ownership & Control
- **Ownership Rule:** Vests in the party whose personnel conceived or first reduced to practice the invention
- **Jointly Developed IP:** Owned jointly unless otherwise agreed in writing; BST has first option to perfect rights in joint IP
- **Revenue/Profit Allocation:** BST receives 100% of ownership rights and profits; ERAU receives 0%
- **Development Expenses:** BST bears 100% of expenses and liabilities; ERAU bears 0%

### Disclosure & Patent Requirements
- Parties must disclose Subject Inventions in writing within 6 months of first disclosure to patent personnel
- Disclosure must include sufficient detail and identification of statutory bars, marked confidential per 35 U.S.C. § 205
- Government retains irrevocable, royalty-free, nonexclusive license for any governmental purpose

## Confidentiality & Publication

**Confidentiality Obligations:**
- Background and Project IP, plus other proprietary information, held in confidence by receiving party
- No use or disclosure without disclosing party's consent, except as permitted in this agreement
- Exceptions: information becomes public without breach, obtained from independent third-party source, or independently developed

**Publication Rights:**
- Either party may publish results; must provide other party with:
  - Right of refusal on publication
  - 21-day review period for comments (given full consideration)
  - Upon request, additional 14-day deferral for patent application filing
- Publishing party may file/have filed patent applications during deferral period

## Liability & Indemnification

**Disclaimers:**
- Each party disclaims all warranties (express/implied) including merchantability, fitness for particular purpose, and freedom from infringement regarding any information, result, design, prototype, product, or process arising from that party

**Indemnification:**
- BST indemnifies and holds harmless ERAU for claims arising from BST's commercialization of STTR results
- Both parties indemnify and hold harmless the Government for claims arising from any commercialization of STTR results

## Termination & Surviving Obligations

**Termination Provisions:**
- Either party may terminate with 60 days' written notice
- Either party may terminate for material breach by other party
- Each party responsible for its share of costs through termination date and termination-related costs
- **Confidentiality, use, and non-disclosure obligations survive termination**

## Follow-On Work
All subsequent licenses, contracts, subcontracts, sublicenses, and arrangements must contain provisions implementing Project IP rights and ensuring parties and Government obtain/retain rights granted herein in all future research, development, or commercialization work.
