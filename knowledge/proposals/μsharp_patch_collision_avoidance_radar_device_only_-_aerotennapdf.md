## Product Description: μSharp Patch Collision Avoidance Radar

**What it is:**
- Miniaturized patch-based collision avoidance radar for UAVs
- Available as device-only evaluation sample
- Smallest collision avoidance radar solution for UAVs (per vendor claims)

**Specifications:**
- Power consumption: <1.5 W at 5 V
- Cost: $699.00 (evaluation/device-only version)
- Full 360° version available at $8,500.00
- CAN interface available upon request

**Key Capabilities:**

- Adaptive sensing range
- Advanced target detection and recognition algorithms
- Miniaturized design with low power consumption
