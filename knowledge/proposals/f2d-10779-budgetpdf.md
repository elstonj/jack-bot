## Executive Summary
This is a budget document for a Direct to Phase II SBIR proposal to develop an all-weather UAS system for persistent base security and rapid response operations. The proposal requests $1,249,936.69 over two years ($682,715.69 Base Year, $567,221.00 Year 2) with a 7% profit margin and includes development of a BST UAS System and sensor gimbal integration.
