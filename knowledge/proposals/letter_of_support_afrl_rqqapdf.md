# Letter of Support - AFRL RQQA

## Document Metadata
- **Type:** Letter of Support (for SBIR/STTR proposal)
- **Client/Agency:** Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL), Aerospace Systems Directorate (RQ), AFRL/RQQA
- **Program/Solicitation:** AFWERX SBIR Phase I - Soil Moisture Mapping (X22.4)
- **Date:** August 9, 2022
- **BST Products/Systems Referenced:** Small unmanned aircraft systems (sUAS), adapted platforms for swarming and soil moisture measurement
- **Key Personnel:** David Grymin, DR-02, USAF (AFRL/RQQA Research Aerospace Engineer); Jack Elston (last editor of document)

## Executive Summary
This is an official letter of support from AFRL/RQQA endorsing Black Swift Technologies for a SBIR/STTR contract award. AFRL recognizes BST's demonstrated capability to deliver reliable, robust, and highly accurate unmanned aircraft systems for demanding environments and believes BST's research and development efforts can address critical mission needs including swarming and soil moisture measurement capabilities.

## Technical Approach
The document does not detail specific technical approaches but indicates BST is pursuing platform adaptations for:
- **Swarming capabilities** for small unmanned aircraft systems
- **Soil moisture measurement** technology integration

## Products & Capabilities Described
**Black Swift Technologies Unmanned Aircraft Systems:**
- Reliable, robust, and highly accurate UAS platforms
- Demonstrated capability to fly scientific payloads in demanding atmospheric environments:
  - High-altitude operations
  - Arctic environments
  - Desert environments
  - Corrosive particulate environments
  - Strong turbulence conditions
- Track record with customers including NASA and NOAA

## Use Cases & Applications
- Advanced unmanned vehicles initiatives for USAF
- Soil moisture mapping and measurement missions
- Small unmanned aircraft system (sUAS) swarming operations
- Scientific payload delivery in extreme environmental conditions
- Support for various UAS missions in AFRL/RQQA's end-user portfolio
