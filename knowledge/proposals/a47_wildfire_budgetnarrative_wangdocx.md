## Executive Summary
This budget narrative describes the roles and effort allocations for NASA Ames team members coordinating with Black Swift Technologies on a wildfire monitoring project. The project integrates UAS observations collected by BST with WRF-SFIRE modeling using NASA High Performance Computing resources to improve fire dynamics prediction and spread modeling.

## Technical Approach
- **Coordination & Data Integration**: Dr. Wang will coordinate collaboration between NASA Ames, Black Swift Technologies, and project teams to incorporate NASA Earth System Observation datasets and UAS data into the WRF-SFIRE modeling system
- **Modeling & Analysis**: Jeremy Benik will deploy and run WRF-SFIRE experiments on NASA's Pleiades HPC system using:
  - Reanalyzed meteorology data (GFS or ERA5)
  - Satellite products (land cover maps, surface temperature/moisture)
  - High-resolution UAS fire observations collected by the project
- **Validation & Feedback**: Team will assess the unique contribution of UAS observations in improving modeled fire dynamics and provide feedback to the measurement/collection team
