## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies was awarded $779,465 in NASA SBIR Phase II funding for a 24-month contract to develop and demonstrate a ruggedized UAS platform for scientific data gathering in harsh environments. The contract emphasizes quarterly demonstration reports, technology development, and commercialization potential assessment, with structured milestone-based payments.

## Technical Approach
The Statement of Work is incorporated by reference from the original proposal (Proposal #2022-1-1118). The technical approach focuses on:
- Development of a ruggedized Unmanned Aerial System designed for operation in harsh/extreme environmental conditions

- Quarterly demonstrations and progress reporting
- Technology transition and commercialization planning
