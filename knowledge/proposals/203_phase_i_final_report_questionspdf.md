## Executive Summary
This is a Google Form template designed to collect Phase I SBIR final report deliverables from small businesses conducting DoD-funded research. The form guides companies through submission of either preliminary reports with pitch decks, or comprehensive final reports covering scientific/technical objectives, accomplishments, and feasibility studies.

## Document Content
This document is a form template rather than a substantive technical proposal or report. It serves as the submission mechanism for Phase I deliverables and includes:

**CLIN Structure:**
- CLIN 0001: Preliminary Report (updated pitch deck and one-pager)
- CLIN 0002: Final Report (using either Small Business or AFVentures template)

**Required Final Report Sections:**

1. **Administrative:** Project title, company members involved
2. **Scientific/Technical Objectives and Accomplishments:**
   - Abstract (≤200 words)
   - Phase I goal statement
   - Up to 3 numbered objectives with completion status
3. **Feasibility Study:**
   - Problem statement confirmation/discovery within DoD
   - Identifying DoD organizations experiencing the problem
   - Contact attempt metrics (number of attempts, discussions)
   - Introduction mechanisms (collider, NEEDs ID, social media, etc.)
   - Assessment of most beneficial engagement mechanism
   - Barriers to DoD collaboration
4. **Phase II Application Intent**
