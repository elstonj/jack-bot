## Executive Summary
This is a blank template letter for Phase II E/X SBIR/STTR applicants to obtain formal commitment of matching funds or customer support from investors or government agencies. The letter documents that an external party commits to funding or supporting the further development of a Phase II E/X proposal and confirms alignment with their programmatic priorities.

## Template Structure & Key Requirements

The template establishes the following required elements:

**Core Commitment Language:**
- Confirmation that investor will provide specified funding amount ($XXX,XXX.XX)
- Funds must be "directly payable to the SBIR/STTR Firm"
- Funds are "unencumbered" and available within 45 business days of selection
- No constraints on fund transfer

**Required Conditional Sections:**

1. **For Government Agency Investors:** Must include brief statement on how resulting technology will be integrated into agency's future activities and whether future funding above Phase II E/X is planned

2. **For Private Sector Investors:** Must include brief statement of how investment furthers the original Phase II technology or commercialization of resulting product

3. **Timeline Requirement:** Must include dates when investor/customer needs the resulting technology or product

**Signature Authority:**
Letter must be signed by authorized representative of investor company/agency with title, contact information, and verification of alignment with "overall programmatic and development priorities"
