# BST Proposal Letter: AREN Support

## Document Metadata
- Type: Proposal Letter / Statement of Work
- Client/Agency: NASA Science Mission Directorate
- Program/Solicitation: AEROKATS and ROVER Education Network (AREN); NASA Science Activation project (NNX16AB95A)
- Date: January 21, 2025
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: AeroPod, Aerovator
- Key Personnel: Kay Rufty (Aerospace Engineer, Co-Investigator), Jack Elston (CEO, Technical Consultant), Maciej Stachura (Technical Consultant)

## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies proposes to support NASA's AEROKATS and ROVER Education Network (AREN) Science Activation project by providing technical expertise and developing educational workshop materials centered on kite-based environmental exploration. BST will contribute three named staff members and a Senior Technician to design, fabricate, and distribute materials to educational institutions, while also evaluating the commercial feasibility of AeroPod and Aerovator products.

## Technical Approach
- **Personnel Support**: Three technical consultants (Kay Rufty as Co-Investigator, Jack Elston and Maciej Stachura as Technical Consultants) plus a Senior Technician from BST will lead educational outreach efforts
- **Workshop Material Development**: Design and fabrication of kite-based educational materials using wood and plastic parts
- **Product Evaluation**: Feasibility assessment of AeroPod and Aerovator commercial product development through prototype testing
- **Tasks**: Planning, writing, design, wood and plastic part fabrication, shipping
- **Work Location**: Remote from NASA; activities based in Denver and Boulder, CO, and Onancock, VA
- **No facility access**: Work performed remotely with no NASA facility access or IT system interfacing required

## Products & Capabilities Described
- **AeroPod**: Commercial product under feasibility evaluation as part of educational/commercial development initiative
- **Aerovator**: Commercial product under feasibility evaluation as part of educational/commercial development initiative

## Use Cases & Applications
- Educational workshops using kites to explore environmental conditions
- Science Activation and STEM outreach to educational institutions and community partners
- Educational material development and distribution for learning programs
