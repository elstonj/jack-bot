## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies entered into a firm fixed-price subcontract with Area I, Inc. valued at $67,860 to design, test, and build a prototype atmospheric turbulence probe capable of measuring three-dimensional winds and turbulent fluxes of momentum, heat, and moisture in support of Area I's NOAA contract.

## Technical Approach
**Statement of Work (Schedule A):**
- Design, test, and build prototype atmospheric turbulence probe
- Measurement capability: three-dimensional winds
- Measurement capability: turbulent fluxes of momentum, heat, and moisture
- Work performed under Firm Fixed Price basis
- Subcontract is fully cost-inclusive at $67,860 for entire performance period

## Products & Capabilities Described
