## Executive Summary
Letter of support from Professor Brian Argrow (Director of CU Boulder's Integrated Remote & In-Situ Sensing Program) endorsing Black Swift Technologies' SBIR Phase I proposal for developing a ruggedized UAS for scientific data collection in harsh environments. Argrow attests to BST's technical expertise and previous successful SBIR/STTR collaborations.
