## Executive Summary
Biographical sketch for Jack S. Elston, CEO and Founder of Black Swift Technologies. Elston holds a PhD in Aerospace Engineering and has been leading BST since 2011. His expertise encompasses unmanned aircraft systems (UAS), networked command and control, meteorological sampling, and autonomous flight guidance—areas directly aligned with BST's core capabilities.

## Research Focus & Expertise
Elston's work spans:
- **Networked command and control systems** for heterogeneous and complex UAS
- **Small fixed-wing unmanned aircraft** for meteorological and environmental sampling
- **Autonomous flight guidance** for severe weather penetration (tornadic storms, supercell thunderstorms)
- **FAA Certificate of Authorization** compliance and integration into National Airspace System
- **Remote sensing payloads** for environmental monitoring (wildfire detection, fire radiative power)
