## Executive Summary
Dr. Maciej Stachura is the Chief Technology Officer of Black Swift Technologies. He holds M.S. and Ph.D. degrees in aerospace engineering from the University of Colorado Boulder and brings extensive experience in UAS flight operations, regulatory certification, and multi-aircraft cooperative control systems.

## Background & Experience

### Academic Training
- M.S. in Aerospace Engineering, University of Colorado Boulder
- Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering, University of Colorado Boulder
- Dissertation focus: Cooperative control algorithms for multi-UAS systems integrating sensing and communication

### Professional Experience at University of Colorado
- Involved in over 300 flight experiments
- Experience with multi-aircraft cooperative flight experiments
- Participation in VORTEX2 field campaign: led the first-ever intercept of a tornadic supercell thunderstorm
- Secured and maintained over 140 FAA Certificates of Authorization (COAs) for legal UAS flight operations across Colorado, Kansas, and Nebraska
- Led completion of NASA Airworthiness Flight Safety Review Board (AFSRB) and Flight Readiness Review Board process for UAS operations in the National Airspace System

### NASA SBIR Experience
- Principal Investigator for Phase I NASA SBIR: soil moisture mapping UAS development, testing, and commercialization
- Principal Investigator for Phase II NASA SBIR: continuation of soil moisture mapping work
