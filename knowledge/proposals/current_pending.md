## Executive Summary
This is a blank administrative template form required by NOAA for grant applicants to disclose current and pending funding support. It documents investigator/personnel funding commitments across multiple projects to ensure complete merit review and prevent over-commitment of personnel time.

## Form Purpose
Standardized disclosure form requiring applicants to list:
- All current funding support
- All pending funding applications
- Any proposals planned for submission in near future
- Transfers of previously funded support

## Information Captured per Entry
For each project/award:
- Project/Proposal Title
- Source of Support (funding agency)
- Total Award Amount ($)
- Total Award Period Covered (dates)
- Location of Project
- Months of Time Committed (broken down by fiscal year)
- Funding Status Category (Current / Pending / Submission Planned / Transfer of Support)

## Key Administrative Details
- **Completion Requirement**: Mandatory submission with NOAA COP application
- **Format**: Compatible with formats used by other federal agencies
- **Estimated Burden**: 30 minutes per response
- **FOIA Status**: Subject to Freedom of Information Act; information made available to public (except unpublished research results)
- **Multiple Entries**: Up to 4 funding entries shown; additional sheets allowed as needed
