## Notable Details

### Warranty & Liability
- **Warranty Period:** 12 months from dispatch for conformity to specification and freedom from defects in design, materials, workmanship
- **Liability Cap:** Limited to contract price; excludes indirect/consequential losses, lost profits, business opportunity, reputation, goodwill
- **Repair/Replacement:** Company's option; repaired goods warranted for 12 months on recurrent defect, remainder of original period otherwise

### Payment Terms
- Default: Net 30 days by telegraphic transfer
- Company may require payment on order or before delivery
- Time for payment is "of the essence"
- Company may suspend services/delivery if payment late
- Website orders require payment at time of order

### Delivery
- Default: EXW (Incoterms 2020) for domestic UK orders; FCA for international orders from Witney, Oxfordshire facility
- Delivery dates are estimates only; time not of the essence
- Buyer responsible for obtaining import licenses and authorizations
- Storage fee: 2.5% of goods price per week if buyer fails to take delivery
- Risk passes to buyer at delivery (or per applicable Incoterm); ownership passes on full payment

### Calibration Services
- Buyer must complete RMA (Return Materials Authorisation) form
- Company may quote separately for examination vs. actual repair/calibration
- Advance payment typically required
- Company provides conformity statement "as at date of calibration" but not liable for drift after dispatch
- Repaired/calibrated items are treated as Deliverables

### Intellectual Property
- Company retains all IP in deliverables and work product
- Buyer receives non-exclusive, royalty-free license for internal use only
- Buyer must disclose any modifications/improvements to Company Software and grant non-exclusive, royalty-free, perpetual license back to Company
- Buyer indemnifies Company against third-party IP claims related to materials Buyer provides

### Software License
- Non-sublicensable except to buyer's staff/contractors
- Not transferable (except firmware with equipment transfer)
- Provided "as is" without warranty of meeting requirements, virus-free status, or uninterrupted/error-free operation
- Buyer responsible for accuracy and adequacy for intended use
- Buyer indemnifies Company against third-party claims

### Defense-Related Use
- **Critical Restriction:** Any defense-related use must be "Passive Defence" only—solely to reduce probability and minimize effects of damage from hostile action, without initiating hostile action
- Buyer must provide written confirmation on request; must procure similar confirmations from all successors
- Breach is material, non-remediable breach

### Termination
- Either party may terminate with written notice if other party: (a) commits material breach not remedied within 30 days, or (b) becomes insolvent/ceases trading
- Force majeure clause: 90-day threshold for termination if delays continue

### Confidentiality & Schematics
- Standard confidentiality obligations for both parties
- Schematics are Confidential Information; non-exclusive, perpetual license granted only for designing electronics to work with Bartington goods
- Buyer may not use schematics for other purposes

### WEEE Compliance
- UK/EU buyers responsible for collection, treatment, recovery, environmentally sound disposal of electrical/electronic waste
- Non-EU buyers must comply with local law

### Dispute Resolution
- Governed by English law; exclusive jurisdiction of English courts
