## Executive Summary
This deliverable describes BST's plan to fabricate and validate a functional prototype of a sled-launched (SL) unmanned aerial system (UAS) at BST facilities, including simulated deployment testing to verify compatibility with sled launch conditions and evaluate autonomy, communication, and power performance.

## Technical Approach
- **Location:** BST's own facilities
- **Prototype Development:** Fabrication of a functional SL UAS prototype
- **Validation Testing:** 
  - Simulated deployment tests to validate UAS compatibility with sled launch (SLC) conditions
  - Autonomy evaluation
  - Communication range testing
  - Power efficiency assessment
- **Risk Mitigation:** Iterative design improvements and thorough pre-deployment assessments to address potential deployment failures during simulated tests
