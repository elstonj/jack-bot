## Executive Summary
This is a short CV for Dr. Hyoshin (John) Park, an Associate Professor at Old Dominion University specializing in AI, autonomous systems, multi-agent coordination, and environmental sensing. He holds U.S. citizenship as of 2024 and is actively engaged in Navy-sponsored research including a Phase II STTR on expendable air-sea profiling in hazardous weather conditions using small unmanned aircraft systems (sUAS). His research integrates physics-informed machine learning with real-time sensor fusion for autonomous operations.

## Key Research Focus
- **Physics-informed, data-driven learning:** Integration of real-time sensor data with hydrodynamic models for multimodal ocean environment mapping
- **Temporal Multimodal Multivariate Learning (TMML):** Adaptive AI fusing acoustic, visual, and flow-field data for environmental/threat prediction
- **Regret-based ergodic patrolling algorithms:** Fleet-level optimization balancing coverage, risk mitigation, and energy efficiency
- **Decentralized coordination:** Multi-agent autonomous surface vehicle (ASV) coordination using asynchronous communication and epistemic planning
