# Monthly Progress Report - November 2020

## Document Metadata
- Type: Monthly progress report
- Client/Agency: NASA
- Program/Solicitation: AREN (Arctic Research on Emerging Nitrogen & nitrous oxide sources) 2020-2021
- Date: November 2020 (reporting period); submitted December 3, 2020
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: AeroPod, MHP (Modular Hovering Platform)
- Key Personnel: Geoff Bland (Task Manager), Kay Rufty (Employee)

## Executive Summary
BST's AREN project team completed significant progress in November 2020 on tutorial video production, website development, MHP hardware design iterations, and test flight operations. The team successfully flew the MHP-equipped AeroPod, processed flight data, and prepared materials for an AGU (American Geophysical Union) poster presentation.

## Technical Approach
- **Video Production**: Captured tutorial videos with voice-over scripts and closed captions for hosting on video platform
- **Website Development**: Embedded new videos into website; troubleshot video playback and code integration issues; developed participation and video page concepts
- **MHP Hardware Development**: Designed and 3D-printed board mount iterations for MHP; sourced mounting hardware; refined tail design based on feedback
- **Flight Testing & Data Processing**: Conducted successful test flight of MHP-equipped AeroPod; collected flight data via Mission Mapper; converted raw data to analyzed form (plotting and outlier analysis)
- **Outreach**: Prepared virtual poster presentation for AGU conference with MHP documentation, imagery, and data visualizations

## Products & Capabilities Described
**AeroPod**
- Used as platform for MHP (Modular Hovering Platform) flight testing
- Successfully flew in November 2020 test flights

- Data logging and submission system for flight data
