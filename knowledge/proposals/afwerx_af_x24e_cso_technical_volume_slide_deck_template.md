## Key Requirements Emphasized
- **RDT&E Focus:** Work must be Research/R&D, not commercial procurement
- **DoD vs. Commercial Variants:** Proposals should explain differences between defense and commercial applications
- **Literature Baseline:** Scientific and technical literature search required per AFI 61-201 and AFRL/CA guidance
- **Deliverables:** Kickoff meeting (within 30 days), preliminary report, final report with SF 298 and DD Form 882
- **Foreign National Disclosure:** Mandatory reporting of any foreign citizens or dual-citizenship individuals on the project
