## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies proposes to support NASA's AEROKATS and ROVER Education Network (a Science Activation educational outreach initiative) through personnel support, workshop materials development, and evaluation of Aeropod and Aerovator product commercialization feasibility. BST will contribute an Aerospace Engineer, Senior Technician, and two Technical Consultants to design, fabricate, and distribute educational materials.

## Technical Approach
BST will:
- Plan and design educational workshop materials centered on using kites for environmental exploration
- Fabricate wood and plastic parts for workshop kits
- Ship materials to educational institutions and partner organizations
- Evaluate prototypes of new systems (Aeropod, Aerovator) as team availability permits
- Perform all work remotely from locations in Denver/Boulder, CO and Onancock, VA with no NASA facility access required

## Products & Capabilities Described
- **Aeropod:** Commercial product under feasibility evaluation for educational applications
- **Aerovator:** Commercial product under feasibility evaluation for educational applications
- Educational kite systems for environmental exploration and STEM learning

## Use Cases & Applications
- STEM education and outreach activities
- Educational workshops using kites as instruments to explore atmospheric/environmental conditions
- Evaluation of commercial potential for aerial platform products in educational contexts
