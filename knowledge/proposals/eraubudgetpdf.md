## Executive Summary
This is a firm-fixed-price cost proposal from Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University (ERAU) to Black Swift Technologies LLC for a Navy STTR Phase II project. ERAU will contribute academic research support over a 4-year period (24-month base + 24-month option) totaling **$333,503.00** to develop and validate air-sea profiling observations using BST's S0 small uncrewed aircraft system (UAS) for operations in hazardous weather conditions.
