## Executive Summary
This is a Connect Tech Inc. product datasheet for the Orbitty Carrier, a small form factor carrier board designed to interface with NVIDIA Jetson TX2, TX2i, and TX1 modules. The carrier provides networking, USB, HDMI, and GPIO connectivity in an extremely compact package suitable for robotics, unmanned systems, and rugged environments.

## Product Specifications

**Orbitty Carrier for NVIDIA Jetson TX2/TX2i/TX1**
- Part Number: ASG003
- Manufacturer: Connect Tech Inc.

**Physical:**
- Dimensions: 87mm x 50mm (3.425" x 1.968")
- Weight: 41 grams (carrier only)
- Operating Temperature: -40°C to +85°C (-40°F to +185°F)

**Power:**
- Requirements: +9V to +14V DC nominal (+19V peak)

**Connectivity:**
- 1x Gigabit Ethernet (10/100/1000)
- 1x USB 3.0
- 1x USB 2.0 OTG
- 1x HDMI (with integrated audio)
- 1x microSD card slot
- 2x 3.3V UART
- I2C interface
- 4x GPIO pins

**Compatibility:**
- NVIDIA Jetson TX2
- NVIDIA Jetson TX2i
- NVIDIA Jetson TX1
