## Product Specifications

### KDE1806XF-2350 Motor
**What it is:** Brushless DC motor for small UAS (sUAS) multi-rotor applications; designed for competition-level FPV racing and extended operational life.

**Key Performance Characteristics:**
- Thrust generation: 770g+ (with 5" x 4.0HQ propeller at 4S voltage)
- Price: $25.95 per unit
- Status: Sold out (at time of webpage capture)

**Technical Features:**
- High-temperature solid-core copper windings: rated 240°C continuous / 340°C maximum
- Japanese-built 0.2 mm silicon-steel stator laminations
- Kevlar tie-wraps for structural integrity
- Triple-bearing ABEC-7 bearing support (maintenance-free, rated for hundreds of flight hours)
- Silicone-wire leads: 200°C rated, 20 AWG, 200mm length
- Pre-soldered 3.5mm 24K bullet connectors (KDEXF-BC35)
- Wire leads field-modifiable for length and soldering

**Recommended ESC:** KDE Direct UAS 20A+ LV Multi-Rotor Series
