## Executive Summary
This is a blank/template proposal checklist for submitting a Navy STTR Phase II proposal in response to the Hazardous Weather solicitation. The document provides a comprehensive administrative and compliance framework that Black Swift Technologies would need to complete when submitting their proposal, including budget structure, special requirements compliance, cost documentation, and cybersecurity certifications.

## Document Structure & Key Sections

### I. General Information
- Budget template with base period and up to 7 optional periods
- Fields for Period of Performance (POP), dollar amounts, and SOW task numbers
- Required points of contact for Electronic Document Access (EDA) notifications
- Company authorization and signature requirements
- Standard 180-day proposal validity requirement

### II. Special Requirements
Compliance checklist covering 14 distinct categories:

1. **Aerial Vehicles** - Requires disclosure if UAVs/manned aircraft will be procured or operated; liability insurance documentation needed if prime/subcontractors operate
2. **Test Driving Vehicles** - Documentation of who performs vehicle testing; FAR 28.307-2(c) liability insurance required
3. **Ammunition/Explosives** - DCMA pre-award safety survey and DSS security survey required if applicable
4. **Active Acoustics** - Disclosure of active sonar or acoustic transmissions; Navy POC contacts provided (Ray Soukup, Robert Headrick)
5. **Animal Use** - Full IACUC documentation required including approval letters, protocols, USDA inspection reports
6. **Biosafety/Biosecurity** - CDC and NIH biosafety compliance discussion required if biological/DNA/synthetic materials involved
7. **Human Subject Research** - IRB approval, consent forms, Federal Wide Assurance (FWA), ethics training documentation required
8. **Recombinant DNA/Synthetic Nucleic Acids** - IBC approval letter and NIH Guidelines compliance required
9. **Government Facilities/Test Sites** - Disclosure and facility POC information required if government facilities will be used
10. **Government Furnished Items** - List required with delivery dates and POC information
11. **Rent-Free Use of GFP** - Detailed accounting and authorization documentation required
12. **Data Rights Assertions** - DFARS 252.227-7013, -7014, or -7018 format required for restricted data/software
13. **DD 254 Security Classification** - Required if classified information/facility access needed; DSS clearance type must be specified
14. **Reports & Deliverables** - Detailed schedule of reports (including monthly technical/financial progress reports, final report) and hardware/prototype/software deliverables with delivery dates and recipients

### III. Cost Content
Compliance and documentation requirements including:
- SAM registration verification and validity dates
- Online Representations and Certifications in SAM (FAR and DFARS clauses)
- ONR-specific Representations and Certifications
- **Small Business Subcontracting Plans** - Required if award exceeds $750,000 (unless small business, non-profit, or entirely offshore); comprehensive plan, master plan with goals, or individual plan options
- DCAA and DCMA cognizant agency information (name, address, POC, phone, email)
- DCAA/DCMA approved accounting system documentation with audit report number and approval date
- Forward Pricing Rate Agreement (FPRA)/Forward Pricing Rate Recommendation (FPRR) disclosure
- Purchasing System approval status (FAR 44.3)
- Estimating System approval status (DFARS 215.407-5-70)
- Property Management System approval (DFARS 245.105) or Property Management Plan description
- Prior ONR awards in last 5 years (award number, program officer, program officer code)
- Cost Proposal Spreadsheet completion per BAA
- Fundamental Research determination for subcontractors (USD AT&L memo 24 May 2010 definition provided)

### IV. Safeguarding Covered Defense Information & Cyber Incident Reporting
DFARS 252.204-7012 compliance checklist:
- Implementation of NIST SP 800-171 security controls for CUI (Controlled Unclassified Information)
- DoD assessment level documentation if applicable
- System Security Plan (SSP) existence and approval status by DON or DoD activity
- Note: Performers may be required to provide SSP within 30 days of contract award
