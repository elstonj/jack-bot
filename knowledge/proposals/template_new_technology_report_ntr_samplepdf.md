## Executive Summary

## Form Structure (for reference)
The form requires completion of 18 sections covering:
1. Technology title
2. Innovator information
3. Employer details
4. Place of performance
5. Employer status (government, university, non-profit, small business, large entity)
6. Origin (contract/grant numbers, effort type)
7. NASA COTR contact
8. Contractor/grantee POC
9. Brief abstract
10. Problem/objective description
11. Technical description of innovation
12. Unique features and benefits
13. Commercial applications
14. Additional documentation references
15. Technology significance level
16. Development state
17. Patent status
18. Development history timeline
19. Signatures and approvals
