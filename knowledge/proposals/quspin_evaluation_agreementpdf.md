## Executive Summary
This is a 60-day equipment evaluation agreement between Black Swift Technologies and QuSpin, Inc. for testing a Gen-2.0 QTFM (QuSpin Total-Field Magnetometer) sensor. BST has the option to return the equipment or purchase it at standard pricing upon completion of the evaluation period.
