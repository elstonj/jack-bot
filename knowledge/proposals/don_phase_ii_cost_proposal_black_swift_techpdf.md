This cost proposal is for a 48-month Navy STTR Phase II contract with a base period of 24 months and Option I (24 months), with no pricing for Options II and III. ### BST Business Model & G&A Application
BST explicitly notes in the proposal that the company does not typically engage in large subcontracted efforts. The majority of technical, program management, and integration activities are performed by internal BST staff, with subcontractors used selectively for specialized capabilities. This is reflected in the G&A treatment: only the first $25K of each subcontract receives G&A burden, as fixed management costs are incurred per subcontract relationship rather than scaling proportionally with dollars. BST states intention to maintain this treatment for future efforts unless materially different subcontracting structure is required.
