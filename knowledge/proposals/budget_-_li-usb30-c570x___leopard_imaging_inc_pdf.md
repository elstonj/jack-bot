## Products & Capabilities Described

**LI-USB30-C570X USB 3.0 Camera Module**
- **What it is:** Compact camera module (26mm × 26mm) based on e2v Sapphire EV76C570 CMOS sensor
- **Specifications:**
  - Resolution: 2M pixels (1600H × 1200V)
  - Frame rate: 60 fps
  - Sensor: e2v EV76C570 Color/Mono CMOS with Global Shutter
  - Pixel size: 4.5 μm × 4.5 μm
  - Optical format: 1/1.8"
  - Interface: USB 3.0
  - Power: USB 3.0 +5VDC
  - Lens mount: CS/M12 (default M12)
  - Output: RAW data USB 3.0 real-time streaming
  - Available in Color (LI-USB30-C570C) and Mono (LI-USB30-C570M) versions
- **Price:** $699.00
- **SDK Support:** C#, external/software trigger, OpenCV (Emgu), RAW to RGB conversion, BMP and RAW file formats
