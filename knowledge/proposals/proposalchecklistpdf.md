## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies submitted a Navy STTR Phase II proposal on February 18, 2026, for hazardous weather research involving validation flights of its S0 UAS. The proposal spans a 48-month performance period (24 months base + 24 months option, starting January 1, 2027) and includes multi-institutional collaborations with University of Miami, Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University, and Old Dominion University. Primary work location is Boulder, Colorado, with government-furnished facility access at NASA Glenn Icing Research Tunnel in Cleveland, Ohio.
