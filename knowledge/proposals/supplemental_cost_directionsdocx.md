## Executive Summary
This is an Air Force guidance document providing instructions to SBIR Phase I proposers on how to complete the "Supplemental Cost Information" section (supporting Volume 3, the Cost Volume). It specifies what cost justifications and documentation are required for various cost categories.

## Content Summary
This is a standardized template/instruction document rather than a BST-specific proposal or technical document. It provides guidance to proposers on cost justification requirements across four main cost categories:

**Direct Labor:** Proposers must justify notable labor costs (significant hours, high hourly wages, or high overhead/fringe rates) with reference to BLS wage data.

**Direct Material Costs:** Materials, parts, and supplies must be itemized with types, quantities, unit prices, purposes, and basis for costs (historical data, vendor quotes, catalogs, etc.).

**Travel Costs:** Travel proposed as Other Direct Costs must be justified with trip counts, number of travelers per trip, specific locations, trip purposes, and basis for flight, lodging, per diem, and ground transportation costs (with reference to GSA per diem rates).

**Special Tooling and Test Equipment:** Equipment purchases must be reviewed for necessity and appropriateness to the Phase I work, must be advantageous to the Government, and must relate directly to the specific topic. Government retains title unless transfer to contractor is more cost-effective.
