## Executive Summary

The company leveraged SBIR-funded development of small UAS systems and sensor fusion algorithms to generate revenue through commercial sales of UAS platforms, satellite calibration flights, and environmental sampling campaigns.

## Products & Capabilities Described

**S2 UAS**
- Small unmanned aircraft system developed under SBIR funding
- Configured for soil moisture mapping applications
- Generated $284,004 in commercial sales revenue
- Capable of operating in various environmental conditions

**Soil Moisture Mapping Systems**
- Payload/system integration with S2 UAS
- Commercial application: agricultural and environmental monitoring

**Ruggedized UAS Platform**
- Designed for harsh environmental conditions
- Capabilities: CO2 sampling, sensor fusion, low-altitude mapping above rugged terrain
- Used for scientific data gathering missions

**Sensor Fusion and Algorithms**
- Technology for mapping at low altitude above challenging terrain
- Applied to satellite calibration missions (MALIBU program)
- Foundation for multiple commercial applications

## Use Cases & Applications

1. **Soil Moisture Mapping:** Agricultural and environmental monitoring via S2 UAS platform
2. **Satellite Calibration:** MALIBU program flights providing ground truth data
3. **CO2/Greenhouse Gas Sampling:** ELEVATE campaign in Costa Rica for atmospheric sampling in harsh environments
