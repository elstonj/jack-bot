- Key Personnel: Jack Elston (last editor); internal notes reference Doug, Danny, Brian, Bryan, Jon, Maciej, Cory, Brad

## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies used Advanced Industries Grant funding to commercialize soil moisture mapping sUAS technology and the SwiftCore Flight Management System. Year 1 efforts generated $160,000 in follow-on funding, $55,000 in SuperSwift airframe sales revenue, and $60,000+ from SwiftCore/SwiftTrainer customizations, while creating 5 FTE jobs and retaining 3.25 FTE jobs in Colorado.

## Technical Approach
The grant supported two primary commercialization pathways:

1. **Soil Moisture Mapping System**: Development and market deployment of sUAS-based soil moisture mapping capability. Enabled by grant funding, the technology secured:
   - $125,000 follow-on NASA investment
   - $35,090 deployment contract
   - Product export classification approvals for overseas sales

2. **SwiftCore Flight Management System Expansion**: 
   - Customization of SwiftCore FMS to support vertical takeoff/landing (VTOL) aircraft for other Colorado companies
   - Development of SwiftTrainer as entry-level mapping system based on SwiftCore capabilities
   - Targeted at surveyor market with early Colorado customer base

## Products & Capabilities Described

### SuperSwift Airframe
- Fixed-wing sUAS platform
- Generated $55,000 in sales revenue during grant period
- Primary platform for soil moisture mapping application

### SwiftCore Flight Management System
- Core flight management/autopilot technology
- Designed for modularity and customization across different aircraft types
- Key features adapted for entry-level use in SwiftTrainer
- Support expanded to VTOL platforms and multiple Colorado-based integrators

### SwiftTrainer
- Entry-level end-to-end mapping system
- Based on SwiftCore FMS architecture
- Targeted at surveyor market
- Commercial product launched during grant period

### Soil Moisture Mapping sUAS
- Complete system for agricultural soil moisture monitoring
- Demonstrated capability to NASA and commercial agricultural/insurance partners
- Data collection to support modeling of soil moisture impact on crop yields and input costs

## Use Cases & Applications
- **Agricultural**: Soil moisture monitoring to optimize irrigation and reduce input costs; assess impact on crop yields
- **Commercial surveying**: SwiftTrainer targeted at surveyor market
- **VTOL applications**: Customized SwiftCore FMS for vertical takeoff/landing aircraft
- **Insurance/Risk Assessment**: Partnerships with agricultural insurance community
