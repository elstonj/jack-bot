## Notable Details

**Proposal Structure (5 Volumes):**
- Volume 1: Technical Abstract / Anticipated Benefits
- Volume 2: White Paper (15-page maximum)
- Volume 3: Cost Volume
- Volume 4: Registration/SBIR.gov submission
- Volume 5: Supporting Documents

**Required Supporting Documentation:**
- Customer Memorandum (MOU) from DAF sponsor/partner (signed)
- Resumes
- Letters of Support
- Funding Agreement Certification
- Lifecycle Certification
- Prohibited Telecom Certification
- Foreign Ownership Disclosure
- Coversheet Supplement
- Additional Cost Information Sheet
- Technical Data Rights Assertions
- Non-proprietary Work Plan
- ESOH Questionnaire

**Program Details:**
- Award Type: Direct-to-Phase-II (open topic)
- Max Award: $1.25M
- Performance Period: 18 months technical + 3 months reporting
- Two topic tracks: X22.4-ODCSO1 (Air Force) or X22.4-ODCSO2 (Space Force)

**Evaluation Criteria (in descending importance):**
1. Defense Need (relevance to DAF/Space Force requirement)
2. Technical Approach & Team (soundness, merit, innovation, team qualifications)
3. Commercialization Potential (Government/private sector market, follow-on commitments, matching funds)

**Submission Timeline:**
- July 5-15: Finalize MOUs, check registrations, CAGE/DSIP setup
- July 18-22: Draft Volumes 1-2
- July 25-29: Finalize white paper, complete supporting documents
- August 1-5: DSIP data entry and final review
- August 8-9: Submit in DSIP (Deadline: Tuesday, August 9, 2022, 12pm EST)
