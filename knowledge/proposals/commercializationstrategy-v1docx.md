## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies outlines a commercialization strategy targeting DoD, other federal agencies, and the private sector through direct sales initially, supplemented by channel partners as market adoption grows. BST projects first sales upon completion of Phase II and believes Phase I/II funding will be sufficient to bring the developed technology to market.

## Commercial Sales Strategy
- **Initial approach:** Direct sales to DoD, federal agencies, and private sector customers
- **Growth phase:** Supplement with indirect sales through channel partners focused on vertical markets (military, ISR, surveying, precision agriculture)
- **Marketing:** Technical journal publications by BST and customers; industry conference participation
- **Business Development:** Personnel dedicated to business development activities

## Market Analysis

### UAS Market
- 2020 market size: $22.5 billion
- Projected 2025 size: $42.8 billion (CAGR: 13.8%)
- Commercial unit sales: 1.0 million units/year by 2021; unit sales expected to double 2020-2025
- Growth sectors: construction (mapping, observation, surveys, inspection); mining; oil & gas; agriculture
- Military/Government use: ISR for personnel, bases, perimeters, borders
- **Key trend:** Small UAV segment projected to grow fastest; fully autonomous segment expected to have highest growth

### Predictive Maintenance Market
- 2018 market size: $2.8 billion
- Projected 2026 size: $23.0 billion (CAGR: 30.2%)
- Drivers: equipment uptime improvement, maintenance cost reduction, IIoT integration, machine learning, real-time condition monitoring
- **Aerospace & Defense** identified as high-growth segment
