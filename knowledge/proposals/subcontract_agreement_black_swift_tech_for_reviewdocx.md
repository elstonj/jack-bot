## Executive Summary
This is a firm fixed-price subcontract agreement between Area I, Inc. and Black Swift Technologies LLC for the design, testing, and prototype development of an atmospheric turbulence probe capable of measuring three-dimensional winds and turbulent fluxes of momentum, heat, and moisture. The work is valued at $67,860 and supports Area I's Prime Contract with NOAA.

## Technical Approach
BST is contracted to:
- **Design** an atmospheric turbulence probe
- **Test** the probe design
- **Build a prototype** atmospheric turbulence probe

**Measurement Capabilities:** The probe must be capable of measuring:
- Three-dimensional winds
- Turbulent fluxes of momentum
- Turbulent fluxes of heat
- Turbulent fluxes of moisture

No specific technical specifications, materials, or performance claims are detailed in this contract document; those details would reside in the Statement of Work (Attachment A), which is referenced but not fully provided in this excerpt.
