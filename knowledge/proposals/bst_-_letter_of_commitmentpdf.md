## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies commits to participate as a subcontractor on Barron Associates' NASA SBIR Phase II program focused on virtual redundancy for sensor failure safety assurance. BST will integrate Barron Associates' control technology innovations into their fixed-wing UAS platforms to improve safety assurance and enable integration into the national aerospace system.

## Technical Approach
- Integration of Barron Associates' virtual sensor redundancy technology into BST's fixed-wing UAS
- Leveraging BST's in-house designed plug-and-play technologies to rapidly adapt systems
- No specific technical details provided in this commitment letter
