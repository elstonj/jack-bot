## Executive Summary
This document describes Black Swift Technologies' facilities, equipment, and testing capabilities in support of the Crested Butte SPLASH project. BST operates a 3,000 sq. ft. facility in Boulder, Colorado, equipped with prototyping, simulation, and testing resources, along with access to multiple flight test sites ranging from local to high-altitude locations.

## Facilities & Infrastructure
- **Main Facility**: 3,000 sq. ft. facility in Boulder, CO
- **Capabilities**: 
  - Prototyping and testing equipment for electrical subsystems and avionics boards
  - Full suite of atmospheric and aircraft simulation tools for mission scenario analysis
  - Multiple full autopilot systems for testing electrical and software interfaces
  - Manufacturing capability for prototype and production aircraft integration
  - Sensor and avionics integration capability
  - Meeting space for project coordination
  - Electronics benches and workshop facilities
- **Outsourcing**: Composite airframe work outsourced to Northwind Composites

## Products & Capabilities Described

**SwiftCore Flight Management System (FMS)**
- Core autopilot platform for custom UAS solutions
- Includes three integrated components:
  - SwiftPilot autopilot
  - SwiftStation ground station
  - SwiftTab tablet user interface
- Multiple full systems available for testing and integration

**S2 (BST UAS)**
- Previously used in NOAA operations
- Demonstrated in flight testing at sod farm test site

## Testing & Flight Operations Infrastructure

**Available Test Sites**:
1. **Sod farm**: 20 minutes east of Boulder office; designated as primary Phase II test site
2. **Pawnee National Grasslands**: 20 x 20 mile test area; BST has applied for 1,000-foot waiver
3. **San Luis Valley**: High-altitude testing location with peaks over 14,000 ft MSL; allows testing up to 15,000 ft MSL under current regulations

**Operational History**:
- Previous flight operations with NOAA using Penguin BE UAS and BST S2
- Team experience with complex atmospheric sampling missions

## Notable Details
- BST formed in 2011
- Facility location provides proximity to diverse test environments (lowland, grassland, and high-altitude alpine)
- High-altitude capability (15,000 ft MSL) enables testing in mountainous terrain relevant to proposed work
- Full simulation and design verification capability supports rapid prototyping cycle
