## Executive Summary
This document provides regulatory guidance on subcontract and consultant agreement requirements for DOD SBIR Phase I proposals, specifying cost caps, documentation requirements, and what must be included in supporting materials.

## Content
This is a compliance/guidance document rather than a substantive project proposal. It outlines DOD requirements for proposals involving subcontractors or consultants:

**Key Requirements Specified:**
- Proposed total of consultant fees, facility leases, and subcontract/purchase agreements may not exceed **one-third of total contract price/cost** (unless written approval from Contracting Officer)
- Offerors must describe involvement of universities or consultants in detail in Cost Volume
- Subcontract costs must be supported with copies of subcontract agreements
- Supporting documents must adequately describe work to be performed
- **Minimum documentation required:** Statement of Work (SOW) with corresponding detailed Cost Volume for each planned subcontract
