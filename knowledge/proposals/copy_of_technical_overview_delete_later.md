## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies proposes adapting its proven soil moisture mapping UAS payload to provide rapid, high-resolution (~15m) data collection for USAF landing zone operations globally. The solution leverages BST's S2 fixed-wing or E2 multi-rotor platforms—designed for extreme weather operations (heavy precipitation, 45+ mph winds, -40°F to +110°F temperatures)—to enable warfighter decision-making and de-risk aircraft landing operations in unconventional locations worldwide, directly supporting USAF's Global Persistent Awareness strategic capability.

## Technical Approach
- **Core Concept**: Adapt existing soil moisture mapping sensing payload (L-band radiometer capable of measuring soil moisture up to 10cm below ground) to AFRL/RQ requirements for landing zone fidelity assessment
- **Platform Selection**: S2 (fixed-wing, 90-minute flight time, 100+ mph dash speed) or E2 (multi-rotor for infrastructure inspection) platforms, both purpose-built for demanding environments
- **Key Technical Capabilities**:
  - All-weather operations: precipitation, extreme winds, temperature extremes
  - Autonomous terrain and wind-aware flight planning
  - Machine vision-based automated landing without pre-planning
  - Modular, field-swappable payload interfaces
  - Integrated solar panels on S2 for extended endurance (4-6 hours)
  - Onboard fault prediction/diagnostics via machine learning
  - IP-rated designs for ingress protection from abrasive/corrosive environments
  - ~600 acres per flight coverage for soil moisture mapping
- **Data Pipeline**: Rapid collection and dissemination of high-resolution data products from landing zones

## Products & Capabilities Described

### S2 (Fixed-Wing VTOL UAS)
- **Specifications**: 3 lbs, 100+ mph dash speed, >3,000 fpm climb rate, silent operation (barely audible at 200')
- **Endurance**: 90 minutes default (4-6 hours with solar panels and smaller payloads)
- **Proposed Use**: Primary platform for landing zone soil moisture assessment; multi-mission capable (thermal, spectral, LIDAR)
- **Capabilities**: Terrain-aware flight planning, wind-aware path planning with energy harvesting, machine vision automated landing

### E2 (Multi-Rotor UAS)
- **Purpose**: Infrastructure inspection platform, complement to fixed-wing for vertical operations
- **Proposed Use**: Alternative for landing zones requiring VTOL, especially in rugged terrain

### S0 (Small VTOL)
- **Specifications**: Backpack-portable or multiple units in small case; fits 90-minute or extended duration payloads
- **Capabilities**: Real-time video or atmospheric monitoring; industry-leading accuracy
- **Operational Profile**: Revolutionary portability for persistent distributed measurements

### S1 (Photogrammetry Platform)
- **Status**: Industry-leading performer in fly-off competitions
- **Application**: Orthomosaic and 3D mapping generation

### SwiftCore/SwiftPilot FMS (Flight Management System)
- **Type**: Uniquely modular avionics with built-in redundancy and flexibility
- **Features**: Custom-designed for infrastructure inspection; open interface for payload integration
- **Status**: TRL-9 (mature technology)

### Soil Moisture Mapping Payload
- **Sensor**: L-band radiometer
- **Coverage**: Up to 600 acres per flight
- **Measurement Depth**: Soil moisture content up to 10cm below surface
- **Resolution**: ~15m (high resolution for landing zone characterization)
- **Proven Track Record**: NASA SBIR-funded development (2020, >$2M); operational with USGS, NOAA, commercial partners

## Use Cases & Applications

### Primary (Defense)
- **Landing Zone Assessment**: De-risk USAF aircraft operations globally by providing soil moisture/trafficability data for unconventional landing zones
- **Perimeter Surveillance**: Security Forces monitoring of air bases (24/7/365 capability)
- **Emergency Response**: Rapid deployment with modular payloads for airfield damage assessment
- **CBRN Detection**: Modular payload adaptability for chemical, biological, radiological, nuclear sensing

### Secondary (Dual-Use/Commercial)
- **Wildfire Mitigation**: UAS-based soil moisture measurements for fire risk assessment (USGS collaboration)
- **Watershed Characterization**: Hydrological modeling inputs (NOAA collaboration)
- **Turf/Sod Health**: Agricultural monitoring (Turf Scout commercial partnership)
- **Infrastructure Inspection**: Power lines, railways, construction sites
- **Natural Hazard Prediction**: Flash flood and landslide early warning via soil saturation data
- **Construction/Civil Engineering**: Soil integrity verification for heavy machinery operations
- **Railway Operations**: Prevent derailments from track movement caused by soil saturation
- **Disaster Response**: Rapid assessment of affected areas
