## Use Cases & Applications

**Army Topics:**
- A19-109: Swarm asset optimization for persistent area coverage and target acquisition/engagement
- A19-118: Multi-frame drone video registration and consolidation for geospatial analysis
- A19-130: Aerostat hybrid enhancement for Low Cost Persistent ISR (LCP ISR) during high-intensity conflict

**Navy Topics:**
- N192-062: Autonomous UAV flight without constant supervisory control or data link
- N192-088: Collision avoidance for unmanned carrier aviation in dense airspace and aerial refueling

**Air Force Topics:**
- AF192-005/006: Pitch day opportunity; challenge areas include:
  - UAS payload systems to defeat other UAS
  - UAS signature identification (optical, infrared, acoustic, radar)
  - UAS avionics trust and verification
  - UAS weather hazard sensing
  - Environmental condition sensing (wind, hydrology, RF spectrum)
  - Sense-and-avoid for mixed manned/unmanned airspace
  - GPS degradation mitigation and resilient PNT
  - Small UAS certification and airworthiness
  - Counter-swarm technologies
  - AI and decentralized UAS swarm control

- AF192-060: Multi-spectral imagery pod for drone/rover deployment for rangeland management, vegetation health, soil characterization, and ESA compliance
  - Annotated as "Same area, different sensor" (implying BST has related sensing capability)

- AF192-D001: Open topic for innovative dual-purpose defense technologies (max 15 months, $750K Phase II)
  
- AF192-D003: Precision air delivery system for small packages (<25 lbs) from high altitude, quiet operation, 100-mile standoff range, deployable from C-17 (45,000 ft, 515 mph)

**STTR Topic:**
- A19B-T002: Universal navigation solution manager using conventional and alternative sensors in contested/degraded/denied environments
  - Annotated as "NOAA SBIR" (potential cross-agency application)
