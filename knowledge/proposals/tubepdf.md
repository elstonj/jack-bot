## Products Described

### Quantum Tube (QT)
- **Description**: Special blended polymer airframe material, extremely durable and forgiving
- **Key Properties**: 
  - Resists squeezing, dropping, throwing
  - Glass-smooth finish with no spiral grooves
  - Readily accepts hobby epoxies and paints
  - Molded in medium gray
- **Sizes Available**: 2.1", 2.5", 3.0", 3.9" diameters
- **Wall Thickness**: ~0.060"-0.072"
- **Lengths**: 36" or 48" standard
- **Weights**: 10.7 oz to 24.1 oz (depending on size/length)
- **Cost Range**: $16.95-$32.95
- **Recommended Use**: Sport rocketry applications up to 0.85 Mach, internal motor mount configurations
- **Limitation**: NOT suitable for transonic/supersonic flight or minimum diameter applications

### Kraft Phenolic Airframe Tubing
- **Description**: Resin impregnated, spiral wrapped, heat-cured tubing
- **Key Properties**:
  - 5x compression strength of cardboard
  - Heat resistant
  - Easy to work with and finish smoothly
  - Fraction of fiberglass cost
- **Sizes Available**: 1.1" to 11.4" diameters
- **Wall Thickness**: 0.062"-0.125" (depending on diameter)
- **Lengths**: 36" or 48" standard
- **Weights**: 4.6 oz to 104 oz
- **Cost Range**: $12.99-$104.99
- **Recommended Use**: 
  - All motor mounts and minimum diameter boosters
  - Any 1.1"-2.5" diameter rocket regardless of speed
  - 3.0"-11.4" diameter up to 0.85 Mach
  - Base for fiber-reinforced applications
  - Transonic/supersonic flights in smaller diameters

### Pre-Glassed Phenolic Airframes (Fiberglass-Wrapped)
- **Description**: Phenolic tubing compression wrapped, baked, and pre-glassed for enhanced strength
- **Key Properties**:
  - Extremely strong, handles nearly any flight profile
  - Comes pre-sanded, ready for priming/painting
  - Smooth finish
  - Wall thickness ~0.095"-0.163" (thicker than base phenolic)
- **Sizes Available**: 2.1" to 11.4" diameters
- **Weights**: 14 oz to 180 oz
- **Cost Range**: $78.99-$236.99 (significantly higher than base phenolic)
- **Recommended Use**:
  - Any rocket expected to exceed 0.85 Mach
  - Applications with rough handling or hard landings
  - When extra strength required and weight is not a constraint

### Phenolic Coupler Tubes
- **Description**: Coupler/connector sections for joining airframe segments
- **Sizes**: 1.5" to 11.4" diameters
- **Lengths**: 3"-16" depending on size
- **Cost Range**: $2.09-$44.49
- **Full-Length Coupler Tubes (CTF)**: 36"-48" length options available
- **Coupler/Bulkhead Assemblies (CBA)**: Pre-assembled coupler with bulkhead, $4.99-$57.79

## Custom Services Offered
- **Custom Cutting**: $1.50 per cut (phenolic), $2.50 per cut (fiberglass-wrapped)
- **Slotting for Fin Mounting**: $2.50-$6.00 per slot depending on tube type
- **Dado Slotting** (surface-mounted fins): $2.50-$6.00 per slot
- **Large Airframe Slotting** (11.4" diameter): $6.00 per slot

## Notable Details
- Document shows strong cost/performance tradeoffs between material types
- Quantum Tube is emphasized as new, easier-to-use material for standard applications
- Phenolic provides better heat resistance and strength-to-cost ratio
- Fiberglass-wrapped phenolic is premium option for extreme performance
- All materials readily available with quick turnaround on custom fabrication
