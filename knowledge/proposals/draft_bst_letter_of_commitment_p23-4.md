## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies commits to supporting Barron Associates as a subcontractor on an Army SBIR Phase II effort focused on Graph Neural Networks for unmanned systems collaborative control. BST will contribute UAV design expertise, systems integration, flight operations knowledge, and will investigate commercialization pathways for the developed technology within BST's product line.
