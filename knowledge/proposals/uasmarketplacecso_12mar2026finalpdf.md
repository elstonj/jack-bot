# UAS Marketplace Commercial Solution Opening

## Document Metadata

- Client/Agency: U.S. Army Project Management Office (PMO) Uncrewed Aircraft Systems Capability Program; Army Contracting Command - Redstone
- Program/Solicitation: UAS Marketplace CSO; Solicitation Number W58RGZ-26-S-C001
- Date: March 12, 2026
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: None
- Key Personnel: None named in document

## Executive Summary
This is an open-ended, perpetual solicitation from the U.S. Army's PMO UAS establishing a marketplace approach to rapidly acquire innovative commercial UAS solutions and related capabilities. The program aims to accelerate adoption of advanced commercial technology, transform UAS capabilities, and grow the industrial base through continuous competition and soldier-driven demand rather than traditional DoD acquisition models.

## Technical Approach
The solicitation operates through two submission pathways:

**Method 1 - Innovative Warfighter Solutions (IWS):**
- Open-ended submissions to any Area of Interest (AOI) without specific requirements
- Three-phase evaluation process: Solution Brief → Live Demonstration/Pitch (optional) → Commercial Solution Proposal
- Rejected submissions retained in "Solution Basket" for future consideration
- Direct pathway for vendors to propose solutions

**Method 2 - Call for Solutions (C4S):**
- Government-issued specific calls for solutions posted on SAM.gov as amendments
- Submission requirements vary by phase (solution briefs, white papers, demonstrations, pitches)
- Independent from IWS procedures; each Call governs its own evaluation process
- Calls designed broadly to maximize efficiency and participation

## Notable Details

**Award Structure:**
- Awards may include FAR contracts under Part 12 (Commercial Procedures), fixed-price or fixed-price-incentive contracts, and non-FAR agreements under 10 U.S.C. §4022 and §4023
- Iterative prototyping allowed under Other Transaction (OT) awards, permitting scope modification and technology adaptation for multiple mission sets through sequential phased awards
- Subject to Congressional appropriations

**Eligibility Requirements:**
- UEI number and SAM.gov registration required
- Registration in Wide Area Work Flow invoicing system required
- Small business size representation required
- Must not be suspended, debarred, or prohibited by Executive Order
- Organizational Conflict of Interests (OCIs) must be disclosed

**Intellectual Property Protection:**
- Proprietary information protected under Trade Secrets Act, Economic Espionage Act, FOIA exemptions, and Executive Order 12600
- Non-Government advisors used in evaluation must sign NDAs
- Companies must label confidential information; submissions not returned

**Submission Details:**
- IWS submissions to: UASMarketplaceIWS@peo.avn.mil (with AOI in subject line)
- General questions to: usarmy.redstone.peo-avn.mbx.uas-marketplace@army.mil
- Solicitation remains open in perpetuity until canceled
- Frequently updated to reflect evolving threats, needs, and innovations

**Strategic Intent:**
- Executes Executive Order 14307 to scale industrial base and accelerate production
- Shifts from traditional DoD acquisition to free-market competition model
- Directly links Soldier demands to industry-provided capabilities
- Incentivizes innovation by enabling continuous competitive environment
