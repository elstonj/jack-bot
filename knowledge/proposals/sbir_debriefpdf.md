## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies received a Phase II award nomination for developing a production-ready Sonobuoy-Launched Unmanned Aerial System (SL UAS) equipped with magnetic anomaly detection (MAD) and in-water passive acoustic sensor deployment capabilities. The system is designed to support Anti-Submarine Warfare (ASW) operations including cued search, detection, tracking, and classification of submarines.

## Technical Approach
BST proposed to:
- Fabricate, integrate, and validate a production-ready SL UAS platform
- Integrate advanced magnetic anomaly detection (MAD) sensors
- Develop capabilities for deploying in-water passive acoustic sensors
- Support ASW cued search, detection, tracking, and classification operations

The evaluators noted BST demonstrated excellent understanding of topic goals, clear explanation of concept and design path, and well-versed personnel in necessary fields.
