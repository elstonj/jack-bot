## Executive Summary
Roccor, LLC provides a letter of commitment to support Black Swift Technologies' 2018 NASA SBIR Phase I proposal, specifically committing to assist with requirements definition, design concept generation, structural analysis, and material survey for deployable aircraft wings intended for Venus operations.

## Technical Approach
Roccor committed to the following work:
- Requirements definition for deployable aircraft wings
- Preliminary design concept generation for deployable wings
- Preliminary structural analysis of the design concept
- Survey of composite material options for harsh Venutian environment compatibility
- Generation of Phase II fabrication and testing plan for prototype system

## Products & Capabilities Described
**Deployable Aircraft Wings:**
- Proposed system for Venus operations
- Requires survivability in harsh Venutian environmental conditions
- Intended to be prototype-tested in Phase II following Phase I design work
- Roccor specializing in deployable technology design and analysis

## Use Cases & Applications
- Venus atmospheric exploration/operations via aircraft
- Deployable wing system for aircraft operating in extreme Venus environment
