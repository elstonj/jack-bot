## Executive Summary
This SOW defines University of Colorado Boulder's Research and Engineering Center for Unmanned Vehicles (RECUV) contribution to a Black Swift Technologies NOAA SBIR Phase 1 project. RECUV will provide aerodynamic design analysis and airframe verification support to assist BST in developing UAV systems for National Weather Service missions.

## Technical Approach
- **CFD Aerodynamic Analysis**: Computational Fluid Dynamics modeling to estimate aerodynamic coefficients and stability derivatives for candidate airframe designs
- **Airframe Design Verification**: Assessment and verification of airframe capabilities against performance requirements
- **Regular coordination** with BST through Technical Exchange Meetings (TEMs)
