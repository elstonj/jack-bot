## Executive Summary
This is a standard federal compliance representation form that applicants must sign when submitting proposals to USDA/NIFA. It requires the applicant to certify that it does not impose internal confidentiality agreements that would prevent employees, contractors, or subrecipients from lawfully reporting fraud, waste, or abuse to federal investigators.

## Document Content
This is a legal/compliance template, not a substantive technical or capability document. It serves as a required certification statement for federal grant applications, based on provisions in the Consolidated Appropriations Act, 2016 (Pub. L. 114-113, sections 743-744).

**Key Compliance Requirements:**
- Applicant must represent it does not restrict reporting of fraud, waste, or abuse to federal agencies
- Exception noted for classified information protocols (Standard Form 312, Form 4414, etc.)
- Requires signature of authorized representative with date and proposal number
