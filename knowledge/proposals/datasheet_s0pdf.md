## Executive Summary
The S0 is a cost-effective, modular VTOL unmanned aircraft system designed for atmospheric research and environmental monitoring in extreme conditions. It operates autonomously up to 15,000 ft AGL with 60-minute endurance and is equipped with sensors for measuring thermodynamic parameters including temperature, wind speed/direction, dewpoint, and atmospheric pressure.

## Technical Approach
The S0 leverages Black Swift's SwiftCore™ Flight Management System to enable:
- Fully autonomous flight operations with minimal operator training
- Sensor-based intelligent control systems that autonomously modify flight paths based on sensor inputs
- Rapid integration of modular sensor payloads
- "Launch-and-forget" automated sampling patterns and scripting

The aircraft features pivoting motors for VTOL capability, eliminating traditional launcher constraints and enabling deployment from any terrain with minimal infrastructure.
