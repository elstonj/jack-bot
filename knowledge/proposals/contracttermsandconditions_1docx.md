### Supply Chain Security (FASCSA)
Contractor must comply with Federal Acquisition Supply Chain Security Act (FASCSA) orders regarding:
- Covered articles (IT, telecommunications equipment, cloud services, processing systems)
- Prohibition of products/services from named sources subject to FASCSA orders
- Quarterly review of SAM.gov for new restrictions
- Reporting requirements within 3-10 business days if prohibited items are identified
- Mandatory flow-down of FASCSA compliance clauses to all subcontractors

### Payment Terms
- Electronic Funds Transfer via System for Award Management (SAM)
- Comptroller General access to contractor records for 3 years after final payment
- Small business subcontractor acceleration payment provisions

### Contract Closeout
For Simplified Acquisition Procedures (SAP):
- Unobligated funds ≤$1,000: Unilateral deobligation modification
- Unobligated funds >$1,000: Bilateral deobligation modification with 60-day signature requirement

### Subcontractor Requirements
Must flow down mandatory FAR clauses including whistleblower protections, labor standards, equal opportunity, trafficking prevention, and FASCSA compliance to all lower-tier subcontractors.
