# Summary of Changes from Draft to Final

## Document Metadata
- Type: Solicitation amendment/clarification document
- Client/Agency: U.S. Air Force Research Laboratory (AFRL)
- Program/Solicitation: AMAC (Advanced Manufacturing and Advanced Composites)
- Date: February 10, 2026
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: None
- Key Personnel: Beck Cotter (last editor)

## Executive Summary
This document summarizes revisions made to the AMAC solicitation between draft and final versions. Changes include added technical definitions in the Statement of Objectives (SOO), updated submission procedures and deadlines, clarified evaluation criteria, and added security and compliance requirements.

## Key Changes by Section

### Statement of Objectives (SOO) - Definitions Added
- Section 7.3.12: Photonics
- Section 7.3.13: High-Power Microwave Technologies
- Section 7.4.6: Integrated Vehicle Health Management
- Section 7.4.13: Microelectronics
- Section 7.4.15: Advanced Simulation and Virtual Prototyping

### Section L - Submission Requirements
- **Deadline**: February 27, 2026, 5:00 PM Eastern Standard Time
- **Submission Method**: Electronic via Enterprise Portal (link provided on SAM.gov)
- **Point of Contact**: afrl.amac.mailbox@us.af.mil
- **Volumes to Submit**: Volumes I, II, and III via Enterprise Portal
- **Representations & Certifications**: Requirement clarified that all required reps and certs must be completed

### Section M - Evaluation and Award
- **Basis for Award**: Added reference to National Industrial Security Program Operating Manual (NISPOM)
- **Evaluation Weights**: Removed duplicated evaluation table for clarity
- **Small Business Participation Factor**: Corrected reference for "Acceptable" and "Unacceptable" ratings to Section L 4.2

### Section B - Supplies or Services
- **Ordering Period**: Added clarification that ordering period will be formally outlined at award for CLINs 1001, 1002, 2001, 2002

### Section I - Contract Clauses
- Added FAR Clause 52.204-2 (Security Requirements) and its Alternate I

## Notable Details
- This is a final solicitation update after draft review; all changes are clarifications or corrections rather than substantive program modifications
- The document emphasizes that the official solicitation and attachments are the governing documents
- Focus on security compliance (NISPOM reference) and electronic submission procedures
