## Executive Summary
SwiftCore is a commercial-grade (TRL 9) modular flight management system that enables autonomous unmanned aircraft operations through a distributed architecture separating flight-critical control from mission computing. The system allows rapid integration of new sensors, autonomy algorithms, and payloads without redesigning the aircraft, and has been operationally proven in extreme environments including hurricanes, Arctic conditions, and volcanic plumes.

## Technical Approach

**Architecture:**
- Distributed, modular avionics platform built on separated flight-critical and mission-computing layers
- Deterministic flight control runs on embedded processors
- Advanced autonomy and mission logic operate on separate computing layer via structured messaging and open systems interfaces
- Supervisory safety controller continuously monitors autonomy modules and enforces operational limits with automatic reversion to validated control logic

**Key Innovation:**
- "Safe sandbox" approach allows rapid experimentation with advanced autonomy while maintaining mission assurance
- Well-defined Interface Control Documents (ICDs) enable third-party payload and mission hardware integration
- Prevents vendor lock-in through modular architecture

**Software/IP:**
- Rigorously maintained in-house software stack
- NDAA-compliant
- U.S.-designed and manufactured
- Maintains full BST control of core avionics intellectual property
