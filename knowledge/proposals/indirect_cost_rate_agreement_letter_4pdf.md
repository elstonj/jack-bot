# Indirect Cost Rate Agreement Letter

## Document Metadata
- Type: Administrative letter / Rate approval
- Client/Agency: NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration)
- Issuing Office: NOAA Grants Management Division (GMD), Acquisition and Grants Office
- Date: February 18, 2021
- BST Contact: Melissa Phillips, Black Swift Technologies
- Sender: Lamar Dwayne Revis, NOAA Grants Officer

## Executive Summary
NOAA grants officer Lamar Revis approves Black Swift Technologies' request to use provisional indirect cost rates for fiscal year 2021, allowing BST to proceed with NOAA-funded work while the formal rate agreement is finalized. This letter permits BST to use the rates cited in their submitted Indirect Cost Rate Proposal pending completion of the standard CPA audit review process.

## Key Financial Rates Approved
- **Provisional Indirect Cost Rate: 18.32%**
- **Fringe Rate: 29.28%**
- Effective: Fiscal Year 2021
- Date of BST's rate proposal submission to NOAA GMD: November 16, 2020

## Administrative Context
- NOAA lacks a contracted CPA firm to conduct the standard audit review at time of letter
- NOAA Contracting Office had not completed solicitation for a new audit firm
- Normal process requires: BST submits proposal → NOAA forwards to CPA firm → CPA reviews → signed agreement returned to BST
- This letter authorizes provisional use pending completion of normal review process
- Formal signed rate agreement will be issued once NOAA secures CPA firm and completes review
