## RFI Context & Requirements

### NASA AFA Initiative Focus Areas
- **Flight Regimes:** Subsonic (including vertical lift), supersonic, hypersonic
- **Technology Categories:** Propulsion, airframe concepts, integrated systems, power systems, GNC (guidance, navigation, control), avionics, sensors, advanced instrumentation, materials, autonomy, airspace operations, communication technologies
- **ARMD Strategic Transformations:**
  1. Pioneer High Speed Flight
  2. Automate Airspace and Safety Management
  3. Transform Airframes and Propulsion
  4. Revolutionize Aerospace Engineering Methods

### Partnership Mechanisms
- Funded Space Act Agreements (FSAA)
- SBIR/STTR Phase III
- Similar partnership models
- **Cost Share Required:** Respondents must assume some level of cost share; NASA contribution may include in-kind resources (expertise, flight assets)

### Timeline
- Flight testing must begin no later than year 3
- Subscale demonstrations and multi-spiral approaches acceptable
- Target flight window: FY26–FY28

### Submission Requirements
- Maximum 8 pages (single-spaced, 12-point font, 1-inch margins)
- PDF format
- Submit to: jennifer.schnarr@nasa.gov
- Deadline: May 7, 2026, 1:00 PM PDT
- Subject line: "AFA RFI Response – Black Swift Technologies LLC"
