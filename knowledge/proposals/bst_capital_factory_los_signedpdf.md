## Executive Summary
Bryan Chambers, President and Co-Founder of Capital Factory (a leading early-stage investor network in Texas), provides a letter of support for Black Swift Technologies' AFWERX SBIR Phase II proposal. The letter endorses BST's experience in the UAS sector and highlights the company's R&D investments in technologies of DOD interest, including air-deployed drones, American-made UAS electronics, and advanced sensing capabilities for soil moisture mapping and methane detection.
