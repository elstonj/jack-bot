## Executive Summary
This document outlines BST's planned work to evaluate and implement modifications to the S0 UAS platform to enable compatibility with the Sonobuoy Launch Container (SLC). The work will involve airframe modifications at BST's prototyping facilities to support SLC initialization and launch dynamics, with risk mitigation through iterative testing and simulation.

## Technical Approach
- **Location**: BST's prototyping facilities
- **Scope**: Identify and implement necessary airframe modifications to the S0 UAS platform
- **Key Requirements**: Accommodate SLC initialization and launch dynamics
- **Methodology**: 
  - Iterative testing and simulation
  - CAD software analysis
  - Physical prototype development
- **Risk Management**: Address potential structural incompatibilities and deployment inefficiencies through testing and simulation
