## Product Description
**LimeSDR Mini (WRL-15029)**
- **Price:** $186.95
- **Core Components:**
  - Altera MAX 10 FPGA
  - Lime Microsystems LMS7002M RF transceiver (dual-channel)
  - SMA connectors (vs. micro U.FL on full-size LimeSDR)
- **Connectivity:** USB, integrates with Snappy Ubuntu Core
- **Comparable Product:** Full-size LimeSDR (WRL-15027, $349.95) available with 4 channels
