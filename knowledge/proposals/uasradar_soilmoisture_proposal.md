## Executive Summary
This proposal presents a concept for using an Unmanned Aircraft System (UAS) equipped with an L-band radiometer to measure and downscale soil moisture data for improved watershed and resource management. The approach combines remote sensing radiometry with UAS deployment to address scientific requirements identified by USGS.

## Technical Approach
The proposal focuses on:
- Integration of L-band radiometry on a UAS platform
- Downscaling soil moisture measurements to finer spatial resolution
- Application to watershed and resource management decision-making

## Products & Capabilities Described
- **UAS Platform with L-band Radiometer**: A remotely piloted aircraft equipped with radiometric sensors for measuring soil moisture via microwave emission at L-band frequencies (typically 1.4 GHz)
  - Use case: Downscaling coarse-resolution satellite soil moisture data to finer spatial resolution for watershed management
  - Application: Resource management and hydrological monitoring
