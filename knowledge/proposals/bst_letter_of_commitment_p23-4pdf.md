# BST Letter of Commitment for US Army SBIR Topic A214-044

## Document Metadata
- Type: Letter of Commitment / Letter of Support
- Client/Agency: US Army (via Barron Associates, Inc., prime contractor)
- Program/Solicitation: SBIR Topic A214-044 "Graph Neural Networks (GNN) for UxS Collaborative Agent Control"; RFP # P23-4
- Date: 12 January 2023
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: General product line (not specifically named)
- Key Personnel: Jack Elston, PhD (Co-Founder and CEO)

## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies commits to supporting Barron Associates as a subcontractor on a US Army SBIR Phase II effort focused on developing Graph Neural Networks for unmanned systems (UxS) collaborative agent control. BST will contribute technical expertise in UAV design, systems integration, and flight operations, and will investigate commercialization pathways for the developed technology within BST's product line.

## Technical Approach
BST's proposed contribution focuses on:
- Technical expertise in UAV design, systems integration, and flight operations
- Investigation of commercialization paths for GNN technology within BST's existing product line
- Integration of developed GNN control algorithms into operational UxS platforms

## Products & Capabilities Described
- **UAV Design & Flight Operations**: BST positions itself as a leader in innovative UAV design and flight operation development
- **Systems Integration**: Capability to integrate advanced control algorithms into operational systems
- **Commercialization pathway**: Ability to transition research technology into commercial products

## Use Cases & Applications
- Unmanned systems (UxS) collaborative agent control
- Multi-agent coordination using graph neural networks
