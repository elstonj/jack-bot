## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies proposes a 7-month effort to develop coordinated drone-based plume tracking capabilities, leveraging neural volumetric capture technology in collaboration with Brookhaven National Laboratory. The project focuses on software development, flight planning implementation, and sensor integration on BST's UAS platforms for advanced plume detection and tracking.
