## Key Contractual Terms

### Payment & Performance
- **Type**: Firm fixed price (FFP) with no cost overrun liability for Creare
- **Invoicing**: Monthly milestone-based invoicing up to not-to-exceed amount
- **Final Invoice**: Must be submitted within 30 days of period of performance completion
- **Certification Required**: Invoices must certify compliance with FAR Part 31 cost principles

### Termination
- **For Convenience**: 30 days written notice
- **For Default**: 10 days to cure after written notice
- **Effective**: Upon receipt of notice; Supplier must stop work immediately
- **Return of Property**: All hardware and software must be returned within 30 days

### Prior Approval Required for:
- Changes to scope or specific aims
- Significant rebudgeting (>25% of total award or $250,000, whichever is less)
- Key personnel changes
- Organizational changes or name changes
- Extensions of period of performance
- Purchase of special tooling or equipment
- Actions requiring additional funds exceeding fixed price ceiling

### Intellectual Property
- **Background IP**: Owned by originating party (Creare or BST); no ownership transfer
- **Project IP**: Owned by party whose personnel conceived or first reduced to practice
- **Jointly Made IP**: Jointly owned unless otherwise agreed; Creare has first option to perfect rights
- **Disclosure Requirements**: FAR Clause 52.227-11 applies

### Work Location & Personnel
- All R&D work must be performed in the United States
- Foreign nationals may not perform work without Government Contracting Officer approval
- American-made equipment preferred in procurement

### Compliance & Regulatory
- **Export Control**: Subject to ITAR and U.S. export control laws; Supplier represents ITAR registration if handling defense articles
- **Audit**: Subject to Federal audit agency review and 2 CFR 200 Subpart F compliance
- **Employment**: E-Verify required; compliance with EEO, OSHA, labor standards, and anti-discrimination laws
- **Debarment Certification**: Supplier certifies no debarment or suspension status

### Insurance Requirements (Appendix A)
- **Workers' Compensation**: State-required minimums; $100K each accident, $100K disease per employee, $500K disease aggregate
- **General Liability**: $1M each occurrence, $1M personal/advertising injury, $1M products/completed operations, $2M general aggregate per project; includes premises, operations, products/completed operations, independent contractors
- **Umbrella Liability**: $2M minimum occurrence and aggregate; follows form over GL and auto liability
- **Additional Insured**: Creare LLC and Affiliates named on GL and Umbrella policies
- **Notice**: 30 days advance notice of cancellation required

### Property Management
- Creare may furnish government or company property
- BST responsible for protection and maintenance (FAR 52.245-1)
- Title remains with Creare/Government
- Return required within 30 days of termination

### Other Notable Terms
- **Governing Law**: State of New York (without conflict of laws provisions)
- **Indemnification**: BST indemnifies Creare for property damage, death, personal injury from BST actions/omissions or those of its subcontractors
- **Patent Indemnification**: BST indemnifies Creare against patent/trademark infringement claims
- **Publications**: Advance approval from Creare required; Government support must be acknowledged
- **Proprietary Data**: Creare-furnished property may not be used for other purposes without written permission
- **Human Subjects Research**: Explicitly not permitted under POs referencing these terms
- **Force Majeure**: Covers Acts of God, government restrictions, pandemics, public health emergencies, wars, insurrections
