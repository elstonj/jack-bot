## Notable Details
**Per Diem Rates for Oakland, Alameda County (FY 2025):**
- Daily lodging rate: $145 (excluding taxes)
- Meals & Incidental Expenses (M&IE) total: $92/day
  - Breakfast: $23
  - Lunch: $26
  - Dinner: $38
  - Incidental expenses: $5
- First and last day of travel: 75% of M&IE total = $69.00
