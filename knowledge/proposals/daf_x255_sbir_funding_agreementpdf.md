## Certification Requirements Addressed
The form certifies compliance with 18 key SBIR program requirements:

1. **Ownership & Control**: Small business meets requirements under 13 CFR 121.702
2. **Corporate Documentation**: If applicable, corporate documents evidence ownership/control requirements
3. **Partnership Agreement**: If applicable, partnership agreement meets requirements
4. **LLC Documentation**: If applicable, LLC articles and operating agreements meet requirements
5. **Citizenship**: Key personnel are U.S. citizens or permanent resident aliens
6. **Employee Count**: Business has ≤500 employees including affiliates
7. **Size Determination**: No SBA size determination currently in effect exceeding 500 employees
8. **PI/PM Time Commitment**: Principal Investigator/Project Manager commits >50% of 40-hour workweek (or approved deviation)
9. **Essentially Equivalent Work**: Work has not been funded elsewhere, or funded portion approved by Funding Agreement Officer
10. **Work Performance Percentage**: 
    - SBIR Phase I: ≥66.67% of research performed by awardee
    - SBIR Phase II: ≥50% of research performed by awardee
    - STTR: ≥40% of research performed
11. **U.S. Performance**: R&D performed in United States (unless waiver granted)
12. **Facility & Employee Performance**: R&D performed at awardee's facilities by awardee's employees
13. **Venture Capital Registration**: SBIR awardee status regarding VC/hedge fund/PE ownership
14. **Covered Small Business Status**: Certification of VC ownership status on application vs. award dates
15. **Dual Funding Notification**: Awardee will notify agency immediately if work is subsequently funded by another Federal Agency
16. **STTR Management Control**: [For STTR only] Small business (not research institution) exercises management direction
17. **Information Sharing Acknowledgment**: Understands information may be shared with Federal, State, local agencies
18. **Officer Authorization & Legal Certification**: Authorized officer certifies truthfulness of all information; acknowledges criminal/civil/administrative penalties for false information
