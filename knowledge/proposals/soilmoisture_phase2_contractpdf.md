## Document Metadata
- **Type:** NASA SBIR Phase II Contract (Standard Form 26 - Award/Contract)
- **Client/Agency:** NASA / NASA Shared Services Center (NSSC), Stennis Space Center
- **Program/Solicitation:** NASA SBIR Phase II; Proposal 12-2 S3.05-8971
- **Contract Number:** NNX14CG09C
- **Date:** Effective 2015 (contract document dated 2015-03-02)
- **Contractor:** Black Swift Technologies LLC, Boulder, CO

- **Period of Performance:** 24 months
- **BST Products/Systems Referenced:** S3 (referenced in proposal number "12-2 S3.05-8971")

## Executive Summary
NASA awarded Black Swift Technologies a Phase II SBIR contract to continue development of a soil moisture mapping unmanned aerial system (sUAS). This is a 24-month R&D contract with a fixed price of $749,751.00, incorporating BST's Phase II proposal as the Statement of Work. The contract spans quarterly demonstrations, reporting, prototype delivery, and new technology disclosures.

## Technical Approach
The work is governed by BST's 2012 Phase II proposal number 12-2 S3.05-8971 titled "Soil Moisture Mapping sUAS," incorporated by reference as the Statement of Work. The proposal details (proposal section and Budget Form C) are marked as proprietary and not for external release.

**Key Contract Deliverables Structure:**
- IT Security Management Plan (30 days after award)
- Quarterly Demonstration Reports #1-7 (15 days after each quarter)
- Final Report, Briefing Chart, Summary Report with NTSR/NTR
- Prototype delivery at end of contract with DD Form 250

## Products & Capabilities Described

### S3 System
- **What it is:** Referenced in the proposal as part of BST's sUAS capability
- **Application:** Soil moisture mapping using unmanned aerial systems

### Soil Moisture Mapping sUAS
- Unmanned aerial system designed for soil moisture measurement/mapping
