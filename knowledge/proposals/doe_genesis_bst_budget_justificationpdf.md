## Technical Approach

The project develops AI-enabled autonomous laboratory capabilities through:

1. **Platform Testing & Validation:** Three S0-VTOL UAS platforms will serve as mobile autonomous laboratories equipped to measure atmospheric thermodynamic parameters (wind, pressure, temperature, humidity)

2. **AI Framework Development:** Dr. Nathan Urban leading AI framework coordination at BNL with intellectual input and supervision of implementation

3. **Atmospheric Science Integration:** Dr. Gijs de Boer providing domain expertise to align boundary layer demonstrations with atmospheric science community needs

4. **Real-Time Adaptive Experimentation:** System designed for dynamic environmental sampling with adaptive decision-making capabilities
