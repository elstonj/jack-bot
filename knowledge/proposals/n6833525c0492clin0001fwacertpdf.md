**Key FWA Areas Emphasized in Tutorial:**
- Eligibility requirements (51% small business ownership, SBA size standards, adequate facilities, PI employment requirements)
- Multiple agency payment disclosures
- Recordkeeping (timesheets, receipts, invoices, lab notebooks, facility agreements, status updates)
- Work performance requirements (SBIR: 2/3 company work Phase I, 1/2 Phase II; STTR: 40% company, 30% research institution)
- PI primary employment requirement (51% for SBIR)
- Technical proposal content integrity (no plagiarism, fabricated letters, or false statements)
- Allowable vs. unallowable costs (no personal expenses, vacations, mortgages, etc.)
- Indirect cost accountability

**Consequences of Violations:**
- Criminal: false statements (5 yrs), theft of federal property (10 yrs), wire fraud (20 yrs)
- Civil: treble damages, up to $11,000 per false claim
- Administrative: contract termination, 3-year debarment, federal contract prohibition

**Status:** Compliance requirement satisfied for Phase I contract administration.
