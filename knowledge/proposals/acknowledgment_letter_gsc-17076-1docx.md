## Executive Summary
NASA Goddard Space Flight Center formally acknowledges receipt of Black Swift Technologies' invention disclosure for a "High Data Rate Compact Logger" and assigns it NASA Case No. GSC-17076-1. The letter outlines BST's rights and obligations regarding patent filing and licensing under the NASA SBIR patent rights clause (37 C.F.R. § 401.14).

## Invention Disclosed
- **Title:** High Data Rate Compact Logger
- **Status:** Invention disclosure submitted under NASA SBIR contract/grant
- **Context:** Developed under 2012 NASA SBIR Phase I program related to Soil Moisture monitoring

## Key Obligations Outlined
1. BST has a specified time period to elect title to the invention
2. After electing to retain title, BST must file initial patent application within required timeframe
3. BST must provide: filing date, application number, patent application copies, and issued patents for all countries where applications are filed
4. BST must complete and return NASA Form 1604 ("License to the Government") to NASA
