# Letter of Support - AcroOptics

## Document Metadata
- Type: Letter of Support
- Client/Agency: Black Swift Technologies (BST)
- Program/Solicitation: NASA 2012 SBIR - Soil Moisture topic
- Date: March 21, 2017
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: Soil Moisture Radiometer Instrument
- Key Personnel: Maciej Stachura (BST CTO); Michael A. Hurowitz (AcroOptics VP Product Development)

## Executive Summary
AcroOptics LLC provides a letter of support committing consulting resources to Black Swift Technologies' effort to develop an improved Soil Moisture Radiometer Instrument in collaboration with the University of Colorado. The support covers productization activities across multiple engineering disciplines.

## Technical Approach
AcroOptics committed to provide expert consulting on:
- System architecture
- Electronics design and implementation
- Mechanical design of the instrument
- Design for manufacturability
- Tooling and production of next revision prototypes
- Certification and compliance analysis
- Project management support for key tasks

## Products & Capabilities Described
**Soil Moisture Radiometer Instrument:**
- An improved design being developed by BST in collaboration with University of Colorado
- Focus on productization (moving from prototype to production-ready design)
- Subject of engineering review and optimization across multiple disciplines

## Use Cases & Applications
Soil moisture measurement/monitoring (specific applications not detailed in this support letter).
