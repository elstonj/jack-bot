## Executive Summary
This document is an out-brief on the PY24 STRATFI/TACFI program, designed to bridge the capability gap between SBIR/STTR Phase II efforts and Phase III scaling. The brief covers program purpose, eligibility criteria, structure (two funding tracks: TACFI at $375K-$1.9M and STRATFI at $3M-$15M), previous cycle results, common reasons for non-selection, and next steps for applicants.

## Program Purpose
- Catalyze relationships between Air Force/Space Force end-users, acquisition professionals, private-sector innovators, and investors
- Bridge the capability gap between SBIR/STTR Phase II efforts and Phase III scaling
- Facilitate delivery of strategic capabilities for the Department of the Air Force

## Eligibility Criteria
Must meet all of the following:
- Company maintains Small Business Concern (SBC) status and SBIR/STTR eligibility
- On an active SBIR/STTR Phase II contract or completed within 2 years of Capability Package submission (date calculated from final Phase II deliverable)
- Subject effort has not already been awarded a Sequential Phase II
- At least 90 days have passed since Phase II execution began
- Not executing a prior STRATFI effort at submission time
- Anticipated work performed in the United States
- Able to achieve minimum matching/investment requirements
- Able to obtain required End-User & Customer Collaboration Agreement

## Program Structure

### TACFI (Tactical Funding Increase)
- Funding range: $375K - $1.9M
- Increased to $1.9M maximum for PY24

### STRATFI (Strategic Funding Increase)
- Funding range: $3M - $15M

## Key Changes for PY24
- Two separate submission windows (one for STRATFI, one for TACFI)
- TACFI maximum increased from previous level to $1.9M
- Updated guidance, documentation, and templates
- New Submission Chart Attachment to Collaboration Agreement
- Government organization responsible for submitting through App Sheet
- Government POC Kick-Off Meetings instituted after submission

## Non-Selection Trends (Common Reasons for Rejection)
### Administrative/Submission Issues
- Incomplete or inaccurate submission packages
- Missing Capability Package elements (technical information, scale of outcome)
- Missing or incorrect End-User/Customer/TPOC (Technical Point of Contact) information
- Inaccurate funds matching (Phase III errors, ineligible third-party funds)
- Misalignment between milestones and funding
- Incorrect document signatories
- Inaccurate Phase II contract numbers
- Document integrity issues (inconsistent information across documents)
- Finance/Contracting acceptance problems
- Contract Award Responsibility issues
- Use of outdated forms (not PY24 versions)

### Technical/Strategic Issues
- Weak or poor Transition Plan
- Insufficient strength of support
- Misalignment with Department of the Air Force/Space Force needs
- Misunderstanding of SBA program intent (supplemental funding is incentive, not reward)

## Next Steps for Non-Selected Companies
1. **Pursue Phase III:** Non-competitive award without SBIR/STTR funds (more info at https://afwerx.com/divisions/afventures/phase-iii/)
2. **Prepare for next Notice of Opportunity (NOO):** Continue business development efforts

4. **Strategic Focus:** Prioritize commercialization efforts
