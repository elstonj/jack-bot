## Executive Summary
Jack S. Elston is the CEO and Founder of Black Swift Technologies, holding a PhD in Aerospace Engineering and MS degrees in both Aerospace and Electrical Engineering from University of Colorado. His career spans UAS design, networked command and control systems, and atmospheric sampling with small unmanned aircraft, with significant publications in these areas since 2008.

## Professional Background

**Education:**
- PhD in Aerospace Engineering (2011) - University of Colorado, Boulder
- MS in Aerospace Engineering (2005) - University of Colorado, Boulder
- MS in Electrical Engineering (2005) - University of Colorado, Boulder
- BS in Electrical Engineering (2003) - University of Colorado, Boulder

**Career Positions:**
- 2011-present: CEO and Founder, Black Swift Technologies LLC
- 2012-present: NSF AGS (Atmospheric and Geospace Sciences) Postdoctoral Fellow
- 2010-2012: Professional Research Assistant on Marginal Ice Zone Observations and Processes Experiment (MIZOPEX)
- 2011: UAS Consultant at Forge Aeronautics
- 2004-2007: Graduate Student Researcher, University of Colorado

## Technical Expertise & Research Focus

**Key Research Areas:**
- Small fixed-wing unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) for meteorological sampling
- Networked command and control of heterogeneous UAS
- Semi-autonomous aircraft guidance for severe weather penetration
- National Airspace System (NAS) integration for small UAS
- Tornado and supercell thunderstorm sampling
