## Executive Summary
This is a template memorandum issued by AFRL/RGK designating a Technical Point of Contact (TPOC) for AFWERX Phase II SBIR proposals post-award. It outlines the TPOC's responsibilities, authorities, limitations, required training, and compliance obligations for monitoring contractor performance and managing contract deliverables.

## Document Purpose & Context
This is a **template/form document** establishing the formal designation and responsibilities for the Air Force's TPOC role in AFWERX Phase II SBIR contracts. It is not a proposal or technical document but rather an administrative/contractual framework document.

## Key TPOC Responsibilities Outlined
1. Monitor cost, technical, and schedule performance; identify and report marginal/unsatisfactory performance
2. Ensure contractor personnel receive required briefings on security, emergency management, and accountability
3. Receive and accept contract deliverables to enable timely contractor payment
4. Maintain liaison with Phase 2 PM (p2@afwerx.af.mil) and Contracting Officer
5. Work with contractor and government organizations to obtain required approvals/waivers for:
   - Security requirements (CACs, DD254 coordination)
   - Program protection compliance
   - Cybersecurity compliance
   - Flight testing and UAS regulations
   - Safety measures
6. Maintain comprehensive project documentation and case files
7. Identify replacement TPOC if unable to continue

## TPOC Authorities & Limitations
**Authorized to:**
- Monitor performance
- Accept deliverables
- Coordinate approvals and resolve execution issues

**NOT Authorized to:**
- Discuss performance or proposal details prior to contract award
- Make commitments or changes affecting price, quality, quantity, delivery schedule, scope, or contract terms
- Instruct contractor on performance methods (only communicate requirements)
- Participate in contractor hiring/termination
- Obligate Government funds or execute contract changes

## Required & Optional Training
**Required:**
- AFWERX-specific TPOC training (online)
- Additional training based on contract-specific activities and regulatory requirements

**Optional:**
- ACQ 1010: Fundamentals of Systems Acquisition Management (8 hrs)
- CLC 106: Contracting Officer's Representative with Mission Focus (3 hrs)
- CLC 011: Contracting for the Rest of Us (2 hrs)

Training deadline: 30 days after award selection

## Compliance & Standards
- Compliance with DOD 5500.7-R (Joint Ethics Regulation)
- Compliance with FAR 3.2 (Contractor Gratuities)
- Conflict of interest avoidance
- Strict adherence to contractor proprietary data and business-sensitive information restrictions
- Personal liability warning for unauthorized acts

## Notable Details
- TPOC role designation is mandatory and must be signed with CAC-enabled digital signature
- Signed letter must be renamed per format: "Proposal#_TPOC Name" and emailed to p2@afwerx.af.mil
- Phase 2 Program Manager contact: p2@afwerx.af.mil (all PM-related correspondence)
- Document applies to open-topic AFWERX Phase II proposals
- Explicit restriction on discussing acquisition plans/strategies that could advantage one contractor over another
