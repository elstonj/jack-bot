## Executive Summary
This document presents technical design details and aerodynamic analysis of Black Swift Technologies' S3 UAS platform, demonstrating significant improvements over the predecessor S2 platform through refined wing aerodynamics, enhanced lift-to-drag ratio, and robust environmental sealing for extreme environment operations.

## Technical Approach
**Aerodynamic Design Improvements:**
- New S3 wing design features improved lift distribution closer to ideal elliptical distribution compared to S2
- Lift-to-drag (L/D) improvements: 9% over S2-VTOL 4m wing; 21% over S2 3m configuration
- Maximum L/D occurs at higher velocity, enabling better dash performance and longer maximum range
- Minimum sink rate reduced by 2% versus S2-VTOL 4m and 16% versus S2 3m

**Environmental Sealing & Durability:**
- All interfaces designed with dust and water proofing
- Gasket-based sealing system for panel interfaces
- 3D printed aluminum heat sink design (demonstrated on S0 UAS) provides ESC cooling while maintaining dust/rain sealing
- Robust design suitable for extreme environment deployment

**Solar Integration:**
- Solar panel placement integrated into S3 wing design for extended endurance
