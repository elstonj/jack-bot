## Executive Summary
This is Volume 5 (Supporting Documents) of a Navy SBIR Phase II proposal for magnetometer work. The document captures BST's assertions regarding data rights for pre-existing proprietary systems (SwiftPilot autopilot, control station, UI, and wind-sensing probe) that will be used or integrated in the proposed work, along with standard certifications and subcontractor information.

## Technical Approach
Not detailed in this volume. However, the proposal will leverage BST's existing autonomous UAS platform with integrated atmospheric sensing capabilities.

## Products & Capabilities Described

**SwiftPilot**
- Autopilot system with 3D wind sensing and atmospheric sensing capabilities
- Developed prior to this project; developed partially at private expense
- Data rights asserted by Dr. Jack Elston

**SwiftStation**
- Control station for SwiftPilot
- Developed prior to this project; developed partially at private expense
- Data rights asserted by Dr. Jack Elston

**SwiftTab**
- UI (user interface) for controlling UAS with SwiftPilot
- Developed prior to this project; developed partially at private expense
- Data rights asserted by Dr. Jack Elston

**SwiftFlow Probe**
- 5-hole probe designed to function in precipitation
- Provides 3D wind measurements
- Developed prior to this project; developed partially at private expense
- Data rights asserted by Dr. Jack Elston

**Web-Based Communications, Terrain and Wind-Aware Planning**
- Developed prior to this project; developed partially at private expense
- Data rights asserted by Dr. Jack Elston
