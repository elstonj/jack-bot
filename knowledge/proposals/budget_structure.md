- Program/Solicitation: 2026 IDIQ (Indefinite Delivery/Indefinite Quantity)
- Date: Created 2025-11-06; Modified 2025-11-18

- Key Personnel: Beck Cotter (last editor)

## Executive Summary
This document outlines the proposed contract structure and pricing mechanisms for the Black Swift S0 platform being offered to NOAA's Aircraft Operations Center under a 2026 IDIQ agreement. It establishes Firm Fixed Price, Time & Materials, and Cost-Plus-Fixed-Fee contract types across ten major activity categories ranging from platform procurement to testing.

## Budget Structure & Contract Types

| Section | Activity | Contract Type | Basis/Notes |
|---------|----------|---------------|------------|
| 4.1 | Platform Procurement | Firm Fixed Price (FFP) | Per aircraft; annually adjustable for component updates, new features, inflation |
| 4.2-4.3 | Training Delivery | FFP per standard course | Includes instructor time, materials, standard deliverables |
| 4.4-4.5 | Operational Support | FFP + T&M for extensions | FFP per deployment (days × travelers); T&M for extensions beyond scope |
| 4.6 | Travel | Reimbursable (FTR-compliant) | GSA regulations; no profit or fee applied |
| 5.1-5.2 | Course Development | FFP, milestone-based | Billed upon completion of defined milestones |
| 6.0 | Documentation | FFP (limited scope) | Based on delivery and acceptance of specified deliverables |
| 7.0-8.0 | Modeling and Integration | T&M or CPFF per Task Order | Milestone-based billing |
| 9.0 | Testing | T&M (Not-To-Exceed) per campaign | Scope/duration varies; ceiling defined per TO |
| 10.0 | Government-directed Design Modifications | CPFF or T&M NTE | For modifications outside baseline system |

## Products & Capabilities Described
- **Black Swift S0 Platform**: A procurement item being offered as firm fixed price per aircraft unit. The document indicates annual pricing adjustments are anticipated to reflect component updates and integration of new development features.

## Key Procurement Categories
1. **Platform Procurement**: Core aircraft procurement with flexibility for annual updates
2. **Training & Support**: Standardized training courses plus operational deployment support
3. **Development Activities**: Course development, documentation, modeling/integration work billed on milestone or T&M basis
4. **Testing & Validation**: Campaign-based testing with not-to-exceed pricing
5. **Modifications**: Separate budget category for government-directed design changes

## Notable Details
- **Flexibility in Pricing**: Annual adjustment mechanism allows for component updates and new feature integration without renegotiating base contract
- **Travel Compliance**: Travel costs treated as reimbursable with no markup, following GSA and FTR regulations
- **Deployment Pricing Model**: Operational support priced as FFP per deployment (calculated by days × travelers), with T&M extension clauses for work beyond initial scope
- **Government-Directed Changes**: Separate contracting mechanism for modifications outside the baseline S0 system
- **IDIQ Framework**: Structure supports indefinite quantity/indefinite delivery model, allowing for multiple task orders across different activity categories
