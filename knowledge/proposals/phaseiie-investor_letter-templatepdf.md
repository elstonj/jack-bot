## Executive Summary
This is a template letter format for securing investor commitment funding to support a NASA SBIR Phase II-E proposal. The letter demonstrates how outside investors can provide matching funds alongside NASA's contribution to further develop SBIR-funded technologies.

## Document Content
This is a **blank template document** rather than a completed proposal or report. It provides the standard format and language required by NASA for Phase II-E proposals seeking outside investor commitment.

## Key Requirements Outlined
The template specifies three critical constraints for investor funding:

1. **Investment Type**: Funds must be in exchange for equity, royalties, technology rights, profit share, advance credit for future products, or combinations thereof. In-kind gifts, service exchanges, and prior purchase orders are NOT acceptable.

2. **Investor Source Restrictions**: 
   - Cannot be another SBIR/STTR firm
   - Cannot be a firm listed as a subcontractor on the same SBIR/STTR

3. **Use of Funds**: Investor money must pay for activities that further development and/or commercialization of SBIR technology (R&D, manufacturing, marketing, etc.)
