## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies has been awarded a five-year IDIQ contract (Feb 1, 2026 - Jan 31, 2031) to provide Black Swift S0 Small Uncrewed Aircraft System (sUAS) platforms, along with operational and technical support services to NOAA. This is a Women-Owned Small Business (WOSB) contract with a minimum order value of $9,900,000 and maximum order capability of $9,900,000 or contractor's proposed price (whichever is higher).
