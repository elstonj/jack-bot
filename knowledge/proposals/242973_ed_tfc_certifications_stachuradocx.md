# Transparency of Foreign Connections Certification

## Document Metadata
- Type: Certification/Compliance Document
- Client/Agency: DOE FECM (U.S. Department of Energy, Fossil Energy and Carbon Management)
- Program/Solicitation: DOE_FECM
- Date: November 12, 2024
- Key Personnel: Rebecca Cotter (certifying officer for Black Swift Technologies)

## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies submitted a formal Transparency of Foreign Connections Certification to the DOE FECM, certifying compliance with federal requirements regarding foreign connections, talent recruitment programs, and foreign business relationships as required under 42 U.S.C. § 6605.

## Certifications Provided
Rebecca Cotter, on behalf of Black Swift Technologies, certifies:

1. **Current and Accurate Information**: All information provided regarding current, pending, and other support (both foreign and domestic) is current, accurate, and complete as of the submission date.

2. **No Malign Foreign Talent Recruitment**: BST is not a party to any malign foreign talent recruitment program.

3. **No High-Risk Country Programs**: BST is not a party to any Foreign Government-Sponsored Talent Recruitment Program of a country of risk, specifically:
   - China
   - Iran
   - North Korea
   - Russia

4. **No Prohibited Foreign Entities**: BST has no relationship with any foreign business entity, offshore entity, or entity outside the United States related to the proposed recipient or subrecipient.

## Legal Authority
The certification references compliance requirements under:
- 42 U.S.C. § 6605 (foreign connections disclosure)
- 18 U.S.C. §§ 287, 1001, 1031 (misrepresentation and false claims)
- 31 U.S.C. §§ 3729-3733, 3802 (False Claims Act provisions)

## Notable Details
This is a required certification document for DOE FECM funding eligibility, indicating BST was pursuing or had submitted a proposal to the DOE's Fossil Energy and Carbon Management program. The certification was signed on November 12, 2024, by Rebecca Cotter.
