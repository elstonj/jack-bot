## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies presents the S0 UAS, a purpose-built unmanned aircraft system designed specifically for high-resolution atmospheric thermodynamic measurements. With autonomous flight capabilities up to 15,000 feet AGL, 90-minute endurance, and a comprehensive sensor suite measuring air temperature, wind speed/direction, dewpoint, and atmospheric pressure, the S0 addresses rapid upper air profiling needs for atmospheric research in demanding conditions.

## Technical Approach
The S0 leverages Black Swift's SwiftCore Flight Management System (FMS) to enable fully autonomous flight operations with complete control, communication, and command capabilities. The aircraft design emphasizes:
- High maneuverability and aggressive control authority for operation in very high wind conditions
- Efficient, low-drag aerodynamic design enabling single-battery climbs to 15,000 ft
- Compact size for rapid handling and deployment in field operations

## Products & Capabilities Described

### S0 UAS
- **What it is**: An intelligent, fully autonomous unmanned aircraft specifically designed for atmospheric sounding and thermodynamic measurements
- **Proposed use**: High-resolution upper air profiling; deployment in extreme atmospheric environments (high-altitude, arctic, desert, corrosive particulates, strong turbulence)
- **Key specifications**:
  - Maximum altitude: 15,000 feet AGL
  - Endurance: Up to 90 minutes
  - Cruise speed: 17 m/s (38 mph)
  - Weight: 3.5 lbs
  - Wingspan: 54.6 inches
  - Wing area: 292 in²
  - Wing loading: 27.5 oz/ft²

### Sensor Suite Specifications
- **Air Temperature**: Accuracy ±0.3°C; Range -40° to 85°C
- **Wind Velocity**: Resolution 0.38 m/s; Accuracy ±0.48 m/s
- **Barometric Pressure**: Resolution 0.012 mbar; Accuracy 1.5 mbar
- **Relative Humidity**: Accuracy ±3% RH at 21°C; Range 0-100% RH
- **Magnetic Field Sensitivity**: 13 nT
- **Accelerometer Resolution**: 0.0002 g
- **Gyroscope Resolution**: 0.6°/sec²
