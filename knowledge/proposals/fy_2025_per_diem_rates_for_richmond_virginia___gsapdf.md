## Per Diem Rates for Richmond, Virginia (FY 2025)

**Daily Lodging Rates (excluding taxes):**
- $157 per night (consistent across all months October 2024 - September 2025)
- Location: City of Richmond

**Meals & Incidental Expenses (M&IE):**
- Full daily rate: $80
  - Breakfast: $20
  - Lunch: $22
  - Dinner: $33
  - Incidental expenses: $5
- First and last day of travel: $60 (75% of full daily rate)
