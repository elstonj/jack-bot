## Executive Summary
Jack S. Elston is CEO and Founder of Black Swift Technologies LLC. He holds a PhD in Aerospace Engineering from the University of Colorado and brings 13+ years of experience in unmanned aircraft systems (UAS), networked command and control of heterogeneous UAS, and meteorological sampling platforms. His academic and professional work spans small fixed-wing UAS design, autonomous flight control, and atmospheric observation missions including tornado and severe weather sampling.
