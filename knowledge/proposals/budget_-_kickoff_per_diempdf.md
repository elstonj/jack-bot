## Notable Details
- **Per Diem Rates (2018 Montgomery County, MD):**
  - Lodging ranges from $175–$253/night depending on month
  - Meals & Incidentals: consistent $69/day year-round
  - Meals Only: $64/day
  - Incidentals only: $5/day
  - 75% of M&IE rates applied to first and last travel days

- **Geographic Coverage:** Applies to all cities within Montgomery County, including Bethesda, Rockville, Gaithersburg, Silver Spring, and multiple military installations (Bethesda Naval Medical Center, NSWC Carderock, NSA Bethesda, Walter Reed National Military Medical Center, etc.)
