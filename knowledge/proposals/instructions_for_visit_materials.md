## Executive Summary
This document outlines NASA's requirements for conducting mandatory Virtual Site Visits with Phase II SBIR awardees, including Black Swift Technologies. The visit is designed to allow NASA to assess technology development progress and facilitate dialogue between the agency and the contractor.

## Document Purpose & Requirements
This is an administrative instruction document sent to Phase II SBIR awardees detailing what they must prepare and submit for a virtual site visit evaluation by NASA.

## Virtual Site Visit Requirements

**Submission Requirements:**
- Briefing charts (content and format to be provided in sample template)
- Facility photos
- Short video walkthrough of facility
- All materials due 4 days before scheduled meeting

**Presentation Format:**
- Virtual meeting using web-based visual and voice telecommunications
- One-hour time slot
- Representation: Account Contact/Program Manager (ACN) and Principal Investigator required
- Content to cover: organization overview, project management, technical progress

**NASA Attendees (typical):**
- Contracting Officer Representative
- Contracting Officer
- Center Technology Infusion Manager
- PMO representative
- Headquarters representative

## Scheduling Information
- Virtual Site Visit window: October 8 – November 13, 2015
- Schedule: Thursdays (occasional Fridays)
- Available time slots: 10:00 am PST or 11:30 am PST
- Sign-up deadline: October 1, 2015, 4:00 pm PT
- Selection: First-come, first-served basis
