## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies received a Phase I nomination for a Navy SBIR proposal to develop a Super-Light (SL) UAS platform integrating high-sensitivity magnetometer and lightweight acoustic sensing capabilities for underwater target detection, classification, and tracking. The evaluation panel praised BST's technical approach, platform experience, and team expertise.

## Technical Approach
- Integration of high-sensitivity magnetometer on the SL UAS platform
- Integration of lightweight acoustic sensor for underwater operations
- Designed for detection, classification, and tracking of underwater targets
- Leverages BST's proven S0 UAS platform, known for ruggedness and performance in extreme conditions

## Products & Capabilities Described

**S0 UAS Platform**
- Rugged platform designed for extreme condition operations
- Demonstrated operational history with NOAA for hurricane and polar region operations
- Suitable for integration of advanced sensing payloads (magnetometer, acoustic sensors)
- Emphasis on reliability and performance in challenging environments

## Use Cases & Applications
- Underwater target detection, classification, and tracking
- Navy maritime operations
- Extended operational envelope building on NOAA hurricane and polar region mission experience
