- Client/Agency: NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration)

- Key Personnel: Jack Elston (Project Coordinator/Principal Investigator), Maciej Stachura (System State Estimation Lead Engineer/Chief Technology Officer), Joshua Fromm (Mechanical Design and Integration), Daniel Troke (Technician), Jake Sahli (Electrical Engineer Intern)

## Executive Summary
This budget narrative supports a NOAA SBIR Phase II project focused on UAS platform development with integrated sensing capabilities. The budget allocates $303,780 in personnel costs over 10 months of effort, $54,680.40 in fringe benefits, and $1,294.88 in equipment, totaling approximately $360,000+ in Phase II funding.

## Technical Approach (Implied from Budget)
The project involves developing or refining a UAS platform with integrated sensing and communications capabilities. Key technical areas include:
- System state estimation and controls (Stachura's focus)
- Mechanical payload integration (Fromm's focus)
- Electrical system implementation (Sahli/Troke's focus)
- Vision-based perception (DepthAI integration)
- LTE and software-defined radio communications (MangOH Red, LimeSDR)
- Optical flow and SLAM capabilities
