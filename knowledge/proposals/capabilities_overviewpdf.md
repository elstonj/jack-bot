## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies is a US-based Group 1 UAS manufacturer founded in 2011 that designs and manufactures purpose-built unmanned aircraft systems, flight management systems, payloads, and ground stations for specialized atmospheric research in extreme conditions. BST is unique as a fully domestic, vertically integrated company with in-house aircraft design, avionics, flight control systems, and payload integration capabilities serving USAF, NOAA, NASA, USGS, and leading research institutions.

## Core Competencies
- Aircraft design and control
- UAS operations in extreme environments (volcanoes, tornadoes, hurricanes, wildfires)
- Payload integration and deployment (multispectral/RGB cameras, LiDAR, EO/IR, trace gas sensors, nephelometer, temperature/pressure/humidity/wind sensors, L-band radiometer)
- On-board augmented intelligence with ML algorithms for fault detection and predictive failure analysis
- Unified Flight Management System (SwiftCore) enabling autonomous flight with real-time telemetry and control
- AI, machine learning, and machine vision software development

## Key Differentiators
- **Vertical integration**: All design, manufacturing, and servicing entirely in USA
- **Only U.S. Group 1 UAS manufacturer with proprietary flight management system and aircraft design capability**
- **Data-centric flight control**: SwiftCore FMS is payload-focused with real-time telemetry and autopilot data link for fully autonomous operation
- **Modular field-swappable payload system**: Rapid field payload changes using common power, data, and mechanical interfaces without specialized tools
- **Smart sensor-based control**: SwiftCore autonomously modifies flight path based on sensor inputs to minimize operator workload and improve data quality
- **In-house FMS development**: Thoroughly vetted and secured (vs. open-source alternatives with suboptimal code)

## Products & Capabilities

### Black Swift S2 UAS
- **Description**: Fixed-wing, rugged workhorse UAS
- **Payload capacity**: 5 pounds (modular, field-swappable nose cone)
- **Endurance**: 110 minutes
- **Ceiling**: 6,000 meters (20,000 feet AGL)
- **Range**: 110 km
- **Original design**: Atmospheric and volcano research, aerial mapping
- **Proven operations**: Arctic to Tropics missions

### Black Swift S0 UAS
- **Description**: Small, ruggedized VTOL or tube-launched fixed-wing UAS
- **Payload options**: Full atmospheric sampling suite or small lightweight EO/IR cameras
- **Origin**: NOAA grant for low-cost electric fixed-wing air-deployed from P-3 Orion "Hurricane Hunter" aircraft
- **Key feature**: Engineered for autonomous flight providing atmospheric boundary layer measurements in hurricane eyewall
- **Advantages**: Economic to construct, operate, and maintain; enables low-cost swarm solutions

### Black Swift E2 Multirotor UAS
- **Description**: Ruggedized multirotor developed for infrastructure inspection
- **Flight duration**: 35 minutes
- **Payload capacity**: 6.5 pounds
- **Features**: Field-swappable payload, operates in inclement weather and demanding field conditions

### SwiftCore™ Flight Management System (FMS)
- **Components**: Fully integrated autopilot, tablet-based ground control station, intuitive graphical user interface (GUI)
- **Development**: Entirely in-house design and code
- **Advantages**: Thoroughly vetted and secured with expert review; superior to open-source alternatives
- **Architecture**: Sandboxed mission-critical core with array of open interfaces for third-party integration
- **Unique feature**: Sensor-based smart control providing autonomous flight path modification based on real-time sensor inputs

## Use Cases & Applications
- **Atmospheric research**: Volcano monitoring, tornado research, hurricane sampling, wildfire assessment
- **Extreme environment operations**: Arctic conditions, tropical regions, coastal BVLOS mapping
- **Planetary exploration**: Upper atmospheric observations of Venus (survival in harsh wind environment with continuous atmospheric sampling and energy extraction)
- **Meteorological profiling**: Atmospheric boundary layer measurements, wind measurements for improved weather forecasting and parachute drop accuracy
- **Infrastructure inspection**: Wind turbine inspection (commercial partnership with Alerion)
- **Telecommunications**: 5G signal mapping and monitoring (commercial project)
