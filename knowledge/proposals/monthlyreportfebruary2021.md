# Monthly Progress Report – February 2021

## Document Metadata
- Type: Monthly progress report
- Client/Agency: NASA
- Program/Solicitation: AREN (Airborne Research and Education Network) 2020-2021
- Date: February 2021 (report submitted March 3, 2021)
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: AeroPod, Monocam, Profiler

## Executive Summary
This monthly report documents BST's progress on the AREN project during February 2021, focusing on resolving technical issues with UHIP data recording, completing video documentation with captions, redesigning AeroPod components for improved airflow and reduced weight, and shipping calibrated AeroPods to multiple NASA and partner facilities.

## Technical Accomplishments

### UHIP System
- Resolved troubleshooting issues that were preventing data recording
- Successfully recorded data after resolution

### Documentation & Content
- Completed final instructional video with closed captioning
- Video uploaded to project website

### Activity Management
- Redesigned activity matrix for better organization of existing activities

### Repository & File Management
- Created centralized AREN Google Drive repository for:
  - Current STL files (3D printer files for parts)
  - Accompanying documentation
  - Archive of older/superseded files

## Products & Capabilities Described

### AeroPod
- **What it is**: Airborne research platform with modular sensor packages
- **Work performed**:
  - Redesigned MHP (Multi-sensor Humidity Package) support bracket to eliminate airflow blockage over temperature/humidity sensors
  - Made iterative adjustments to other components for weight reduction and fit optimization
  - Parts fabrication (3D printing) of multiple units
  - Calibration and assembly of units for shipment

### Monocam
- Single camera sensor package for AeroPod
- Three units calibrated, assembled, and shipped to partners:
  - AmericaView (Chris McGinty)
  - AmericaView (Lisa Wirth)
  - Glenn Davison

### Profiler
- Sensor package configuration shipped to NASA Goddard (Yuekui Yang)
