## Document Purpose
This is a standard DCAA form (not substantive content) used to evaluate contractor accounting system adequacy for federal contracting. The checklist verifies compliance with:
- Cost Accounting Standards (CAS) per 48 CFR Chapter 99
- Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) Part 31 (Cost Principles)
- Generally Accepted Accounting Principles (GAAP)
- Internal cost segregation and tracking capabilities

## Executive Summary
This is a blank DCAA preaward survey checklist template that prospective defense contractors must complete to demonstrate their accounting systems meet federal requirements before receiving contract awards. The document is part of BST's Navy STTR Phase II proposal package for a Hazardous Weather project, indicating BST is preparing to submit cost-type contract proposals to the federal government.

## Key Compliance Areas Assessed

### Initial Documentation Requirements
- Verification that contractor has read SF1408 requirements
- Confirmation contractor has reviewed DCAA Publication DCAAM 7641.90 ("Information for Contractors")
- Access to DCAA website resources

### Prior Audit History (Question 1)
- Whether accounting system has been previously audited by DCAA
- Timing of most recent audit (within 1 year, 3 years, or >3 years)
- Determination of acceptability for contract award
- Any changes to system since audit

### Cost Accounting Standards (Questions 2 & 2.A-2.C)
- CAS applicability (Full or Modified)
- Submission and adequacy of CASB Disclosure Statement (CASB DS-1)
- DCAA notification of non-compliance issues
- Consistency of current proposal with disclosed CAS practices

### Third-Party Audit Documentation (Question 3)
- CPA or other federal agency audits
- Requirement to attach most recent audit report (excluding annual financial audit)

### System Operational Status (Question 4)
- Whether accounting system is fully operational
- Documentation of any portions in setup, anticipated, or non-existent

### GAAP Compliance (Question 5)
- Alignment with Generally Accepted Accounting Principles

### DCAA Audit Readiness (Question 6)
- System readiness for DCAA audit
- Timeline for readiness if not currently prepared

### Cost Estimation & Principles (Question 7)
- Loaded hourly labor rates proposed
- Consistency with established estimating and accounting principles
- Compliance with FAR Part 31 Cost Principles
- N/A if no active proposal

### Direct & Indirect Cost Management (Questions 8-13)
- **Question 8**: Proper segregation of Direct Costs from Indirect Costs
- **Question 9**: Identification and accumulation of Direct Costs by contract
- **Question 10**: Logical and consistent allocation method for Indirect Costs to intermediate and final cost objectives
- **Question 11**: Cost accumulation under general ledger control
- **Question 12**: Timekeeping system identifying labor by cost objectives
- **Question 13**: Labor distribution system charging Direct and Indirect labor appropriately

### Cost Tracking & Determination (Questions 14-15)
- **Question 14**: Interim (at least monthly) cost determination through routine posting
- **Question 15**: Exclusion of non-allowable costs per FAR 31

### Contract-Specific Cost Tracking (Question 16)
- Identification of costs by contract line item and by units
- Capability to treat each unit/line item as separate contract if required

### Preproduction vs. Production Costs (Question 17)
- Segregation of preproduction costs from production costs

### Financial Limitation Clauses (Question 18)
- Financial information for Limitation of Cost (FAR 52.232.20)
- Financial information for Limitation on Payments (FAR 52.216-16)

### Progress Payment Support (Question 19)
- Financial data to support Progress Payment requests

### Follow-On Acquisition Pricing (Question 20)
- Design enabling adequate, reliable data for pricing follow-on contracts

### Cost-Type Contract Intent (Questions 21-22)
- **Question 21**: Organization's plans to submit proposals on Cost-Type (FAR 16.3) contracts/task orders
- **Question 22**: Current performance on Cost-Type contracts/task orders

## Instruction Notes
- All questions require explanatory narrative text; insufficient detail will result in incomplete submission
- Responses must not refer to previous answers
- If contractor plans to bid on Cost-Type contracts, must provide explanatory narratives for Questions 3-20
- Contractor must attach DCAA audit reports if available (Question 1)
- Contractor must attach CAS compliance documentation if subject to CAS (Question 2)
- Contractor must attach external audit reports (Question 3)
- Annual financial audits should NOT be submitted
