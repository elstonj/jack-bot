# ESTCP BAA Pre-Proposal, Full Proposal, and Revised Proposal Checklists

## Document Metadata
- Type: Compliance checklist / guidance document
- Client/Agency: ESTCP (Environmental Security Technology Certification Program) / DoD
- Program/Solicitation: ESTCP BAA (Broad Agency Announcement) - Environmental Technology (non-federal)
- Date: Created/Modified 2025-02-06
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: None

## Executive Summary
This document is an administrative checklist for Black Swift Technologies or other organizations submitting proposals to the ESTCP BAA solicitation. It outlines compliance requirements for pre-proposals, full proposals, and revised proposals, including formatting, required sections, cost documentation, and submission procedures through the SEMS (SERDP/ESTCP Proposal Management System).

## Document Content Overview
This is a procedural/compliance document, not a technical proposal or capability brief. It contains no information about BST's products, technical capabilities, or proposed work. Instead, it serves as internal guidance for proposal preparation.

## Key Requirements Outlined

### Pre-Proposal Checklist
- Max 5 pages
- Required sections: Proposal Number, Title, PI, ESTCP Topic Area, Lead Organization, Problem Statement, Technology Description (with performance objectives table), Expected DoD Benefit, Schedule/Milestones (Gantt chart), Technology Transition, Performers (with CVs), Funding
- Required appendices: CVs, Acronym List, Literature citations
- Funding must match SEMS entry and be rounded to nearest dollar
- Submission confirmation email required

### Full Proposal Checklist
- Max 15 pages (plus non-counted appendices)
- No limited distribution markings unless directed
- Abstract section (not counted in page limit)
- Technical section with 10+ specific subsections including Problem Statement, Technology Demonstration, DoD Benefit, Milestones, Technology Transition, Equipment Disposition, Performers, Response to ESTCP Review Comments
- Cost section with multiple required spreadsheet templates (Main Cost, CAP, Materials, Travel, Publications, Cost by Task)
- All costs rounded to nearest dollar
- Separate cost sheets for subcontractors ≥$50,000
- Requirement to present poster at SERDP/ESTCP symposium during each active year
- Zero-dollar contingency year required
- Signed and dated signature form required before deadline
- 1-year minimum proposal expiration date recommended
- Submission confirmation email required

### Revised Proposal Checklist
- Response to ESTCP review comments as cover memo (first page)
- All Full Proposal Checklist requirements must still be met
- Zero-cost contingency year in all documents
- CAGE code, UEI number, and SAM account expiration date required in SEMS
- Small Business Subcontracting Plan required if >$750,000 and lead org is NOT small business
- Statement required regarding Small Business Subcontracting Plan if <$750,000 or lead org is small business

## Notable Details
- System used: SEMS (sems@serdp-estcp.org)
- Multiple checkpoints for submission confirmation and status verification
- Emphasis on cost accuracy and matching between proposal file and SEMS entries
- Requirement for symposium poster presentation with associated costs
- Detailed appendix requirements with CVs, acronym lists, and cost breakdowns
