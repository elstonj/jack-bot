## Executive Summary
This document describes iMet calibration facilities and equipment available to Black Swift Technologies for validating temperature, humidity, and pressure sensors as part of a NOAA SBIR Phase I proposal. The facilities support high-precision environmental sensor calibration to support iMet development.

## Technical Approach
BST proposed to use specialized laboratory calibration equipment to validate iMet sensor accuracy:

**Temperature Calibration:**
- Equipment: Thermotron SM-16-3800 chamber (Holland, MI)
- Capability: 0.2°C calibration accuracy in air across -70°C to +50°C operating range
- Note: For uncertainties better than 0.1°C, temperature bath required

**Humidity Calibration:**
- Equipment: Thunder Model 3900 Low Humidity Generator
- Method: Two-pressure two-temperature principle for continuous humidified gas stream delivery

**Pressure Calibration:**
- Equipment: Paroscientific Model 745 high-accuracy pressure reference
- Specifications: NIST traceable; 0-1034 hPa operating range
- Supporting equipment: Vacuum pump (capable of <0.5 hPa) and acrylic chamber (capable of holding <5 hPa)
- Flexibility: Sensors can connect directly via laboratory-grade tubing or be placed in acrylic chamber depending on design

## Products & Capabilities Described

**iMet:**
