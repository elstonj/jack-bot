## Summary
This is a procedural document outlining requirements for including consultant agreement letters in SBIR Phase I proposals submitted to DoD. It specifies format and content requirements for agreements with consultants and subcontractors.

## Key Requirements Stated

**Consultant Agreement Format:**
- Separate agreement letter required for each consultant
- Must include:
  - Description of services/assistance to be provided
  - Number of hours required
  - Hourly rate

**Customer Discovery Consultants:**
- If a proposing firm engages a customer discovery firm (to help determine feasibility of solutions for specific DoD customers), the associated agreement must be included with the proposal
- Applies to any Special Topic submissions
