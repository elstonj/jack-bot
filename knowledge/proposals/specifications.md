## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies was responding to a USDA-ARS specification for a lightweight, drone-deployable L-band passive remote sensing system designed for soil moisture monitoring. The system must meet strict weight and power constraints to enable deployment on multi-rotor aerial platforms.

## Technical Approach
- **System Type**: L-band passive remote sensing payload
- **Operating Frequency**: 1.4 GHz with dual polarization capability
- **Deployment Platform**: Quad/hex/octo-copter style drone
- **Antenna Configuration**: Vertical mounted antenna, maximum 40 cm height
- **Power Budget**: 10W or less
- **Weight Constraint**: Less than 4 kg (total system including antenna)

## Products & Capabilities Described
**L-band Passive Remote Sensing System**
- Single-unit system for soil moisture measurement
- Dual-polarization capability at 1.4 GHz
- Designed for integration with multi-rotor drone platforms
- Compact form factor meeting strict weight and power budgets

## Use Cases & Applications

- **Platform**: Autonomous aerial platforms (copters)
- **Operational Context**: USDA-ARS research applications in hydrology and remote sensing
