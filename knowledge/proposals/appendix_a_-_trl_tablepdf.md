## Executive Summary
This document provides standard DoD Technology Readiness Level (TRL) definitions across the 9-level scale, from TRL 1 (basic principles observed) through TRL 9 (proven through successful mission operations). It serves as a reference framework for evaluating technology maturity in the DARPA-ERIS proposal context.

## Technical Approach
Not applicable—this is a definitions and reference document, not a technical proposal.

## TRL Scale Summary
| Level | Stage | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| 1 | Scientific Research | Basic principles observed and reported |
| 2 | Applied Research | Technology concept and application formulated |
| 3 | R&D Initiation | Analytical and experimental proof of concept |
| 4 | Lab Integration | Component/breadboard validation in lab |
| 5 | Simulated Environment | Component/breadboard validation in relevant environment |
| 6 | Prototype Testing | System/subsystem model demonstration in relevant environment |
| 7 | Operational Prototype | System prototype demonstration in operational environment |
| 8 | System Qualification | Actual system completed and qualified through test |
| 9 | Operational Deployment | System proven through successful mission operations |
