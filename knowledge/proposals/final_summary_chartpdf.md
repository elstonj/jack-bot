## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies developed the S3 UAS, a ruggedized uncrewed aerial system designed to operate in extreme environments (volcanic plumes, wildfires, severe storms) to gather critical scientific data for model validation and safety applications. The project successfully demonstrated in-situ measurement capabilities in previously inaccessible locations, including deployment to Makushin Volcano in Alaska for imagery and volcanic gas collection, while advancing VTOL and hurricane-capable flight algorithms.

## Technical Approach
- **S3 UAS**: Ruggedized platform developed to extend operational envelope into harsh environments where ground systems, crewed aircraft, balloons, and satellites have limitations
- **S0 VTOL**: Parallel vertical takeoff/landing concept developed during the project (design concept Feb 2024; successful hover test Apr 2024)
- **Measurement Algorithms**: Development of algorithms for high-wind operations (hurricane-class environments)
- **Mission Planning Tool**: Terrain- and communications-aware automated mission planning system
- **Sensor Integration**: Coordination with external sensor companies for gas sensing capabilities
