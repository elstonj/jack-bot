## Executive Summary
Ainstein AI expresses organizational support and interest in partnering with Black Swift Technologies on a NASA SBIR Phase II proposal focused on developing sensor fusion and algorithms for low-altitude mapping above rugged terrain. Ainstein commits to providing radar sensors and engineering support as a subcontractor/supplier.

## Technical Approach
The proposal aims to develop sensor fusion capabilities and algorithms enabling UAS to map terrain at low altitude over rugged topography. Ainstein's contribution involves integrating radar sensors for collision avoidance and terrain following.
