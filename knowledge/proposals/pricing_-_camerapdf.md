## Products & Capabilities Described

**See3CAM_CU130 (13MP USB 3.0 Camera Board)**
- **Sensor:** Aptina AR1820HS (13 megapixel)
- **Resolution & Frame Rates (USB 3.0):**
  - Ultra HD (3840×2160): 30 fps (MJPEG), 9 fps (YUV422)
  - Full HD (1920×1080): 60 fps (MJPEG), 30 fps (YUV422)
  - HD (1280×720): 60 fps (both formats)
  - 13MP native (4224×3156): 14 fps (MJPEG), 5 fps (YUV422)
- **Output Formats:** Uncompressed YUV422, compressed MJPEG
- **Processor:** Dedicated Image Signal Processor (ISP) with auto white balance and auto exposure control
- **Connectivity:** USB 3.0 Micro-B or Type-C; backward compatible with USB 2.0
- **Power:** 5V, max 2.6W
- **Operating Temperature:** -30°C to 70°C
- **Physical:** 30×30×30.2mm (without lens), 14g (without lens), 19g (with lens)
- **Features:** Fixed focus with M12 lens holder, electronic rolling shutter, GPIO header, external hardware trigger, zero-lag shutter for still capture
- **Price:** Starting at US$139 (volume pricing)
