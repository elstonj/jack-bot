## Executive Summary
This document is a Google Forms template designed to collect Phase I final reports from SBIR contractors. It provides a structured submission format for companies to report on their Phase I objectives, accomplishments, feasibility studies, and DoD engagement efforts. The form offers optional use of government-provided templates and guides respondents through required deliverables including abstract, objectives assessment, and commercialization feasibility.

## Document Structure & Content
This is a **submission form template**, not a completed report. It contains:

### Deliverable Options (CLIN-based):
- **CLIN 0001**: Preliminary Report (updated pitch deck + company one-pager)
- **CLIN 0002**: Final Report (full technical and feasibility report)

### Required Final Report Sections:

**Administrative Section:**
- Phase I Project Title
- Company members involved
- Option to use provided template vs. submit custom report

**Scientific/Technical Objectives and Accomplishments:**
- Abstract (200 words max)
- Phase I goal statement
- Objectives 1-3 with yes/no completion assessment
- Explanation required for any unmet objectives

**Feasibility Study Section:**
- Problem statement confirmation/discovery within DoD
- Identifying affected DoD organizations
- Quantification of DoD engagement:
  - Number of contact attempts
  - Number of substantive discussions
  - Outreach mechanisms used (collider, NEEDS ID, social media, etc.)
  - Identification of most effective introduction method
- Barriers to DoD collaboration
- Phase II application intent (yes/no/maybe)
