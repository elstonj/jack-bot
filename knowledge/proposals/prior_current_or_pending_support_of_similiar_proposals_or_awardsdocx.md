# Prior, Current, or Pending Support of Similar Proposals or Awards

## Document Metadata
- Type: Compliance/instructional document (coversheet supplement guidance)
- Client/Agency: Department of Defense (DoD)
- Program/Solicitation: SBIR/STTR (Small Business Innovation Research/Small Business Technology Transfer)
- Date: September 15, 2020 (modified); February 3, 2021 (created)
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: None
- Key Personnel: Jack Elston (last editor)

## Executive Summary
This is a compliance instruction document for DoD SBIR/STTR proposal submissions, requiring applicants to disclose any similar proposals funded, currently funded, or pending with other Federal Agencies or DoD Components. The document emphasizes that failure to disclose such support results in proposal disqualification.

## Content
This is not a technical proposal or capability document. It is an instructional/regulatory guidance document that:

1. **Requirements**: Mandates disclosure of similar proposals or awards on the Proposal Cover Sheet and Coversheet Supplement
2. **Scope of Disclosure**: Applies to proposals that are "substantially the same" as those:
   - Previously funded
   - Currently being funded
   - Pending with another Federal Agency or DoD Component
3. **Consequences of Non-Disclosure**: 
   - Proposal disqualification
   - Proposals deemed "essentially equivalent" (reusing parts or whole proposals from previously awarded work) are subject to rejection
4. **Special Emphasis**: Notes that special topics are broader in nature than traditional SBIR/STTR topics, making disclosure identification "particularly important"

## Notable Details
- Applicants with no similar proposals or awards should state: "No prior, current, or pending support for proposed work."
- This document appears to be a template or guidance document prepared by BST for internal use when responding to DoD SBIR/STTR solicitations.
