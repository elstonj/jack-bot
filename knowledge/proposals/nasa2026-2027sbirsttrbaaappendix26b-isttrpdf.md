## Executive Summary
This document is a NASA solicitation for Small Business Technology Transfer (STTR) Phase I proposals under the FY2026-2027 Broad Agency Announcement. It outlines 11 STTR subtopics across 8 technical themes, with maximum Phase I funding of $225,000 and 13-month project duration. The solicitation seeks partnerships between small businesses and research institutions to develop innovative technologies across aeronautics, space transportation, lunar infrastructure, quantum computing, space weather, in-situ science, and space-based biotechnology and materials applications.

## Use Cases & Applications
This solicitation addresses 11 research topics:

### Aeronautics Research (AERO)
1. **AERO.9.T26B: Full-Scale AAM/VTOL Interdisciplinary Investigations** (Lead: AFRC)
   - Advanced Air Mobility (AAM) and eVTOL aircraft design, testing, and validation
   - Focus: aerodynamics, propulsion, flight dynamics, controls, acoustics
   - Full-scale or subscale testing with methods to extend results to full-scale
   - Expected TRL at completion: 2-6
   - Applications: urban air mobility, air taxis (projected $trillions market in 15-20 years)

2. **AERO.10.T26B: Hybrid Powertrain Technologies** (Lead: GRC)
   - High-power electrified aircraft propulsion (EAP) for regional and single-aisle aircraft
   - Focus: superconducting electric machines and cryo-compatible power electronics
   - Target: 10+ MW total power, 1.5-2.5 kV, 500-1000 Hz operation
   - Specific needs: cryo-compatible gate drives, hermetic connectors, lightweight cryo fans, EMI filtering
   - Expected TRL at completion: 2-4

### Space Transportation (GO)
3. **GO.4.T26B: Modernization of CFD Tools for Advanced Propulsion Applications** (Lead: MSFC)
   - Computational fluid dynamics models for subcritical liquid propellant injection and combustion
   - Applications: Rotating Detonation Rocket Engines (RDREs), hypergolic engines, traditional chemical engines
   - Model development for liquid break-up, phase change, multiphase combustion kinetics
   - Expected TRL at completion: 2-5

4. **GO.5.T26B: Very Low Earth Orbits (VLEO) Control Surfaces** (Lead: GRC)
   - Aerodynamic components and propulsion systems for spacecraft in 80-400 km altitude
   - Focus: 80-160 km region (D and E regions of ionosphere)
   - Addresses drag mitigation, aerodynamic maneuvering, orbital control
   - Applications: orbital "dipper" missions, atmosphere-space coupling research
   - Expected TRL at completion: 2-3

### Lunar Surface Infrastructure (LIVEI)
5. **LIVEI.2.T26B: Bulk Regolith Manipulation and Site Preparation** (Lead: KSC)
   - Lunar civil engineering designs and robotic construction technologies
   - Applications: landing zones, berms for radiation/ejecta mitigation, foundations, access ramps, shelters
   - Focus areas: topography mapping, geotechnical measurement, rock removal, compaction, autonomous operations
   - Related to Lunar Innovation Park and Fission Surface Power concepts
   - Expected TRL at completion: 2-5
