## Executive Summary
This document is a federal per diem rate reference table for Florida locations in 2019, used as supporting material for budget justification in a NOAA SBIR Phase II proposal. It provides meal and lodging reimbursement rates for various Florida cities and counties.

## Purpose
Supporting documentation for proposal budget preparation, establishing allowable federal reimbursement rates for travel expenses to Florida locations during the proposal period.

## Content
Federal per diem rates for 23 Florida destinations with location-specific rates, plus general statewide rates:

**General Florida Per Diems (for unlisted areas):**
- Meals: $55.00/day
- Lodging: $94.00/night

**Selected Location Examples:**
- Key West (Monroe County): $66 meals / $282 lodging (highest lodging rate)
- Fort Lauderdale (Broward County): $66 meals / $208 lodging
- Cocoa Beach (Brevard County): $71 meals / $168 lodging
- Gainesville (Alachua County): $61 meals / $106 lodging

**Notable Details:**
- Rates effective October 2018
- Document notes 2019 federal pay freeze
- 20 Florida areas with specified location rates
- Source: FederalPay.org
