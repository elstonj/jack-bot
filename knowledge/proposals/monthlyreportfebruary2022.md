# Monthly Progress Report – February 2022

## Document Metadata
- Type: Monthly progress report
- Client/Agency: NASA
- Program/Solicitation: AREN (Arctic Radiation and Environmental Networking) 2020-2021
- Date: February 2022 (reporting period); submitted March 9, 2022
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: AeroPod, TerraROVER, Monocam, Profiler, Urban Heat Island Packages, Minikit
- Key Personnel: Geoff Bland (Task Manager), Kay Rufty (Employee/Reporter)

## Executive Summary
This brief monthly progress report documents manufacturing and assembly activities for February 2022 on the AREN project, including 3D printing and assembly of AeroPod and TerraROVER components, shipment of sensors and equipment to project stakeholders, and construction of Urban Heat Island measurement packages.

## Technical Approach
Activities centered on manufacturing, assembly, and logistics:
- 3D printing optimization: dialed in printer settings for new filament material; troubleshot and unclogged Monoprice printer
- Component assembly and testing: assembled and soldered 5 Urban Heat Island Packages
- Packaging and shipping coordination: packaged and shipped Monocam, Profiler, Profiler mount, and Minikit units to team members; coordinated UPS rerouting for address correction

## Products & Capabilities Described

**AeroPod**
- Modular component-based system; parts being manufactured via 3D printing
- Components shipped as part of project deliverables

**TerraROVER**
- Ground-based platform; parts manufactured and assembled during this period

**Monocam**
- Camera system; shipped to Carmen (team member/stakeholder) in February

**Profiler**
- Sensor instrument; shipped to Carmen; mount component shipped separately to Geoff Bland

**Minikit**
- Compact sensor kit; multiple units assembled and shipped to Andy (team member)

**Urban Heat Island (UHI) Packages**
- Integrated measurement packages; 5 units assembled and soldered during February
- Designed for monitoring urban thermal characteristics

## Use Cases & Applications
- Arctic Radiation and Environmental Networking (AREN): arctic operations and environmental monitoring
- Urban Heat Island monitoring: temperature and thermal mapping in urban environments

## Notable Details
- Project involved multiple distributed team members receiving equipment shipments (Carmen, Andy, Geoff Bland)

- Strong emphasis on production pipeline management and quality control of printed components
