## Professional Summary
Jack S. Elston is CEO and Founder of Black Swift Technologies LLC. He holds a Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering (2011) and dual Master's degrees in Aerospace Engineering and Electrical Engineering from University of Colorado, Boulder. He is also an NSF AGS Postdoctoral Fellow (2012-present).
