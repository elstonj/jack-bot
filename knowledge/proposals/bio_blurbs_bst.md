## Company Overview

### Founding & Location
- Founded: 2011
- Location: Boulder, Colorado

### Core Mission & Positioning
Black Swift Technologies is recognized for delivering reliable, robust, and highly accurate unmanned aircraft systems capable of flying scientific payloads in demanding atmospheric environments. The company specializes in operating in harsh conditions including:
- High-altitude environments
- Arctic operations
- Desert conditions
- Corrosive particulate environments
- Strong turbulence

### Applications & Use Cases
Purpose-built scientific aerial platforms used globally for specialized atmospheric research missions:
- Wildland fire monitoring and assessment
- Volcano monitoring
- Tornado research
- Hurricane sampling

## Products & Capabilities Described

### SwiftCore Flight Management System (FMS)
**What it is**: Advanced end-to-end avionics solution serving as the technological anchor for all BST solutions

**Key capabilities**:
- Fully autonomous flight control
- User control, communication, and commanding interface
- **Sensor-based autonomous control**: Minimizes operator workload; autonomously modifies flight path based on sensor inputs to improve data quality
- Industry-leading sensor-based flight control
- Accurate flight tracking in high-wind conditions
- Designed for ease of use

**Design philosophy**: Engineered for nomadic scientific field campaigns in harsh environments

**Approvals & Deployments**:
- Approved and used by NASA for major scientific missions
- Deployed by NOAA
- Commercial users including:
  - Engineering and survey professionals
  - Construction and mining managers
  - Atmospheric and environmental monitoring organizations (scientific and academic)
  - Worldwide user base
