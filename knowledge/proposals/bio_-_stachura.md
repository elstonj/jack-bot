## Executive Summary
This is a biographical summary of Dr. Maciej Stachura, Black Swift Technologies' Chief Technology Officer, highlighting his extensive experience in UAS operations, cooperative control algorithms, field campaigns, and FAA/NASA certifications.

## Professional Background
- **Education:** M.S. and Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering from University of Colorado Boulder
- **Current Role:** Chief Technology Officer, Black Swift Technologies (co-founder)
- **Academic Experience:** Over 300 flight experiments conducted during graduate studies

## Key Expertise Areas

### UAS Operations & Regulatory Experience
- Secured and maintained over 140 FAA Certificates of Authorization (COA) for legal flight operations across Colorado, Kansas, and Nebraska
- Led completion of NASA Airworthiness Flight Safety Review Board (AFSRB) and Flight Readiness Review Board processes for UAS operations in National Airspace System

### Research & Development Experience
- **VORTEX2 Field Campaign:** Involved in first-ever intercept of a tornadic supercell thunderstorm
- **Multi-aircraft Cooperative Flight Experiments:** Conducted numerous collaborative platform experiments
- **Soil Moisture Mapping UAS:** Served as PI for Phase I and Phase II NASA SBIR programs focused on development, testing, and commercialization of soil moisture mapping UAS

### Technical Specialization
- Doctoral research: Cooperative control algorithms for multi-UAS experiments combining sensing and communication into unified framework
- Conducted successful multi-UAS flight experiments demonstrating and evaluating control algorithms
- Communication-aware information gathering systems

## Notable Publications & Research
