## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies submitted a Phase I SBIR proposal to NOAA to develop a cost-effective air-deployed unmanned aircraft system (UAS) for boundary layer observations in turbulent environments. The requested funding was $119,872.94 for a 6-month Phase I period.

## Project Information
**Project Title:** The S0 UAS, an Air-Deployed Solution for Boundary Layer Observations in Turbulent Environments

**Solicitation Subtopic:** 8.2.13 - Developing a Cost Effective Air-Deployed Unmanned Aircraft System (UAS) for Use in Turbulent Environments

**Requested Amount:** $119,872.94

**Proposed Duration:** 6 months

## Products & Capabilities Described
- **S0 UAS:** An air-deployed unmanned aircraft system designed for atmospheric boundary layer observations in turbulent environments, with emphasis on cost-effectiveness and operational capability in challenging conditions.
