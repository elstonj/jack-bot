## Executive Summary
This is an instruction document for completing NASA Form 1679 (New Technology Report/NTR), used to disclose innovations developed under NASA contracts or grants. It provides guidance on the online e-NTR submission process, required documentation, and what constitutes reportable new technology.

## Document Content
This document is primarily administrative and instructional in nature, not a substantive proposal or report. It serves as a template guide and does not contain:
- Technical information about BST capabilities or products
- Specific project results or findings
- Use cases or applications
- BST innovations or technologies

## Key Administrative Details
- NTR submission required only if new technology developed
- Must be completed via online e-NTR website: www.invention.nasa.gov
- Requires electronic approval from all innovators before official submission
- Physical signed copy must be uploaded to EHB (Electronic Handbook)
- Support contacts: NTTS Support Desk (757-865-2233), Janelle Nguyen (818-354-3511)
- NASA COTR designated: Robert Jones
- Reference for reportable technology: https://invention.nasa.gov/what-is-reported.php
