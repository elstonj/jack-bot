## Executive Summary
Resume of Maciej Stachura, Chief Aerospace Engineer at Black Swift Technologies, documenting his extensive background in unmanned aircraft systems, controls engineering, state estimation, and atmospheric sensing. Stachura has led BST's NASA SBIR efforts and brings 13+ years of experience in UAS development and autonomous systems from academic and commercial backgrounds.
