## Executive Summary
This presentation documents flight test data for a novel aerial drone platform designed for exploration of Saturn's moon Titan. The data compares propeller configurations (9x6 and 8x8) across different speed regimes to evaluate platform performance.

## Technical Approach
The platform was tested with two propeller configurations:
- **9x6 Propellers**: Tested at both slower and higher speed regimes
- **8x8 Propellers**: Tested at both slower and higher speed regimes

Flight testing evaluated trajectories and performance characteristics across these configurations and speed ranges.

## Products & Capabilities Described
**Aerial Drone Platform for Titan**
- Designed for atmospheric exploration of Titan
- Modular propeller configurations allowing performance optimization
- Capable of operation across different speed profiles (slower speed / higher speed modes)
- Trajectory analysis conducted for different propeller/speed combinations
