## Executive Summary
Webinar providing guidance to Small Business applicants on TACFI proposal submission requirements, eligibility criteria, and common mistakes to avoid. Covers document requirements, matching funds rules, signature formats, and submission procedures.

## Key Program Information

**Funding & Timeline:**
- Secretary of Defense confirmed Small Business programs are not affected by administration funding pause
- NOO (Notice of Opportunity) due dates remain unchanged
- TACFIs are fully funded with full amount provided upfront
- No spend plan required for TACFI proposals

**Submission Process:**
- Government partner must submit the proposal (Small Business has no portal access)
- Submissions made through https://dod.teams portal
- SBC (Small Business Concern) has no access to submission portal
- Use AppSheet Ingest Checklist for submission verification

## Document Requirements

**Format & Structure:**
- Document load reduced from 15 to 8 required documents
- Quad Chart (1 page) with formatting integrity MUST be maintained—no color or formatting changes allowed
- 4 additional sections follow the Quad Chart
- Template 25.2 is the only required format—do not use other template versions
- Only provide information explicitly requested (no excess information)

**Signatures:**
- Must use correct format: O-6 (military) or G-15 (civilian) signature authority

**Points of Contact:**
- Alternate POCs are a REQUIREMENT
- SIPR POC must be provided

## Eligibility & Common Mistakes

**Critical Requirements:**
- Verify eligibility before submission (ineligibility is a common rejection reason)
- Quad Chart integrity is non-negotiable
- Must have a DAF (Department of the Air Force) end-user
- End-user must be GS-15 or O-6 rank

## Matching Funds Rules

**Eligibility:**
- Cannot source matching funds from SBIR programs
- Must be from defense or dual-use applications only
- Cannot mix government and private sector sources—must be either government OR private, not both
- Private sector letter required as part of proposal

**Timing Requirement:**
- Matching funds must meet 6-month guidance: measured from Phase III contract award date to proposal submission date (minimum 6 months required)

**AFWERX Review:**
- AFWERX provides guidance on matching source validity but does not conduct deep-dive screening of appropriateness

## Notable Details
- Transition Strategy white paper should assume success ("these are the things we're doing")
- Government partner acts as sole submission entity; small business partners coordinate separately
- Emphasis on following exact requirements and avoiding common ineligibility triggers
