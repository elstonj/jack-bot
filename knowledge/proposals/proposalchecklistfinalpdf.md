## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies submitted a Phase II STTR proposal to the Navy titled "Expendable Air-sea Profiling Observations in Hazardous Weather Conditions" (N2-9533). This checklist documents BST's compliance with Navy proposal submission requirements, including special requirements, cost content, and regulatory certifications for a 24-month base period with option periods extending through FY 31.

## Technical Approach
- BST proposes to operate its **S0 UAS** during various validation flights
- Work involves hazardous weather operations, specifically air-sea profiling in challenging conditions
- Partnership with subcontractors: University of Miami, Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University (Daytona Beach), and Old Dominion University (Norfolk, VA)
- Planned use of NASA Glenn Icing Research Tunnel for testing/validation (contact: Dennis Eck, Glenn POC)

## Products & Capabilities Described
**S0 UAS (Unmanned Aerial System)**
