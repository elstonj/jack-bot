## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies submitted a budget justification for a NOAA-funded tropical cyclone operations program. The proposal budgets approximately $2.6 million total for personnel, procurement of 125 S0 UAS units, storm deployment travel, and indirect costs to support P-3 operational missions and emerging uncrewed systems research.

## Budget Breakdown

### Personnel
- **Dr. Jack Elston (CEO, PI):** ~400 hours at $98.56/hr = ~2.9 calendar months equivalent
- **Dr. Maciej Stachura (CTO):** ~400 hours at $84.13/hr = ~2.9 calendar months equivalent
- **Fringe Benefits Rate:** 29.280% (includes health insurance, life/AD&D, PTO, sick time, 401k match, workers' compensation, unemployment)
- **Basis:** Labor hours derived from historical comparable project data; rates competitive for local market and employee qualifications

### Equipment: $2,250,000.00
- **125 Black Swift S0 UAS units** @ $18,000 per unit
- **Purpose:** Support 1-2 drone deployments for P-3 operational missions (N42) + UAS for N43

### Travel: $50,000
- **8 separate storm deployment events** budgeted
- **Per-deployment cost:** $6,250 average (2 team members per event)
- **Breakdown includes:** Round-trip airfare, lodging, per diem (meals/incidentals), ground transportation, baggage/equipment shipping
- **Justification:** Increase from prior year average ($5,100) due to rising airfare/lodging costs and premium short-notice travel expenses typical of severe weather response

### Indirect Costs

- **G&A:** $50,496.00 (18.32% rate on Total Direct Costs base of $275,633)
