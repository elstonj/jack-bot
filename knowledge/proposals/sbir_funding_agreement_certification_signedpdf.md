# SBIR Funding Agreement Certification

## Document Metadata
- Type: Funding Agreement Certification (compliance/eligibility form)
- Client/Agency: Department of Defense (DoD)
- Program/Solicitation: DoD SBIR/STTR Program
- Date: October 16, 2020
- Signatory: Dr. Jack S Elston, CEO & Founder
- Company: Black Swift Technologies LLC

## Document Purpose
This is a standard DoD SBIR/STTR eligibility certification form completed at the time of award. It certifies that Black Swift Technologies meets all Small Business Act requirements and program compliance obligations for SBIR/STTR funding.

## Key Certifications Completed (All Marked "Yes" or "X")

**Ownership & Control:**
- Business meets ownership and control requirements (13 CFR 121.702)
- Company structure: Limited Liability Company
- All required organizational documentation evidences compliance

**Size & Eligibility:**
- Company has no more than 500 employees (including affiliates)
- No SBA size status determination finding exceeds 500-employee standard
- Not majority-owned by multiple venture capital operating companies, hedge funds, or private equity firms

**Research Requirements:**
- Principal Investigator will meet primary employment requirements (>50% time as employee of prime contractor)
- Required percentage of work will be performed by prime contractor per SBIR Phase requirements
- All R&D will be performed in the United States
- Performance will take place in firm's facilities with firm's employees (except as identified in proposal)

**Funding & Disclosure:**
- Proposal for equivalent work has not been submitted to another Federal agency or DoD Component for funding
- Firm will notify DoD immediately if any portion of proposed work is funded elsewhere
- Not teamed with Federally Funded Research and Development Center (FFRDC) or Federal Laboratory
