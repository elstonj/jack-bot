- Key Personnel: Maciej Stachura (BST contact)

## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies developed a low-cost, simple-to-operate small unmanned aerial system (sUAS) for mapping volumetric soil moisture content across agricultural-plot-sized areas. The system successfully obtained NASA and FAA approval for legal flight operations, with the first prototype ready for deployment in June 2015 and commercialization underway.

## Technical Approach
- Volumetric soil moisture measurement from aerial platform
- Measurement depth: ~5cm-20cm below surface
- Spatial resolution: up to 15m
- Fully autonomous flight capabilities
- FAA-approved for national airspace operations
- Low-cost, operationally simple design

## Products & Capabilities Described

**Soil Moisture Mapping sUAS**
- What it is: A small unmanned aerial system for remote soil moisture sensing
- How it was proposed/used: Agricultural monitoring, environmental assessment, scientific research participation
- Key specifications:
  - Volumetric soil moisture content measurement
  - 5cm-20m depth penetration
  - 15m resolution mapping
  - Autonomous flight capabilities
  - Regulatory approvals for safe sUAS operations in national airspace

## Use Cases & Applications
- **Precision agriculture**: Water use optimization and crop management
- **Government/Science**: Soil integrity evaluation for vehicle activity planning
- **Disaster response**: Understanding soil and vegetation conditions relative to wildfires, flash floods, and landslides
- **Research**: Participation in SMAP Cal/Val activities (SMAPVEX15 deployment planned for Arizona)

## Key Accomplishments
- Obtained both NASA and FAA legal flight operation permissions
- First prototype completed and ready for deployment
- Initial deployment planned for Oklahoma (June 2015)
- Second deployment planned for Arizona (SMAPVEX15 participation)
- Secured $240,000 in investment for business development
- Received Colorado state grant for commercializing innovative technologies

## Commercialization Status
Product developed for commercial sale with:

- Identified markets across research, government, and precision agriculture sectors
