## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies proposes to develop an all-weather UAS system based on the S3 platform for persistent base security and rapid response operations at Air Force bases, particularly in extreme environments like Grand Forks AFB. The system leverages BST's proven heritage in harsh-environment operations (high altitude, Arctic, volcano, hurricane, and severe weather missions) and integrates the modular SwiftCore avionics architecture to reduce operator workload and enable advanced autonomous capabilities.

## Technical Approach
- **Platform**: S3 VTOL fixed-wing UAS as the base system
- **Avionics**: SwiftCore modular architecture with well-defined intelligent node interfaces
- **Key Features**:
  - Modular payload system for mid-flight mission conversion
  - User-friendly autonomous operation with on-board vision monitoring (aircraft and terrain)
  - Open system interfaces for integration of third-party equipment and additional functionality
  - Subsystem tracking, preventative maintenance, safety redundancy, and automated sensor-driven control capabilities
- **Duration**: 18 months (Phase II base performance period)
- **Work Location**: 100% at BST facilities by BST employees
- **Work Distribution**: 100% BST, 0% subcontractors

## Products & Capabilities Described

### S3 (VTOL Fixed-Wing UAS)
- **What it is**: A vertical takeoff and landing fixed-wing unmanned aerial system with proven heritage in extreme environments
- **Proposed Use**: Persistent base security perimeter surveillance and rapid incident response missions
- **Heritage Applications**: High altitude observations, remote volcano monitoring, Arctic surveys, hurricane and fire weather observations, flights into tornadic thunderstorms
- **Key Capability**: All-weather operation suitable for locations with significant temperature and wind extremes

### SwiftCore Avionics System
- **What it is**: A truly modular avionics architecture with well-defined interfaces between intelligent nodes
- **Proposed Use**: Core enabler for the S3 base security system
- **Key Features**:
  - Simplified third-party component integration through open interfaces
  - Advanced capabilities: subsystem tracking, preventative maintenance, safety redundancy, automated sensor-driven control
  - Designed and programmed exclusively by UAS technology experts
  - Proven field performance across diverse difficult applications and multiple aircraft platforms
  - Applicable to replacement of proprietary interfaces on other airborne assets

## Use Cases & Applications
- **Primary**: Persistent perimeter security and rapid response at Air Force bases
- **Specific Application**: Grand Forks Air Force Base operations in extreme temperature and wind conditions
- **Mission Flexibility**: Modular payload system allows conversion on flight line for different data-gathering missions
- **Operational Benefits**: Reduced operator workload, reduced training requirements, reduced programmatic risk, enabled preventative maintenance capabilities
