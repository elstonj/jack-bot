Kay Rufty completed production and assembly tasks for the AREN project in December 2021, including manufacturing and maintenance of AeroPod and TerraROVER components, creating an AGU (American Geophysical Union) conference poster with flight data analysis, and fabricating assembly documentation and mounts for field instruments.
