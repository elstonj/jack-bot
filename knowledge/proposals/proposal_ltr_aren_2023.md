## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies commits to supporting NASA's AEROKATS and ROVER Education Network (AREN), a Science Activation outreach initiative. BST will contribute engineering and technical expertise to develop educational workshop materials using kites for environmental exploration, while evaluating the feasibility of the AeroPod commercial product line.

## Technical Approach
- Development and fabrication of materials for kite-based educational workshops
- Design, fabrication of wood and plastic components
- Prototype evaluation of new systems by BST team members
- All work performed remotely from three locations: Denver/Boulder, CO and Onancock, VA
- No requirement for NASA facility access or IT system integration

## Products & Capabilities Described
**AeroPod:**
- Described as a "commercial product" under feasibility evaluation
- Context suggests it can be adapted or integrated into educational/outreach applications
- Development materials to be evaluated by engineering team

## Use Cases & Applications
- **Primary Application:** Educational outreach and science activation
- **Method:** Kite-based atmospheric and environmental exploration
- **Audience:** Wide variety of educational participants and institutions
- **Target Distribution:** Educational institutions and program partners
