## Executive Summary
Progress report on the MALIBU Tempest project, a NASA-contracted UAS system integrating BST's SwiftPilot and SwiftStation with a Tetracam MCA payload. Core airframe and avionics integration were substantially complete; FAA certification was pending with expected flight clearance by mid-July 2015.

## Technical Approach
The project centered on building and integrating a complete UAS system:
- **Airframe:** Tempest airframe design and construction
- **Avionics Integration:** SwiftPilot and SwiftStation avionics systems built and ready for integration with Tempest airframe
- **Payload:** Tetracam MCA (multispectral camera array) payload integration planned with both airframe and avionics
- **Regulatory Path:** FAA certification and N-number registration filed to enable flight operations

## Products & Capabilities Described

### Tempest
- UAS airframe (design and construction completed by report date)
- Airframe integration with avionics in progress

### SwiftPilot

- Built and ready for payload and airframe integration

### SwiftStation
- Ground control/command station system
- Built and ready for deployment
