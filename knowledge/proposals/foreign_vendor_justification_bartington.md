## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies requests justification to procure a magnetic sensor from Bartington Instruments (UK) for a Navy Phase II SBIR magnetometer project. The acquisition is government-directed as part of a comparative evaluation against a domestic QuSpin Inc. sensor, and Bartington is identified as the sole manufacturer of the required sensor configuration with no equivalent domestic alternative.

## Technical Approach
- Comparative evaluation between two sensor technologies: Bartington (foreign) and QuSpin Inc. (domestic)
- Assessment of performance and cost trade-offs across multiple sensor technologies
- Determination of optimal solution for Navy applications based on comparative results
- Evaluation criteria include design, performance characteristics, and form factor compatibility
