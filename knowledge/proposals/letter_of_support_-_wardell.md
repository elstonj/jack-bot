## Executive Summary
Arapahoe SciTech (AST) commits to supporting Black Swift Technologies on a USDA SBIR Phase I project focused on developing a ruggedized unmanned aircraft system with sensors for measuring hazardous airborne emissions from wildfires on forest lands. AST will provide consulting and labor support valued at $19,990 over an 8-month phase.

## Technical Approach
Arapahoe SciTech will provide consulting and technical support across multiple payload and flight operation domains:
- **Payload evaluation and selection** guidance
- **Sensor integration requirements** advising as part of payload design
- **In situ sampler design** consultation
- **Payload testing** oversight
- **Flight operations planning** for in situ plume measurement and sampling
- Support for Phase II planning and proposal development

Secondary contributions include literature review on wildfire emission research and assistance with presentations, publications, and documentation.
