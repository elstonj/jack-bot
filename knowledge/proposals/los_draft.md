## Executive Summary
This is a draft letter of support for Black Swift Technologies' SBIR proposal to develop the S3 UAS, a next-generation unmanned aerial system designed for long-duration, autonomous missions in GPS-denied, austere, and severe weather environments including CBRN zones and conflict areas.

## Technical Approach
The S3 UAS integrates the following capabilities:
- **Vertical Takeoff and Landing (VTOL)** functionality
- **Autonomous GPS-denied navigation** for operations in denied/degraded environments
- **Environmental resilience** for severe weather and austere conditions
- **Solar-based recharge capability** for extended deployment without external logistics
- **Persistent operation:** Days to months of deployment without human intervention
