## Executive Summary
This is a NASA-provided legal template for Small Business Act SBIR/STTR Support Agreements, used when small businesses receive SBIR/STTR awards and intend to utilize federal laboratory or FFRDC (Federally Funded Research and Development Center) resources. The template establishes the framework for reimbursable support that NASA facilities, equipment, and services can provide to SBIR/STTR contractors.

## Notable Details

**Statutory Authority:** 15 U.S.C. § 638(ee)

**Key Contractual Provisions:**
- Contractor must reimburse NASA for resources used (no U.S. Government funds transfer to contractor)
- Advance payments required; limited to costs for 30 days of activities
- NASA provides resources "as-is" with no warranties
- Contractor remains fully responsible for SBIR/STTR contract performance regardless of NASA support
- NASA provides reasonable advance notice of scheduling conflicts
- Either party may terminate with 30 days written notice
- Contractor waives claims against NASA for injury, death, or property damage except in cases of willful misconduct
- Data developed is governed by the SBIR/STTR contract rights-in-data clause
- In case of conflict, the SBIR/STTR contract takes precedence over this support agreement
- Payment options: electronic transfer via https://paygov.nssc.nasa.gov or check to NASA Shared Services Center (NSSC)
