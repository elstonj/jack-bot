## Executive Summary
This document is a three-pool indirect cost rate model for Black Swift Technologies LLC for fiscal year 2018, used to calculate fringe benefit rates, overhead rates, and G&A (General & Administrative) rates for cost allocation and proposal purposes.

## Document Purpose
This is an accounting/financial spreadsheet used to:
1. Calculate indirect cost rates from company trial balance data
2. Allocate costs into appropriate pools (Direct, Fringe, Overhead, G&A, Unallowable)
3. Determine rates for applying indirect costs to proposals and projects
