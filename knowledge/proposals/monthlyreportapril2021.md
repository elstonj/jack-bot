# Monthly Progress Report - April 2021

## Document Metadata
- Type: Monthly progress report
- Client/Agency: NASA
- Program/Solicitation: AREN (Arctic Relevance Earth Networks) 2020-2021
- Date: April 2021 (reporting period); submitted May 6, 2021
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: TerraROVER, AeroPod, AlbedoPod, Monocam, Profiler
- Key Personnel: Geoff Bland (Task Manager), Kay Rufty (Employee)

## Executive Summary
April 2021 progress on NASA's AREN project included unsuccessful testing of the TerraROVER platform, extensive manufacturing and maintenance of AeroPod and TerraROVER components using 3D printing, and shipment of multiple sensor units (AlbedoPods, Monocams, Profilers) to project partners.

## Technical Approach
- 3D printing manufacturing focus: testing ABS filament with optimized settings, experimenting with print bed coverings for adhesion, varying wall thickness and infill percentages for strength optimization
- Quality control: troubleshooting Monoprice printer filament clogging issues
- Production tracking: created spreadsheet system to monitor parts printed by production team
- Assembly and calibration of sensor packages for deployment
