# MonthlyReportJune2020

## Document Metadata
- Type: Monthly Progress Report
- Client/Agency: NASA
- Program/Solicitation: AREN (Airborne Remote sensing Education Network) 2020-2021

- Key Personnel: Geoff Bland (Task Manager), Kay Rufty (Employee)

## Executive Summary
Kay Rufty completed various development and outreach tasks for the NASA AREN program in June 2020, including video production for training content, aerospace design work on the Aerotube system, assembly and shipment of AeroPods to partners, and website redesign planning for educational training modules.

## Technical Approach
- **Video Documentation**: Continued recording and editing voice-over video content overlaid on video recordings (likely for training/educational purposes)
- **Aerotube Development**: 
  - Used OnShape CAD software for 3D modeling
  - Researched and scaled Beech 18 H-tail dimensions as design reference for Aerotube
  - Addressed and researched CAD modeling problems during design process
- **AeroPod Testing**: Conducted test flights with limited success; continued refinement
- **Manufacturing**: Printed, assembled, and shipped multiple AeroPods to partners
- **Website Redesign**: Conceptual brainstorming and flowchart mapping for reformulated training module interface

## Products & Capabilities Described

### Aerotube
- A vehicle/airframe under development using Beech 18 H-tail dimensions as a design reference
- Being designed and modeled in OnShape CAD software
- Appears to be part of the educational/research platform for AREN

### AeroPod
- Small aircraft being produced and assembled by BST
- Multiple units printed, assembled, and shipped to partners during June
- Capable of flight testing; test flights were conducted with mixed results
- Used in educational/operational context for AREN program

## Use Cases & Applications
- **NASA AREN Program**: Airborne remote sensing education and training
- **Educational Outreach**: Remote sensing training modules, video-based educational content
- **Partner Operations**: AeroPods shipped to partner organizations for use (likely educational or research missions)
