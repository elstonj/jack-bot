## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies received discretionary Technical and Business Assistance (TABA) from EKase Consulting, LLC to support commercialization of its "Expendable Air-Sea Profiling Observations" technology for hazardous weather conditions via small uncrewed aircraft systems. The TABA provider delivered comprehensive market research and competitive analysis to identify customer segments, potential partners, and market trends across DoD, government agencies, and commercial sectors.

## Products & Capabilities Described

**Expendable Air-Sea Profiling Observations Technology**
- Designed for small uncrewed aircraft systems (UAS) to conduct air-sea profiling in hazardous weather conditions
- Topic Title: "Expendable Air-Sea Profiling Observations in Hazardous Weather Conditions via Small Uncrewed Aircraft System"
- Intended for operational use in challenging meteorological environments

## Technical Approach
EKase Consulting employed a two-pronged market research methodology:

**Secondary Research:**
- Literature review of market research reports analyzing trends in: Uncrewed Aircraft Systems (UAS), Military UAVs, Drone Services, and Weather Forecasting Systems
- Competitive landscape analysis identifying competing products from Dragoon Technology LLC and stoa tech, inc.

**Primary Research:**
- Identification and outreach to subject matter experts, potential customers, and prospective partners
- Validation of research findings through direct contact with industry prospects
- Creation of detailed contact lists across multiple customer segments
