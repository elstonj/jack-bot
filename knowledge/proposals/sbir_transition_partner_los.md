## Executive Summary
This is a letter of support from USAF endorsing Black Swift Technologies' Phase II SBIR proposal to develop an expendable sonobuoy-launched UAS (modified S0 airframe) for anti-submarine warfare (ASW). The letter validates Phase I findings demonstrating the S0's compatibility with P-8A Poseidon sonobuoy launcher specifications and confirms its capability for magnetic anomaly detection (MAD) and acoustic payload operations.

## Technical Approach
- **Platform**: Modified S0 UAS airframe adapted for tube-launch deployment
- **Launch System**: P-8A Poseidon's LAU-126A sonobuoy launcher (expendable deployment)
- **Payloads**: Magnetic Anomaly Detection (MAD) sensor + acoustic payload
- **Onboard Computing**: 275 TOPS edge processor for AI/autonomous target tracking
- **Key Performance Metric**: Magnetic noise floor of ~1.5 pT/√Hz (validated in Phase I), enabling high-fidelity magnetic anomaly detection
- **Endurance**: Sufficient for ASW operations
