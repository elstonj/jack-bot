# 22.4 Proposal Ideas

## Document Metadata
- Type: Internal planning/brainstorming document
- Client/Agency: U.S. Department of Defense (DOD, AFRL, AFSOC)
- Program/Solicitation: SBIR Phase I and D2P2 (potentially)
- Date: October 23, 2023
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: S0, S2, S3 (implied)
- Key Personnel: Jack Elston (last editor), Dave G., Matt Corrigan (Autonodyne contact)

## Executive Summary
Internal document outlining potential SBIR Phase I proposal ideas and D2P2 (Defense to Products to Prosperity) concepts for BST. Ideas focus on UAS-based capabilities including data fusion, AI/edge computing, advanced sensing, swarming, and specialized platforms for DOD applications.

## Proposal Ideas (Phase I)

### 1. Data Fusion
- Fusing high-resolution UAS data with low-resolution satellite or other data sources
- Status: Concept stage

### 2. UAS Cyber Testing
- NDAA compliance and Blue UAS not required
- UAS procured for cyber testing exempt from Section 848 of NDAA
- Status: Concept stage

### 3. UAS Magnetometer
- Application: Detection of mines, unexploded ordnance (UXO), improvised explosive devices (IED)
- Note: DOD likely has prior magnetometer UAS experimentation
- Status: Early concept

### 4. Advanced Edge AI Capability
- Automated threat recognition with sensor-directed flight control on small UAS (sUAS)
- Includes automated threat recognition and messaging
- Training approach: Synthetic data
- Status: Promising concept (noted as appealing to DOD)

### 5. Avionics Hardware & Software
- MOSA/SOSA-compliant autopilot hardware
- Potential partner: Oak Ridge National Laboratory (partnership feasibility uncertain under SBIR)
- Note: Previous proposal rejected; rewrite recommended to address MOSA/SOSA compliance
- Status: Revise and resubmit

### 6. Advanced Sensing
- Proposed use of Q3 Spectrometer variant to detect WMD signatures (nuclear, biological, chemical)
- Alternative technology: Nexus-RT's emerging technology in same area
- Status: Concept stage

### 7. Meteorological UAS
- Target agency: AFSOC (only identified inroad)
- Specific interest: AFRL interest in tube-launched S0 deployment from cargo planes before parachute drops
- Status: Identified customer interest (AFRL)

### 8. Swarming
- Status: Concept only (no details provided)

### 9. Universal Robotic Controller Integration
- Note: Assessed as potential non-starter due to existing commercial competitors at PEO level
- Status: Low priority

### 10. Envelope Testing in Challenging Atmospheric Conditions
- Context: AFRL interest in envelope testing for Preventive Maintenance P2 program
- BST strength: Operating in high wind and turbulence conditions
- Assessment: Feasibility for Phase I unclear
- Status: Under consideration

### 11. S0 for Hyper-Local Weather Forecasting
- Proposed application: Using S0 to provide localized data for improving weather forecasting models
- Status: Concept stage

## Products & Capabilities Described

- Tube-launched configuration for deployment from cargo aircraft
- Potential for meteorological data collection
- Potential for hyper-local weather forecasting data
- Emerging application: Airport soil moisture monitoring

### S2
- Integration platform for testing external technologies (proposed use with Caliola's GPS-denied navigation systems)

### Q3 Spectrometer
- Referenced as existing or variant technology
- Proposed modification for WMD signature detection
