## Executive Summary
This document provides the detailed budget justification for a University of Miami-led Navy STTR Phase II project focused on developing expendable air-sea profiling observations using small uncrewed aircraft systems (sUAS) for operations in hazardous weather conditions. The four-year project totals $606,247 in costs, with Dr. Jun Zhang overseeing research and a dedicated postdoctoral researcher conducting the proposed work.

## Technical Approach & Research Plan
The project involves:
- Development of expendable profiling systems for deployment from small uncrewed aircraft
- Air-sea observation capabilities in hazardous weather environments
- Data collection, analysis, and dissemination through peer-reviewed publications
- Field work involving multiple deployment trips
