# Black Swift Technologies Cost Share Letter

## Document Metadata
- Type: Letter of commitment/support
- Client/Agency: Southern Methodist University (SMU), Department of Energy (DOE)
- Program/Solicitation: DE-FOA-0003343, Area of Interest 3 - Long-Term UOW Monitoring
- Date: October 29, 2024
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: S2 UAS, Quanta3 methane spectrometer
- Key Personnel: Saeed Salehi (SMU contact), Stuart Stein and Jeff Stein (Hyde Park West Telecommunications)

## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies commits to support SMU's DOE proposal for long-term monitoring of orphaned well (UOW) methane emissions. BST will provide its S2 methane leak detection platform and custom eddy covariance expertise, with in-kind contributions valued at $163,960, to help compare novel monitoring technologies against commercial state-of-the-art solutions.

## Technical Approach
- Deployment of **Black Swift S2 UAS** equipped with **Quanta3 methane spectrometer** for methane emissions detection and quantification
- Application of custom **eddy covariance techniques** adapted from flux tower methodologies for precise gas exchange measurements
- Field trials to evaluate technology effectiveness, accuracy, and precision
- Comparison with commercially available monitoring technologies

## Products & Capabilities Described

### S2 UAS (Unmanned Aerial System)
- **Specifications:**
  - Flight endurance: up to 90 minutes
  - Wingspan: 3 meters
  - Payload capacity: 2 kg
  - Capable of operating in demanding environments: rugged terrain, low temperatures
- **Application:** Detecting and quantifying methane emissions in remote/hard-to-reach locations (oil and gas fields, mines, landfills, agricultural areas)
- **Quantity committed:** 2 aircraft valued at $64,194 each ($128,288 total)

### Quanta3 Methane Spectrometer
- Custom-built sensor integrated with S2 UAS
- Provides highly sensitive methane detection platform
- Quantity committed: 1 custom-designed sensor valued at $35,572

## Use Cases & Applications
- Orphaned well emissions monitoring and remediation
- Methane and toxic gas detection
- Long-term monitoring solutions for well sites
- Remote oil and gas field inspection
- Mine, landfill, and agricultural area monitoring
