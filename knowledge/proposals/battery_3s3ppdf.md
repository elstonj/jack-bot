## Products & Capabilities Described

### Battery Pack Types

**Type 1: Panasonic Sanyo NCR18650GA cells**
- Configurations: 1S, 4S, 6S, 10S, 12S series with 3P-15P parallel arrangements
- Voltage range: 4.2V to 50.4V maximum
- Capacity range: 10.5Ah to 112Ah
- Discharge current: 30A to 640A continuous
- Weight: 800g to 3200g
- Price range: €155–€590
- Notes: Requires smart charger with overcharge protection and cell balancing

**Type 1b: Panasonic Sanyo NCR20700B cells**
- Configurations: 6S and 10S with 3P-9P parallel
- Voltage: 25.2V or 42V maximum
- Capacity: 12.75Ah to 38.2Ah
- Discharge current: 45A to 135A continuous
- Weight: 1170g to 5850g
- Price range: €200–€920

**Type 1c: "Bamf" NCR20700A cells (Coming Soon)**
- Higher discharge capability: 30A continuous, 45A peak per cell
- Configurations: 6S and 10S with 4P-14P parallel
- Capacity: 13.2Ah to 46.2Ah
- Discharge current: 120A to 420A continuous
- Pricing: Not yet available

**Type 2: Cell-level fused configurations**
- Available with NCR18650GA and NCR20700B cells
- Same voltage/capacity specs as Type 1
- Additional safety feature: cell-level fuses for protection against internal short-circuits (Tesla Motors technology)
- Slightly higher pricing than Type 1 (e.g., 6S6P NCR20700B: €366 vs. €390)

**Ground Control Station Batteries**
- 4S3P: 14.8V, 10.5Ah, 20A, €120
- 6S3P: 22.2V, 10.5Ah, 20A, €180
