# SBIR Funding Agreement Certification – Life Cycle Certification

## Document Metadata

- Program/Solicitation: NOAA 2018 SBIR Phase II
- Date: 2019-02-19
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: None
- Key Personnel: Jack Steward Elston (CEO, signatory)

## Executive Summary
This is a completed SBIR life cycle certification form required by the Small Business Administration for Phase II SBIR awardees. Black Swift Technologies, signed by CEO Jack Steward Elston, certifies compliance with SBIR program requirements regarding principal investigator time commitment, work location, funding overlap, and research percentage requirements.

## Certification Requirements Verified
All required boxes were checked (marked with "x") certifying that BST:

1. **Principal Investigator Time**: Met the requirement that the PI spent more than one half of his/her time as a BST employee (no deviation needed)

2. **Work Funding Status**: Confirmed that the work had not been submitted for funding by another Federal agency, or if submitted, had not been funded elsewhere (specific box marked indicating compliance)

3. **Research Percentage**: Committed to performing at least 50% of the research (standard Phase II requirement)

4. **Work Completion Status**: Verified work completion status and research percentage performance

5. **U.S. Performance**: Confirmed that research/R&D was performed in the United States (no waiver needed)

6. **Facility & Employee Requirements**: Confirmed work was performed at BST facilities with BST employees as indicated in the SBIR application

7. **Notification Obligation**: Agreed to immediately notify the Federal agency if work subsequently becomes funded by another agency

8. **Information Disclosure**: Acknowledged that submitted information may be shared with Federal, State, and local agencies

9. **Authority & Accuracy**: CEO certified authority to sign on behalf of BST and attested to the truth and accuracy of all submitted information
