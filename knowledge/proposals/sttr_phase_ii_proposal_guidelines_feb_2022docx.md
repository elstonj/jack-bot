## Executive Summary
This document provides procedural guidelines for small businesses submitting Phase II proposals to the Navy's Small Business Technology Transfer (STTR) program managed by the Office of Naval Research. It outlines eligibility requirements, proposal preparation standards, funding limits, and evaluation criteria for Phase II submissions following successful Phase I awards.

## Proposal Requirements Summary

### Eligibility & Participation
- Phase I awardees must submit an initial Phase II proposal alongside their Phase I final report
- Only firms submitting initial Phase II proposals are eligible for full Phase II consideration
- Selection for full Phase II based on: Phase I quality, naval relevance, technical merit, team qualifications, commercialization potential
- Principal Investigator must maintain primary employment (>50% time) with small business or research institution throughout award period

### Performance & Funding
- **Phase II Base**: 24 months, up to $1M funding
- **Phase II Option 1**: 24 months, up to $1M (requires 1:1 cost match; STTR funds $500K, matching funds $500K)
- **Work allocation requirement**: Minimum 40% by small business, 30% by research institution
- All work must be performed in the United States (50 states, territories, possessions, Puerto Rico, Northern Mariana Islands, Trust Territory of Pacific Islands, District of Columbia)
- Technology Readiness Level 4 (minimum) at exit for options ≥$1.5M STTR funding

### Proposal Submission
- Electronic submission to DoD SBIR/STTR website (https://www.dodsbirsttr.mil) required
- One document combining: technical proposal, checklist, cost proposal
- Technical content limited to 50 pages (including option, excluding supporting materials)
- Format: 1-inch margins, 10-point font minimum, consecutive page numbering
- Contract type: Cost Plus Fixed Fee (CPFF)
- Contractor must have job-order-based accounting system for CPFF contracts

### Cost Proposal Elements
- Direct labor (all key personnel named, other personnel by category with hours and hourly rates)
- Consultants/subcontractors (by name, hours, hourly costs)
- Special tooling, testing, test equipment, and material (with competitive quotes or justification)
- Travel costs (justified; includes mandatory STP meeting attendance in Washington, DC during Years 1-2)
- Technical and Business Assistance (TABA): optional up to $25K per Phase II award/$50K per project for external commercialization/risk-mitigation services (excludes STP participation; provider cannot be firm, affiliate, investor, or otherwise-required subcontractor)
- General & Administrative (G&A) rate (if applicable)
- Facility Capital Cost of Money (FCCM) rate (if applicable)
- Fixed fee/profit (if applicable)
- Approved accounting system documentation

### Intellectual Property & Research Institution Agreement
- Small business must negotiate written agreement with research institution allocating:
  - Ownership of inventions/innovations and rights to revenues/profits
  - Patent/IP protection rights
  - Follow-on research and commercialization rights
- Agreement must be finalized and signed within 15 days of award notification
- Small business must submit agreement to Navy STTR PM on request
- Small business maintains "controlling position" in all agreements

### Evaluation Criteria
- Phase I effort quality
- Potential to satisfy existing or future Naval need
- Technical merit and innovation of proposed approach
- Qualifications of PI, key investigators, supporting staff, consultants
- Commercialization potential (Government or private sector)
- Program officer concurrence

### Additional Requirements
- Annual electronic representations and certifications via SAM.gov (ORCA)
- DFARS compliance
- Proprietary/classified information markings per DoD solicitation (no proprietary/classified info in abstract/benefits sections)
- Company Commercialization Report (online submission summarizing value of all prior SBIR/STTR awards)
- Optional Navy success story submission for follow-on funding achievements
- Mandatory STP meeting attendance (budget included unless receiving Company Directed TABA)
- Human, animal, and recombinant DNA research requires review and approval per ONR guidance

## Notable Details
- Navy reserves right to make no award, one award, or multiple awards under any topic
- Notification to submit full Phase II does not guarantee funding
- Research Institution may seek fundamental research waiver (DFAR 252.204-7000) with sufficient documentation
- TABA funding excludes STP participation eligibility
- Technology Transition Agreement (TTA) required when Phase II STTR funding ≥$1.5M
- DTIC registration recommended for first-time awardees to access technical information databases
- Success story submissions important for commercialization evaluation criteria; companies without prior Navy Phase II awards not penalized
- Contracting office: Naval Air Systems Command Lakehurst
