## Executive Summary
This document provides detailed pricing and specifications for Black Swift's S2 unmanned aircraft system and associated launcher equipment, support services, and accessories as part of a NASA SBIR Phase III volcano monitoring project budget.

## Products & Capabilities Described

### Black Swift S2™ UAS Flight System
- **Cost:** $39,900
- **Includes:** S2 airframe, avionics package with SwiftCore Flight Management System, SwiftTab Android-based control tablet, SwiftStation ground control unit, toolkit, tripod, battery, charger, and hard shipping/storage case
- **Warranty:** Manufacturer's defects covered for 12 months; Flight Management System software warrantied for 3 years from purchase with free upgrades
- **Nosecone Options (customer must select one):**
  - Carbon Fiber Nosecone: $1,275
  - Kevlar Nosecone: $1,425

### S2 Pneumatic Launcher
- **Cost:** $17,626
- **Includes:** Launch rail, pneumatic box and control system
- **Purpose:** Enables rapid launch capability for the S2 platform

### Support Equipment & Services
- **S2 Battery (spare):** $815 (6S 14000 mAh 18650 battery pack with quick release case)
- **S2 Flight Training:** $3,000 (2 days including flight simulator and actual flying; additional support available at $150/hour)
- **S2 Flight Services:** $2,500/flight day plus travel expenses (includes Part 107 certified pilot in command and dedicated observer; optional S2 rental at $1,000/flight day)
- **Flight Secure Maintenance Package:** $2,500/year or $250/month
  - Log file review with performance matrix reporting
  - Pre-flight planning assistance (up to 3 flights/year)
  - Data set triage and troubleshooting
  - Airframe inspection and maintenance (every 100 flights)
  - Technical phone support
  - Access to training webinars and video tutorials

Volcano monitoring operations (as part of NASA SBIR Phase III effort).
