## Executive Summary
This document justifies the selection of Dr. John Park at Old Dominion University (ODU) as a sole source subcontractor for a Navy STTR Phase II project on hazardous weather. The justification argues that Dr. Park's specialized expertise in coupled modeling and adaptive observations, developed during Phase I, is uniquely positioned to support the project's technical objectives and that substituting another provider would introduce unacceptable risk and cost/schedule impacts.

## Technical Expertise Justified
- **Coupled modeling**: Multiphysics modeling capabilities
- **Adaptive observations**: Specialized expertise in adaptive sensing strategies
- **Mission optimization**: Ability to align onboard observation priorities with mission objectives to maximize scientific return

## Phase I Continuity
The justification emphasizes that Dr. Park has:
- Established domain-specific expertise developed during Phase I
- Institutional knowledge essential for Phase II continuity and technical success
- Established approaches that would be difficult to replicate with alternative providers

## Risk Mitigation Rationale
Substituting another provider would:
- Require substantial duplication of effort
- Introduce significant cost and schedule impact
- Necessitate replication of established approaches
- Require rebuilding of domain-specific expertise

## Notable Details
- This is a Phase II follow-on to a Phase I effort
- The document frames ODU as the only reasonable source within required timeframe and technical constraints
- Dr. Park's institutional knowledge from Phase I is positioned as critical, non-substitutable value
