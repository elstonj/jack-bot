## Executive Summary
This monthly report documents ongoing activities throughout 2023 for the AREN project, including participation in production and group meetings, development and testing of educational kite and aerial platform systems (Aeropod, Tower kite, TerraRover), coordination of workshops and outreach events (Summer Institute Workshop, CU Boulder Kite Day), and logistical support including 3D printing, assembly, and distribution of materials to project partners.

## Technical Approach
- Development of educational kite designs, including paper kite variants and the Tower kite
- Integration of Aeropod systems with minicam capability for educational use
- 3D printing and assembly of Aeropod kits and TerraRover components
- Troubleshooting equipment accuracy issues with external partner (Kestrel company)
- Wind tunnel testing of miniature 3D-printed kite models
- Camera setup and configuration (Gitup3 cameras) for TerraRover applications
- Version control documentation for TerraRover system

## Products & Capabilities Described

**Aeropod**
- Aerial platform system with minicam capability
- Shipped as kits for assembly by end users (35 kits assembled and distributed to Summer Institute Workshop participants in July)
- 3D-printed components produced iteratively throughout the year
- Used in educational/workshop context

**Tower Kite**
- New kite design assembled, documented, and test-flown (April)
- Assembly instructions provided and refined with participant feedback (June)
- Used as educational platform for kite-based aerial observation

**TerraRover**
- Mobile platform system using Gitup3 cameras
- Version control documentation developed (January)
- 3D-printed spare parts produced (October, December)
- 30+ Gitup3 cameras configured and shipped to distributor for workshop use (November)
- Dedicated workshop planned for participants

**Paper Kites**
- Research and testing of paper kite designs for classroom use (February)
- Miniature 3D model of "frustrationless flyer" kite designed for wind tunnel testing (June)
- Printable template created for best paper kite design (June)
