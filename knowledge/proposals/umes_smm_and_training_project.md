## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies proposed a commercialization and training partnership with the University of Maryland Eastern Shore (UMES) to demonstrate the Soil Moisture Mapping SuperSwift UAS system. The project included training UMES personnel on the SwiftTrainer platform before conducting soil moisture mapping flights with the SuperSwift equipped with a passive L-band radiometer, with data processing support to generate actionable soil moisture maps.

## Technical Approach
- **Phase 1**: Delivery and on-site training in Maryland using the SwiftTrainer UAS as a lower-cost, lower-complexity training platform
- **Phase 2**: Flight deployment of the full Soil Moisture Mapping sensor suite on the SuperSwift sUAS
- **Phase 3**: Processing of multi-sensor data (L-band radiometer, NDVI, surface temperature) to generate soil moisture maps
- **Phase 4**: Technical support to UMES personnel for data interpretation and use

The approach used SwiftTrainer as a stepping stone to prepare UMES for operating the more sophisticated SuperSwift platform.

## Products & Capabilities Described

### SwiftTrainer UAS
- Simpler, lower-cost training platform
- Included: aircraft, hard case, batteries, charger, 24MP Survey Camera, ground station, tablet computer
- Primary purpose: hands-on mission planning and execution training before SuperSwift operations
- Cost: $14,979 (including equipment and 4 hours of training)

### SuperSwift sUAS
- Primary operational platform for soil moisture mapping
- Equipped with passive L-band radiometer for soil moisture measurement
- Capable of NDVI (vegetation) and surface temperature sensing
- Demonstrated in previous flights at Canton, OK test site

### Sensor Suite
- Passive L-band radiometer (core soil moisture measurement)
- NDVI sensor (normalized difference vegetation index)
- Surface temperature sensor
- Data processing generates calibrated soil moisture maps

## Use Cases & Applications
- **Soil Moisture Mapping**: Multi-spectral soil moisture characterization over test areas
- **Academic/Research Institution Training**: Hands-on UAS operation and remote sensing data collection and interpretation at UMES
- **Test Site**: UMES area in Maryland, with reference to previous Canton, OK demonstration flights

## Project Financials

- **Cost Breakdown**:
