## Executive Summary
This SOW describes atmospheric and thermal modeling work to assess the feasibility of fixed-wing autonomous flight operations on Titan, including determination of optimal flight windows and landing/takeoff conditions through integration of flight path modeling with General Circulation Model (GCM) atmospheric data.

## Technical Approach
- Use mesoscale atmospheric models of Titan to evaluate fixed-wing flight suitability
- Assess Titan's thermal and surface conditions for autonomous landing and takeoff operations
- Integrate flight path modeling with GCM (General Circulation Model) output
- Identify regions and times of day conducive to:
  - Sustained autonomous flight
  - Landed scientific investigations
