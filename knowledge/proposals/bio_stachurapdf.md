## Executive Summary
Biographical sketch of Maciej Stachura, Co-Founder and Chief Technology Officer of Black Swift Technologies. Documents his academic background in aerospace engineering (BS, MS, PhD from University of Colorado and University of Toronto) and career progression from controls engineer to UAS consultant to BST co-founder, with significant publications in UAS systems, cooperative target localization, meteorological sampling, and airspace integration.
