## Key Results
**Lodging Rates (Daily, excluding taxes):**
- October 2024: $275
- November-December 2024: $196
- January-February 2025: $196
- March-May 2025: $276
- June-August 2025: $183
- September 2025: $275

**Meals & Incidental Expenses (M&IE):**
- Total daily rate: $92
  - Breakfast: $23
  - Lunch: $26
  - Dinner: $38
  - Incidental expenses: $5
- First/last day of travel: $69.00 (75% of total)

## Notable Details
Document applies to District of Columbia and surrounding areas (Alexandria, Falls Church, Fairfax VA; Arlington and Fairfax counties VA; Montgomery and Prince George's counties MD). Used as supporting documentation for travel budget in Navy STTR Phase I proposal.
