## Executive Summary
Dr. Jack Elston is the CEO and co-founder of Black Swift Technologies with extensive expertise in unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) for in-situ atmospheric measurements, particularly in hazardous conditions. His Ph.D. research developed networked UAS and control algorithms for sampling severe thunderstorms, culminating in the Tempest UAS which conducted the first unmanned intercept of a tornadic supercell. At BST, he serves as technical lead for avionics and created the SwiftCore Flight Management System autopilot.
