## Executive Summary
This document provides procedural instructions for small businesses participating in NASA's SBIR/STTR program to apply for Phase II Enhancement (Phase II-E) or Phase II eXpanded (Phase II-X) contract options, which offer opportunities to extend and commercialize Phase II R&D efforts with matched funding from outside investors or government programs.

## Notable Details

**Phase II-E/X Application Requirements:**
The application package must include six specific components:
1. Completed Phase II E/X Application Form
2. Letter of Commitment from qualified funding source stating cash investment amount, confirmation that funds are in response to Phase II R&D, and that matching funds will be available within 45 days of approval
3. Concise Statement of Work (PDF) defining specific objectives, detailed work plan, tasks, methods, performance schedules, milestones, and deliverables
4. Detailed Cost Proposal (under one page, PDF) with estimated costs for each cost element
5. Summary Briefing Chart (from template, non-proprietary information)
6. Updated Commercialization Plan per solicitation guidelines

**Submission Process:**
- Submit through NASA SBIR/STTR Contract Administration and Closeout Electronic Handbook (EHB) at https://ehb8.gsfc.nasa.gov/contracts/public/firmHome.do
- Print hard copy for records before submission
- No changes allowed after submission
- Evaluation begins only after complete submission
