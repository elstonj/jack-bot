## Executive Summary
Biographical summary of Dr. Jorge Andres Diaz, President and CTO of NTCR Consulting, submitted as supporting material for a NASA 2016 SBIR Phase II proposal on volcano monitoring. Diaz is a subject matter expert in miniature mass spectrometry systems and UAV-based in situ gas measurement for harsh environments, with extensive NASA contractor experience.

## Technical Expertise
- **Primary Focus**: UAS sensor and payload integration; miniature mass spectrometry (MS) systems for portable, in situ gas analysis
- **Specialization**: Harsh environment deployment (volcanic sites) using both ground and airborne platforms
- **Key Systems Developed**:
  - miniGas payload (designed for UAV integration; commercialized by Intermet Systems)
  - UAV-MS-XPR3/MPH systems (small UAS volcanic plume characterization)

## NASA Experience
- Served as NASA contractor/subcontractor on Shuttle and Constellation Program projects
- Co-Investigator on multiple international atmospheric airborne campaigns:
  - Tropical Clouds Systems and Process (TCSP)
  - Tropical Composition, Cloud and Climate Coupling (TC4)
  - Aura Validation Experiment (AVE)
  - Ticosonde Project (tropical high-frequency radiosonde balloon launches)
  - LAVA GC/MS instrument development (RESOLVE Payload, Resource Prospector Lunar Mission)

## Credentials & Recognition

- Published in *International Journal of Mass Spectrometry* (IJMS) and *Journal of the American Society of Mass Spectrometry* (JASMS)
