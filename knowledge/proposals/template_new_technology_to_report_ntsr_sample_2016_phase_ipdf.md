## Notable Details

**Document Purpose:**
- Template for completing NASA's required New Technology Summary Report
- Outlines NASA's definition of "reportable items" and "subject inventions" for different business types
- Small businesses must disclose all patentable subject inventions
- Large businesses must disclose all reportable items (broader definition including non-patentable innovations)

**Key Submission Requirements:**
- Detailed disclosures due within 2 months of innovator's disclosure
- Interim NTSR due 12 months from effective date for multi-year efforts
- Final NTSR due within 3 months of completion
- Must be submitted through e-NTR website (www.invention.nasa.gov) before uploading to Electronic Handbook (EHB)
- Contact: NTTS Support Desk (757) 865-2233
