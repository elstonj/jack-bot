## Products & Capabilities Described

**E+E EE03 OEM Humidity/Temperature Module**
- **What it is:** A plug-in humidity and temperature sensor module with digital two-wire output protocol
- **Key specifications:**
  - Measuring range: RH 0–95% (0–100% with protective coating); Temperature –40 to 185°F (–40 to 85°C)
  - Accuracy: ±3% RH (10–100% range); ±0.54°F (±0.3°C)
  - Cable length up to 32.8 ft (10 m)
  - Embedded microprocessor with traceable calibration and temperature-corrected humidity output
  - Compact, interchangeable plug-in design
  - Price: starting at $96.00
- **Features:** Long-term stability, optional protective coating for salt air/harsh environments, simple 2-wire digital protocol for low-cost integration
