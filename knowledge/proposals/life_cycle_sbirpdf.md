## Executive Summary
This is a standard SBA SBIR/STTR Life Cycle Certification form completed by Black Swift Technologies for a Navy Phase II SBIR award on a magnetometer project. The document certifies BST's compliance with all SBIR program requirements regarding principal investigator time commitment, work performed percentages, domestic performance, and prevention of duplicate funding.

## Certification Requirements Confirmed
Black Swift Technologies certified compliance with the following (all boxes checked affirmatively):

1. **Principal Investigator/Project Manager Time Commitment**: PI/PM spent more than 50% time as BST employee (based on 40-hour work week)

2. **No Duplicate Funding**: Work has not been submitted for funding to other Federal agencies, or any funded portions have been disclosed and approved

3. **Work Performance Percentage**: For SBIR Phase II, BST will perform at least 50% of the research (as required)

4. **Work Completion Status**: Noted as "N/A because work is not completed" - indicating this is a mid-project or ongoing Phase II certification

5. **STTR Requirement**: Not applicable (this is SBIR, not STTR)

6. **Domestic Performance**: R&D is performed in the United States

7. **Facility and Employee Performance**: R&D is performed at BST's facilities with BST employees, as indicated in the SBIR application

8. **Notification Obligation**: BST confirmed it will notify the Navy immediately if any portion of funded work is subsequently funded by another Federal agency
