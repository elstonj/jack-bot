# Statement of Work - Creare/BST Spectrometer Integration

## Document Metadata
- Type: Statement of Work (SOW)
- Client/Agency: Creare
- Program/Solicitation: Not specified
- Date: October 10, 2023
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: S2
- Key Personnel: Dr. Jack Elston (CEO and Co-Founder, Primary Investigator), Dr. Maciej Stachura (CTO and Co-Founder, Senior Engineer)

## Executive Summary
BST proposes to provide consulting and engineering support to Creare for integrating a spectrometer sensor into the S2 platform. The work includes investigation of FAA approval requirements, sensor design guidance, and gimbal design requirements over a 6-month period.

## Technical Approach
The project encompasses four main technical workstreams:
1. **FAA Approval Investigation** (20 hours @ $250/hr): Assess regulatory requirements for integrating spectrometer sensor into S2
2. **Sensor Design Guidance** (30 hours @ $250/hr): Provide engineering guidance for sensor integration
3. **Gimbal Preliminary Design** (50 hours @ $350/hr): Design gimbal system to support spectrometer payload
4. **Meetings and Reporting** (20 hours @ $250/hr): Project coordination and documentation

## Products & Capabilities Described

### S2
- BST's small UAS platform proposed as the host vehicle for spectrometer integration
- Requires gimbal design and sensor integration work to accommodate spectrometer payload

### Spectrometer Sensor
- Third-party sensor being integrated by Creare
- Requires design guidance for integration into S2
- Requires gimbal support for proper operation
