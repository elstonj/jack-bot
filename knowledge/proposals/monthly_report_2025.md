# Monthly Report 2025

## Document Metadata
- Type: Monthly progress report
- Client/Agency: NASA
- Program/Solicitation: AREN (Atmospheric Research and Education Network) 2020-2021
- Date: Covers January-July 2025; document created 2024-12-16, modified 2025-07-21

- Key Personnel: Not named in document

## Executive Summary
This monthly report documents progress on the NASA AREN project from January through July 2025. Work focused on preparing for and executing educational workshops, assembling and distributing educational kits (minikites and Aeropod), equipment testing and comparison (anemometers and cameras), and project closeout activities.

## Technical Approach
The project involved:
- Assembly and distribution of educational hardware kits (minikite kits, Aeropod workshop materials)
- Hands-on workshop development and delivery
- Data analysis preparation using CODAP (Common Online Data Analysis Platform)
- Equipment evaluation (anemometer comparison and selection; camera testing for TerraROVER)
- Creation of instructional and operational documentation
- Website maintenance and content updates

## Products & Capabilities Described

**Minikite Kits**
- Educational flying kits assembled in batches (50 kits assembled and shipped in March)
- Part of broader AREN educational program

**Aeropod**
- Educational workshop platform
- Materials were boxed and shipped for workshop delivery (April, June, July)
- Associated workshop curriculum development occurred throughout project period

**TerraROVER Camera**
- Camera system requiring operational documentation
- Operation guide created in April 2025
- Underwent testing in February

**Anemometer Equipment**
- Multiple anemometer models tested and compared (April)
- Comparison report written; best-performing unit ordered

## Use Cases & Applications
- Educational workshops for learners (3-day summer workshop presented in July)
- Atmospheric data collection and analysis using CODAP
- Community outreach at CU Kite Day Event (April)
- Educational kit distribution to partners

## Key Results
- Completed 2025 annual report (July)
- Successfully delivered 3-day summer workshop with 20+ attendees (July)
- Shipped 50 minikite kits and multiple Aeropod workshop kits
- Selected and ordered optimal anemometer equipment based on comparative testing
- Created operational guides and instruction documentation for equipment
- Conducted follow-up sessions and analysis training with workshop participants
- Prepared project for closeout with inventory management and distribution

## Notable Details
- Project operates under AREN framework with regular coordination (bi-monthly production meetings, AREN group meetings, monthly Aerovator meetings)
- Strong emphasis on educational outreach and hands-on learning
- Partnership component with external organizations receiving shipped materials
- Website updates and digital content creation (CODAP analysis files, presentation materials)
- Project appears to be concluding after 2025 (references to "project wrap up," "close out," inventory disposal)
- Community engagement through university partnerships (CU Kite Day) and workshop evaluator feedback
- Focus on accessibility through published instructional materials and guided analysis examples
