**Environmental & Sustainability:**
- Sustainable products and services (Executive Order 14057)
- Ozone-depleting substances and refrigerant restrictions

**Payment & Records:**
- Electronic funds transfer via System for Award Management
- Privacy/security safeguards
- Comptroller General examination rights (3 years after final payment)
- Contractor records retention

**AGAR-Specific Clauses:**
- Contract closeout procedures with deobligation thresholds ($1,000 or less = unilateral; more than $1,000 = bilateral modification)
- Inspection and acceptance at USDA-ARS Hydrology and Remote Sensing Laboratory
- Delivery location specified as above facility
- Packing for domestic shipment per ICC/carrier regulations
- Marking of deliverables with contract number

### Evaluation Criteria
- Lowest Priced Technically Acceptable (LPTA) response meeting salient specifications
- Award to responsible offeror whose conforming offer is most advantageous to Government

### Key Compliance Requirements
- Offerors must search SAM.gov for FASCSA orders and excluded parties
- Reasonable inquiry required regarding covered articles/sources
- Quarterly SAM.gov reviews during contract performance
- 3-business-day reporting requirement if covered articles/prohibited sources identified
- 10-business-day follow-up reporting on mitigation actions
- Supply chain security attestations regarding telecommunications equipment
- Environmental and labor certifications

### Significant Terms
- Contract includes representations and certifications in SAM
- Small business program provisions (rerepresentation post-award, limited subcontracting requirements)
- Whistleblower protections for contractor employees
- Privacy training requirements
- Text messaging while driving policies
