## Notable Details
**This document is a template/guideline only.** It does not contain substantive information about BST proposals or capabilities. It serves as a reference for completing mandatory certifications for NASA SBIR Phase II proposals. Key certification requirements include:

- Small business ownership and control (13 CFR 121.702)
- Maximum 500 employees (including affiliates)
- No adverse SBA size determinations
- Verification of higher education ownership (if applicable)
- Disadvantaged/Women-owned/Veteran-owned business status declarations
- Federal tax compliance and criminal record certifications
- Covered telecommunications equipment restrictions (FAR 52.204-24, 52.204-26)
- SBA Company Registry registration with control ID
