## Executive Summary
This report documents technical and business assistance provided by EKase Consulting to Black Swift Technologies under Navy SBIR Phase I contract N6833525C0270. EKase conducted comprehensive primary and secondary market research to support commercialization of BST's "Expendable Air-Sea Profiling Observations" technology, including market trend analysis, customer identification across DoD/government/international/commercial sectors, competitor analysis, and strategic networking recommendations.

## Technical Approach
EKase Consulting employed a dual-phase research methodology:
- **Secondary Research:** Literature review of market research reports analyzing trends in UAS, Military UAVs, Drone Services, Weather Forecasting Systems, and Offshore Wind Energy; identification of competing products
- **Primary Research:** Identification and outreach to subject matter experts, potential customers, and prospective partners; validation of research findings through industry contact
- **Deliverables:** Comprehensive market research report with status reports submitted at regular intervals to Navy program contacts

## Products & Capabilities Described
**Expendable Air-Sea Profiling Observations Technology**
- BST system for collecting air-sea profile data in hazardous weather conditions
- Deployed via Small Uncrewed Aircraft System (sUAS)
- Commercial focus areas include weather forecasting, offshore wind energy site assessment, and military applications

## Use Cases & Applications
- **Military/DoD:** Battlespace awareness operations (Navy PMA-263), ISR-SOF (Air Force), Army program offices
- **Government Agencies:** U.S. Coast Guard, NOAA, NASA, USDA applications
- **International:** Meteorological agencies in Japan, Taiwan, South Korea, Singapore, and India
- **Commercial:** Weather data subscription services, offshore wind farm siting/assessment (DNV, Stantec clients)

## Key Results
Market research identified:
- **Global market trends** with financial projections across UAS, Military UAVs, Drone Services, Weather Forecasting Systems, and Offshore Wind Energy sectors
- **Specific customer contacts:**
  - Navy: Battlespace Awareness programs (PMA-263)
  - Air Force: AFLCMC ISR-SOF
  - Army: Multiple program offices
  - Other U.S. Government: USCG, NOAA, NASA, USDA
  - International: Meteorological agencies (Japan, Taiwan, South Korea, Singapore, India)
  - Commercial: Weather service providers and wind energy companies
- **Competitor Analysis:** Identified SBIR-competing companies including Dragoon Technology LLC and stoa tech, inc.
- **Promotion Strategy:** Recommendations for Commercial UAV Expo, American Meteorological Society Annual Meeting, and targeted LinkedIn groups for engagement
