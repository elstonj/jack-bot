## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies proposes to provide the S0 sUAS platform—a commercially available system specifically designed for tropical cyclone reconnaissance—along with comprehensive operational, technical, and sustainment support services under a five-year IDIQ contract for NOAA. The S0 is operationally deployed from the NOAA WP-3D Hurricane Hunter aircraft and has demonstrated reliable performance in hazardous, low-altitude, high-wind environments.

## Technical Approach

### Platform Design & Philosophy
- **Mission Focus:** Air-launched tropical cyclone (TC) reconnaissance
- **Aircraft Integration:** Optimized for tube-launch deployment from NOAA WP-3D aircraft
- **Maturity Level:** Meets NOAA Readiness Level 7/8; commercially available and operationally deployed
- **Risk Strategy:** Emphasizes operational readiness, repeatability, and low execution risk

### Core Performance Specifications

**Endurance & Range:**
- Minimum endurance: 100 minutes
- Communication range: >150 nautical miles (nm) in TC conditions
- Lightweight airframe design

**Data Collection Capabilities:**
- Highly accurate in situ atmospheric measurements: pressure, temperature, humidity, 2D winds
- Data quality meets or exceeds NRD41 dropsonde technology standards
- Real-time transmission of High-Density Observation Bulletin (HDOB) and IWG-formatted situational awareness data
- Long-range backhaul transmission to WP-3D aircraft

**Environmental Resilience:**
- Designed to withstand TC conditions
- Operates reliably in high winds, precipitation, and marine boundary layer conditions
- Demonstrated operational success rates exceeding 85% in relevant mission environments
