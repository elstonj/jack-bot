## Executive Summary
This is a standard compliance certification form required by USDA as part of a grant application, in which Black Swift Technologies LLC certifies its eligibility to receive federal funding by confirming the absence of felony convictions and tax delinquencies.

## Compliance Certifications

**Entity Status:**
- Black Swift Technologies LLC is a properly incorporated entity filed in one of the fifty U.S. states or territories.

**Felony Convictions:**
- BST has not been convicted of any felony criminal violation under Federal or State law in the 24 months preceding the application date.
- No officer or agent of BST has been convicted of a felony criminal violation for actions taken on behalf of BST under Federal or State law in the 24 months preceding the application date.

**Tax Liability:**
- BST does not have any unpaid Federal tax liability that has been assessed, for which all judicial and administrative remedies have been exhausted or have lapsed, and that is not being paid in a timely manner pursuant to an agreement with the responsible authority.
