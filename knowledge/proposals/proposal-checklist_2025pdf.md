# Proposal Checklist 2025

- **BST Products/Systems Referenced:** None explicitly mentioned (this is a generic checklist template)

## Executive Summary
This is an administrative compliance checklist template for Navy STTR Phase II proposals, revised August 2024. It covers all mandatory and conditional requirements for proposal submission, including special technical requirements, cost/accounting compliance, cybersecurity controls, and deliverables documentation. The checklist is designed to ensure offerors (including Black Swift Technologies) address all Navy and federal acquisition regulations before submitting proposals.

## Sections & Key Requirements

### I. General Information
- Requires specification of proposed total budget amount and Period of Performance (POP) for base and up to 7 option periods
- Requires identification of Government Fiscal Year (FY) funding profile (FY 27-31 shown in template)
- Requires contact information for authorized representative and key program contacts
- Proposals must be valid for minimum 180 days from submission date

### II. Special Requirements (Conditional Compliance Items)

**1. Aerial Vehicles**
- Must declare if proposal involves procurement or operation of manned or unmanned aerial vehicles
- If prime or subcontractor performs operations, must provide liability insurance (FAR 28.307-2(d))

**2. Test Driving Vehicles**
- Must declare vehicle testing requirements
- Must provide liability insurance if prime/subcontractor performs testing (FAR 28.307-2(c))

**3. Ammunition or Explosives**
- Must declare if using ammunition or explosives
- Requires preaward safety survey from DCMA and security survey from Defense Security Service (DSS) if prime/subcontractor conducting testing

**4. Active Acoustics**
- Must declare use of active sonar or acoustic transmissions
- Navy technical points of contact: Ray Soukup and Robert Headrick

**5. Animal Use**
- Requires IACUC approval, full/abbreviated appendix, approval protocol, and most recent USDA inspection report if applicable
- Contact: Ms. Suzanne May (Navy)

**6. Biosafety and Biosecurity**
- Must address biocontainment strategies, compliance with CDC Biosafety in Microbiological and Biomedical Laboratories (BMBL), and NIH Guidelines for Recombinant DNA

**7. Human Subject Research (HSR)**
- Requires IRB approval/exemption letter, approved protocol, consent forms, Federal Wide Assurance (FWA) documentation, and proof of ethics training
- Applies to research per 32 CFR 219, DODI 3216.02, or SECNAVINST 3900.39
- Contact: Ms. Suzanne May (Navy)

**8. Recombinant DNA/Synthetic Nucleic Acid Molecules**
- Requires Institution Biosafety Committee (IBC) approval, compliance statement with NIH Guidelines, and DHHS approval of IBC letter

**9a. Government Facilities/Test Sites**
- Must identify all government facilities to be used (prime and subcontractors)
- Must provide facility POC information (name, email, phone)

**9b. Government Furnished Items (GFI)**
- Must identify Government Furnished Equipment, Materials, and Information needed
- Requires government POC and award number for each item

**9c. Rent-Free Use of Government Property (GFP)**
- Must provide accountable contract identification, authorization, dates of use, and rent equivalent appraisal if using government property rent-free

**10. Assertion of Data Rights**
- If restricting use/release/disclosure of deliverable data or software, must provide assertions per DFARS 252.227-7013, -7014, or -7018
- Government may challenge improperly formatted assertions or those not acknowledging prior federal funding

**11. DD 254 – Security Classification Specification**
- Must declare if access to classified information/facilities required
- Must provide prime contractor and subcontractor security details (CAGE code, security code, security office address)
- Must declare current DSS Facility Clearance status and clearance level if applicable

**12. Reports and Deliverables**
- Must provide detailed list of all reports, hardware, prototypes, and software deliverables
- Required standard deliverables: Monthly Technical and Financial Progress Reports, Final Report
- For software: must specify if deliverable is in source and/or object form
- Must identify delivery date, place, and recipient for each deliverable

**13. Location of Work**
- Must specify full address and percentage (%) of work performed by prime and each subcontractor/consultant
- Total percentages must equal 100%

**14. Government Fiscal Year Funding Profile**
- Must break down proposed budget by fiscal year (FY 27-31) for base and each option period

### III. Cost Content

**1-2. System for Award Management (SAM) & Representations and Certifications**
- Offeror must be registered and active in SAM (https://www.sam.gov)
- Must complete Online Representations and Certifications in SAM (FAR and DFARS clauses)
- Must include ONR Specific Representations and Certifications

**3. Subcontracting Plans**
- Required if proposed award (including options) exceeds $750,000
- Required for universities, non-profits, and large for-profit entities
- NOT required for small business concerns or contracts performed entirely outside U.S. and outlying territories
- Types: Comprehensive Plan, Master Plan with Goals, or Individual Plan
- Reference: FAR 52.219-9
- Contact: Ellen Simonoff (Navy)

**4-5. DCAA and DCMA Compliance**
- Must provide cognizant DCAA/DCMA contact information (name, address, POC, phone, email)
- Must declare if organization has DCAA/DCMA approved/recommended accounting system
- If approved: provide audit report number and date
- If no approved system: complete Pre-award Survey of Prospective Contractor Accounting System Checklist
- Must declare if organization has Forward Pricing Rate Agreement (FPRA) or Forward Pricing Rate Recommendation (FPRR)
- Must declare if other DCAA/DCMA audits performed by other agencies
- First-time government contractors should review DCAA "Information for Contractors" pamphlet and FAR Part 31.205
- May reference HHS Rate Agreement if applicable

**6. Purchasing System**
- Must declare if approved Purchasing System exists (FAR 44.3) per Administrative Contracting Officer determination
- If yes: provide audit report number and date

**7. Estimating System**
- Must declare if approved Estimating System exists (DFARS 215.407-5-70) per Administrative Contracting Officer determination
- If yes: provide audit report number and date

**8. Prior Awards**
- Must list most recent ONR award(s) received in last 5 years (award number, program officer, program officer code)

**9. Property System**
- Must declare if acceptable Property Management System exists (DFARS 245.105) per ACO determination
- If no approved system: must provide Property Management Plan (PMP) description using voluntary consensus standards or industry best practices

**10. Cost Proposal Spreadsheet**
- Must complete cost proposal spreadsheet per BAA instructions

**11. Fundamental Research Determination**
- Must declare if subcontractor performing fundamental research (basic/applied research with published results)
- If yes: both prime and subcontractor must provide determinations that work has been scoped, negotiated, and determined to be fundamental research

### IV. Safeguarding Covered Defense Information and Cyber Incident Reporting

**DFARS 252.204-7012 Compliance**
- Must implement security requirements for Covered Defense Information and Cyber Incident Reporting
- Must implement CUI Security Requirements and Relevant Security Controls per NIST Special Publication 800-171 (Rev. 1)
- Must provide list of unimplemented items and implementation timeline if not fully compliant

**NIST SP 800-171 Assessment**
- Must declare if implementation has been assessed by DoD
- If assessed: must identify assessment level (basic, medium, high)

**System Security Plan (SSP)**
- Must have SSP or other documentation addressing cybersecurity on internal unclassified information systems
- If approved by Navy or DoD: must identify approving activity
- Affected performers required to provide SSP for government review and approval within 30 days of contract award (per ONR BAA Appendix 2, F.2(i)(9))
