## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies conducted three separate flight test sessions with the Penguin BE aircraft at Brush Airport, Colorado in November 2017. The flights included pilot training with dummy payload, operational mapping missions, and takeoff/landing practice, with mixed results including one hard landing that caused nose gear and wing damage.
