## Executive Summary
This document presents Black Swift Technologies' indirect cost rate model for fiscal year 2018, establishing the company's fringe benefit, overhead, and G&A rates required for SBIR Phase II cost proposals. The document contains detailed cost accounting worksheets breaking down labor, materials, facilities, and administrative expenses across multiple cost pools.

## Technical Approach
The rate model follows standard federal indirect cost accounting methodology:
1. Trial balance of all expense accounts categorized into cost pools (Direct, Fringe, Overhead, G&A, IR&D/B&P, Unallowable)
2. Calculation of fringe benefit rate as percentage of total labor
3. Allocation of fringe benefits to all cost pools
4. Determination of overhead rate based on direct labor and fringe
5. Determination of G&A rate based on total cost input
