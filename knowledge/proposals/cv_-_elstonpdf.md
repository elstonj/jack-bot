## Executive Summary
Jack Elston is the founder and owner of Black Swift Technologies, established in August 2011. He holds advanced degrees in electrical engineering and aerospace engineering from University of Colorado, with extensive research background in unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) for severe weather sampling, particularly tornadic supercells. He combines academic research experience with practical UAS development and has published extensively on UAS guidance, control, and atmospheric sensing applications.

## Educational Background
- B.S. Electrical Engineering, University of Colorado (2003)
- M.S. Electrical Engineering, University of Colorado (2005)
- M.S. Aerospace Engineering, University of Colorado (2007)
- Ph.D. Aerospace Engineering, University of Colorado (2011)

## Professional Experience
