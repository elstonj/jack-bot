## Executive Summary
This document provides instructions to Black Swift Technologies for preparing letters of support to accompany their AFWERX SBIR Phase II proposal on soil moisture mapping. Letters of support validate market need and commercial viability of the proposed technology.

## Purpose of Letters of Support
Letters demonstrate that the proposed innovation addresses valid market needs and validate commercial impact claims. They serve as signals to evaluators that genuine demand exists (commercial, defense, or government) for the solution, helping justify government R&D investment.

## Recommended Structure for Letters
1. **Signer Identification**: Signer explains who they are and their role/organization
2. **Need Validation**: Validate the need for the solution within their organization/industry
3. **Interest Expression**: Express significant interest in SBIR research outcomes and intentions to purchase and use the end product

## Submission Requirements
- Format: Single .pdf file
- Upload location: Volume 5 in DSIP (submission system)

## Important Clarifications
- Letters of support are **not binding contracts** or commitments of funds
- They are purely signals of market demand to justify government investment
- Appropriate signers include: potential technology end-users (customers), corporate partners/collaborators, and actual or potential investors
