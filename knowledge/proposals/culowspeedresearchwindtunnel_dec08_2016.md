## Technical Specifications

### Facility: CU Low-Speed Research Wind Tunnel
- **Type**: Open-return Wenham (Blower) type low-speed tunnel
- **Test Section**: 0.8 m square × 3.5 m long
- **Power**: 100 HP motor
- **Maximum Velocity**: 68 m/s (Mach 0.2) empty test section
- **Flow Quality**: Freestream uniformity <0.5%, turbulence intensity <0.5%
- **Key Feature**: Modular, reconfigurable test section with significant unobstructed optical access for non-invasive measurements

## Available Instrumentation

- **Pressure Measurement**: Multiple Scanivalve DSA 3217 static pressure scanners
- **Hot-Wire Anemometry**: A.A. Lab Systems AN-1003 five-channel constant-temperature anemometer system with miniature (5 mm diameter) hot-wire probes
- **Flow Visualization**: LaVision Stereoscopic Particle Image Velocimetry (SPIV) System
- **Data Acquisition**: National Instruments hardware with LabVIEW software control
- **Auxiliary Equipment**: Power supplies, power amplifiers, function generators
