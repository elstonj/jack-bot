## BST's DEI Strategy (as outlined in template)

### Education & Workforce Development
- Promotes training for undergraduate, graduate, and early-career engineers
- Sponsors engineers to attend conferences and flight events for professional development and skill-building
- Engages early-career interns to create webinars and technical/marketing content on social media (YouTube, Instagram)
- Aims to lower barriers to advanced learning concepts for early-career professionals

### Hiring & Staffing Approach
- Intentionally broadens staff diversity through hiring choices
- Prioritizes early-career engineers and those without PhDs
- Views diverse hiring as mutually beneficial—brings new energy/ideas to development and lowers barriers disproportionately affecting underrepresented backgrounds
- Advertises positions in locations where minority engineers are likely to learn of opportunities
- Template notes tracking of early-career and historically underrepresented personnel in proposals

### Partnerships
- References "longstanding partnerships" with partner institutions to support education and workforce development
- Partner institutions (to be named in specific proposals) are committed to inclusive, equitable, and accessible work environments
