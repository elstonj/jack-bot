## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies and By Light partner to respond to an ONR solicitation for three prototype UAV platforms plus 12 fully mission-integrated UAVs with emphasis on cost efficiency, long endurance (20-25 hours), and payload flexibility. The proposal was due August 8, 2025, with a compressed one-week development timeline coordinated across distributed teams.

## Technical Approach

**Solution Scope:**
- Three prototype UAV platforms
- 12 fully mission-integrated UAVs
- Ground control station and communications suite
- Emphasis on cost efficiency, long endurance (20–25 hrs), and payload flexibility

**Key Design Constraints:**
- Must be fuel-powered (electric, fuel cell, and solar ruled out for range/budget)
- Efficient, glider-style airframe with integrated fuel capacity in wings
- No VTOL; launcher deployment assumed (similar to Mustang platform)
- Design and cost optimization critical

**Technical Development:** Maciej Stachura led early technical concepting and design evaluation, coordinating with Jeff Adelman and Bryan Sparling on requirements and design options.

## Products & Capabilities Described

**Mustang Platform (Reference):**
- Existing fuel-powered UAV used as design baseline/comparison

**Proposed Solution Platforms:**
