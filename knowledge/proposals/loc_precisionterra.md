## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies commits to serve as a subcontractor to PrecisionTerra on a NASA SBIR Phase II project, providing UAS flight operations, platform provision, payload integration, and data collection services. BST will support technology validation in relevant environments including wildfire-prone areas.

## Technical Approach
BST proposes to:
- Manage complete flight coordination, planning, and regulatory approvals for testing in target environments
- Provide appropriate UAS platforms (quadcopter, fixed-wing, or VTOL configuration to be determined by technical monitor)
- Integrate PrecisionTerra's equipment (software-defined radios, IMUs) onto BST aircraft
- Support high-rate data logging at 1000 Hz (millisecond) collection rates for performance analysis
- Provision configured U-Blox receivers if needed to meet data collection requirements

## Products & Capabilities Described
**UAS Platforms:**
- Flexible platform options: quadcopter, fixed-wing, and VTOL configurations
- Capability to carry and integrate third-party payloads
- Support for high-frequency data logging infrastructure

## Use Cases & Applications
- Technology validation in wildfire-prone areas
- Testing in relevant operational environments (unspecified beyond wildfire context)
- Support for continued development and validation of PrecisionTerra technology
