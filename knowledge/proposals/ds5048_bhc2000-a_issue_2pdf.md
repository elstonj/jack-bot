## Executive Summary
The BHC2000-A is a 2-meter three-axis Helmholtz coil system manufactured by Bartington Instruments for calibration of magnetic field sensors and creation of controlled magnetic environments. Available in 1, 2, or 3-axis configurations, it uses bifilar winding technology and is designed for testing satellites, submersed assemblies, and other magnetic sensors requiring known magnetic field conditions.

## Product Description

### BHC2000-A Overview
- **Purpose**: Calibration of three-axis magnetic field sensors and controlled magnetic environment generation
- **Design**: Three-axis Helmholtz coils with 2-meter coil diameter (effective)
- **Key Technology**: Bifilar windings providing two identical circuits per coil with independent connecting terminals
- **Delivery**: Flat-pack assembly to facilitate shipping and installation
- **Total Weight**: 235kg (~38kg per coil)

### Configuration Options
Available in four variants:
- **1A-A**: Single axis (Y)
- **2A-A**: Two axes (X, Y)
- **2B-A**: Two axes (Y, Z)
- **3-A**: Three axes (X, Y, Z)

## Technical Specifications

### Performance Characteristics
| Parameter | Specification |
|-----------|---------------|
| Maximum DC field (single axis, standard) | 1.0 mT |
| Maximum DC field (with PA1 Power Amplifier) | 300 μT |
| Maximum frequency (with PA1) | 50 Hz |
| Orthogonality Error | <0.2° (when adjusted with PA1) |
| Isolation Voltage | 100V |
| Homogeneous volume (<1% error) | 480mm cube |
| Homogeneous volume (<5% error) | 925mm cube |

### Mechanical Specifications
- **Coil Effective Diameter**: 
  - X-axis: 2057mm ±4mm
  - Y-axis: 1941mm ±4mm
  - Z-axis: 1998mm ±4mm
- **Overall Dimensions**: 2113mm H × 2043mm W × 2086mm D
- **Coil Maximum Aperture**: 1068mm × 1045mm
- **Number of Turns**: 31+31 (X/Z axes), 30+30 (Y-axis)

### Electrical Specifications
**Field/Current Ratio (Parallel Windings)**:
- X-axis: 24.8 μT/A ±1%
- Y-axis: 25.3 μT/A ±1%
- Z-axis: 25.1 μT/A ±1%

**Field/Current Ratio (Series Windings)**:
- X-axis: 49.5 μT/A ±1%
- Y-axis: 50.6 μT/A ±1%
- Z-axis: 50.1 μT/A ±1%

**DC Resistance per Axis**:
- Parallel: 0.68–0.76Ω ±5% (varies by axis)
- Series: 1.36–1.52Ω ±5% (varies by axis)

**Inductance & Current**:
- Maximum coil inductance per axis: 12.1–14.1 mH ±5%
- Maximum current (parallel windings): 40A
- Maximum current (series windings): 20A
- Coil former field ratio: 0.82 µT/A ±3%

### Environmental
- **Operating Temperature**: +15 to +30°C
- **Storage Temperature**: +10 to +40°C
- **Compliance**: RoHS
- **Connector Type**: M4 brass terminals
