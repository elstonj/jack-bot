## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies completed installation of UHIP on TerraROVER, performed extensive AeroPod manufacturing and assembly work, and shipped multiple configured AeroPods to NASA partners. The team also conducted equipment maintenance, resupply management, and participated in AREN program meetings during March 2021.

- Multi-sensor aerial platform capable of various sensor configurations

  - Gitup2 Monocam (camera payload)

  - PocketLab sensor versions
  - Wind Vector Probe configuration
  - Betsy Stevenson albedo instrument configuration
- Can be equipped with specialized scientific instruments and shipped to remote locations
- Includes carabiner attachment systems

- Compatible with UHIP instrument package
