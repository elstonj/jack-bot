## Executive Summary
This budget justification supports a DOE Genesis proposal for "AI-Enabled Mobile Autonomous Laboratories for Real-Time Adaptive Experimentation in Dynamic Environmental Systems." The project is led by Dr. Jack Elston and proposes to develop and validate autonomous systems for environmental monitoring and experimentation, utilizing three Black Swift S0-VTOL unmanned aircraft systems over a 9-month period.
