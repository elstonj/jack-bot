## Executive Summary
This is a blank submission template provided by DARPA for the ERIS program, not a completed Black Swift Technologies proposal. It outlines the required structure and sections BST would need to complete when submitting a formal proposal response to this solicitation.

## Template Structure Required
The template mandates the following proposal components:

### 1. Company Introduction Section
- One-chart company overview to introduce BST to DARPA and DoD
- Should position company relative to customer expectations

### 2. "White Space" Positioning Chart
- Graphical representation showing two figures of merit (e.g., cost vs. performance)
- Should illustrate past state, current state, and enabled future capability
- Demonstrates revolutionary capability through low cost & high performance positioning

### 3. Problem Statement & Goals Section
- **Problem**: Simple 1-2 sentence statement
- **Breakthrough**: Key advance or insight being leveraged (1-2 sentences)
- **Objective**: Goal of the effort (1-2 sentences)
- **Impact**: What becomes possible that wasn't before (1-2 sentences)
- **Effort Description**: What will be done and produced to demonstrate feasibility (1-2 sentences)
- **Task Items**: 2-3 statement of work bullets suitable for contract incorporation
