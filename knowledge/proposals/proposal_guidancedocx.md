## Products & Capabilities Described

### Drone Sonde System
- **What it is:** Lightweight, low-cost VTOL UAS with autonomous atmospheric profiling capability
- **Specifications:** Profiles atmosphere from surface to 25,000 feet; includes base station for autonomous recovery and recharging
- **Funding:** NOAA Contract No. 1305M218CNRMW0034
- **Contact:** Vincent Garcia (NOAA), 301-628-1011

### Quad-Biplane
- **What it is:** VTOL system with variable-pitch control and biplane wing configuration
- **Applications:** Shipboard deployment, atmospheric measurement
- **Specifications:** Payload capacity over 15 lbs; designed for safe ship deck landing
- **Funding:** NOAA Contract No. WC133R17CN0079
- **Advantages:** Compact, improved flight performance, engineered for shipboard recovery without high-risk tactics

### Navigation System for UAVs
- **What it is:** Small, low-cost, low-power navigation suite for autonomous flight
- **Capabilities:** Vision-aided navigation, adaptive terrain mapping, vision-based obstacle avoidance, bio-inspired flight control algorithms
- **Applications:** Military theater operations, FAA-compliant commercial UAV operations
- **Key Functions:** Obstacle avoidance, dynamic mapping, automated takeoff/landing
- **Funding:** DARPA-funded, partnership with Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University
- **Contract:** Dept. of Interior Contract No. D16PC00110; TPOC: Thomas Bramhall
