## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies submitted a budget proposal to NOAA's OMAO UxS Operations Center for a three-year effort to improve NOAA forecasts by advancing UAS (uncrewed aerial systems) capabilities to sample tropical cyclones from the ocean surface to the stratosphere. The proposal is staffed by Jack Elston as PI and Maciej Stachura as engineering support.
