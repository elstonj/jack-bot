## Executive Summary
BST proposed to ruggedize and modify an existing Soil Moisture Mapping sUAS (developed under NASA funding) to meet Army mission requirements for soil integrity assessment. The system would provide both soil moisture and soil strength data through sensing and modeling algorithms, enabling Army personnel to plan vehicle paths based on soil integrity maps.

## Technical Approach
- Leverage existing Soil Moisture Mapping sUAS as the data source platform
- Ruggedize and modify the aircraft, ground station, and data interfaces to meet Army specifications and standards
- Implement and test software algorithms for soil integrity modeling
- Generate soil integrity maps to support vehicle path planning
- Design interfaces and data formats compliant with Army standards
- Partner with strategic company experienced in Army acquisition programs

## Products & Capabilities Described

### Soil Moisture Mapping sUAS
