## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies proposes SwiftFire, an innovation for detecting and managing wildfires on forest lands under USDA SBIR Topic 8.1. The 8-month Phase I project ($181,500 budget) aims to develop technology that addresses the technical problem of wildfire detection and management, with commercial applications in forest fire prevention and firefighting operations.
