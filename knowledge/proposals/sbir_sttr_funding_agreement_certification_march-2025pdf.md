## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies, LLC completed and certified compliance with SBA SBIR/STTR eligibility requirements for a Navy Phase II SBIR award in the magnetometer program. The certification confirms BST meets small business status, ownership/control requirements, employee thresholds, and performance obligations for the funded research and development project.

## Certification Compliance Checklist
All required boxes were checked (X) affirming the following key certifications:

1. **Business Structure**: BST is structured as a Limited Liability Company (LLC)
   - Not a corporation (Item 2: N/A)
   - Not a partnership (Item 3: N/A)
   
2. **Ownership & Control**: 
   - Meets SBA requirements per 13 CFR 121.702
   - Not majority-owned by venture capital, private equity, or hedge funds (Item 13: No)
   - Not a Covered Small Business Concern under new VC/PE acquisition rules (Item 14: No)

3. **Size Standards**:
   - Confirmed ≤500 employees including affiliates (Item 6)
   - No adverse SBA size determination currently in effect (Item 7)

4. **Performance Requirements**:
   - Principal Investigator/Project Manager will spend >50% time as BST employee (Item 8)
   - BST will perform ≥50% of the research (Phase II SBIR requirement) (Item 10)
   - R&D will be performed in the United States (Item 11)
   - R&D will be performed at BST facilities with BST employees (Item 12)

5. **Compliance Commitments**:
   - No essentially equivalent work submitted elsewhere or funded by another federal agency (Item 9)
   - Will immediately notify Navy if work is subsequently funded by another federal agency (Item 15)
   - Understands information may be shared with federal, state, local agencies (Item 17)
