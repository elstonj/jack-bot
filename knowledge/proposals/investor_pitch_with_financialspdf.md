## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies presented an investor pitch in February 2015 seeking AI-ESCR grant funding to accelerate growth and market entry. The pitch demonstrated BST's core UAS technologies and product portfolio across autopilots, sensor systems, and applications ranging from soil moisture mapping to atmospheric sampling, projecting significant revenue growth and job creation with grant support.

## Technical Approach
BST's core technical capabilities include:
- **Autopilots and ground control components** with intuitive user interfaces
- **Sensor integration** with custom algorithms
- **Small aircraft platforms** optimized for specific capabilities
- **EO/IR Stabilized Gimbal System** (actively under development at pitch time):
  - FLIR Tau 2 LWIR thermal imaging camera
  - Compatible with fixed-wing and multi-rotor platforms (specifically DJI Phantom)
  - Project timeline: Kickoff November 2014, Beta units shipped February 2015, 4-month program duration
  - Objective: Advance stabilization and control beyond low-cost commercial systems
