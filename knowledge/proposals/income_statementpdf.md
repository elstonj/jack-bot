## Executive Summary
This document contains four income/profit and loss statements spanning 2012 through early 2015, showing BST's financial performance during its early growth phase. The company demonstrated significant revenue growth from $38K (2012) to $347K (2014), with net income improving from $780 (2012) to $113.5K (2014), driven primarily by billable expenses and product sales.
