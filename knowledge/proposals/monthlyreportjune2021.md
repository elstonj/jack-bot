# Monthly Progress Report - June 2021

- Client/Agency: NASA
- Program/Solicitation: AREN (Autonomous Robotics for Exploration Needs) 2020-2021
- Date: June 2021 (reporting period); submitted July 8, 2021

## Executive Summary
This monthly report documents production and assembly activities for the AREN program during June 2021, focusing on manufacturing updates to the Swiftflow Aeropod, preparation for a Summer Institute event, and calibration/shipping of Monocams equipped with Pocketlab Weather sensors.

## Production Activities

### Swiftflow Aeropod
- Ordered materials for design updates
- Printed additional parts for fulfilling Aeropod orders
- Addressed 3D printer maintenance issues (extruder clogging)
- Tested new print temperatures and settings with new filament

### TerraROVER
- Printed and cleaned extra parts as part of production inventory

### Monocams
- Printed, cleaned, and assembled 32 Monocams with Pocketlab Weather attachment (Summer Institute preparation)
- Printed, calibrated, assembled, and shipped 2 Monocams to Brent Yannis (external shipment)

### Pocketlab Weather Profilers
- Charged all units for Summer Institute deployment

## Summer Institute Preparation
