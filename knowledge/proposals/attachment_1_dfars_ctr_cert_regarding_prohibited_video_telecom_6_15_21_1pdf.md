# DFARS Contractor Certification Regarding Prohibited Video Telecom Services and Equipment

## Document Metadata
- **Type:** Contractor Certification / Compliance Document
- **Client/Agency:** Department of Defense (DoD) – Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) Program
- **Program/Solicitation:** Navy SBIR Phase I – Magnetometer
- **Date:** January 21, 2025
- **BST Products/Systems Referenced:** None (compliance document only)
- **Key Personnel:** Dr. Jack Elston (President/Authorized Signatory)

## Executive Summary
This is a mandatory DoD compliance certification required for SBIR contract proposals. Black Swift Technologies certifies that it does not provide covered defense telecommunications equipment or services (as defined under DFARS 252.204-7016/7017/7018) as part of its offered products or services to the Government. The document confirms BST's compliance with the National Defense Authorization Act Section 1656 prohibition on using telecommunications equipment from Huawei, ZTE, or entities controlled by China or Russia.

## Certification Details

### Key Representations Made by BST
- **Does NOT provide** covered defense telecommunications equipment or services as part of offered products/services
- **Will NOT provide** covered defense telecommunications equipment or services in performance of any award resulting from this solicitation
- No disclosure of covered telecommunications equipment required (since BST represents non-provision)

### Covered Defense Telecommunications Definition
The certification defines prohibited equipment/services as:
1. Telecommunications equipment from Huawei Technologies Company or ZTE Corporation (or subsidiaries/affiliates)
2. Telecommunications services provided by those entities or using their equipment
3. Equipment/services from entities owned/controlled by, or connected to, governments of China or Russian Federation

### Covered Missions Referenced
The prohibition applies to:
- Nuclear deterrence mission of DoD (nuclear command, control, communications, integrated tactical warning, continuity of Government)
- Homeland defense mission of DoD (ballistic missile defense)
