## Technical Specifications
- **Sensor Type:** Miniature laser rangefinder (LW20 variant)
- **Range:** >100m in sunlit conditions; capable of near-zero distance measurement
- **Form Factor:** Very small, suitable for small drones and self-driving vehicles
- **Enclosure Rating:** IP67 (water splash and dust resistant)
- **Interface Options:** I2C or Serial outputs
- **Measurement Rate:** Up to 678 readings per second
- **Output Data:** Provides distances to both first and last return signals per measurement
- **Cost:** $329.00 USD
- **Manufacturer:** LightWare Optoelectronics

## Key Features
- Encoded laser beam allowing multiple units to target same object without interference
- Enables multiple redundancy and safe operation of multiple UAVs in same airspace
- Sealed, rugged design suitable for field operations
- Professional-grade performance in compact package
