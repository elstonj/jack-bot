## Executive Summary
This is a product reference sheet from LTI detailing their TruSense laser sensor product line, including distance/level measurement sensors (S-series), distance sensors (S200/S210/S230), fluid level sensors (S300/S310/S330), a versatile Universal Laser Sensor, and traffic detection sensors (T-series). The document was included as a reference in a Black Swift Technologies NOAA SBIR proposal, likely for sensor integration or comparison purposes.

## Products & Capabilities Described

### Distance/General Purpose Sensors (S-Series: 200, 210, 230)

**TruSense S200** (Part No. 7005785)
- Non-contact distance measurement sensor
- User-configurable target modes: first, last, strongest, and advanced multi-target modes
- RS232 serial output with trigger output capability
- Small, economical design with long range and high accuracy
- Performs under temperature variations, background noise, and low-dielectric materials
- No frequent calibration required
- OEM version available without housing (Part No. 7005910)

**TruSense S210** (Part No. 7006740)
- Same capabilities as S200 plus visible alignment laser pointer
- RS232 and trigger output
- Ideal for applications requiring alignment laser
- OEM version available (Part No. 7006741)

**TruSense S230** (Part No. 7006750)
- Features 4-20mA analog output with HART capability
- Visible alignment laser pointer
- Dual communication: 4-20mA and RS232 serial
- User-configurable target modes
- Long range, high accuracy
- OEM version available (Part No. 7006751) without housing

### Fluid Level Sensors (S-Series: 300, 310, 330)

**TruSense S300** (Part No. 7006800)
- Specialized for non-contact fluid level measurement
- Accurate measurements on highly reflective surfaces
- Can be shot through protective screens and near flat walls
- Reflection stabilization for reliable results
- User-configurable target modes: first, last, strongest, multi-target
- RS232 and trigger output
- Highly repeatable, accurate over long ranges
- Little to no required calibration
- OEM version available (Part No. 7006801)

**TruSense S310** (Part No. 7006805)
- Fluid level measurement with visible alignment laser
- RS232 and trigger output
- Same advanced capabilities as S300
- Ideal for applications requiring laser pointer
- OEM version available (Part No. 7006806)

**TruSense S330** (Part No. 7006815)
- Fluid level measurement with 4-20mA output and HART capability
- Visible alignment laser pointer
- Both analog (4-20mA) and serial (RS232) communication
- Advanced measurement modes
- OEM version available (Part No. 7006816)

### Universal/Industrial Sensor

**Universal Laser Sensor** (Part No. 7005400)
- Highly versatile industrial sensor with four measurement modes
- User-configurable pulse and update rates up to 4500 Hz
- Networking capability: up to 64 addressable units
- Extensive measurement selections including output power and update rate control
- Long range capability and high accuracy
- Designed for rugged and adverse ambient conditions
- Includes interface package: User's Manual, configuration cable, interface software
- OEM version available without housing (Part No. 7005395) for system integration with weight/cost savings

### Traffic Sensors (T-Series)

**TruSense T100** (Part No. 7005880)
- Identifies difficult targets: liquids, non-reflective materials, fast-moving objects
- Non-contact, non-intrusive
- Recognizes small targets at long distances, narrow openings, sharp angles
- Performs under temperature variations, vapor pressure, background noise
- Ideal for traffic management applications, event triggering, data capture

**Speed System Kit** (Part No. 7035125)
- Combines T100 and T200 sensors with optics and spacer bar
- Counts, profiles, and classifies vehicles
- Measures vehicle height, length, and speed
- Calculates time and distance between vehicles
- Supports red-light system and license plate integration

## Common Features Across Product Line

- **Non-contact measurement**: No physical contact required
- **Calibration-free or low-calibration**: Minimal maintenance
- **Target flexibility**: User-configurable modes (first, last, strongest, multi-target)
- **Environmental robustness**: Perform under temperature variations, background noise, diverse material properties
- **Output options**: Various configurations available (RS232, trigger outputs, 4-20mA analog, HART)
- **OEM variants**: Most products available without housing for system integration
- **Laser pointers**: Optional visible alignment lasers for targeting
- **Long range performance**: Designed for distance measurement over significant ranges
