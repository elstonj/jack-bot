- Date: February 16, 2019 (signed)
- Submitted by: Jack Steward Elston, CEO
- Company: Black Swift Technologies LLC

## Executive Summary
This is a completed Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) funding agreement certification form submitted by Black Swift Technologies LLC to NOAA for a Phase II award. The document certifies BST's eligibility as a small business and compliance with SBIR program requirements regarding ownership, control, employee count, principal investigator time commitment, work performance location, and funding status.

## Key Certifications Affirmed (All Checked "Yes" or Applicable)
1. Meets ownership and control requirements per 13 C.F.R. §121.702
2. LLC structure (articles of organization and operating agreement) meets requirements
3. Key personnel are U.S. citizens or permanent resident aliens
4. Has no more than 500 employees (including affiliates)
5. SBA has not issued an adverse size determination
6. Principal investigator will spend >50% time as BST employee
7. Work has not been submitted for funding by another Federal agency
8. Will perform at least 50% of SBIR Phase II research (Phase II requirement)
9. Research/R&D will be performed in the United States
10. Research will be performed at BST facilities with BST employees
11. Not majority-owned by venture capital/hedge fund/private equity firms
12. Not a "Covered Small Business Concern"
13. Will immediately notify if work is subsequently funded elsewhere
14. Understands information may be shared with Federal, State, and local agencies
15. Authorized officer confirms all submitted information is true and correct
