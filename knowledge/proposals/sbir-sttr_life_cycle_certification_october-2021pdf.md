## Executive Summary
This is a standard federal compliance certification form required of all SBIR/STTR Phase I and Phase II awardees at specified intervals during their funding agreements. It certifies the awardee's compliance with program requirements regarding personnel commitment, work equivalency, research percentage performance, location, and facility usage.

## Document Purpose
This is a boilerplate certification document issued by the Small Business Administration (SBA) to be completed by awardees, not a Black Swift Technologies-specific proposal or report. The document itself contains no BST-specific information.

## Certification Requirements Outlined
The form requires awardees to certify compliance with eight key areas:

1. **Principal Investigator/Project Manager commitment:** >50% time as employee
2. **Essentially Equivalent Work:** Disclosure that work has not been duplicatively funded
3. **Work performance requirements (upon completion):**
   - SBIR Phase I: ≥66.67% of research
   - SBIR Phase II: ≥50% of research
   - STTR Phase I or II: ≥40% of research
4. **Completion verification:** Confirmation work is completed and percentage thresholds met
5. **[STTR only] Management control:** Small Business Concern exercises management direction
6. **U.S. performance:** R&D performed in United States unless waived
7. **Facility/personnel requirement:** Work performed at awardee's facilities with awardee's employees
8. **Notification obligation:** Awardee commits to notify agency if work is subsequently funded elsewhere
