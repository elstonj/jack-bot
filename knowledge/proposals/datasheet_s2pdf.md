## Executive Summary
The Black Swift S2 is a purpose-built, hand-launchable scientific research UAS designed for demanding atmospheric environments including high-altitude, corrosive particulates, and strong turbulence. It offers larger payload capacity, longer endurance, higher ceiling, and greater range than competing vector wing airframes while maintaining terrain-following autonomous flight capabilities.

## Technical Approach
The S2 is engineered as a rugged, durable platform specifically hardened for harsh field deployment. Key design features include:
- Enclosed nose cone payload system for clean, uncontaminated atmospheric measurements
- Modular field-swappable payload interface (common power, data, mechanical connections; no specialized tools required)
- Proprietary flight planning UI enabling rapid mission programming (minutes from setup to data collection)
- SwiftTab Android tablet-based mission monitoring and mapping software with gesture-based controls
- Autonomous launch, flight, and landing capability in mountainous/difficult terrain

## Products & Capabilities Described

### S2 UAS
- **What it is:** Purpose-built aerial platform for scientific payloads in demanding atmospheric environments
- **Key specifications:**
  - Max gross takeoff weight: 9.5 kg (20.8 lbs)
  - Wingspan: 3.0 m (10.0 ft)
  - Payload capacity: 2.3 kg (5 lbs)
  - Flight ceiling: 6,000 m (20,000 ft)
  - Max endurance: 110 min; nominal: 90 min
  - Max range: 110 km (60 nm); nominal: 92 km (50 nm)
  - Cruise speed: 18 m/s (35 kts); stall speed: 12 m/s (24 kts)
  - Max winds endured: 15 m/s (30 kts)
  - IP rating: IP42
  - Geotagging position accuracy: <4 m in all directions
  - Telemetry data rate: 9,500 bps serial stream
- **Payload system:**
  - Nose cone: 20.3 cm (8 in) diameter × 63.2 cm (24.9 in) length
  - Power available: 50 W total
  - Field-swappable modular design

### SwiftTab Software

- Proprietary system enabling rapid mission programming in minutes
