## Executive Summary
This document presents biographical summaries of two key Black Swift Technologies leaders—CEO Dr. Jack Elston and CTO Dr. Maciej Stachura—highlighting their extensive expertise in unmanned aircraft systems for atmospheric sampling, severe weather research, and autonomous flight operations. Both researchers bring decades of combined experience in in-situ measurement campaigns, multi-UAS coordination, and specialized UAS design for hazardous environments.
