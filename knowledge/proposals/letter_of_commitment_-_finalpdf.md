# Letter of Commitment - STTR Phase II

## Document Metadata
- Type: Letter of Commitment
- Client/Agency: U.S. Navy (STTR Program)
- Program/Solicitation: STTR Phase II - Hazardous Weather
- Date: February 10, 2026
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: Small Uncrewed Aircraft System (for air-sea profiling)
- Key Personnel: Jack Elston (Principal Investigator, Black Swift Technologies); Dr. Josh Wadler (ERAU contact); Nanette Guzman (ERAU Executive Director)

## Executive Summary
Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University (ERAU) formally commits to supporting Black Swift Technologies' STTR Phase II project on expendable air-sea profiling observations in hazardous weather conditions using small uncrewed aircraft systems. ERAU will contribute $333,502 in total cost (base and option years) including salary, fringe benefits, and indirect costs.

## Technical Approach
The project proposes to develop expendable air-sea profiling observation capabilities using small uncrewed aircraft systems for operations in hazardous weather conditions. Specific technical details are not provided in this commitment letter.

## Products & Capabilities Described
- **Small Uncrewed Aircraft System**: Proposed for collecting air-sea profiling observations in hazardous weather environments

## Use Cases & Applications
- Air-sea profiling observations in hazardous weather conditions (likely hurricane, extreme storm, or severe maritime environmental monitoring)
