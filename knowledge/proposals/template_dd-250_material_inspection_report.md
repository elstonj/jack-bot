## Document Summary
This is a standard government Material Inspection and Receiving Report (DD-250 form) documenting the shipment and acceptance of hardware deliverables from Black Swift Technologies to NASA. The form confirms receipt of an S2 UAS system with associated payloads and communication systems for the volcano monitoring project.
