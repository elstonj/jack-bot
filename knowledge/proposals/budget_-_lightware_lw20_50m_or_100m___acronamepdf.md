## Products & Capabilities Described

**LightWare LW20/C Laser Rangefinder**
- Small form factor, sealed unit designed for water splash and dust resistance
- Range options: 50m (LW20/B variant) or 100m (LW20/C variant)
- Measurement speed: up to 678 readings per second
- Provides distances to both first and last signals with each measurement
- Serial or I2C communications ports
- Power saving mode capability
- Laser beam encoded to allow multiple units to operate simultaneously without interference
- Price: $329.00 for 100m variant
- Class 1M or Class 1 eye-safe laser certification

**Scanning LiDAR Capability**
- Can be integrated with small digital servo (e.g., Corona DS-919MG) to create scanning LiDAR
- Capable of capturing 360 points per second while scanning
- Can stream data or make decisions based on preset alarms
- Designed for sense-and-avoid and navigation applications
