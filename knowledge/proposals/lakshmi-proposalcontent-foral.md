## Executive Summary
This document presents the credentials and proposed expertise of Dr. Venkat Lakshmi for an Army project focused on determining terrain trafficability through remote sensing of soil moisture. Lakshmi would contribute soil moisture retrieval and land-atmosphere interaction expertise to map spatial heterogeneity of soil conditions at meter-to-kilometer scales, enabling real-time assessment of whether terrain can support vehicle movement.

## Technical Approach
**Soil Moisture Retrieval & Trafficability Mapping:**
- Use L and P band radiometer and P band radar sensors to retrieve soil moisture data
- Map spatial heterogeneity of soil moisture at scales determined by sensor altitude (15m to 1km range)
- Apply soil moisture data to determine vehicle trafficability for both:
  - **Off-road movement**: Calculate soil strength (function of soil type + soil moisture) against specific vehicle loading (pounds per square inch)
  - **Pavement-supported movement**: Assess whether underlying soil moisture allows pavement to support vehicular forces without collapse

**Key Technical Insight:**
Soil moisture spatial heterogeneity is critical but cannot be determined by conventional satellite remote sensing due to:
- Low spatial resolution (kilometers vs. meters needed)
- Slow temporal repeat (2x daily vs. faster variations needed)

Airborne/UAV sensors overcome both limitations.

## Products & Capabilities Described

**L and P Band Radiometer**
- Retrieves soil moisture from microwave emissions
- Provides spatial resolution determined by altitude
- Can map trafficability-relevant soil moisture variations

**P Band Radar**
- Extension/complement to radiometer approach
- Further soil moisture characterization capability

## Use Cases & Applications

**Terrain Trafficability Assessment for Military Operations:**
- Off-road vehicle movement in inhospitable terrain
- Areas around rivers, wetlands, and coastal regions
- Regions susceptible to thunderstorms
- Regions with absent in-situ soil moisture measurements
- Real-time terrain capability assessment to inform force mobility decisions
