## Executive Summary
BST is participating in NASA's AEROKATS and ROVER Education Network as a "Science Activation" project to promote learning and outreach. BST will support educational activities focused on using kites for environmental exploration, develop workshop materials, and evaluate the commercial feasibility of AeroPod and Aerovator products. All work is budgeted at $117,701.50 for 2025.

## Technical Approach
BST's support includes:
- Planning, writing, and design of educational materials
- Wood and plastic part fabrication
- Prototype development and evaluation
- Material distribution to educational institutions and partners
- All work performed remotely from Denver/Boulder, CO and Onancock, VA (no NASA facility access required)

## Products & Capabilities Described
- **AeroPod**: Commercial product under feasibility evaluation
- **Aerovator**: Commercial product under feasibility evaluation
- Both products appear to involve kite-based systems for environmental exploration/data collection

## Use Cases & Applications
- Educational outreach and science activation
- Kite-based environmental exploration and learning
- Workshop development for educational institutions
