## Executive Summary
This document qualifies the key personnel who would support Black Swift Technologies' proposed involvement in the DARPA Albatross program. It highlights the credentials of two co-founders and technical leaders (Dr. Jack Elston and Dr. Maciej Stachura) with extensive backgrounds in UAS operations in hazardous conditions, atmospheric sampling, and flight management systems, along with four additional engineering and software staff members who would contribute to the project.

## Key Personnel Profiles

**Background:**
- PhD from University of Colorado at Boulder
- Dissertation work: developed meshed network UAS, complex control algorithms for in-situ sampling of tornadic supercell thunderstorms
- Extensive experience conducting in-situ atmospheric measurements from UAS in hazardous conditions

**Technical Leadership:**
- Technical lead on all avionics work at BST
