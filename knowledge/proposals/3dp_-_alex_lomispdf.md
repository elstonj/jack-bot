## Executive Summary
This is a JawsTec 3D printing quote for 27 manufactured parts totaling $2,698.80 with a 24-business-day lead time. The parts represent structural and mechanical components for what appears to be an AeroPod aircraft platform, including fuselage sections, tail surfaces, motor mounts, sensor housings, and battery bracket components.

## Technical Approach
Parts were specified for two primary manufacturing processes:
- **MJF (Multi Jet Fusion) in Nylon 12**: Most structural brackets, mounts, and housings (23 parts) - Dark Purple color, raw finish
- **SLS (Selective Laser Sintering) in Nylon PA2200**: Larger structural elements and sensor housings (3 parts) - White color, raw finish
- **SLA (Stereolithography) in Transparent Plus**: One precision optical component (1 part)
- **SLM (Selective Laser Melting) in Aluminum**: One thermal management component (1 part)
