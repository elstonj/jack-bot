## Executive Summary
This is a DOE-mandated disclosure and certification form template that entities (prime recipients and subrecipients) must complete as part of DOE's foreign connections and national security due diligence. The form requires comprehensive disclosure of foreign ownership, affiliations, malign talent recruitment program participation, technology transfers, and security-sensitive equipment sourcing.

## Document Type & Purpose
This is a **blank template and instructional document** provided by DOE for applicants/awardees to use when submitting proposals or receiving awards. It is not a proposal, report, or project-specific document. Rather, it is a regulatory compliance requirement form that Black Swift Technologies (or any entity receiving DOE funding) would need to complete and submit.

## Key Disclosure Requirements

### Section 1: Identifying Information
Basic entity information: legal name, UEI, website, addresses, points of contact, project title, and proposal/award numbers.

### Section 2: Foreign Connections Disclosure
Entities must disclose:

1. **Malign Foreign Talent Recruitment Program (MFTRP) Participation**: Whether any principal investigators, project managers, or business owners participate in MFTRPs.

2. **Ownership Structure**: 
   - Complete capitalization table with equity interests
   - Citizenship of natural person owners
   - Entity charts showing parent/subsidiary relationships
   - Screening against Bureau of Industry and Security Entity List, DoD 1260H List, and Treasury OFAC lists

3. **Foreign Ownership**: Whether entity is wholly/partially owned by foreign countries of concern or foreign state-owned entities

4. **Foreign Business Relationships**: 
   - Parent companies, joint ventures, subsidiaries in foreign countries of concern
   - Contractual or financial obligations with foreign entities
   - Venture capital/institutional investment with foreign affiliations

5. **Technology Transfers**: IP licensing or technology sales to foreign countries of concern in the past 5 years, particularly in same technology area as project

6. **Foreign Business Entities**: Any related entities outside the US

7. **Board Composition**: Complete director and board observer information including citizenship

8. **Foreign Indebtedness**: Any loans or financial obligations to foreign entities

9. **Security-Sensitive Equipment** (FASC-prohibited UAS, foreign-coded equipment, internet-connected foreign equipment, equipment with foreign access)

10. **Battery Energy Storage Systems (BESS)**: If applicable, detailed supply chain disclosure of vendors, inverters, battery management systems, battery packs, and battery cells

## Definitions (Appendix A)

**Foreign Country of Concern**: People's Republic of China, Democratic People's Republic of Korea, Russian Federation, Islamic Republic of Iran, or other countries designated by Secretary of State as countries of concern or risk.

**Malign Foreign Talent Recruitment Program**: Programs offering compensation (cash, research funding, honorific titles, career advancement) in exchange for:
- Unauthorized transfer of IP or nonpublic information to foreign governments/entities
- Recruitment of trainees/researchers
- Establishing foreign labs/companies or accepting foreign positions in violation of award terms
- Inability to terminate the foreign agreement except in extraordinary circumstances
- Conflict of interest or commitment contrary to award terms
- Non-disclosure requirements or omission of US institution/agency acknowledgment

## Certifications (Section 3)

Authorized officials must certify:
- Information is true, complete, and accurate
- Covered individuals have been informed of MFTRP prohibitions
- Covered individuals have certified non-participation in MFTRPs
- Understanding of criminal/civil penalties for false statements
- Commitment to notify DOE within 5 business days if MFTRP participation discovered during award period
- Ongoing obligation to update disclosures during award performance

## Notable Details

1. **Entity Type Exemptions**: DOE National Laboratories and domestic government entities exempt; higher education institutions only required to respond to items marked with asterisk (*)

2. **Confidentiality**: Sensitive commercial, financial, or trade secret information can be marked confidential per Appendix B procedures

3. **Ongoing Responsibility**: Entities must update disclosures if circumstances change during award period

4. **Filing Convention**: Documents must be saved as "BusinessSensitive_AwardNumber_EntityName(Prime or Sub)_TFC"

5. **Contact**: Questions directed to rtesinfo@hq.doe.gov
