## Executive Summary
This video narration describes BST's development of an atmospheric sounding UAS built on the S0 platform, combining custom sensors and wind estimation algorithms to provide timely atmospheric data for mission-critical decision-making in a compact, inexpensive, and fully automated system.

## Technical Approach
- **Foundation**: Air-deployed S0 platform
- **Sensor Integration**: Custom sensor suite for atmospheric sampling
- **Algorithms**: Cutting-edge wind estimation algorithms
- **Data Standards**: Standardized data product formats designed for quick assimilation into atmospheric models
- **Operational Modes**: Fully automatic operation with capability for complex sampling patterns and advanced mission profiles

## Products & Capabilities Described

### S0 Platform

### Atmospheric Sounding System
- Purpose-built for atmospheric sampling
