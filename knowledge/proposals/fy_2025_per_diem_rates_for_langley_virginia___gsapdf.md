## Document Contents
This document provides federal travel reimbursement rates for Langley, Virginia area and is used for budgeting purposes in the associated Air Force proposal (AF25D-T008).

### Lodging Rates (Daily, excluding taxes)
- **Standard Rate**: $110/night (applies October 2024 - September 2025)
- **Williamsburg/York Counties**: $110-$134/night (seasonal variation: higher rates April-August at $134)

### Meals & Incidental Expenses (M&IE)
- **Standard Rate**: $68/day total ($16 breakfast, $19 lunch, $28 dinner, $5 incidentals); 75% rate ($51) for first/last travel day
- **Williamsburg/York**: $80/day total ($20 breakfast, $22 lunch, $33 dinner, $5 incidentals); 75% rate ($60) for first/last travel day
