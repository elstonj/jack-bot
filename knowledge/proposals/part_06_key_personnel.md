## Personnel Summary

### Dr. Jack S. Elston - Principal Investigator
**Role:** CEO and co-founder of BST; PI for Phase II SBIR

**Background:**
- Ph.D. from University of Colorado Boulder in unmanned aircraft systems and complex meshed networks for atmospheric sampling
- Developed the Tempest sUAS, which conducted the first-ever intercept of a tornadic supercell thunderstorm (VORTEX2 project, NSF/NOAA funded)
- Recipient of NSF Atmospheric and Geospace Sciences Postdoctoral Research Fellowship for improved thermodynamic sampling of severe storms with sUAS

**Expertise:**
- In-situ atmospheric measurements from small unmanned aircraft systems (sUAS)
- Operations in hazardous conditions
- Wind estimation and energy harvesting for sUAS
- Multi-vehicle cooperative flight operations

**At BST:**
- Technical lead on all avionics work
- Creator of the SwiftCore Flight Management System autopilot
- Direct involvement in development of four different unmanned aircraft systems
- Authored/participated in over 100 COA (Certificate of Authorization) applications
- Conducted hundreds of flight experiments including Arctic deployments

**Phase II Role:** Full-time employment; lead engineer for payload integration, electronic design, firmware development, and overall project management

### Dr. Jorge Andres Diaz - Senior Personnel
**Affiliation:** Chief Technical Officer, NTCR Consulting S.A.

**Background:**
- Ph.D. from University of Minnesota
- NASA contractor/subcontractor on Shuttle, Constellation Program projects

**Key Expertise:**
- Design, integration, and deployment of portable MS systems for in-situ gas analysis at volcanic sites
- Both ground and airborne platform experience

- Recent involvement: LAVA GC/MS instrument development for RESOLVE Payload (Resource Prospector Lunar Mission)
- Developed miniGas payload for UAV integration (now commercialized by Intermet Systems)
- Co-developed UAV-MS-XPR3/MPH systems for small UAS volcanic plume characterization

**Recognition:**
- Multiple patents and awards
- International Gold Medal from World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO)
- National Scientific Award
- Research featured on cover pages of International Journal of Mass Spectrometry (IJMS) and Journal of the American Society of Mass Spectrometry (JASMS)
