## Notable Details
- Deliverables are submitted through the Navy SBIR Program portal at https://www.navysbirprogram.com/navydeliverables/
- Process involves logging in, selecting contract records from dropdown, entering titles and descriptions, uploading files, and confirming correct file submission
- Documents appear to follow naming convention: N000414C1234FinalReport.pdf (contract number + document type)
- Submissions are displayed in descending order by submit date
- Technical support contact: Carol Jenkins (Carol.A.Jenkins.ctr@navy.mil)
- Content-related questions should go to technical point of contact in contract
