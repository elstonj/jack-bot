## Executive Summary
This is a standard Small Business Technology Transfer (STTR) Program eligibility and compliance certification completed by Black Swift Technologies LLC in October 2020 for a Navy Phase I STTR award focused on hazardous weather applications. The document certifies that BST meets all required eligibility criteria for small business STTR funding.

## Company Structure & Eligibility
- **Business Type**: Limited Liability Company (LLC)
- **Ownership & Control**: Certified to meet requirements in 13 CFR 121.702
- **Employee Count**: Confirmed ≤500 employees (including affiliates)
- **No Size Protests**: SBA has not issued size determinations finding BST exceeds 500-employee standard
- **Citizenship**: Key individuals verified as U.S. citizens or permanent resident aliens
- **Venture Capital Ownership**: BST is NOT majority-owned by venture capital operating companies, hedge funds, or private equity firms

## STTR-Specific Compliance
- **Research Performer**: BST (Small Business Concern) exercises management direction and control of the STTR award
- **Work Percentage**: BST will perform at least 40% of research/R&D (STTR Phase I requirement)
- **Principal Investigator/Project Manager**: Will spend >50% of time (based on 40-hour workweek) as employee of BST or Research Institution
- **Geographic Requirement**: R&D will be performed in the United States (waiver has been granted)
- **Facility Requirement**: R&D performed at BST's facilities with BST employees
