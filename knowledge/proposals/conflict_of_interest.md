## Executive Summary
This is a blank USDA conflict of interest disclosure template used for grant applications. It was created as part of Black Swift Technologies' 2018 USDA proposal submission materials and serves as a standard compliance form requiring disclosure of potential conflicts.

## Document Content
This is a **template document with no substantive content**—it contains only instructions and a blank form structure.

### Instructions Summary
The form requires the Project Director/Principal Investigator (PD/PI) to list alphabetically (last name first):
- All co-authors on publications within past 3 years (including pending/submissions)
- All collaborators on projects within past 3 years (current and planned)
- All thesis advisees/advisors and postdoctoral advisees/advisors
- All persons in the field with consulting/financial arrangements or other conflicts within past 3 years

### Form Structure
- Name field (to be filled by applicant)
- Table with columns:
  - Name
  - Co-Author (mark with X)
  - Collaborator (mark with X)
  - Advisees/Advisors (mark with X)
  - Other – Specify Nature

## Notable Details
- Form includes note that individuals in applicant's field are **not** in conflict of interest unless they fall within one of the specified categories
- Instructions specify form must be converted to PDF before attachment to electronic application
- Additional pages permitted as necessary
