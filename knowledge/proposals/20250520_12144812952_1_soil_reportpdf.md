## Use Context for BST's Soil Moisture Mapping Program
This soil report was generated as baseline reference data for BST's AFWERX SBIR Phase II soil moisture mapping testing at Boulder Reservoir on May 19, 2025. The two soil units represent contrasting drainage characteristics:

- **LoB (Longmont clay)**: Poorly drained with shallow water table (24-30 inches), higher salinity, and moderate water retention—relevant for soil moisture detection
- **SeE (Samsil-Shingle complex)**: Well-drained with deep water table (>80 inches), shallow bedrock, and very low water retention—contrasting soil moisture response

These characteristics provide diverse soil moisture conditions useful for validating BST's soil moisture mapping sensor and processing algorithms.
