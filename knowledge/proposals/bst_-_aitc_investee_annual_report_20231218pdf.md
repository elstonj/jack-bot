## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies LLC is a Boulder, Colorado-based company engaged in R&D for methane detection using specialized sensors flown on unmanned aerial vehicles (UAVs). As of the 2023 reporting period, BST had 10 full-time equivalent employees (all Colorado-based), generated $1.784 million in revenue year-to-date, and has been generating revenue since 2012. The company has received no third-party equity or debt investment to date.
