## Executive Summary
This document provides a standardized checklist and submission requirements for Phase I proposals under NASA's FY2025 SBIR solicitation. It outlines the required forms, documentation, and compliance items that small businesses must include when submitting Phase I proposals to NASA.

## Content Overview
This is a submission requirements document, not a technical proposal or report. It contains:

**Key Requirements for Phase I Proposals:**
- Submission limited to one subtopic per proposal package
- Maximum funding: $150,000 for technical effort
- Technical and Business Assistance (TABA) funding cap: $6,500 (if applicable)
- Project duration maximum: 6 months
- Technical proposal limited to 15 pages (8.5" x 11", 1-inch margins)

**Required Submission Components:**
- Proposal Contact Information
- Proposal Certifications and Summary
- Proposal Budget with letters of commitment
- Technical Proposal (9 required parts per section 3.1.3.5)
- Briefing Chart
- NASA Evaluation License Application (if proposing NASA technology/TAV)
- I-Corps Interest Form
- Small Business Concern (SBC) level forms and certifications
- Audit information and Prior Awards Addendum
- Commercialization Metrics Report (CMR)
- Disclosure of Foreign Affiliations
- Supporting documentation of budgeted costs

**Critical Deadline:** Submission acknowledgement must be received by 5:00 PM ET on March 10, 2025

## Notable Details
This document references the NASA SBIR/STTR Resources website for form previews and additional guidance. It emphasizes compliance with detailed format and content requirements outlined in Chapter 3 of the solicitation document.
