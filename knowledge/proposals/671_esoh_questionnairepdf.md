## Document Purpose
This is a blank or template ESOH (Environment, Safety and Occupational Health) questionnaire submitted as a supporting document for Black Swift Technologies' 2019 SBIR Phase II proposal on Atmospheric Sounding. The form was completed and submitted to the U.S. Air Force as part of the proposal package.

## Content Summary
This is a standard DoD SBIR/STTR compliance questionnaire designed to identify environmental, safety, and occupational health hazards or special requirements associated with proposed research. The questionnaire includes 10 questions covering:

1. Hazardous materials use (per Federal Standard 313D and 40 CFR)
2. Explosives or ammunition use
3. Hazardous processes (heavy equipment, lasers, RF radiation, high voltage, high pressure/temperature)
4. Work location on USAF installations
5. Chemical/hazardous material delivery to USAF facilities
6. Hazardous waste generation
7. Class I ozone-depleting substances (ODSs)
8. Radioactive materials
9. Asbestos, radiation, or chemical-generating components for USAF delivery
10. Special atmospheric or water resource requirements
