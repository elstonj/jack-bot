## Executive Summary
JPL contracted Black Swift Technologies to integrate a P band reflectometry instrument onto a UAS and conduct operational flights at a test site near Fraser, Colorado from February through June 2018. BST was responsible for payload integration, local validation flights, operational sorties, data delivery, and hardware support.

## Technical Approach
- Integrate P band reflectometry instrument into UAS airframe
- Conduct local validation flights to confirm full payload functionality with JPL sign-off
- Deploy to Fraser, Colorado site for field operations
- Execute 2-4 operational days per month with up to two flights per day
- Reserve additional days for weather contingency flights
- Develop and deliver custom nose cone for radiometer instrument integration

## Products & Capabilities Described

**UAS Platform**
- BST-supplied aircraft (model not specified in this SOW)
- Capable of carrying P band reflectometry payload
- Equipped for high-altitude/remote site operations in Colorado

**P Band Reflectometry Instrument**
- Payload to be integrated into UAS
- Requires custom nose cone integration
- Delivery of nose cone to JPL post-campaign for future use

## Use Cases & Applications
- P band reflectometry science campaign near Fraser, Colorado
- Ground moisture/soil property measurement (implied by reflectometry instrument)
- Extended field campaign with monthly operational windows

- Ideal operational window: February through June 2018
