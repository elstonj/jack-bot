## Executive Summary
This is an administrative guidance document for NASA SBIR applicants explaining how to complete the Prior Awards Addendum form. It outlines requirements for reporting prior Phase I and II SBIR/STTR awards and clarifies data entry procedures for both NASA and other government agency awards.

## Notable Details
- **Purpose**: The Prior Awards Addendum captures prior Phase I and II award history for small businesses applying to NASA SBIR
- **Threshold**: Required if a firm has received more than 15 SBIR or STTR Phase II awards in the prior five fiscal years; recommended for all firms regardless
- **Data Retention**: NASA retains prior award information across solicitation cycles to reduce future data entry burden
- **Required Information for Non-NASA Awards**: Agency, program type (SBIR/STTR), phase, solicitation year, funding agreement/contract number, award date, project title, and topic/subtopic
- **NASA Awards**: Automatically populated by the system
- **Administrator**: Only the designated Firm Admin (typically the first person to register the company) can update this form
