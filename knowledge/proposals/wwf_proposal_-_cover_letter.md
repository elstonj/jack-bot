## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies proposes to provide affordable, reliable, turn-key small unmanned aircraft systems (sUAS) solutions for wildlife conservation efforts to combat illegal poaching and support conservation activities in developing regions. BST brings together a partnership combining their own expertise with Conservation Drones and prior experience deploying systems for Kenya Wildlife Services.

## Technical Approach
- Development and deployment of SwiftSentinel sUAS platform
- Systems designed to require minimal training for field operators
- Turn-key solutions emphasizing reliability and affordability
- Focus on ease of deployment in remote conservation settings

## Products & Capabilities Described

### SwiftSentinel sUAS
- Affordable, reliable small unmanned aircraft system
- Designed for conservationists with minimal training requirements
- Successfully tested with Kenya Wildlife Services
- Turn-key deployment model
- Developed through partnership between Black Swift Technologies, Rocketship Systems Inc., and Autonomous Systems Research (since 2012)

## Use Cases & Applications
- Curbing illegal wildlife poaching
- General wildlife conservation activities
- Operations in Africa (Kenya), Asia (Nepal, Cambodia, Indonesia), and other regions
- Support for local conservation groups operating in remote areas
