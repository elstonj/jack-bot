## Products & Capabilities Described

### KDE4215XF-465 Brushless Motor
- **Price:** $148.95
- **Application:** Heavy-lift electric multi-rotor sUAS
- **Key Features:**
  - High-temperature solid-core copper windings (240°C continuous / 340°C maximum)
  - Japanese 0.2 mm silicon-steel stator laminations
  - Kevlar tie-wraps for vibration reduction
  - Triple-bearing support (ABEC-7 bearings)
  - Laser-etched serialization on new production
  - Active-centrifugal fan for cooling
  - 200°C silicone-wire leads (15 AWG, 760mm)
  - 3.5mm 24K Bullet Connectors (pre-soldered)
