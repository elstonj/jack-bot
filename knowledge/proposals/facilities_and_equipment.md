## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies operates a state-of-the-art facility in Boulder, Colorado equipped for UAS development, testing, and deployment. The facility supports environmental monitoring applications including wildfire detection, hurricane observation, and atmospheric profiling, backed by proven operational experience and strategic partnerships with NOAA and academic institutions.

## Facilities & Infrastructure
- **Location**: Boulder, Colorado
- **Key Components**:
  - Dedicated UAS integration lab
  - Specialized workspaces for sensor calibration and payload development
  - Software and hardware resources for real-time data processing
  - Mission planning capabilities
  - System prototyping facilities
  - Equipment for testing under heavy precipitation conditions

## Capabilities & Operational Experience
- **Hurricane Operations**: Deployed BST S0 UAS during 2023 and 2024 hurricane seasons with demonstrated success
- **Environmental Monitoring**: Wildfire detection, hurricane observation, atmospheric profiling
- **Rapid Iteration**: UAS design integration area enables efficient adaptation to project requirements
- **Weather Testing**: Specialized equipment validates system performance in heavy precipitation conditions

## Strategic Partnerships
- **NOAA**: Direct integration of expertise and datasets; access to facilities and resources for testing and data validation through funded projects
- **Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University**: Access to wind tunnel facilities for characterizing and improving wind measurement system precision and accuracy
