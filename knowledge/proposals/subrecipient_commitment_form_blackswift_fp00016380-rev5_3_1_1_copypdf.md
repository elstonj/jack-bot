## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies is entering into a subaward agreement with the University of Miami to serve as a subrecipient on a NOAA-funded project aimed at improving tropical cyclone forecasting through advancing Uncrewed Aerial System (UAS) capabilities. The project runs from October 1, 2025 to September 30, 2028, with BST receiving $327,952 in funding and providing no cost sharing.

## Project Details
**Proposal Title:** Improving NOAA Forecasts by Advancing the Capabilities of UAS to Sample Tropical Cyclones

**Period of Performance:** October 1, 2025 – September 30, 2028

**Funding Amount:** $327,952 (no cost sharing from BST)
