## Notable Details

**Regulatory Framework Covered:**
- **AECA (22 USC §2778)**: Arms Export Control Act; implements ITAR (International Traffic in Arms Regulations, 22 CFR §120-130) and USML (United States Munitions List, 22 CFR §121). Jurisdiction: Department of State (DDTC).
- **ECR (50 USC §4801-4851)**: Export Control Reform; implements EAR (Export Administration Regulations, 15 CFR §730-774) and CCL (Commerce Control List, 15 CFR §774). Jurisdiction: Department of Commerce (BIS).

**Distribution Statements (A-F):**
- Statement A: Approved for public release, unlimited distribution
- Statements B-F: Increasingly restrictive, from U.S. Government agencies only to controlled distribution under specific authority

**Key Penalties:**
- **AECA Criminal**: Up to $1,000,000 fine and/or 20 years imprisonment per violation
- **ECR Criminal**: Up to $1,000,000 fine and/or 20 years imprisonment
- **ECR Civil**: Up to $300,000 or 2x transaction value (whichever greater), license revocation, export prohibition

**Transfer Controls:**
- Apply to tangible and intangible transfers (emails, faxes, verbal communications)
- Apply to all forms and methods of transfer
- No legal distinction between tangible/intangible
- Applies to transfer of controlled technology to foreign persons regardless of location
