## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies is subcontracted to work with PI Jack Elston on advancing UAS capabilities to improve NOAA forecasts by sampling tropical cyclones across altitude ranges from the ocean surface to the stratosphere. The three-year effort is budgeted at $327,952 total, with BST providing personnel, systems engineering, platform design, and sensor integration work.

## Technical Approach
The project involves design, development, and implementation of modifications to UAS platforms for tropical cyclone sampling. Key technical responsibilities include:
- Design and development of platform modifications (Dr. Elston and Dr. Stachura)
- Systems engineering and controls work
- Sensor hardware integration
- Algorithm generation
- Software/hardware interface development
- Platform systems integration and validation

## Products & Capabilities Described
**UAS Platforms for Tropical Cyclone Research:**
- Proposed capability: Sample tropical cyclones from ocean surface to stratosphere
- Application: Improving NOAA weather forecasts
- Technical work: Design modifications to enable enhanced sampling capabilities across vertical profile
