## Notable Details

**Regulatory Framework Covered:**
- Arms Export Control Act (AECA, 22 USC §2778) and International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR, 22 CFR §120-130)
- Export Control Reform (ECR, 50 USC §4801-4851) and Export Administration Regulations (EAR, 15 CFR §730-774)
- United States Munitions List (USML, 22 CFR §121) and Commerce Control List (CCL, 15 CFR §774)

**Distribution Statements (A-F):**
- Statement A: Unlimited public release
- Statements B-F: Progressively restrictive distribution (U.S. Government agencies only, through DoD Components only)

**Penalties:**
- AECA violations: Up to $1,000,000 fine and/or 20 years imprisonment
- ECR violations: Criminal penalties up to $1,000,000 fine and/or 20 years imprisonment; civil penalties up to $300,000 or twice the transaction value (whichever is greater), license revocation, or export prohibition

**Key Compliance Points:**
- Export controls apply to tangible and intangible transfers (emails, faxes, conversations)
- Providing controlled technology to foreign nationals—regardless of location—is deemed equivalent to physical export
- Transferees must certify understanding and compliance with export control regulations
- Registrations with Department of State may be required for USML-controlled items
