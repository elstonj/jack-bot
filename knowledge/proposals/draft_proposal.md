## Executive Summary
BST proposes a phased approach to monitor oil and gas wells for CrowHeart Security, beginning with validation flights in Colorado, followed by ramping production surveys of ~100 wells per week across 412 area and nearby locations. The engagement spans 4 phases from validation through capability expansion, with pricing structured to transition from high-touch development ($100/survey initially) to production rates ($50/well surveyed).

## Technical Approach
- **Phase 0 (Validation)**: 1-2 week mutual agreement on survey requirements (resolution, imagery type, features to identify), followed by 1-3 pilot flights over Colorado target areas to validate approach before contract execution
- **Phase 1 (Ramp-up)**: Build and equip 2 dedicated S1 drones; high-touch development with on-site BST engineers (Jack/Maciej); iterative refinement of flight patterns, obstacle avoidance, landing sites; collaborative data analysis; local technician hiring from Laramie/Rawlins/CSU area; ramp to 100 surveys/week within 2 weeks
- **Phase 2 (Production)**: Transition to 3 surveys per week (100 wells per survey) with as-needed support; standardized data delivery per Phase 1 specifications
- **Phase 3 & 4**: Scale operations and expand UAS-enabled services (methane monitoring, other remote sensing)
