## Executive Summary
This is a standardized CU Boulder subrecipient commitment form completed by Black Swift Technologies as a subcontractor on a University of Colorado Boulder research proposal. The form documents BST's certifications, representations, compliance status, and financial/administrative qualifications required for federal funding eligibility.

## Document Purpose & Content
This is a compliance and administrative checklist form rather than a technical or substantive proposal document. It requires the subrecipient (BST) to confirm:

### Representations & Certifications Covered
1. **Overhead & Fringe Rates** - Federal F&A and fringe benefit rates
2. **Small Business Status** - Declaration of business classification
3. **Cost Sharing** - Any matching funds or in-kind contributions
4. **Human Subjects Research** - IRB approval status if applicable
5. **Animal Subjects Research** - IACUC approval status if applicable
6. **Conflict of Interest** - COI policy compliance
7. **Debarment & Suspension** - Federal eligibility certification
8. **Fiscal Responsibility** - Financial system adequacy and audit compliance
9. **Drug-Free Workplace** - Certification of compliance with 41 USC 702
10. **Lobbying Restrictions** - Compliance with 31 USC §1352 (if funding >$100,000)
11. **SAM.GOV Registration** - System for Award Management status
12. **FAPIIS/Responsibility Matters** - Federal Awardee Performance and Integrity System compliance
13. **Audit Status** - Single Audit Act compliance and audit findings disclosure

## Notable Details
- Form requires signature by BST's Authorized Official
- Requires disclosure of parent entity ownership/control if applicable
- Requires attachment of supporting documentation (F&A rate agreements, audit reports, IRB approvals, etc.)
- This is a **blank template form** in the provided text; specific BST responses/certifications are not filled in within this document
- Project context: Crested Butte SMM (SPLASH) project at CU Boulder

## Limitations
This document contains no technical information about BST's products, capabilities, or proposed work. It is purely an administrative/compliance form. The actual technical proposal, budget, and scope of work for the SPLASH project would be contained in separate documents.
