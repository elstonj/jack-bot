## Key Compliance Areas Outlined

The tutorial covers potential FWA risk areas including:
- **Eligibility Verification:** Small business status (51%+ ownership, <500 employees), adequate facilities, PI primary employment
- **Multiple Agency Payments:** Disclosure of overlapping or related federal funding
- **Recordkeeping:** Timesheets, invoices, receipts, lab notebooks, facility agreements, project status documentation
- **Work Performance Requirements:** 
  - SBIR: ≥2/3 work by SBC in Phase I, ≥1/2 in Phase II
  - STTR: ≥40% by SBC, ≥30% by single Research Institution in both phases
- **PI Employment Requirements:** SBIR requires PI primary employment (≥51%) with SBC; STTR allows PI employment with either firm or Research Institution
- **Cost Allowability:** Distinguishes legitimate direct/indirect costs from personal expenses
- **Technical Proposal Integrity:** Prohibition on plagiarism, fabricated letters of support, falsified content

## Consequences of FWA Violations

**Criminal:** False statements (5 years), theft of federal property (10 years), wire fraud (20 years); fines up to $250K; criminal forfeiture of full grant amount
**Civil:** Treble damages (3x actual damages), fines up to $11,000 per false claim under False Claims Act
**Administrative:** Contract termination, debarment (typically 3 years), prohibition from federal contracting, subcontracting restrictions
**Whistleblower Incentive:** Up to 30% of judgment award under qui tam provisions
