# USDA Wildlife Services UAS Procurement

## Document Metadata
- Type: Combined Synopsis/Solicitation (Request for Quotation - RFQ)
- Client/Agency: United States Department of Agriculture (USDA), Animal Plant Health Inspection Service (APHIS), Wildlife Services (WS), Cedar City, UT
- Program/Solicitation: AG-6395-S-16-0211
- Date: August 26, 2016
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: None explicitly named (this is a vendor solicitation)
- Key Personnel: Jason L. Wilking (USDA Minneapolis Contracting Office point of contact)

## Executive Summary
USDA/APHIS/Wildlife Services issued a request for quotation for four unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) to support aerial animal damage management operations. This is a small business set-aside commercial acquisition with simplified procurement procedures, seeking VTOL or STOL aircraft meeting specified performance and payload requirements.

## Technical Requirements Specified
The solicitation requests UAS meeting the following minimum specifications:

**Aircraft Characteristics:**
- VTOL or STOL capable (Vertical or Short Take Off and Landing)
- Weight: less than 55 lbs
- Maximum wingspan: 14 ft
- Minimum flight time: 45 minutes
- Electric propulsion system
- Cruise speed: 20-40 mph minimum, max speed not to exceed 80 mph
- Service ceiling: minimum 500 ft AGL (Above Ground Level)

**Autonomous & Navigation Capabilities:**
- GPS (Global Positioning System)
- Autopilot with waypoint following, auto-return to launch, loiter, and circle-around-point-of-interest functions
- Autonomous flight capabilities

**Payload & Sensor Systems:**
- Minimum payload capacity: 2.5 lbs
- Thermal camera with 360-degree pan and minimum 90-degree tilt with stabilization
- FPV (First Person View) capability with minimum 5-mile range video signal

**Control & Operations:**
- Two-controller option (one for UAS flight, one for camera control)
- Portable handheld monitors with wireless video receivers
- Ground control station with telemetry and video
- Digital video recording
- Easily disassembled for transportation and assembled in field
- Turn-key system with customer support

## Use Cases & Applications
- Aerial animal damage management operations
- Wildlife Services missions in cedar City, UT region

## Procurement Details
- **Quantity:** 4 UAS units
- **Contract Type:** Firm fixed price commercial supply contract
- **Period of Performance:** Award through delivery, not to exceed one year
- **Set-Aside:** Small Business Set-Aside
- **NAICS Code:** 336411
- **Small Business Size Standard:** 1,500 employees
- **Evaluation Criteria:** Trade-off process considering technical capability (quality/performance to fit government needs), price, and past performance
- **Quotation Deadline:** September 2, 2016 at 1:30 PM CST
- **Submission:** Email to Jason.l.wilking@aphis.usda.gov, fax to 612-336-3550, or mail to Minneapolis Contracting Office
