# BST Proposal Letter AREN Support

## Document Metadata
- Type: Proposal letter / Statement of work commitment
- Client/Agency: NASA Science Mission Directorate
- Program/Solicitation: AEROKATS and ROVER Education Network (AREN); NASA Science Activation project (NNX16AB95A)
- Date: January 23, 2025
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: AeroPod, Aerovator
- Key Personnel: Kay Rufty (Aerospace Engineer, Co-Investigator), Jack Elston (CEO, Technical Consultant), Maciej Stachura (Technical Consultant)

## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies commits to supporting NASA's AEROKATS and ROVER Education Network (AREN), a Science Activation project promoting learning and outreach. BST will provide personnel and technical support to develop educational workshop materials using kites for environmental exploration, while also evaluating the feasibility of commercial product development for AeroPod and Aerovator systems.

## Technical Approach
BST will support AREN activities through:
- **Personnel contribution**: Named Co-Investigator Kay Rufty (Aerospace Engineer), Technical Consultants Jack Elston and Maciej Stachura, and a Senior Technician
- **Workshop material development**: Design and fabrication of wood and plastic parts for educational kites
- **Product evaluation**: Assessment of AeroPod and Aerovator commercial product development feasibility
- **Execution**: All work performed remotely from locations in Denver and Boulder, CO, and Onancock, VA; no NASA facility access or IT system integration required
