## Executive Summary
This Milestone #5 report documents Black Swift Technologies' progress on a Phase II SBIR project to validate runway integrity through soil moisture measurements from a small UAS. The team successfully resolved RF noise interference issues, completed flight test approval processes, and is positioned to begin local field testing at Sunny Slope Sod Farm in Longmont, CO to collect soil moisture and soil integrity ground truth data.

## Technical Approach
**System Architecture:**
- L-band radiometer sensing instrument as primary moisture measurement tool
- Auxiliary sensors for surface temperature and NDVI (Normalized Difference Vegetation Index)
- Real-time soil moisture calculation capability
- Onboard and post-processing algorithms to compute soil integrity (cone index) from L-band soil moisture data and soil type classification

**RF Noise Resolution (Key Technical Challenge Addressed):**
- Problem: Radio frequency interference detected in initial field and bench tests from two sources:
  - Wideband radiation noise from radiometer digital back end
  - Narrowband noise from drone E2 systems
- Solution implemented:
  - Conductive spray coating on digital back end case for RF shielding
  - RF absorber layer added to digital back end
  - Additional RF absorber sheet layer below metal plate isolating antenna from airframe
  - Result: 5 dB noise level reduction; remaining narrow frequency noise manageable through pre-flight calibration and post-processing filtering

**Bench and Outdoor Testing:**
- Bench test data validated against S-parameter measurements and simulation
- Outdoor testing verified sensitivity and orthogonality between calibration reference and antenna measurements
- Tests performed with antenna viewing cold space (~5K) and concrete road (~260K)
- Cross-correlation analysis confirmed expected system performance

**L-band Radiometer System**
- Primary sensor for soil moisture measurement
- Undergoes refinement and modification from baseline instrument
- Integrated with auxiliary sensors
- Produces calibrated radiometric data for soil moisture retrieval

**Soil Integrity Algorithm Suite**
- Computes cone index (soil bearing capacity measure) from L-band soil moisture data
- Accounts for soil type variations
- Dual implementation: onboard real-time processing and post-processing refinement
- Under development and refinement based on field test data collection
