## Executive Summary
This is a blank DOE budget justification template for SBIR/STTR grant applications. It provides standardized guidance and required sections for applicants to explain and justify all budget elements in proposed awards.

## Document Purpose
This is a **template document**, not a completed proposal or report. It contains no substantive information about Black Swift Technologies, its products, capabilities, or specific projects.

## Template Sections Outlined
The document provides required sections and instructions for budget justifications:

- **A/B. Senior/Key Person & Other Personnel** - Justification of positions, hours, rates, and basis for compensation
- **C. Equipment** - Itemization and justification of equipment purchases ($5,000+ per unit threshold)
- **D. Travel** - Justification, breakdown, and basis for travel costs
- **F. Other Direct Costs** - Multiple subsections:
  - Materials and Supplies
  - Publication Costs
  - Consultant Services
  - ADP/Computer Services
  - Subawards/Consortium/Contractual Costs
  - Equipment or Facility Rental/User Fees
  - Alterations and Renovations (machining, fabrication)
  - Other/Miscellaneous
- **H. Indirect Costs** - Rate justification and Federal agency approval documentation

## Notable Details
- Template requires consistency between narrative justifications and actual budget figures
- Emphasis on supporting administrative/managerial labor with detailed justification
- Equipment defined as standalone items costing $5,000+ per unit
- Items under $5,000 should be classified as materials/supplies
- DOE National Laboratory work requires specific compliance certification under DOE Order O 481.1C
- Consultant documentation must include signed agreements and rate verification

## Utility for BST
This template would be used by Black Swift Technologies when submitting SBIR/STTR proposals to DOE, but contains no project-specific information.
