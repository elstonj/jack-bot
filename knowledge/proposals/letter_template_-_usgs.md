# Letter Template - USGS

## Document Metadata
- Type: Letter of commitment/support (template)
- Client/Agency: USGS (U.S. Geological Survey)
- Program/Solicitation: NASA SBIR Phase II, Contract NNX17CP13C
- Date: June 19, 2019
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: S2 UAS
- Key Personnel: Jack Elston (CEO, Black Swift Technologies)

## Executive Summary
USGS template letter confirming financial commitment to support further development of the S2 UAS and sensing system under NASA SBIR Phase II contract. The funding would extend a planned USGS volcanic sampling campaign and enable technical improvements to the system.

## Technical Approach
Development and refinement of the S2 UAS platform and its integrated sensing system for scientific data gathering in harsh environments. The Phase II work focuses on technical improvements to enable broader adoption.

## Products & Capabilities Described
**S2 UAS**
- Ruggedized unmanned aircraft system for scientific data gathering
- Designed for operation in harsh environments
- Integrated sensing system for remote sensing applications
- Originally developed under NASA SBIR Phase II contract

## Use Cases & Applications
- **Volcanic sampling campaigns**: Primary near-term application for USGS
- Volcanic monitoring and data gathering for multiple volcanoes of interest to USGS
- Long-term potential for broader remote sensing applications beyond volcanology

## Notable Details
- This is a cost-sharing arrangement with NASA matching USGS funding contributions
- USGS views the S2 technology as significant to the viability and adoption of UAS in scientific research
- The additional funding would enable both extended field campaigns and technical improvements to the system
- Technology addresses USGS's need to simplify and improve volcanic sampling missions
