## Executive Summary
This document outlines Black Swift Technologies' three-year budget allocation for a NASA ROSES 2024 A.25 project involving geo-gas emissions estimation and flight control using the S2 UAS. The total project budget is $126,020 across direct labor, engineering support, and travel costs.
