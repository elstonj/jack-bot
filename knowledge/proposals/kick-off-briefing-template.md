## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies is conducting a Phase I SBIR/STTR effort to quantify the capabilities of their S0 UAS platform for providing higher-resolution, more accurate, and cost-effective atmospheric data in hazardous weather conditions compared to existing platforms. The work focuses on measuring vertical wind, turbulence, and wave properties while identifying and characterizing error sources in atmospheric measurements.

## Technical Approach
- **Objective 1:** Quantify S0 UAS capabilities for atmospheric data collection with improvements in resolution, accuracy, and cost-effectiveness versus legacy systems
- **Objective 2:** Identify main error sources in S0 measurements, particularly for wind and turbulence parameters
- **Objective 3:** Demonstrate new/unique observations (vertical wind, turbulence, wave properties) unavailable from existing platforms
- **Objective 4:** Achieve direct sampling capability in areas of operational interest
