## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies submitted a final New Technology Summary Report to NASA documenting four new technology items developed during a NASA-funded project (NNX14CG09C) from November 2014 to April 2016. All four items were disclosed to NASA as required, and no additional disclosures were being submitted with this report.
