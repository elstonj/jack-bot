## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies completed a FAR 16.301-3 preaward accounting system checklist as part of Navy STTR Phase II proposal preparation. The checklist assesses BST's accounting system readiness for cost-reimbursement contracts and DCAA audit compliance. BST confirmed willingness to bid on cost-type contracts and readiness for DCAA audit, though the company was actively updating accounting systems to meet federal requirements.

## Accounting System Status

### Items Fully Compliant (Marked "Yes")
1. System in accord with generally accepted accounting principles
2. Proper segregation of direct from indirect costs
3. Identification and accumulation of direct costs by contract
4. Logical and consistent allocation of indirect costs to cost objectives
5. Accumulation of costs under general ledger control
6. Interim (monthly) determination of costs through routine posting
7. System designed to develop reliable data for pricing follow-on acquisitions
8. System currently in full operation

### Items Not Yet Implemented (Marked "No")
- Item 2.e: Timekeeping system identifying employees' labor by cost objectives
- Item 2.f: Labor distribution system charging direct/indirect labor appropriately
- Item 2.h: Exclusion of non-allowable costs per FAR 31
- Item 2.i: Cost identification by contract line item and units
- Item 2.j: Segregation of preproduction from production costs
- Item 3.a: Financial information for limitation of cost clauses
- Item 3.b: Financial information for progress payment requests

## System Modifications in Progress
BST indicated it was "currently modifying our accounting system" to support compliance in the following areas:
- Labor tracking and identification (items 2.e, 2.f)
- Allowable cost exclusions (item 2.h)
- Contract line item and unit identification (item 2.i)
- Preproduction/production cost segregation (item 2.j)
- Limitation of cost financial reporting (item 3.a)
- Progress payment request support (item 3.b)
