## Research Expertise
Dr. Sedlacek is an atmospheric chemist specializing in:
- **Primary Focus:** Atmospheric aging impacts on optical and microphysical properties of carbonaceous aerosols (black and brown carbon)
- **Methods:** Aircraft- and ground-based field campaigns; targeted laboratory studies; model aerosol systems
- **Instrumentation Development:** Novel optical-based instrumentation for aerosol optical property measurements
- **Current Role:** BNL SFA Technical Manager for Aerosol Processes team; Instrument Mentor for Single Particle Soot Photometer (SP2), 7-wavelength Aethalometer, and 3-wavelength CAPS extinctometer under DOE-ARM program
