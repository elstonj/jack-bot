## Executive Summary
This is a standard SBA SBIR/STTR funding agreement certification form that Black Swift Technologies completed as a requirement for receiving an SBIR Phase II award from the Air Force AFWERX program for soil moisture mapping research. The form verifies that BST meets all eligibility requirements for small business status and program compliance.

## Document Content
This is a boilerplate federal certification form rather than a substantive technical or proposal document. It requires the awardee to certify compliance with 18 specific requirements, including:

**Eligibility Requirements:**
- Small business status (≤500 employees including affiliates)
- Ownership and control by U.S. citizens or permanent resident aliens
- No conflicting SBA size determinations

**Performance Requirements:**
- Principal Investigator/Project Manager spending >50% time on project
- Awardee performs minimum 50% of research (SBIR Phase II requirement)
- R&D performed in the United States
- Work performed at awardee's facilities by awardee's employees (except as approved)

**Conflict/Duplication Requirements:**
- Work not submitted for duplicate federal funding
- Notification if work is subsequently funded by another federal agency

**Certifier Responsibility:**
- Authorized officer signature required
- Acknowledgment of criminal and civil penalties for false certification
