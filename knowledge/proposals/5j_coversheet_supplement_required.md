## Executive Summary
This is a required administrative supplement to the DoD SBIR Phase II proposal coversheet for Black Swift Technologies' soil moisture mapping project. The document provides a template for completing eligibility certifications, foreign nationals disclosure, and other compliance requirements mandated by the Air Force SBIR solicitation.

## Document Purpose
This is a coversheet supplement template document designed to accompany the main Volume 1 DoD Proposal Coversheet in DSIP (DoD SBIR submission system). It serves as a checklist and instruction guide for completing required administrative and compliance disclosures.

## Required Sections to Complete

1. **Certification/Eligibility Check**: All eligibility and certification requirements must be met and documented in DSIP Volume 1.

2. **Prior, Current, or Pending Support**: Required disclosure of any similar proposals submitted to other Federal Agencies or DoD Components, including:
   - Name of Federal Agency
   - Submission date or award date
   - Proposal title and SBIR topic number
   - Principal Investigator information
   - Contract number if awarded

3. **Foreign Nationals Table**: Disclosure required for any foreign national team members including:
   - Full name
   - Country of origin
   - Visa/work permit status
   - Specific work plan tasks assigned

4. **Venture Capital Ownership** (if applicable): Requires disclosure of ownership structure if company is majority-owned by VC firms, hedge funds, or private equity firms.

5. **Firm Affiliations** (if applicable): Detailed disclosure of affiliations per 13 CFR 121.103.

6. **Research Involving Human Subjects, Animal Subjects, or Recombinant DNA** (if applicable): Requires Federal Assurance of Compliance and appropriate documentation.

7. **Teaming Partners or Subcontractors** (if applicable): Must be disclosed in coversheet, cost volume, and supplemental cost information.
