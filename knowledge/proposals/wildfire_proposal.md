## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies proposes deploying small unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) to improve situational awareness for wildland firefighting operations. The system would integrate infrared imaging, on-board wind estimation, and communications relay capabilities to provide incident commanders and deployed firefighters with real-time fire extent, wind data, and enhanced communication coverage—particularly valuable in mountainous terrain where communications are blocked.

## Technical Approach

**Core System Architecture:**
- Small UAS airframes provided through partnership with Rocketship Systems (Fort Collins-based small business)
- Integration of BST avionics, sensing, and communication subsystems
- On-board computing for compression, aggregation, and data transmission

**Four Primary Technical Components:**

1. **Infrared Imaging**: Thermographic camera systems (light enough for small UAS) connected to on-board computer for fire extent visualization and fire weather product generation

2. **Wind Estimation**: In situ wind measurement algorithms developed by BST team members through University of Colorado research (previously demonstrated in severe storm sampling work); combined with instrumentation calibrated and verified in the M2AV project

3. **Communications Relay**: UAV-based relay system to overcome terrain-blocked communications in mountainous areas; algorithms determine optimal aircraft positioning for maximum bandwidth and connectivity; enables personnel tracking and real-time alerting

4. **Multiple Vehicle Operations**: Simultaneous deployment of multiple UAS to increase coverage, obtain more frequent observations, extend communication relay chains, and allow incident commanders to scale asset allocation

## Products & Capabilities Described

**UAS Platform:**
- Rugged, proven airframe with field track record
- Small enough for on-site operations and deployment from within active firefighting airspace (unlike larger NASA Ikhana)
- Equipped with integrated avionics and sensing suites

**Wind Estimation System:**
- Algorithm-based in situ wind measurement capability
- Previously validated through severe storm sampling (Tempest UAS work)
- Provides wind aloft data unavailable through conventional means to incident commanders and forecasters

**Communications Relay System:**
- Terrain-aware routing algorithms
- Enables multi-hop relay through aircraft chains
- Personnel and equipment tracking capability

**Multispectral/IR Imaging Suite:**
- Thermal imaging systems
- Real-time fire weather product generation

## Use Cases & Applications

- **Primary Use**: Wildland firefighting situational awareness and operational support in Colorado and similar mountainous terrain
- **Specific Mission Functions**: 
  - Real-time fire extent mapping
  - Wind field characterization (chinook-like downslope wind events)
  - Communications relay in terrain-blocked areas
  - Personnel tracking and incident command coordination
