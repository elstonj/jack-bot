## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies proposes to develop sensing and control algorithms enabling safe operation of tethered small UAS systems deployed from ground vehicles in obstacle-rich environments. The solution combines airborne and ground vehicle sensors with advanced fusion algorithms to enable obstacle detection and avoidance at 45+ mph operational speeds while maintaining tether management, supporting military convoy operations and situational awareness.

## Technical Approach

**Problem Statement:**
- Tethered UAS systems overcome flight endurance limitations of small UAS (limited power density) by providing power and data via tether to ground vehicle
- Current limitations: tether drag and obstacle avoidance create significant operational constraints
- Primary challenge: perception and control for obstacle detection and avoidance while managing tether dynamics

**Proposed Solution:**
- Selection and integration of ground vehicle and airborne sensors
- Development of sensor fusion algorithm combining information from both platforms
- Algorithm designed to detect obstacles at sufficient distance (enabling 45+ mph+ operations) for aircraft reaction time
- Advanced flight control algorithms to manage both obstacle avoidance and tether dynamics
- Integration with UAS control systems for autonomous obstacle avoidance
- Edge computing for onboard data processing and dissemination

**Leverage of BST Capabilities:**
- Extensive operational experience developing UAS systems
- Established expertise in vision-based sensing systems
- Strong flight control algorithm development background

## Products & Capabilities Described

**Tethered UAS System:**
- Small unmanned aircraft system paired with ground vehicle
- Powered and data-linked via tether to ground vehicle
- Designed for extended operational deployment (overcomes power density limitations of traditional batteries)
- Target operational speed: 45+ mph

**Machine Vision/Sensor Systems:**
- Airborne sensors (mounted on UAS)
- Ground vehicle sensors (mounted on vehicle)
- Requirement: sufficient resolution for hazard detection at distance adequate for avoidance maneuvers

**Advanced Control Systems:**
- Flight control algorithms for obstacle avoidance
- Tether dynamics management
- Distributed control capable of ingesting sensor data for autonomous reactions

**Sensor Fusion Algorithm:**
- Integration of airborne and ground-based sensor data
- Obstacle detection and range computation
- Information distribution to participating vehicle systems

## Use Cases & Applications

**Primary Application: Military Ground Vehicle Operations**
- Convoy situational awareness (single vehicles and coordinated convoys)
- Airborne sensor suite deployment with each ground vehicle
- Early warning capabilities for threat detection
- GPS-denied environment localization support
- Extended transmission range in urban canyon environments
- Support for Next Generation Combat Vehicle (NGCV) operations

**Operational Benefits:**
- Enhanced information gathering from previously inaccessible vantage points
- Improved situational awareness across coordinated vehicle formations
- Extended deployment duration vs. battery-powered UAS
- Persistent aerial capability tethered to mobile platform
