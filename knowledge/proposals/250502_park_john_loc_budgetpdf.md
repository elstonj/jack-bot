## Executive Summary
Old Dominion University Research Foundation proposes to support Black Swift Technologies as prime contractor in developing expendable air-sea profiling observations for hazardous weather conditions using small uncrewed aircraft systems. The work focuses on integrating the S0 system with Navy launch platforms, validating performance, and enhancing onboard data processing capabilities for remote oceanic operations.

## Technical Approach

### Task 2: Engineering Enhancements and Error Analysis
- Develop integration plan for S0 compatibility with Navy-specific launch platforms:
  - Sonobuoy launch systems
  - External pod mounts on P-8A Poseidon aircraft
  - CH-53E helicopters
- Conduct simulations and mechanical tests to validate platform compatibility
- Perform detailed error analysis of current S0 data identifying sources of uncertainty in:
  - Wind measurements
  - Turbulence estimation
  - Height calculations

### Task 3: Calibration and Validation Activities
- Plan and execute calibration/validation activities to improve S0 data accuracy
- Wind tunnel testing
- Field deployments in controlled conditions
- Comparative studies with:
  - Instrumented towers
  - NCAR dropsondes
- Refine algorithms for vertical velocity and turbulence estimation for robust oceanic performance

### Task 5: Onboard Data Processing Development
- Enhance onboard data processing with quality control (QC) and derived metrics computation:
  - Wind spectra
  - Wave spectra
  - Energy at the edge
- Develop computationally efficient algorithms for:
  - High-rate data processing onboard
  - Compressed transmission of environmental information
  - Bandwidth optimization
- Investigate adaptable artificial intelligence algorithms:
  - Train surrogates of expensive physics models
  - Optimize flight trajectories based on new observations

## Products & Capabilities Described

### S0 (Expendable Profiling System)
- **What it is**: Small uncrewed aircraft system designed for air-sea profiling in hazardous weather conditions
- **Current state**: Existing system with known performance limitations
- **Proposed enhancements**:
  - Integration with military launch platforms (sonobuoys, helicopter pods, P-8A external mounts)
  - Improved data accuracy through calibration/validation
  - Onboard data processing and compression
  - AI-enhanced flight trajectory optimization
- **Specifications/Performance claims under development**:
  - Operates in remote oceanic environments
  - Measures wind, turbulence, and height
  - Transmits compressed environmental data via limited bandwidth

## Use Cases & Applications

- **Hazardous weather observations**: Air-sea profiling in adverse conditions
- **Navy maritime operations**: Integration with P-8A Poseidon maritime patrol aircraft
- **Naval aviation**: Deployment from CH-53E helicopters
- **Anti-submarine warfare context**: Sonobuoy launch system integration (P-8A primary role)
- **Remote oceanic monitoring**: Data collection in areas distant from shore infrastructure
