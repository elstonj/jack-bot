## Executive Summary
Jack S. Elston is the CEO and Founder of Black Swift Technologies, holding advanced degrees in aerospace engineering and electrical engineering from University of Colorado. His research focuses on unmanned aircraft systems (UAS) for atmospheric and meteorological sampling, with expertise in networked command and control, UAS guidance systems, and sensor payload development.

## Technical Expertise & Research Focus

**Key Technical Areas:**
- Networked command and control of complex UAS
- UAS guidance systems for severe weather penetration (tornadic supercells, pre-tornadic storms)
- Small fixed-wing unmanned aircraft for meteorological sampling
