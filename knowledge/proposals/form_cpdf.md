## Executive Summary
This budget form documents Black Swift Technologies' proposed costs for a NASA Climate Change Research and Predictions (CCRPP) SBIR project involving deployment of unmanned aircraft systems (SuperSwift and SwiftTrainer) equipped with multispectral sensors for soil moisture monitoring at test sites in Arizona and Maryland. Total project cost of $354,231.62 with $177,157 in investor matching funds.
