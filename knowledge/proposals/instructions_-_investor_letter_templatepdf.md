## Executive Summary
This is an instructional template for investor commitment letters required to support NASA SBIR Phase II+ (Phase IIE) proposals. It outlines the format, required content, and regulatory constraints for third-party investment commitments that must accompany SBIR funding requests.

## Document Purpose & Use
This template was created to guide Black Swift Technologies in obtaining and formatting investor commitment letters when submitting Phase IIE proposals to NASA's SBIR program. The letter serves as formal verification of external funding commitment to match or supplement NASA's Phase II award.

## Required Investor Letter Content
1. **Investment Commitment Statement**: Confirmation of specific dollar amount (format: $XXX,XXX) to be invested in the SBIR firm
2. **Project Reference**: NASA contract number and project title
3. **Use of Funds**: Description of how investment will support further development, integration, and testing of Phase II technologies
4. **Expected Outcomes**: List of specific technical and/or commercialization outcomes expected from the investment
5. **Alignment Statement**: Verification that investment aligns with investor's programmatic and development priorities
6. **Investor Credentials**: Name, title, phone, email of authorized investor representative

## Investment Eligibility & Constraints
**Acceptable Investment Forms:**
- Equity stakes
- Royalty shares
- Technology rights
- Profit percentages
- Advance credits for future products
- Any combination of the above

**Acceptable Use of Funds:**
- Further R&D
- Manufacturing development
- Marketing and commercialization
- Technology development and commercialization

**Ineligible Investment Sources & Forms:**
- In-kind gifts
- Exchange of services
- Prior purchase orders
- Other SBIR/STTR firms
- Firms listed as subcontractors on the SBIR/STTR proposal
- (For STTR: research institutions cannot be the outside investor)
