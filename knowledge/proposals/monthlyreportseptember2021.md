# Monthly Progress Report – September 2021

## Document Metadata
- Type: Monthly progress report
- Client/Agency: NASA
- Program/Solicitation: AREN (Airborne Research and Education Network)
- Date: September 2021 (reporting period); submitted October 8, 2021
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: AeroPod, SwiftPod, TerraROVER, Monocam

## Executive Summary
This September 2021 progress report documents manufacturing and assembly activities for the AREN program, focusing on AeroPod and TerraROVER component production, camera mount redesign and iteration, and preparation of supplies for future SwiftPod orders.

## Technical Approach
Work centered on:
- 3D printing and post-processing (cleaning) of AeroPod and TerraROVER parts
- Iterative camera mount design and fabrication using modeling and 3D printing
- Camera integration for three Gitup camera variants (Gitup1, Gitup2, Gitup3)
- 3D printer troubleshooting and maintenance (Lulzbot Taz 6 printer clogging issues)
- Preparation of leader lines and supplies for SwiftPod orders

## Products & Capabilities Described

**AeroPod:**
- Small aerial platform with modular camera mounts
- Used with multiple camera types (Gitup1, Gitup2, Gitup3)
- Camera mounts redesigned with tab mechanism for improved modularity

**SwiftPod:**
- Future product in production pipeline
- Leader lines and supplies being prepared for orders

**TerraROVER:**
- Ground-based platform; components being manufactured

**Monocam AeroPods:**
- Assembled and shipped units delivered to team members (Mike Jabot and Geoff Bland)
