## Summary
This is a **blank sample template** used to guide submission of Phase I SBIR final project summary reports to NASA/JPL. It does not contain actual project data, results, or technical content from Black Swift Technologies or any specific project.

## Template Structure
The form includes the following required sections:

1. **Firm** (contractor name field)
2. **Contract Number** (identifier field)
3. **Project Title** (proposal name field)
4. **Identification and Significance of Innovation** (200 words / 2,000 characters max)
5. **Technical Objectives and Work Plan** (200 words / 2,000 characters max)
6. **Technical Accomplishments** (200 words / 2,000 characters max)
7. **NASA Application(s)** (100 words / 1,000 characters max)
8. **Non-NASA Commercial Application(s)** (200 words / 2,000 characters max)
9. **Principal Investigator** contact information
10. **Offeror** (company) contact information
