## Products & Capabilities Described

### S3 UAS
- VTOL (vertical takeoff and landing) capable
- Twin-motor configuration
- Designed for stable, enduring operations in challenging environments
- Capable of carrying multi-spectral, infrared, and thermal sensors
- Application: wildfire detection, environmental monitoring

### Multi-hour Duration sUAS
- Air-deployable from manned aircraft (NOAA WP-3D)
- Command & control range: up to 150 nm
- Meteorological/oceanographic data collection capability
- Designed for severe storm operations (expendable/unrecoverable design)

### L-band Radiometer
- Co-developed with NASA, OMS, CET
- UAS-based soil moisture mapping
- Can be adapted for cone index/runway integrity assessment

## Use Cases & Applications
1. Tropical cyclone structure and intensity prediction (in-flight deployment from WP-3D)
2. C-130 runway integrity assessment for unimproved landing zones
3. Wildfire detection, monitoring, and prediction (pre-season fuel mapping, fire detection, satellite validation)
4. Collaborative atmospheric sensing (multi-platform coordination)
5. Educational outreach and community resilience
