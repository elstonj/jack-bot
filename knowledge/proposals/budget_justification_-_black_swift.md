# Budget Justification - Black Swift (USDA 2018 Proposal)

- Program/Solicitation: USDA grant/program (specific solicitation not named)

- Key Personnel: Maciej Stachura (PI), Jack Elston (Co-PI)

## Executive Summary
This budget justification supports a USDA proposal for soil moisture monitoring work using Black Swift's S2 UAS platform equipped with a specialized soil moisture payload. The 8-month project involves two key personnel and purchase of a complete S2 system with integrated soil moisture instrumentation, supported by subcontractor services from OMS.

## Technical Approach
- Purchase and deployment of a complete S2 UAS with integrated soil moisture payload
- S2 specially designed to house the soil moisture payload with antenna/front-end sizing optimized for the instrument
- Extensive electromagnetic interference (EMI) mitigation design modifications implemented to ensure accuracy and repeatability of soil moisture measurements
- Field testing at a local site (no travel required)
- Subcontractor support for mission planning, post-processing, and data analysis

## Products & Capabilities Described

**S2 UAS**
- Complete turnkey system including ground station, user interface software, tablet-based computer, carrying case, aircraft, launcher, and batteries
- Specifically redesigned to accommodate soil moisture payload
- EMI mitigation engineering to support precision soil moisture sensing
- Purchased at list price with $1,000 in spare parts for field maintenance and repairs

## Use Cases & Applications
- Soil moisture monitoring and measurement (agricultural/hydrological application)
- Local field demonstration/validation
