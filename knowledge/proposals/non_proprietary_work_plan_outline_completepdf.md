## Executive Summary
This is a non-proprietary outline of Black Swift Technologies' Phase I work plan for a DoD/USAF SBIR proposal. The plan involves three months of stakeholder engagement to identify USAF mission applications for BST's technology, documentation development, and formulation of a Phase II prototype pathway.

## Technical Approach
The Phase I approach consists of three main tasks executed over a 3-month period:
1. **Month 1**: Develop presentation demonstrating BST technology applicability to a USAF mission
2. **Months 1-2**: Meet with USAS/DoD stakeholders to identify specific use cases where BST's solution provides value
3. **Month 3**: Develop documentation and final reports
