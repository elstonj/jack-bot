## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies submitted a proposal to the FY26 ESTCP solicitation for an advanced UAS-based fuel moisture mapping system to support wildfire risk mitigation and prescribed fire management on DoD lands. The proposal was not selected for full review due to high volume and limited staff resources.

## Technical Approach
Not detailed in this document (abstract/white paper stage only). Proposal concept involved:
- UAS-based (uncrewed aerial system) platform
- Fuel moisture mapping capability
- Application to wildfire risk mitigation and prescribed fire management

## Products & Capabilities Described
- **Advanced UAS-Based Fuel Moisture Mapping System**: Proposed for DoD land management applications. Intended to support both wildfire risk assessment and prescribed fire operations planning.

## Use Cases & Applications
- Wildfire risk mitigation on DoD installations
- Prescribed fire management planning and execution on DoD lands

## Outcome
Proposal declined at white paper/abstract stage. No feedback provided due to high proposal volume (FY26 ESTCP received substantial submissions). Encouragement to resubmit to future ESTCP solicitations.
