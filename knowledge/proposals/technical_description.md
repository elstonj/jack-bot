## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies proposes the sale and delivery of four SwiftTrainer unmanned aircraft systems to USDA for animal damage management operations. The proposal emphasizes the SwiftTrainer's low cost of ownership, ease of operation, rapid operator training (less than one day), and industry-leading accuracy in geo-tagging aerial imagery—critical for quickly generating actionable damage assessment information for USDA field personnel.

## Technical Approach
BST's approach centers on providing a turn-key, fully autonomous fixed-wing mapping platform designed for simplicity and accuracy. Key technical elements include:

- **Autonomous Operations:** Hand-launched with autonomous belly landing capability; operates in unimproved areas
- **Advanced Flight Management:** SwiftCore FMS (entirely USA-designed and manufactured) enables accurate ground track maintenance even in high winds
- **Rapid Image Processing:** Industry-leading geo-tagging accuracy (1-2m correction post-processing vs. 10m+ for competitors) combined with fast image acquisition (<1s between photos) enables rapid ortho-mosaic generation
- **Optional EO/IR Upgrade:** Dual-operator configuration with streaming thermal + standard video over independent radio link; uses FLIR Vue 640x512 camera with 19mm lens (30Hz, 10cm/pixel at 400ft, 5cm/pixel at 200ft)

**What it is:** Hand-launched, fixed-wing electric mapping platform with integrated SwiftCore avionics

**Specifications:**
- Coverage per flight: 600 acres

- Mapping camera resolution: 24MP
- Time between photos: <1 second

**Performance claims:**

## Competitive Advantages Documented

**1. Accurate Ground Tracks**
- SwiftTrainer maintains tight, regularly-spaced flight lines
- Competitor platforms (Pteryx, Falcon Unmanned) show less accurate, irregular spacing that can cause image overlap loss and require mission re-flights

**2. Accurate Geo-tags**
- SwiftTrainer: 1-2m post-processing correction
- Pixhawk-based systems: >10m correction
- BST invested significant R&D in SwiftPilot to achieve this accuracy

**3. Fast Image Acquisition**
- SwiftTrainer: <1 second between images
- SenseFly Ebee (leading competitor): 3.8 seconds between images
- Enables operations at 400 ft with >60% overlap and in higher wind conditions
