## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies provides justification for its overhead, G&A, and profit rates applied to federal contract proposals. The document outlines BST's cost allocation model and confirms that these rates have been proposed and accepted on all federal awards for FY 2024.

## Cost Rates Applied

### Overhead Rate
- **Rate:** 46.67% applied to direct labor and fringe
- **Basis:** Internal cost allocation model
- **Covered Costs:** Payroll (DL), health insurance, rent, utilities, supplies
- **Justification:** Covers real and necessary indirect costs for research, training, and development; ensures project benefits from institutional resources and infrastructure; maintains regulatory and sponsor compliance
- **Status:** Proposed and accepted on all federal awards for FY 2024

### General & Administrative (G&A) Rate
- **Rate:** 18.32% applied to all direct costs
- **Basis:** Overhead functions plus all administrative functions active across all projects
- **Justification:** Covers real and necessary indirect costs; ensures project benefits from institutional resources and infrastructure; maintains regulatory and sponsor compliance
- **Status:** Proposed and accepted on all federal awards for FY 2024

### Profit/Fee
- **Characterization:** Widely considered reasonable for a small business undertaking R&D work
- **Specific Rate:** Not stated in document

### Fringe Benefit Rate
- **Rate:** 29.28% applied to direct labor
- **Components:** Employee health insurance, 401(k), sick time, PTO

## Notable Details
- All rates have been accepted by federal sponsors for FY 2024
- Direct labor hourly rates are stated as competitive based on industry standards, years of experience, and education level
- Document emphasizes compliance with regulatory and sponsor requirements
