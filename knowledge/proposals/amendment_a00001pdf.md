# Amendment A00001 to NOAA 2018 SBIR Solicitation

## Document Metadata
- Type: Solicitation Amendment (Standard Form 30)
- Client/Agency: NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration)
- Program/Solicitation: NOAA 2018-1 SBIR (Small Business Innovation Research)
- Solicitation Number: NRMW0000-18-00004
- Amendment Number: A0001
- Date Issued: December 15, 2017
- Original Solicitation Date: October 18, 2017
- Administered By: NOAA/AGO/EAD, Kansas City, MO
- Contracting Officer: Joan Clarkston, Contract Specialist

## Summary
This is a minor administrative amendment to the NOAA 2018-1 SBIR solicitation that updates the NAICS (North American Industry Classification System) code classification for eligible contractors.

## Key Changes
- **NAICS Code Update**: Changed from 541712 to 541715
  - 541712: Research, Development, and Testing Services (Engineering)
  - 541715: Research, Development, and Testing Services (Physical, Engineering, and Life Sciences)

## Administrative Details
- All other terms and conditions of the original solicitation remain unchanged
- Offerors were required to acknowledge receipt of this amendment prior to the offer submission deadline
- Acknowledgment could be made by:
  - Completing items 8 and 15 on the amendment form and returning it
  - Acknowledging receipt on each copy of the offer submitted
  - Separate letter or electronic communication referencing solicitation and amendment numbers

## Notable Details
This amendment slightly broadens the NAICS classification to include physical sciences and life sciences research, development, and testing services, potentially expanding the pool of eligible small businesses for this NOAA SBIR program.
