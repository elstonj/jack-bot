## Executive Summary
This is a University of Miami standard services agreement template for purchasing services from external contractors/counterparties. The document establishes terms and conditions for service delivery, payment, intellectual property, confidentiality, compliance, and risk management. It includes provisions for multiple statements of work to be executed under this master agreement framework.

## Notable Details

**Standard Provisions:**
- Services defined in separate Statements of Work (Appendix A)
- Payment upon submission of detailed invoices with receipts
- Term: Specifiable period with option for consecutive one-year renewals
- Termination: For cause (20 days notice to cure) or immediate (insolvency, confidentiality breach, Force Majeure >90 days)
- Insurance: Commercial General Liability minimum $1,000,000 per occurrence
- Reporting to: Jun Zhang

**Regulatory Compliance:**
- FCPA (Foreign Corrupt Practices Act) compliance
- ITAR/EAR (export control) compliance
- FERPA (student education records privacy)
- GDPR (Personal Data Processing) with required Data Processing Addendum
- HIPAA (Protected Health Information) with potential Business Associate Agreement
- Title IX (sexual misconduct reporting)
- Debarment certification (healthcare programs)
- Non-discrimination and diversity requirements

**Intellectual Property & Confidentiality:**
- Counterparty warrants services performed in professional, workmanlike manner per industry standards
- Confidentiality clause covers oral, written, digital, and electronic forms
- 5 business day reporting requirement for unauthorized disclosure
- University may share pricing data with GPO and benchmarking providers
- Confidentiality obligations survive agreement termination

**Liability & Indemnification:**
- Counterparty indemnifies University for losses, damages, claims arising from services, including IP infringement, property damage, breach, and legal violations
- Records access: University retains 3-year examination rights; 4-year access for HHS/Comptroller General

**Force Majeure:**
- Covers natural events (storms, floods, earthquakes, hurricanes), war, government actions, strikes, public health emergencies
- Specific mention of "hurricanes projected to make landfall near University's premises necessitating evacuation"
- Obligations excused only during duration of event with reasonable mitigation efforts

**Other:**
- No assignment permitted
- Non-exclusive arrangement
- Governed by Florida law, exclusive jurisdiction Miami-Dade County
- Conflict of interest disclosures required (Appendix B)
- Subcontracting requires prior written University approval
- MBE (Minority Business Enterprise) engagement encouraged
