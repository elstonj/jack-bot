Electronic Handbooks are Internet-based tools designed to support paperless documentation and management of complex distributed processes, with applications in NASA SBIR program management and Department of Justice programs.

## Technical Approach
EHBs processes are represented as "Internet-based plays" where different "actors" (users in different roles) communicate through the Internet to execute distributed processes. The system guides each actor through their responsibilities in the process.

Development occurs in six lifecycle stages:
1. Outline or Playwriting
2. Example or Rehearsal
3. Implementation or Staging
4. Utilization or Performance
5. Revision or New Production
6. Cross-Subprocess or Cross-Play Analysis
