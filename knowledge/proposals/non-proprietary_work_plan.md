## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies proposes a Phase I effort to validate and adapt its S3 autonomous UAS platform for Department of the Air Force operations, with initial focus on base security, forward airfield support, and drone detection. The work emphasizes end-user discovery, problem refinement, and technical adaptation to ensure alignment with real DAF operational needs and establish a foundation for Phase II demonstration.

## Technical Approach
**Phase I Strategy (90-day effort):**

1. **DAF End-User Discovery & Engagement (Days 5-60)**
   - Direct outreach to 10-20 DAF stakeholders
   - Focus areas: base perimeter surveillance, mobile drone detection, C-130 landing zone scouting, distributed ISR
   - Leverage existing engagement with Joint Base Elmendorf-Richardson (JBER)
   - Expand across AFWERX and Air Force Security Forces ecosystem

2. **Problem Refinement & Gap Analysis (Days 15-60)**
   - Detailed interviews with Air Force leaders and end-users
   - Document operational challenges, mission limitations, and capability gaps
   - Identify needs around: GPS-denied navigation, weather resilience, unattended operation, TAK interoperability

3. **Technical Adaptation & Specification Definition (Days 40-80)**
   - Structured engineering study to define required modifications
   - Hardware adaptations (e.g., icing resistance, cold-weather operation)
   - Software modifications and autonomy behaviors
   - Payload-specific requirements (EO/IR, drone detection modules)
   - TAK (Team Awareness Kit) data flow standards

4. **Phase II Trial Definition (Days 40-90)**
   - Collaborate with early end-users to define demonstration objectives
   - Identify trial location (JBER or alternate Air Force installation)
   - Establish success criteria and evaluation roadmap
   - Define integration and sustainment pathway

5. **Final Report & Phase II Plan (Days 60-90)**
   - Synthesis of customer discovery, technical adaptation, and mission alignment findings
   - Identification of at least one engaged DAF end-user
   - Refined problem-solution fit statement
   - Proposed Phase II demonstration scenario
