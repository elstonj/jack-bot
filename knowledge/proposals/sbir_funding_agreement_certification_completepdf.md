## Notable Details

**Eligibility Certifications Marked "Yes":**
- Company meets ownership and control requirements (13 CFR 121.702)
- Corporate documentation evidences proper ownership/control structure
- No more than 500 employees (including affiliates)
- No SBA size status determination finding exceeds 500-employee threshold
- PI will meet primary employment requirements
- Required percentage of work will be performed by prime contractor
- R&D will be performed in the United States
- Performance will take place in firm's facilities with firm's employees
- Not majority-owned by multiple venture capital/hedge fund/private equity firms
- Proposal has not been submitted to another Federal agency for identical work
- Will notify DoD if work is funded by another Federal agency
- Understands information may be shared with Federal/State/local agencies
- Authorized officer certifies all information is true and correct

**Form Structure:**
This is a 4-page compliance checklist with 17 numbered certification questions. Black Swift Technologies marked most items as compliant (indicated by X marks in the document). One item was marked "Not Applicable" (question 17 regarding Federally Funded Research and Development Centers).

**Legal Significance:**
The document includes explicit warnings about criminal and civil penalties (18 USC 1001, False Claims Act, Program Fraud Civil Remedies Act) for false information, suspension/debarment from Federal procurement, and termination of SBIR/STTR awards.
