## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies submitted a cost proposal for the WAANDER program, a DARPA-funded initiative to develop wind-powered autonomous aircraft with albatross-like navigation and endurance capabilities. The project is a 2-year effort (4/1/2025 - 3/31/2027) with a total proposed cost of $2,996,033.00 under a firm fixed-price, milestone-based contract structure.

## Technical Approach
- Development of wind-powered autonomous aircraft designed to achieve extended endurance and range through energy harvesting from wind patterns, mimicking albatross flight mechanics
- Autonomous navigation system capable of sustained operations
- Design approach leverages biological inspiration (albatross flight dynamics) for optimal performance
