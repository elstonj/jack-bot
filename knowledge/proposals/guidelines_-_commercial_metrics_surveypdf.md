## Executive Summary
This document provides guidelines for completing the Commercial Metrics Survey (CMS) required by the Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) and Small Business Technology Transfer (STTR) programs. The survey allows companies to demonstrate their commercialization capabilities and enables federal agencies to track the overall commercialization success of their SBIR/STTR investments.

## Content Overview
This is a procedural/instructional document rather than a technical proposal or capability brief. It outlines the administrative requirements and data collection process for the NASA SBIR Commercial Metrics Survey.

## Key Survey Sections & Requirements

### Firm Information
- Basic firm data (pre-populated from registration: name, address, EIN, DUNS, CAGE)
- Optional codes: SIC and NAICS

### Business Certification Questions
- Company founding year
- Total SBIR/STTR awards received (Phase I and Phase II with award years)
- Current and historical employee counts
- IPO history related to SBIR/STTR technology
- Merger/acquisition history
- Patent and copyright counts resulting from SBIR/STTR
- Total firm revenue (last fiscal year, via dropdown)
- Percentage of firm revenue from Federal SBIR/STTR funding

### Commercialization Narrative
Firms must prepare brief narrative describing:
- Commercialization successes not captured quantitatively
- Mitigating factors for low commercialization
- Recent organizational/personnel changes to improve commercialization success

### Company Financials
- Required: Balance Sheet, Profit & Loss Statement, Cash Flow Statement
- Preferred: 2-3 years of data
- Alternative: Pro Forma Cash Flow for firms less than one year old
- File size limit: 5 MB (formats: pdf, doc, docx, xls, xlsx)

### Infusion/Commercialization Information
Requires selection of Phase I or II Award and reporting:
- Commercialization status (Follow-On Sales; Follow-On Investments; No Sales/Investments; Both)
- Description of how technology was commercialized
- Checkboxes indicating applicable outcomes:
  - Technology in fielded Federal system/acquisition program
  - Manufacturing-related technology
  - Cost savings/avoidance achieved
  - Product launched
  - Post Phase II Award funded
  - Patents resulted
  - None of above

### Post Award Information
- Phase III Awards (contract/PO numbers, titles, dates, descriptions, agencies, programs)
- Related funding sources and amounts
- Separate competition participation flag
- Civilian Commercialization Readiness Pilot Program (CCRPP) participation
- Post Award Point of Contact information
- Supporting documentation (max 2 MB, PDF only)

## Notable Details
- **Firm Admin Authority**: Only the designated Firm Admin (typically first person to register) can update the form
- **Currency Requirement**: Survey must be updated within the past year to complete SBIR/STTR proposal submission
- **Conditional Fields**: Additional details required if certain checkboxes selected (e.g., agency name and cost savings amount if cost savings reported)
- **Multiple Award Handling**: Supports tracking multiple Phase I/II awards and related Phase III contracts
- **Edit Capability**: Previously started surveys can be edited via pencil icon; completed surveys can be exported to PDF or deleted
