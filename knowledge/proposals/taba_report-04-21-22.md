## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies engaged EKase Consulting to provide market research and commercialization support for its Expendable Sonobuoy-Launched Unmanned Aerial Vehicle designed for Anti-Submarine Warfare (ASW) cued search, detection, tracking, and classification. The TABA engagement ($6,500 expended) delivered validated market analysis, competitive intelligence, and a comprehensive "hit list" of government and commercial decision-makers across multiple potential customer sectors.

## Technical Approach
EKase Consulting employed a dual research methodology:

**Primary Research:**
- Identification of subject matter experts, potential customers, and prospective partners
- Generation of detailed contact lists with specific points of contact (names, emails, roles)
- Status reporting at regular intervals

**Secondary Research:**
- Literature reviews and analysis of market research reports
- Trend analysis across global sectors
- Competitor analysis, including SBIR competing awards profiling
- Market quantification and financial projections

## Products & Capabilities Described

### Expendable Sonobuoy-Launched Unmanned Aerial Vehicle
- **What it is:** UAS platform designed for anti-submarine warfare applications
- **Primary capability:** ASW cued search, detection, tracking, and classification
- **Proposed deployment context:** Launched from sonobuoys; expendable platform
- **Compliance pathway:** Identified as potential candidate for Blue UAS (DIU) and Green UAS (AUVSI) certification programs to address NDAA foreign component restrictions

## Use Cases & Applications
The TABA report identified BST's technology as relevant to multiple distinct market sectors:

1. **Military ASW Operations** (Navy primary focus)
   - Air Anti-Submarine Warfare Systems Program (PMA-264)
   - Small Tactical UAS Program (PMA-263)
   - Office of Naval Research (ONR)

2. **Unexploded Ordnance (UXO) Detection**
   - Magnetic detection methods ($2.07 billion market segment in 2024)
   - Acoustic detection methods
   - Total UXO market valued at $8.98 billion (2024)

3. **Marine Mammal Research**
   - Federal funding: $87.87 million identified
   - Primary funders: NOAA (52% of funding), Navy (26%)
   - Commercial and academic customer base

4. **Abandoned Well Detection (Oil & Gas)**
   - Identified as emerging market driven by state regulations (Colorado and others)
   - Commercial sector opportunity

5. **Other Military Applications**
   - Air Force ISR-SOF operations
   - Army aviation and maneuver operations

6. **International Applications**
   - Canadian Fisheries and Oceans operations
   - UK joint defense programs
   - Norwegian marine research
   - Japanese government agencies
