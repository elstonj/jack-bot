## Executive Summary
This document details the component-level breakdown and sourcing for a photogrammetry payload integration on the S2 aircraft for volcano monitoring applications under a NASA SBIR Phase III contract. It includes itemized costs, supplier invoices, and notes on BST-manufactured vs. commercial components, with a total payload system cost of $2,527.00.

## Technical Approach
The photogrammetry payload integrates commercial and BST-designed components:
- **Image capture**: Sony camera with SEL16F28 lens and battery adapter
- **Flight control**: BST Actuator Boards (qty 4) for servo/motor control via PWM
- **Navigation/Positioning**: BST GNSS board with magnetometer (GNSS-MAG v06)
- **Power**: Custom 6S4P 18650 lithium battery pack (21.6V, 14,000mAh), voltage regulators (5V and 7.5V)
- **Airframe integration**: Kevlar nose cone, BST Generic Payload Scaffold, forward bulkhead, custom 3D-printed mounting hardware
- **Structural**: Hacker 0806 motor/drive component, DB9 and USB connectors, mounting hardware

### Suppliers & Sourcing
