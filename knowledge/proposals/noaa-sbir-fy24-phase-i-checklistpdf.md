## Executive Summary
This is a compliance checklist for organizations applying to the NOAA SBIR FY24 Phase I program. It outlines eligibility requirements, necessary registrations, and complete application components required for submission through Grants.gov.

## Content Overview

### Eligibility Requirements
- Applicant must qualify as a Small Business Concern (SBC) per SBA regulations
- Letter of Intent required by specified deadline
- Principal Investigator must be employed by the company at time of award and throughout duration
- All work must be performed in the United States; supplies/materials/equipment should be American-made (waivers available if needed)
- Minimum two-thirds of research/analytical effort must be performed by the applicant small business
- At least two-thirds of total funding must be allocated to the applicant

### Required Registrations
- System for Award Management (SAM) — registration takes over one month, annual renewal required
- Small Business Administration (SBA) — registration takes up to 90 days
- Grants.gov — registration typically takes 3-10 business days

### Application Components Required

**Project Narrative (10-15 pages, minimum 10-point font):**
- SBIR Cover Page
- Abstract (no proprietary information)
- 13 Technical Content sections (with N/A option if not applicable)
- Bibliography and References Cited
- Letters of Commitment (from consultants, subawards, subcontractors)
- Letters of Support (optional but strongly recommended)

**Budget Documentation:**
- Detailed budget narrative with amounts matching SF-424 and SF-424A forms
- Awardee and sub-awardee budget narratives (if applicable)

**Required Forms:**
- SF-424 (Application for Federal Assistance)
- SF-424A (Budget Information – Non Construction Programs)
- SF-424B (Assurances – Non-Construction Programs)
- CD-511 (Certification Regarding Lobbying)
- SF-LLL (Disclosure of Lobbying Activities, if applicable)
- NICRA (Indirect Cost Rate Agreement, if applicable)
- Applicant Fraud, Waste, and Abuse (FWA) Awareness Training
- R/RD Personal Data (Research & Related Personal Data)
- Disclosure of Foreign Relationships

## Notable Details
- All forms must be signed by the Authorized Organizational Representative (AOR)
- Applicants should add noaa.sbir@noaa.gov to contacts for timely follow-up communications
- Technical support available through Grants.gov Applicant Support for submission issues
- Application rejection may result from failure to meet any of these requirements
