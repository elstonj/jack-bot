## Executive Summary
This is a standard federal ESOH compliance questionnaire submitted as part of Black Swift Technologies' SBIR Phase I proposal for a Soil Moisture Mapping project under AFWERX sponsorship. The document captures BST's responses to 10 required questions regarding hazardous materials, processes, substances, and environmental/safety considerations related to the proposed research effort.

## Document Purpose & Content
This is a compliance/administrative document—a mandatory questionnaire rather than a technical proposal. It consists of 10 yes/no questions with space for detailed responses where applicable, covering:

1. Use of hazardous materials (per Federal Standard 313D and 40 CFR 260-279)
2. Use of explosives or ammunition
3. Hazardous processes (heavy equipment, lasers, RF radiation, high voltage >600V, high pressure >60 psig, high temperature >50°C)
4. On-site work at U.S. Air Force installations
5. Purchase, storage, use, or delivery of chemicals/hazardous materials to USAF facilities
6. Generation of hazardous chemical or waste
7. Class I ozone-depleting substances
8. Radioactive materials
9. Asbestos, radiation, or chemical-generating components for USAF delivery
10. Special atmospheric or water resource requirements
