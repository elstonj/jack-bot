## Executive Summary
This document provides procedural instructions for Black Swift Technologies and other SBIR applicants on how to complete and submit Volume 4 (Company Commercialization Report) as part of DoD SBIR Phase II proposal submissions. It outlines the requirement to download the CCR from SBIR.gov, upload it to the DSIP system, and explains the workflow for firms with and without prior SBIR/STTR awards.

## Content Summary
This is procedural/administrative guidance with no technical content related to BST's products or capabilities. The document explains:

- **Purpose of CCR**: Required reporting of funding outcomes from prior SBIR and STTR awards
- **Maintenance requirement**: Companies must update their CCR upon completion of final deliverables, then annually for minimum 5 years
- **Submission process**: Five-step procedure to access, update, and download CCR from SBIR.gov, then upload to DSIP
- **Conditional requirements**: 
  - Required if firm has prior Phase I or Phase II SBIR/STTR awards
  - Not required for firms with no prior awards
- **Critical warning**: Uploading a new CCR automatically reopens ALL proposals under any open BAAs/CSOs and requires recertification/resubmission

## Notable Details
- Air Force will NOT evaluate the CCR during proposal evaluation—it is a compliance/administrative requirement only
- Only Firm Admin has authority to upload CCR to Firm Forms section of DSIP
- System triggers automatic reopening of all open proposals if CCR is updated, creating significant administrative burden
- Template attribution: "SBIR Advisors Template (10252022/mm)"
