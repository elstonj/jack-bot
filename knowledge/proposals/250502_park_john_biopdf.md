## Executive Summary
Dr. Hyoshin (John) Park is an Associate Professor of Engineering Management & Systems Engineering at Old Dominion University with extensive expertise in computational data science, intelligent transportation systems, machine learning, and autonomous systems. His research spans traffic prediction, emergency response optimization, sensor networks, autonomous vehicles, robotics, and recently extends to hurricane sensing and space applications. He maintains active collaborations with NASA JPL and has published 158 peer-reviewed papers with strong presence in top-tier venues.
