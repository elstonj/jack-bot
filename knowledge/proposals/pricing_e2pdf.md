## Executive Summary
This document presents pricing for the Black Swift E2 autonomous inspection UAS system and its associated payloads, training, maintenance, and flight services. The E2 is positioned as an American-made, purpose-built platform for structural and industrial inspections capable of operating in extreme environmental conditions.

## Products & Capabilities Described

### Black Swift E2 UAS Flight System - $28,094
- **What it is:** American-designed, manufactured, and serviced autonomous inspection drone
- **Components included:** 
  - E2 UAS airframe
  - SwiftCore™ Flight Management System with SwiftTab™ Android-based control tablet interface
  - Futaba 14SG Handset
  - SwiftStation™ RTK-enabled Ground Control Unit
  - Toolkit, tripod, battery, charger, hard-sided shipping/storage case
- **Key features:** Forward-mounted payload bay (full field-of-view including upward looking), quick-change payload capability, compact/easily transportable (folds for case transport), adjustable battery placement
- **Warranty:** 12 months manufacturer defects coverage; Flight Management System software warrantied 2 years with free upgrades

### E2 Photogrammetry Payload - $12,104
- Sony A7R IV camera with 28mm Sony lens mounted on gimbal
- Note: Additional flight-ready payloads available; custom payload integration offered

### E2 Battery (Spare) - $3,669
- Custom Li-ion 42,000mAh (6-cell system)

### E2 Flight Training - $3,000
- 2 days of instruction including flight simulator and actual flight training
- Additional support available at $174/hour

### E2 Flight Services - $2,500/flight day plus travel
- Part 107 certified pilot in command and dedicated observer provided
- Optional E2 rental available at $1,000/flight day

### Black Swift Flight Secure Maintenance Package - $2,500/year or $250/month
- Log file review with performance matrix reporting
- Pre-flight planning assistance (up to 3 flights/year)
- Dataset triage and troubleshooting
- Airframe inspection and maintenance (every 100 flights)
- Technical phone support
- Access to training webinars and online video tutorials
