## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies will support NASA's AEROKATS and ROVER Education Network as a named participant, providing engineering and technical support for educational workshops using kites for environmental exploration. The project includes development of materials, prototype evaluation, and feasibility assessment of commercial products (AeroPod and Aerovator), with work conducted remotely from Colorado and Virginia locations.

## Technical Approach
- Planning and design of kite-based educational workshop materials
- Fabrication of wood and plastic components for distributed materials
- Development and evaluation of prototypes for new systems (AeroPod and Aerovator)
- Material development, shipping, and distribution to educational institutions and partner organizations
- All work performed remotely; no NASA facility access or IT system interface required
