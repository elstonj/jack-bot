## Executive Summary
This is a mandatory DoD compliance certification form signed by Black Swift Technologies confirming adherence to federal regulations prohibiting the use of covered defense telecommunications equipment or services (specifically from Huawei, ZTE, or entities connected to China or Russia) in covered DoD missions. ## Regulatory Framework & Key Requirements
The document incorporates three DFARS provisions:

**DFARS 252.204-7016 (Covered Defense Telecommunications Equipment or Services Representation)**
- Requires offerors to review the System for Award Management (SAM) exclusion list
- Requires representation of whether covered telecommunications equipment/services are provided

**DFARS 252.204-7017 (Prohibition on Acquisition of Covered Defense Telecommunications Equipment or Services)**
- Applies if contractor represents that it DOES provide covered defense telecommunications equipment/services
- Requires detailed disclosures including: description of equipment/services, proposed use, entity providing services, and equipment producer information

**DFARS 252.204-7018 (Prohibition on Acquisition of Covered Defense Telecommunications Equipment or Services - Jan 2023)**
- Defines "covered defense telecommunications equipment or services": equipment from Huawei Technologies, ZTE Corporation, or entities owned/controlled by governments of China or Russia
- Defines "covered foreign countries": People's Republic of China and Russian Federation
- Defines "covered missions": nuclear deterrence mission and homeland defense mission (ballistic missile defense)
- Defines "critical technology": ITAR-controlled items, Commerce Control List items, nuclear equipment, select agents/toxins, and emerging/foundational technologies
- Prohibits contractors from providing equipment/systems for covered missions using covered telecommunications equipment unless waivered
- Requires reporting to https://dibnet.dod.mil within 3 business days if covered equipment is identified during contract performance, with follow-up reporting within 30 business days
