## Executive Summary
This document justifies the selection of the University of Miami under Dr. Jun Zhang as the sole-source research institution partner for a Navy STTR Phase II project on hazardous weather. Dr. Zhang's specialized expertise in tropical cyclones, boundary layer meteorology, and air-sea interactions, combined with unique research infrastructure, makes him uniquely qualified and difficult to replicate elsewhere.

## Research Institution Selection Rationale

**Selected Partner:**
- University of Miami, led by Dr. Jun Zhang

**Justification for Sole Source Selection:**
1. **Specialized Expertise**: Dr. Zhang has recognized expertise in:
   - Tropical cyclones
   - Boundary layer meteorology
   - Air-sea interactions
   - All directly relevant to key technical components of the project

2. **Established Track Record**: Prior research contributions through University of Miami and NOAA provide a foundation uniquely suited to the proposed effort

3. **Unique Infrastructure & Capability**: Dr. Zhang's team offers:
   - Access to specialized research infrastructure
   - Established analytical techniques
   - Difficult to replicate elsewhere without significant investment

4. **Risk Mitigation & Timeline**: Selection ensures:
   - Continuity in the work
   - Minimized technical risk
   - Leverage of existing work supporting project goals
   - Capability to meet required schedule and budget constraints

## Notable Details
- No other source identified as capable of providing this combination of expertise and capability within project constraints
- Document demonstrates BST's strategy of partnering with specialized academic institutions for weather/hazardous environment research
