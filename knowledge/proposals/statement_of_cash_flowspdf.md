## Executive Summary
This document contains four statements of cash flows spanning 2012-2015 (through February 25), showing Black Swift Technologies' operating cash generation, member distributions, and equity contributions during the company's early years. The statements indicate the company transitioned from minimal cash flow in 2012 ($1,880 from operations) to more substantial operating cash generation by 2014 ($132,745), with significant member distributions occurring in 2014.
