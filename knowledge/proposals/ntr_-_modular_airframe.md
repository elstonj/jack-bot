## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies modified the Apollo airframe into a highly capable modular platform featuring tool-less, field-swappable payload bays and aircraft components. The design prioritizes rapid sensor swapping and ruggedness for deployment in rough terrain, cold weather, and inclement conditions while maintaining hand-launch/landing capability.

## Technical Approach
The CAPS airframe abstracted the Apollo reference design into functional modules designed for tool-free field assembly and removal. Key modifications include:
- **Forward-mounted modular payload bay** replacing traditional nose cone
- **Standardized mechanical and electrical connections** enabling interchangeable payload modules
- **Forward-swept wing design** providing spanwise efficiency and favorable center-of-gravity positioning for larger forward-mounted payloads
- **Two payload bay designs** in 3D CAD to support various sensor sizes and types
- **Tool-less assembly/disassembly** throughout airframe for rapid component replacement in field conditions

## Products & Capabilities Described

**Apollo/CAPS Airframe**
- Base: Rocketship Systems Apollo reference design
- Key feature: Tool-less field-swappable nose cone (payload bay) and other major components
- Supports hand launch and landing in unimproved terrain
- Designed to operate in rough terrain, inclement weather, and cold temperatures
- Enables rapid payload cycling during field operations without tools

## Use Cases & Applications
- **Multi-platform cooperative missions** requiring coordinated sensing
- **Plume monitoring** (volcanic, industrial, etc.)
- **Atmospheric remote sensing**
- **Radiation and particulate monitoring**
- **Radio frequency monitoring and localization**
- **Low-altitude operations** in hazardous environmental flight conditions
- **Arctic and remote field deployments** with challenging logistics

## Notable Details
- **Competitive Advantage:** Tool-less swappable components unique among small UAS designs; enables operations in cold/inclement weather where manual assembly would be impractical
- **Design Philosophy:** Balances maximum performance with ease of use through modularity
- **Payload Flexibility:** Multiple sensors can be carried on a single deployment and cycled throughout operations without tools, allowing focus on data gathering rather than logistics
- **Ruggedness:** Designed to handle rough terrain operations and quick replacement of damaged components
