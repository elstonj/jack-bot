## Executive Summary
This is a regulatory compliance document required under DFARS provision 252.209-7002 for BST's response to an Air Force AFWERX SBIR Phase II proposal. The form discloses any foreign government ownership or control of Black Swift Technologies that would be reportable under DoD contracting rules.

## Regulatory Context
The document establishes the legal framework for disclosure requirements:

**Applicability**: Required for any DoD contract where an offeror (contractor) is being evaluated for award, particularly for national security programs.

**Key Definitions**:
- "Effectively owned or controlled" - A foreign government (or entity controlled by one) has power to control election, appointment, or tenure of company officers or board majority, directly or indirectly
- "Entity controlled by a foreign government" - Any domestic or foreign organization effectively owned/controlled by a foreign government, OR any individual acting on behalf of a foreign government
- "Foreign government" - Includes any state, country government (excluding U.S.), and any political subdivision, agency, or instrumentality thereof

**Proscribed Information Categories** (access restrictions apply):
- Top Secret information
- Communications Security (COMSEC) material (excluding unkeyed cryptographic items)
- Restricted Data (per Atomic Energy Act of 1954)
- Special Access Program (SAP) information
- Sensitive Compartmented Information (SCI)

## Prohibition & Waiver Authority
- **Contract Award Prohibition**: No national security program contract may be awarded to an entity controlled by a foreign government if that entity requires access to proscribed information, **unless** the Secretary of Defense or designee waives application of 10 U.S.C. 4874

## Disclosure Requirements
The form requires disclosure of:
1. Any foreign government interest in BST that constitutes "control"
2. For subsidiary entities: reportable interests in immediate parent, intermediate parents, and ultimate parent
3. Offeror point of contact for disclosure questions
4. Entity information (name, address)
5. Description and percentage of foreign government ownership/control
6. Identification of foreign government(s) involved
