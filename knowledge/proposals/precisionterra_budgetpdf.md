## Executive Summary
Budget proposal for Black Swift Technologies' participation in a NASA-funded PrecisionTerra project focused on developing a software-based positioning, navigation, and timing (PNT) solution for GPS-degraded environments. Total project budget request: $91,833 for budget period 1.

## Technical Approach
Software-based positioning, navigation, and timing solution designed to operate reliably in GPS-degraded environments. Project involves:
- Software development (Dan: 550 estimated hours)
- Design/integration work (Alex: 100 hours)
- Technical support (Nate: 20 hours)
- Data analysis/PI oversight (Maciej: 50 hours)
- Field data collection via 2-3 flights (30-minute duration each)

## Products & Capabilities Described

- **E2**: Equipment rental; $1,000 allocated (specific function not detailed in budget)

## Use Cases & Applications
