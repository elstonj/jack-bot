## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies will design, build, and flight-test two carbon fiber HALO airframes for ByLight, integrating BST avionics and gimbal systems. The effort spans 8 weeks across two scopes: airframe design and validation (Scope 1, 4/3/26–5/30/26) and construction and flight testing of two operational platforms (Scope 2, within one month of fuselage receipt). The total fixed-price contract is $105,991.

## Technical Approach

**Scope 1: Halo Airframe Design & Testing (4/3/26–5/30/26; $47,894)**
- Design and construct pouch cell battery with higher capacity to fit within fuselage without Hawkeye Munitions payload
- Interim mount BST Avionics into current Halo design
- Integrate LW20/C Laser Altimeter
- Design attachment points for car launch system (retractable for skid landing compatibility)
- Attach boom, tail, and tail surfaces; confirm center of gravity and aerodynamic properties
- Replace/update corroded car launch fasteners and railing as needed
- Conduct full 400km mission flight test
- Validate airframe durability for multiple skid landings and car launches
- Validate 6G dive flight maneuver capability
- Analyze flight test results; identify and correct CAD design weaknesses
- Design retractable hard-mount points for car launch
- Design gimbal deployment mechanism (Trillium/alternative)
- Design hot-swap avionics system (SwiftCore or EchoPilot compatibility)
- Deliver CAD files with manufacturing recommendations to ByLight

**Scope 2: Two Carbon Fiber HALO Builds (within 1 month of fuselage receipt; $58,097)**
- Build two carbon fiber Halo (CF Halo) airframes; attach wing and tail sets
- Test gimbal implementation, avionics hot-swap system, retracting launch mount points, laser altimeter functionality
- Build smaller battery design for Hawkeye payload configuration
- Flight test both CF Halo aircraft; tune autopilots for flight efficiency
- Validate 400km range capability (ISR non-munitions configuration)
- Ensure platform consistency and durability across multiple launches and skid landings
- Package and ship both CF Halo aircraft to ByLight headquarters

## Products & Capabilities Described

**SwiftCore / EchoPilot (Avionics)**
- BST avionics systems designed for hot-swap integration into Halo airframe
- Integrated into interim mounting configuration (Scope 1) and permanent hot-swap system (Scope 2)

**Laser Altimeter (LW20/C)**
- LiDAR altimeter integrated into Halo design
- Functionality validated during Scope 2 flight testing

**Trillium Gimbal / Alternative Gimbal**
- Gimbal deployment mechanism designed for Halo airframe
- CAD design for deployment mechanism included in Scope 1 deliverables
- Implementation tested during Scope 2 flight testing

**Halo Airframe**
- Carbon fiber construction (CF Halo)
- Fuselage designed for battery integration and avionics/gimbal mounting
- Capable of car launch and skid landing operations
- Structural validation for 6G dive maneuvers
- Designed for 400km range ISR missions (non-munitions configuration)

## Use Cases & Applications

- **ISR (Intelligence, Surveillance, Reconnaissance):** Non-munitions configuration with 400km operational range
- **Hawkeye Munitions Integration:** Smaller battery design accommodates Hawkeye payload; range performance with munitions not guaranteed (stated as potentially <400km due to increased drag)
- **Car Launch & Skid Landing Operations:** Airframe designed for repeated launch/recovery cycles via automotive launch system and skid landings
- **High-G Flight:** Platform validated for 6G dive maneuvers
