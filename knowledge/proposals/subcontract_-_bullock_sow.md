## Executive Summary
This subcontract statement of work defines Co-Investigator Bullock's role in developing scientific goals, investigations, and payloads for a Venus lower atmosphere glider platform. His work focuses on atmospheric visibility modeling and integration of Venus atmospheric models into flight simulations.

## Technical Approach
- Development of scientific goals and investigations for the Venus glider mission
- Scientific payload design and integration
- Modeling of Venus surface visibility at various altitudes
- Incorporation of Venus atmospheric models into flight simulation software

## Products & Capabilities Described
**Venus Lower Atmosphere Glider**
- An atmospheric glider designed to operate in Venus's lower atmosphere
- Requires integration of Venus atmospheric models for flight simulation
- Expected to carry scientific payloads for atmospheric investigation

## Use Cases & Applications
- Venus atmospheric exploration and characterization
- Lower atmosphere measurements and observation of Venus surface visibility

- Focus on atmospheric modeling and visibility presents challenges specific to Venus's harsh environment (extreme temperatures, pressures, sulfuric acid clouds)
