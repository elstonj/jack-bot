## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies proposes the S0 VTOL, a lightweight, rapidly deployable fixed-wing VTOL aircraft for expeditionary ISR, counter-UAS operations, and environmental characterization. The system enables persistent airborne sensing from austere locations without runway infrastructure, combining fixed-wing persistence with VTOL operational flexibility for DoD counter-UAS and air defense missions.

## Technical Approach
**S0 VTOL Design Philosophy:**
- Fixed-wing VTOL hybrid configuration emphasizing rapid deployment from austere sites
- Lightweight design enabling small-team launch and operation
- Modular, open-architecture avionics for mission adaptation and integration with C5ISR ground systems
- Supervisory autonomy with degraded-PNT (positioning, navigation, timing) capability
- High-wind and complex-terrain operational capability

**Operational Advantages Over Alternatives:**
- Outperforms multirotor systems in range and endurance
- Faster response than multirotor platforms
- Greater area coverage per sortie
- Extended custody maintenance with reduced operator burden and fewer sorties

## Use Cases & Applications

**Military (DoD)**
- ISR extension for counter-UAS (counter-unmanned aerial systems) operations

- Deployment from austere, unprepared sites
- Expeditionary overwatch supporting air defense operations
- Environmental characterization in high-wind, complex terrain environments
- Integration with existing DoD C5ISR architectures

**Commercial**
