## Products & Capabilities Described

### HS-5065MG Servo (Hitec)
**What it is:** A high-performance digital programmable micro servo with metal gears and precision positioning.

**Specifications:**
- Dimensions: 0.92" × 0.45" × 0.94" (23.37 × 11.43 × 23.88 mm)
- Weight: 0.42 oz (11.9 g)
- Stall Torque: 24.99 oz/in @ 4.8V; 30.55 oz/in @ 6.0V (1.8–2.2 kg·cm)
- Speed: 0.14 sec/60° @ 4.8V; 0.11 sec/60° @ 6.0V
- Voltage Range: 4.8V – 6.0V
- Max Travel: 128° (out of box); 181° (reprogrammed)
- Operating Temperature: −20°C to +60°C
- Output Shaft: 25-tooth (B1) spline
- Gear Material: Metal with top ball bearing
- Motor Type: Carbon brush
- Feedback: 5K Ω potentiometer
- Current Drain (idle): 3 mA @ both 4.8V and 6.0V
- Current Drain (no-load): 200 mA @ 6V; 240 mA @ 7.4V
- Current Drain (stall): 2 A @ 4.8V; 3 A @ 6V

**Programmable Features:**
- Dead band width
- Direction of rotation
- Speed of rotation (slower)
- End points (up to 120°)
- Neutral points
- Fail-safe on/off and fail-safe points
- Resolution (default high; can be reprogrammed for normal)
- Overload protection (default off)

**Intended Use (inferred):** Light-duty actuation for micro aerial systems, given inclusion in NOAA Phase II proposal materials.
