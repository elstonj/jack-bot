## Executive Summary
Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University is a subcontractor to Black Swift Technologies on a Navy STTR Phase I project focused on improving Black Swift S0 sampling capabilities in challenging atmospheric regions, particularly for air-sea profiling observations in hazardous weather conditions. The 12-month effort (July 2025–June 2026) is valued at $96,441 and includes a University of Miami subcontractor.
