## Notable Details

**Regulatory Framework Covered:**
- Arms Export Control Act (AECA, 22 USC §2778)
- International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR, 22 CFR §120-130)
- United States Munitions List (USML, 22 CFR §121)
- Export Control Reform (ECR, 50 USC §4801-4851)
- Export Administration Regulations (EAR, 15 CFR §730-774)
- Commerce Control List (CCL, 15 CFR §774)

**DoD Distribution Statements (A through F):** Document establishes standard classification framework from public release (Statement A) to restricted dissemination (Statement F).

**Penalties for Violations:**
- AECA: up to $1,000,000 fine and/or 20 years imprisonment
- ECR (criminal): up to $1,000,000 fine and/or 20 years imprisonment
- ECR (civil): up to $300,000 or twice the value of transaction (whichever greater), license revocation, export prohibition

**Export Control Warning:** Documents containing export-controlled technical data must be marked with export control warnings noting restriction under AECA or Export Administration Act.

**Key Compliance Points:**
- Export controls apply to both tangible (models, blueprints, photographs) and intangible (technical services, oral descriptions) transfers
- Controls cover all transfer methods: emails, faxes, face-to-face conversations
- Providing controlled technology to foreign persons (regardless of location) is deemed equivalent to physical export
- Transferees are responsible for obtaining necessary authorizations before transfer
