## Executive Summary
This draft annual report documents Black Swift Technologies' progress on a Colorado OEDIT-funded methane leak detection project. BST developed and tested methane sensing and analysis capabilities integrated with their S2 UAS platform, partnering with anchor customer PDCE and analysis partner Battelle. The project experienced technical and programmatic delays but is positioning for commercialization with early-stage investment rounds in progress.

## Technical Approach

**Core Development:**
- Built second-article Q3 methane sensor
- Integrated sensor to S2 UAS platform
- Developed test criteria/objectives with anchor customer PDCE
- Conducted initial well-site demonstrations (January 2023)
- Partnered with Battelle Corporation for methane sensing analysis and eddy-covariance methodology application

**Key Technical Innovations:**
- Absolute three-axis wind speed measurement from BST UAS platform
- Real-time flight control updating based on sensor inputs
- Development of VTOL fixed-wing UAS capability
- Application of eddy-covariance analysis to BST UAS sensor data
