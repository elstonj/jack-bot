## Executive Summary
BAE Systems solicits Black Swift Technologies to submit an Unclassified Firm Fixed Price Milestone (FFP Milestone) proposal for tasks supporting the DARPA Albatross Program. The effort is valued at a NTE of $2,500,000 with anticipated performance from April 1, 2025 to March 31, 2027. This is a subcontract under BAE Systems' prime contract with DARPA.

## Notable Details

### Contract & Financial Terms
- **Contract Type**: Firm Fixed Price Milestone (FFP Milestone)
- **NTE Value**: $2,500,000
- **Period of Performance**: April 1, 2025 – March 31, 2027
- **Proposal Validity**: 180 days
- **NAICS Code**: 541330
- **Size Standard**: $25.5M
- **Anticipated Award Date**: January 2025 (estimated)
- **Payment Terms**: F.O.B. Destination; no advance payments available

### Proposal Requirements
- **Format**: Unclassified, one copy in editable Microsoft Excel with all formulas intact (no PDF spreadsheets)
- **Cost Breakdown Required**: 
  - Summary and detailed cost breakdown by calendar year and cost element
  - Direct labor by category with hours and rates
  - Indirect costs (fringe, overhead, G&A, fee) with rates
  - Travel with detailed trip-by-trip justification
  - Subcontracts, materials, equipment, other direct costs
  - Non-recurring engineering (NRE) must be segregated and identified separately

### Compliance & Security Requirements
- **Export Control**: Data is export-controlled under ITAR and EAR; BST prohibited from disclosing to foreign nationals or entities without authorization
- **Counterfeit Parts Prevention**: Must comply with BAE Systems counterfeit parts prevention provisions if delivering assemblies
- **CUI Protection**: Controlled Unclassified Information must be protected with physical/electronic barriers; no public posting; encrypted mobile communications
- **Data Rights Assertions**: BST must identify any proprietary technical data, software, or IP to be delivered with restrictions (or state "NONE")
- **Patent Documentation**: BST must provide documentation of ownership or licensing rights for all patented inventions to be used
- **Organizational Conflicts of Interest**: Full disclosure required regarding any SETA, A&AS, or advisory support to DARPA technical offices; BST must state current support or state "NONE"

### Personnel & Team Requirements
- **Key Personnel Documentation**: Up to one-page per person showing qualifications, previous accomplishments, work in related areas, hours of effort by phase, and other current/proposed commitments
- **Key Personnel Table**: Must show individual names, project status (current/pending/proposed), and hours per phase
- **Team Member List**: All team members, subcontractors, consultants must be listed with role, organization, non-US status, and FFRDC/Government entity designation

### Audit & Financial Requirements
- **Cost or Pricing Data**: Required if proposal exceeds $2,000,000 (must complete DFARS Adequacy Checklist, Enclosure G)
- **CAS Compliance**: If exceeding $2,000,000, must return CAS Notices & Certification form (Enclosure H)
- **Accounting System**: DCMA-approved accounting system required; provide approval letter if cost-type award anticipated
- **Pre-award Audit**: Proposer must state whether BAE Systems auditor will have access to financial data
- **Indirect Rates**: If approved/negotiated by Government agency, provide copy of memorandum; if not approved, provide detailed basis of estimate
- **Small Business Subcontracting Plan**: If BST is a large business and proposal exceeds $700,000, FAR 52.219-9 compliant plan required

### Reporting & Documentation
- **Monthly Financial Status Reports**: Minimum reporting requirement per Statement of Work
- **Equipment/Facilities**: If GFE/CFE required, provide list with need dates; if using Government facilities, provide alternative offers (with and without rent)
- **Debarment Certification**: By submitting quotation over $30,000, BST certifies principals are not debarred, suspended, or proposed for debarment

### Intellectual Property & Proprietary Considerations
- **Data Rights Format**: Must use DFARS 252.227-7028 format for proprietary data assertions
- **Proprietary Proposal Option**: If BST has proprietary pricing information not releasable to BAE Systems, may submit proprietary version directly to DARPA (with prior notification to BAE Systems); non-proprietary version still required in BAE Systems prime submission
- **Open Source Software**: Commercial open source tools permitted but BST may be required to indemnify Government against legal liability; if delivering open source, must make applicable assertion
- **Publication Restrictions**: May apply

### Other Notable Provisions
- **No Obligation to Reimburse**: BAE Systems not obligated to pay costs for proposal preparation/submission
- **Best and Final Offer (BAFO)**: BAE Systems reserves right to request BAFO
- **Award Without Discussion**: Award may be made without proposal discussion; initial submission should represent best price and technical position
- **Proprietary Handling**: All RFP information treated as confidential; no public release without prior written BAE Systems approval
- **Non-Disclosure**: BST must not disclose RFP information to anyone without need-to-know
