# Black Swift Technologies SMU Letter of Support

## Document Metadata
- Type: Letter of commitment/support
- Client/Agency: Department of Energy (DOE), Southern Methodist University (SMU)
- Program/Solicitation: DE-FOA-0003343, Area of Interest 3 - Long-Term UOW Monitoring
- Date: October 29, 2024
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: S2 UAS, Quanta3 methane spectrometer, Black Swift S2 Methane Leak Detection (MLD) solution
- Key Personnel: Saeed Salehi (SMU Principal Investigator), Stefan Metzger (Ph.D., subcontractor for data analysis), Stuart Stein and Jeff Stein MBA (Hyde Park West Telecommunications, project management)

## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies commits to subcontract with Southern Methodist University on a DOE-funded proposal to develop and test advanced methane emissions monitoring technologies for Undocumented Orphaned Wells (UOWs). BST will provide field trial services using its S2 UAS platform equipped with a Quanta3 methane spectrometer, conducting at least 50 drone flights over three years for a total subcontract value of $388,546.

## Technical Approach
- **Primary sensor platform**: Black Swift S2 UAS integrated with custom-built Quanta3 methane spectrometer for methane leak detection and quantification
- **Field deployment method**: Unmanned aerial system conducting drone flights to remote, hard-to-reach locations
- **Complementary methodology**: Custom eddy covariance techniques (derived from flux tower methodologies) for precise gas exchange measurements
- **Validation approach**: Cross-validation with Xplorobot measurements (full 3 years) and Licor Methane Systems evaluation (6 months, Year 2)
- **Supplementary monitoring**: Subcontracted flux tower continuous monitoring via Dr. Stefan Metzger for Years 2-3 ($45,000/year)

## Products & Capabilities Described

### Black Swift S2 UAS
- **Type**: Rugged, high-endurance fixed-wing unmanned aircraft
- **Specifications**:
  - Flight endurance: up to 90 minutes
  - Wingspan: 3 meters
  - Payload capacity: 2 kg
- **Capabilities**: Long-range environmental monitoring, operation in demanding environments (rugged terrain, low temperatures)
- **Proposed use**: Primary platform for methane leak detection in hard-to-reach locations including remote oil/gas fields, mines, landfills, agricultural areas

### Quanta3 Methane Spectrometer
- **Type**: Custom-built methane detection and quantification instrument
- **Proposed use**: Payload integration with S2 UAS for sensitive methane emissions detection
- **Features**: Advanced capabilities enabling unique, highly sensitive detection platform

### Black Swift S2 Methane Leak Detection (MLD) Solution
- **What it is**: Integrated system combining S2 UAS with Quanta3 methane spectrometer
- **Capabilities**: Detection and quantification of methane emissions
- **Performance claim**: "Unique and highly sensitive platform"

### Flux Tower Monitoring Equipment (subcontracted)
- **Technology Readiness Level**: TRL-5
- **Components**: 7 m tower, sensors, data logger
- **Services included**: Annual maintenance, FluxMapTM analytics, data delivery, non-private data license
- **Duration**: 3 months monitoring in Years 2 and 3
