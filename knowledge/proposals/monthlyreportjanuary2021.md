## Executive Summary
This January 2021 progress report documents work on the AREN project, focusing on UHIP instrument assembly and integration, tutorial video production for public engagement, and website development. Primary challenges centered on resolving microSD data logging issues with an otherwise operational UHIP package.

- Development of assembly instruction improvements

**Website & Documentation:**
