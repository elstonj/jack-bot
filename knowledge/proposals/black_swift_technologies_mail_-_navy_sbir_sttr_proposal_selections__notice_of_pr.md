- Client/Agency: U.S. Navy (NAVAIR SBIR/STTR Program)

- Date: May 5, 2025 (notification sent); July 8, 2025 (email forwarded internally)
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: None specifically named
- Key Personnel: Beck Cotter (BST recipient), Megan E. Sloane (Navy contact), Kristi DePriest (NAVAIR SBIR/STTR Program Manager)

## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies has been selected as an apparently successful offeror for a Navy SBIR Phase I contract (N251-016-0021) for an "Expendable Sonobuoy-Launched Unmanned Aerial Vehicle for ASW Cued Search, Detection, Tracking, and Classification." This is an official notification that BST's proposal was evaluated and recommended for contract award.

## Technical Approach
Not detailed in this notification letter. The technical approach would be contained in BST's original proposal, which is not included in this document.

## Products & Capabilities Described
No specific BST products are named in this notification. However, the topic title indicates the project involves:
- An unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV)
- Expendable sonobuoy launcher/deployment capability
- Anti-submarine warfare (ASW) application
- Search, detection, tracking, and classification functions

## Use Cases & Applications
- **Anti-Submarine Warfare (ASW):** Cued search, detection, tracking, and classification of submarines
- **Sonobuoy Integration:** Deployment and coordination with expendable sonobuoy systems
