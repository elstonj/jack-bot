## Executive Summary
University of Colorado Boulder's Integrated Remote and In Situ Sensing (IRISS) lab offered pilot services for flight operations support of NOAA's Penguin B aircraft near Boulder, Colorado, at a rate of $1,200/day for the August-September 2017 timeframe.

## Technical Approach
IRISS would provide pilot services on an as-needed basis for flight operations support. The engagement involved no formal deliverables beyond the services themselves; results and data generated would be retained by the University for academic, educational, and research purposes while BST (the customer) maintained title to any direct deliverables.

## Products & Capabilities Described
- **Penguin B Aircraft**: A NOAA platform that BST was operating; the University was contracted to provide experienced pilot support for flight operations in the Boulder, CO area.

## Use Cases & Applications
- Flight operations support for NOAA's Penguin B aircraft
- Regional operations near Boulder, Colorado

## Contract Terms
- **Rate**: $1,200/day (billed in full-day increments), plus applicable sales tax
- **Performance Period**: August 1, 2017 – September 30, 2017
- **Payment Terms**: Net 30 from invoice receipt
- **Quote Validity**: 45 days from quotation date
- **Termination**: Either party may terminate with 30 days' prior written notice
