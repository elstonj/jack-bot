## Executive Summary
This document outlines Jack Elston's research funding portfolio at BST as of late 2019/early 2020, including four active NASA/NOAA/USAF SBIR projects totaling approximately $2.8M focused on developing specialized UAS platforms for scientific observations in harsh and challenging environments (volcanic plumes, Venus atmosphere, hurricanes, atmospheric profiling), plus one pending NOAA project for GPS-denied navigation capabilities.

- **Period:** 06/05/2017 to 08/01/2021

1. **Volcanic plume monitoring** - Scientific data collection in hazardous volcanic environments

3. **Hurricane boundary layer sampling** - Severe weather reconnaissance and atmospheric measurement
4. **Atmospheric profiling** - Rapid vertical and lateral atmospheric characterization
5. **GPS-denied navigation** - Operations in areas without reliable GPS coverage
