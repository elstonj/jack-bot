## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies submitted a three-year budget proposal to NOAA for advancing UAS (uncrewed aerial systems) capabilities to sample tropical cyclones across altitudes from ocean surface to stratosphere. The project aims to improve NOAA forecasting capabilities through enhanced atmospheric sampling technologies.
