## Key Terms

**Confidential Information Definition:**
- All technical, business, and financial information exchanged between parties
- Includes summaries, analyses, compilations, data, studies, or documents based on disclosed information
- Applies whether or not marked as confidential

**Exceptions to Confidentiality:**
Information is not protected if it:
1. Becomes generally available to the public (not due to breach)
2. Was already available from other non-confidential sources
3. Was known to receiving party prior to disclosure (confirmed by written records)
4. Is disclosed with prior written approval

**Duration:**
- 3-year term from October 2, 2025
- Trade secrets protections continue indefinitely under applicable law

**Key Obligations:**
- All confidential information must be kept confidential
- Information limited to representatives with need-to-know who are bound by same terms
- Required disclosure by law must be preceded by notice to allow protective order requests
- No warranty regarding accuracy or completeness of shared information
- No IP rights granted; confidential information remains property of disclosing party

**Remedies:**
- Equitable relief (injunctive relief, specific performance) available for breach
- Attorney's fees recoverable in enforcement actions
- Waiver of bond requirements for injunctive relief

**Governing Law:**
- Colorado law applies
- Jurisdiction: Boulder County, Colorado state courts and federal courts in Denver, Colorado
