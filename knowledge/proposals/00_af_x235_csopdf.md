# AF_X23.5 CSO (Commercial Solutions Opening)

## Document Metadata
- **Type:** SBIR Commercial Solutions Opening (CSO) Solicitation Guidance
- **Client/Agency:** Department of the Air Force (DAF)
- **Program/Solicitation:** SBIR X23.5 CSO; Topic Number AFX235-CSO1
- **Date:** Issued 12 January 2023 (pre-release); proposals accepted 19 January – 23 February 2023
- **BST Products/Systems Referenced:** None mentioned in this document
- **Key Personnel:** Jack Elston (last editor)

## Executive Summary
This is the Air Force's Small Business Innovation Research Commercial Solutions Opening solicitation guidance document for fiscal year 2023. It establishes submission requirements, timelines, eligibility criteria, and evaluation frameworks for Phase I proposals from small businesses seeking to develop innovative commercial solutions addressing Air Force and Space Force capability gaps across 14 critical technology areas.

## Document Content Summary
This is a solicitation guidance document, not a proposal or technical report. It does not describe Black Swift Technologies products, capabilities, or technical approaches. Instead, it provides:

### Key Solicitation Parameters
- **Maximum Phase I Funding:** $75,000
- **Maximum Period of Performance:** 3 months
- **Technical Volume Limit:** 25 slides
- **Work Performance Requirement:** Minimum 2/3 of R&D work must be performed by proposing firm
- **Submission Portal:** DoD SBIR/STTR Innovation Portal (DSIP) at https://www.dodsbirsttr.mil/
- **Deadline:** 23 February 2023, 12:00 PM ET (no exceptions)

### Critical Technology Areas Addressed
- FutureG
- Trusted AI and Autonomy
- Biotechnology
- Advanced Computing and Software
- Integrated Sensing and Cyber
- Directed Energy
- Hypersonics
- Microelectronics
- Integrated Network Systems-of-Systems
- Quantum Science
- Space Technology
- Renewable Energy Generation and Storage
- Human-Machine Interfaces
- Advanced Materials

### Organizational Eligibility
- Must qualify as Small Business Concern (SBC) under 13 C.F.R. § 121.702
- Not more than 500 employees (including affiliates)
- More than 50% U.S. ownership and control
- Principal Investigator must have primary employment with firm (>50% time)
- Work must be performed in U.S. (with rare exceptions approved by DAF)

### Key Administrative Requirements
- Registration in DSIP and SAM.gov required
- Login.gov account authentication required
- Security Program Plan documentation required (per AFRLI 61-113)
- Research and Related Key/Senior Person Profile required (Attachment 6)
- Program Security Questionnaire required (Attachment 7)
- Classified proposals NOT accepted
- Performance benchmarking requirements apply to firms with multiple prior SBIR/STTR awards

### Air Force/Space Force Strategic Alignment
Proposals must address one or more of the following core missions/competencies:
- **USAF:** Air Superiority; Intelligence, Surveillance, and Reconnaissance; Rapid Global Strike; Command and Control; Organize/Train/Equip Airmen; Blue Sky
- **USSF:** Space Security; Combat Power Projection; Space Mobility and Logistics; Information Mobility; Space Domain Awareness; Organize/Train/Equip Guardians; Blue Sky

### Notable Administrative Details
- Foreign nationals, foreign affiliations, and foreign government participation subject to strict controls
- Malign Foreign Talent Recruitment Program participation disqualifying
- No proposals owned by venture capital operating companies (VCOCs), hedge funds, or private equity in majority part without additional scrutiny
- Conflicts of interest disclosure required for federal employee applicants
- Proposals involving human subjects, animal subjects, recombinant DNA, or UAS require additional approvals (not covered under Phase I funding)
- Awards will be executed as firm-fixed-price contracts or Other Transactions for Prototype
- Feedback (not FAR Part 15 debriefs) provided to non-selected proposals

### Review Timeline
- Proposal reviews anticipated complete within approximately 90 calendar days of solicitation close
- Notification via email to PI and Corporate Official listed on Cover Sheet

## Notable Gaps
This document contains no technical content, product descriptions, capability demonstrations, or results data. It is purely procedural and regulatory guidance for potential SBIR applicants. No information about Black Swift Technologies is present.
