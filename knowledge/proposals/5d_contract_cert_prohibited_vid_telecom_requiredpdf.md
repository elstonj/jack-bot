## Executive Summary
This is a mandatory DoD SBIR/STTR contractor certification that requires contractors to represent whether they provide covered defense telecommunications equipment or services. The document incorporates three DFARS (Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement) clauses that implement statutory prohibitions on procuring telecommunications equipment or services from specified foreign entities and their use in covered defense missions.

## Technical Approach
This is not a technical proposal but a compliance instrument. It establishes:
- **Certification requirements** for contractors to declare whether they provide covered defense telecommunications equipment/services
- **SAM (System for Award Management) review procedures** to identify excluded entities
- **Prohibition mechanisms** against use of specified foreign telecommunications in covered missions
- **Reporting requirements** if prohibited equipment/services are identified during contract performance

## Regulatory Framework
The certification enforces provisions from three DFARS clauses:

### DFARS 252.204-7016 (Representation of Covered Equipment/Services)
- Contractors must represent whether they provide covered defense telecommunications equipment or services
- Review required of SAM exclusion list

### DFARS 252.204-7017 (Prohibition on Acquisition)
- If contractor represents it provides covered telecommunications equipment/services, must specify whether it **will** or **will not** provide such in the DoD award
- If providing such equipment/services, must disclose:
  - Description of all covered equipment/services (brand, manufacturer, model number)
  - Explanation of proposed use and permissibility under prohibitions
  - Entity providing services (name, unique entity identifier, CAGE code)
  - Entity producing equipment (name, entity identifier, CAGE code, OEM/distributor status)

### DFARS 252.204-7018 (Prohibition Details)
Implements Section 1656 of National Defense Authorization Act for Fiscal Year 2018 (Pub. L. 115-91)

## Covered Defense Telecommunications Equipment/Services Definitions

**Covered equipment/services include:**
1. Telecommunications equipment produced by Huawei Technologies Company or ZTE Corporation (or any subsidiary/affiliate)
2. Telecommunications services provided by these entities or using their equipment
3. Telecommunications equipment/services from entities owned/controlled by, or connected to, governments of "covered foreign countries" (People's Republic of China or Russian Federation)

## Covered Missions
Prohibition applies specifically to:
1. **Nuclear deterrence mission** - including nuclear command, control, and communications; integrated tactical warning and attack assessment; continuity of Government
2. **Homeland defense mission** - including ballistic missile defense

## Critical Technology Definition
Expanded definition covering:
- Defense articles/services on U.S. Munitions List
- Items on Commerce Control List (controlled for national security, WMD proliferation, regional stability, etc.)
- Nuclear equipment and materials covered under 10 CFR parts 810 and 110
- Select agents and toxins
- Emerging and foundational technologies per Export Control Reform Act of 2018

## Reporting Requirements
If contractor identifies covered telecommunications equipment/services during performance:
- **Within 3 business days:** Report contract number, order numbers, supplier name, brand, model number, item description, mitigation actions
- **Within 30 business days:** Report additional mitigation information, describe prevention efforts, outline future prevention measures
- Reports submitted to: https://dibnet.dod.mil

## Notable Details
- False statements in certification subject to penalties under 18 U.S.C. § 1001
- Contractors must flow-down substance of clause to all subcontracts, including commercial product/service subcontracts
- Waiver process available through Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement 204.2104
- Document includes blank certification signature block requiring authorized company representative signature
- This is a mandatory form for all DoD SBIR/STTR awards; non-compliance could result in contract termination
