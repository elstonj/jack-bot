## Executive Summary
This is a Notice of Opportunity for Phase II SBIR/STTR companies to apply for supplemental TACFI funding ($375K–$2M over up to 24 months) to bridge the capability gap between Phase II efforts and Phase III scaling. The program seeks to catalyze partnerships between DAF end-users, acquisition professionals, and the private sector to deliver strategic capabilities aligned with DAF Operational Imperatives.

## Document Purpose & Scope
This document serves as **guidance and overview** for the PY25.2 TACFI Program, not as a submission template. It outlines program background, eligibility criteria, submission requirements, and disclaimers for small business applicants.

## Eligibility Criteria
Companies must meet ALL of the following to qualify:

1. **Small Business Concern (SBC) status** per SBA policy at time of award; eligible for SBIR/STTR
2. **Active or recent Phase II**: Currently on active SBIR/STTR Phase II OR completed Phase II within 2 years of capability package submission (measured from final deliverable date)
3. **No prior sequential Phase II** award on the same effort
4. **Minimum 90 days elapsed** since beginning of original Phase II contract execution through capability package submission
5. **No concurrent STRATFI efforts**, though concurrent TACFI efforts permitted (with exceptions for sequential Phase II or unrelated work)
6. **U.S.-based work** requirement

## Award Details
- **Funding Range**: $375K – $2M
- **Period of Performance**: Up to 24 months
- **Nature**: Supplemental funding as sequential Phase II or Phase II-B modification

## Required Submission Documents

All submissions must use mandatory templates; incomplete submissions will not be considered. Required files:

1. **SBC Registration Memo** (dated ≤30 days before submission)
   - File label: `TACFI SBCRegistration{SBCName}`

2. **SBC Private Funding Capitalization Table** (Excel format, confidential)
   - File label: `TACFI PrivateFundingCapitalizationTable{SBCName}`
   - If VCOC majority-owned, include proof of SBC registration

3. **Capability Package** (core technical submission)
   - File label: `TACFI CapabilityPackage{FirmName}`
   - Must detail: proposed solution, linkage to Phase II work, value to Government
   - Phase II effort identified must remain consistent throughout opportunity

4. **Matching Funds Documentation**
   - Combined file: `TACFI GovernmentFundingLetter{FirmName}` OR `TACFI PrivateFundingLetter{FirmName}`
   - Must demonstrate direct buy-in and intended use for all funding sources

5. **Collaboration Agreement**
   - File label: `TACFI CollaborationAgreement{FirmName}`
   - Must convey: benefit to DAF, senior leader buy-in, willingness to award within 90 calendar days, Phase III transition/acquisition strategy

6. **Delegation of Contracting Responsibility Acceptance** (memo or email)
   - File label: `TACFI ContractingResponsibilityAcceptance{FirmName}MEMO` or `EMAIL`

7. **Quad Chart & White Paper** (centerpiece of submission)
   - File label: `TACFI SubmissionQuadChart&WhitePaper{FirmName}`
   - Must align with all supporting documents

## Program Strategic Context
- Aligned with 2022 National Defense Strategy (NDS) emphasis on leveraging American entrepreneurial advantages

- Focus on DAF Operational Imperatives and critical capability gaps

## Security & Due Diligence
Per SBIR and STTR Extension Act of 2022 (Public L. 117-183):
- DoD will conduct risk-based due diligence review on all submissions
- Assessment areas: cybersecurity practices, patent analysis, employee analysis, foreign ownership, financial ties to foreign entities
- Uses Foreign Affiliation/Relationship disclosure form (Attachment 02.03)
- DoD may deny funding based on security risk assessment
- Also uses open-source analysis and analytical tools to check for nondisclosures per 15 U.S.C. 638(g)(13)

## Program Governance & Disclaimers
- **Not an RFP**: Does not constitute a commitment to contract award or positive investment decision
- **Voluntary submission**: Information submitted is voluntary; Government will not pay for submission development or request fulfillment costs
- **No guarantee**: Responding does not guarantee award
- **Applicable regulations**: Subject to 13 C.F.R. §§ 121.701-705, SBA size standards, affiliation rules, Combined SBIR/STTR Policy Directive (2023)
- **Majority VCOC eligibility**: Firms majority-owned by multiple VCs, hedge funds, or PE funds may be eligible per 15 U.S.C. 638(dd)
- **Sequential Phase II authority**: Per SBIR/STTR Program Policy Directive (May 3, 2023), agencies may offer Phase II-B awards or one additional sequential Phase II award
