## Executive Summary
Jun Zhang is a Scientist at the University of Miami with 15+ years of experience in tropical cyclone research, boundary layer physics, and hurricane observation. He has extensive expertise in in-situ aircraft observations, dropsonde analysis, and hurricane intensity forecasting, with significant involvement in NOAA hurricane field programs and uncrewed aircraft systems (UAS) research. He is a highly-published researcher with leadership roles across multiple federal research initiatives and professional organizations.
