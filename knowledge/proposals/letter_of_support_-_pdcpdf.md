# Letter of Support - PDC Energy

## Document Metadata
- Type: Letter of Support
- Client/Agency: PDC Energy (oil and gas exploration and production company)
- Program/Solicitation: Colorado Advanced Industry Accelerator grant application
- Date: August 31, 2022
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: BST S2 MLD (S2 with Methane Leak Detection)
- Key Personnel: J. Scott Canonico (PDC Vice President – Environment, Health & Safety); Jack Elston (BST, last editor)

## Executive Summary
PDC Energy provides formal support for Black Swift Technologies' Advanced Industry Accelerator grant application. PDC, a major Colorado oil and gas operator, has been collaborating with BST to demonstrate methane detection technology using BST's fixed-wing drone systems as a cost-effective alternative to traditional truck-based facility inspection methods.

## Technical Approach
BST proposed using their fixed-wing drone carrier equipped with methane leak detection (MLD) technology to conduct facility emissions monitoring. This approach aims to replace or supplement PDC's current inspection methods, which rely on teams of technicians traveling to sites by truck with optical gas imaging cameras.

## Products & Capabilities Described
**BST S2 MLD**
- Fixed-wing drone system with integrated methane leak detection capability
- Designed for remote facility inspection and emissions monitoring
- Proposed benefits: improved program coverage, faster response time, reduced costs

## Use Cases & Applications
- **Methane leak detection and repair (LDAR)** at oil and gas facilities
- Inspection of multiple facilities across Weld and Adams Counties, Colorado
- PDC currently inspects each facility at least annually, often multiple times per year; BST system intended to improve efficiency and reduce operational costs

## Notable Details
- **Client Context**: PDC Energy is a major regional operator producing 200,000+ barrels of oil equivalent per day, employing 550+ direct employees in Colorado
- **Partnership Status**: BST and PDC had been collaborating for approximately one year at time of letter; PDC positioned as potential "first commercial customer"
- **Regulatory Driver**: Inspections required by regulatory requirements and industry best practices
- **Competitive Advantage Addressed**: Cost reduction and operational efficiency vs. traditional truck-based optical gas imaging inspections
- PDC emphasizes the "unique technology" potential of the BST S2 MLD system
