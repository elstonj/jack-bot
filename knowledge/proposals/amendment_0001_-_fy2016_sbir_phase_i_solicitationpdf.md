## Key Program Details (General SBIR Framework)

### Phase Structure
- **Phase I**: Feasibility research, up to $120,000, up to 6 months
- **Phase II**: R&D/prototype development, up to $400,000, up to 24 months
- **Phase III**: Commercialization using non-SBIR funding sources

### Key Eligibility Requirements for Applicants
- Must qualify as Small Business Concern (≤500 employees)
- Phase I: minimum 2/3 of research performed by awardee
- Phase II: minimum 1/2 of research performed by awardee
- Principal Investigator must have primary employment (>50% time) with awardee
- All work must be performed in the United States (with rare exceptions requiring waivers)
- No venture capital majority ownership permitted

### Important Restrictions
- NOAA does not accept unsolicited proposals
- Proposals must address published subtopics in Section 8
- Potential awardees may not participate in topic development or proposal reviews
- Phase II proposals only available to Phase I awardees
- Phase I to Phase II transition rate benchmark: minimum 0.25 for companies with >20 Phase I awards in past 5 years

### Certifications Required
- Size, ownership, and SBIR program requirement certification
- Human subjects research compliance (if applicable)
- Vertebrate animal research compliance (if applicable)
- Various funding agreement certifications
