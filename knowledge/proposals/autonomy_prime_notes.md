## Executive Summary
Internal notes outlining USAF AFWERX requirements for an Autonomy Prime RFI response. AFWERX seeks information on Group 2/3 UAS aircraft suitable for use as modular, open-architecture testbed platforms at a Proving Ground facility for high-tempo autonomy testing and demonstration. BST's next-generation S2-VTOL is positioned as a candidate platform.

## Technical Approach
AFWERX is developing a portfolio of UAS testbed aircraft with the following design philosophy:
- **Open architecture design** with an **isolation layer** that separates test articles from core flight functionality
- Architecture enables rapid iteration and high-tempo testing of varied autonomy technologies
- Configurations of interest: VTOL and CTOL (Fixed-Wing) platforms
- UAS Category: Group 2 and Group 3 (mid-size)
- Emphasis on **modularity, reliability, and cost** over raw performance

## RFI Response Requirements

- Concise length

**Required Content:**
- Technology description and maturity level
- Purpose and intended impact to USAF mission areas/tasks
- Pricing overview
- Commercial market assessment and use cases
- Certification requirements and plans
- Demonstration or testing plans
- Requested development/testing support (e.g., air/ground range space, testbed aircraft, warfighter input, simulation support, test planning, USAF data access)

**Testbed UAS-Specific Requirements:**

- Production/manufacturing status, availability, and cost
