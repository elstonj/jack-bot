## Executive Summary
NASA's Science Mission Directorate is soliciting proposals for the Science Activation Program (SciAct) under ROSES-2025, seeking collaborative projects that engage the public in active participation with NASA science during 2026-2030. The program aims to inspire people of all ages, build STEM workforce capacity, and enable substantive public contribution to NASA science initiatives.

## Program Overview
- **Program Status**: Second phase of 5-year awards; originally began in 2016
- **Period of Performance**: Calendar years 2026-2030 (not to exceed 5 years)
- **Key Focus Areas**: Projects facilitating learning and enabling participants to experience, contribute to, or shape NASA science
- **Target Audience**: People of all ages and backgrounds

## Objectives
The program seeks to:
1. Inspire curiosity and wonder in science
2. Equip people to engage with science matters affecting society and their lives
3. Prepare people for STEM careers
4. Build future STEM workforce
5. Enable wide range of public participation in shaping science
