## Executive Summary
Embry-Riddle Aeronautical University (ERAU) commits institutional support and resources to Black Swift Technologies' Navy STTR Phase I proposal for developing expendable air-sea profiling observations using small uncrewed aircraft systems in hazardous weather conditions. ERAU serves as a research subcontractor with a 12-month performance period valued at $96,441.
