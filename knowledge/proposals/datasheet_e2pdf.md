## Executive Summary
The Black Swift E2 is a compact, autonomous UAS specifically designed for automated industrial inspections and infrastructure monitoring. It features forward-mounted payload capability, proprietary navigation technology, and autonomous flight capabilities with real-time data delivery to operators via handheld Android tablets.

## Technical Approach
The E2 utilizes advanced navigation technology and computer vision/machine learning algorithms to enable completely autonomous flights with precise, reliable navigation around complex structures. The aircraft features:
- Proprietary flight planning user interface for rapid mission programming (minutes to plan)
- SwiftTab software running on Android tablets for mission monitoring, mapping, and gesture-based flight control
- 900 MHz ISM telemetry frequency for operator communication
- 2.4 GHz FASST radio control frequency

## Products & Capabilities Described

### Black Swift E2 UAS
- **What it is**: A compact, autonomous quadrotor UAS designed for industrial inspections in extreme environmental conditions
- **Key design feature**: Forward-mounted payload bay (enabling full field-of-view including upward looking capability) with quick-change capability requiring only a screwdriver
- **Flight autonomy**: Capable of completely autonomous flights with intelligent navigation around complex structures
- **Operator interface**: Real-time actionable data delivery to handheld operator tablets

### SwiftTab Software
- Proprietary flight planning and mission management software
- Runs on Android tablets (handheld deployment)
- Gesture-based controls for intuitive operation
- Supports mission monitoring, mapping, and flight planning with minimal operator training

## Specifications

**Vehicle Characteristics**
- Airframe: Proprietary carbon fiber and polyamide 12
- Frame weight (empty): 1.42 kg (3.13 lbs)
- Electronics and battery weight: 3.83 kg (8.44 lbs)
- Maximum payload capacity: 1.99 kg (4.39 lbs)
- Maximum takeoff weight: 8.78 kg (19.36 lbs)
- Motors: 4× 360Kv
- Battery: Custom Li-ion, 41,400 mAh (6 cells)
- Propellers: 4× 18.5 inches

**Mission Capabilities**
- Flight time (full payload): 20 minutes
- Maximum wind endurance: 15 m/s (30 knots)
- Radio control frequency: 2.4 GHz (FASST)
- Telemetry frequency: 900 MHz (ISM)
