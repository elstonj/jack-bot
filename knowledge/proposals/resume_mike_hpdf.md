## Executive Summary
Michael A. Hurowitz is the Founder, CEO, and CTO of Orbital Micro Systems, Inc., with 15+ years of experience in RF engineering, remote sensing, and space technology commercialization. He serves as Principal Investigator for the AFWERX Soil Moisture Mapping Phase II project and brings expertise in microwave radiometer development, environmental monitoring systems, and technology commercialization from both government and commercial space sectors.
