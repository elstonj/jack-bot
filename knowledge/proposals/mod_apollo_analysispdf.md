## Executive Summary
This document presents aerodynamic analysis of modular airframe configurations for the Cooperative Wind Sensing SBIR project. The analysis includes computational fluid dynamics (CFD) simulations examining flow characteristics across different wing sizes at various angles of attack, including stall conditions.

## Technical Approach
- CFD-based flow visualization and aerodynamic analysis
- Evaluation of two wing configurations: 54-inch and 102-inch spans
- Analysis conducted at multiple angles of attack (α), including 0° cruise condition and 16° stall condition
- Streamline visualization used to assess flow behavior and aerodynamic performance

## Products & Capabilities Described
