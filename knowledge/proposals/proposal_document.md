## Executive Summary
This document excerpt outlines NASA's solicitation for proposals to conduct airborne measurements as part of the ABoVE program, either through flying sensors on different aircraft, integrating instruments onto existing platforms, or expanding coverage of foundational measurements.

## Technical Approach
Proposals should address one or more of the following approaches:
1. Flying additional sensors on alternative aircraft platforms
2. Integrating new sensors onto platforms already conducting ABoVE measurements
3. Extending or expanding flight lines of existing foundational measurements at feasible cost

All proposals must include:
- Aircraft-instrument integration costs
- Instrument operation costs
- Flight hour costs
- Mission peculiar costs (e.g., per diem)
- Data processing costs

Letters of support from responsible parties at NASA aircraft facilities are required when NASA aircraft are the intended platform.

## Use Cases & Applications
- Arctic-Boreal region research
- Expansion of spatial and temporal coverage over specific field sites
- Research areas of scientific interest
- Support for ABoVE Tier 2 science questions

## Notable Details
- Proposed airborne measurements can be combined with and complement foundational measurements
- Emphasis on cost-feasible approaches
- Formal support documentation required from NASA aircraft program managers
