## Executive Summary
This budget justification supports a Black Swift Technologies proposal for "Coordinated Drone-Based Plume Tracking" under DTRA funding. The project proposes to develop and test neural volumetric capture technology integrated with UAS sensors, with collaboration from Brookhaven National Laboratory (BNL). Total budget request is $199,983 for a 7-month period of performance.

## Products & Capabilities Described

## Technical Approach
The project focuses on:
1. Development and testing of **neural volumetric capture** software
2. Flight planning software for plume tracking operations
3. Integration of volumetric capture sensors on BST-operated UAS
4. Laboratory and field testing at Brookhaven National Laboratory
5. Preliminary analysis of collected data
