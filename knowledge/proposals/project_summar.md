## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies proposed development of the S0 UAS, an air-deployed Unmanned Aircraft System designed for boundary layer observations in turbulent environments. The system was intended to improve tropical cyclone intensity forecasts through targeted in situ measurements, addressing NOAA's need for enhanced data collection capabilities in severe weather conditions.

## Technical Approach
- **Air-deployed UAS concept**: S0 designed as a deployable system for release from aircraft into turbulent environments
- **Primary application**: Boundary layer observations during tropical cyclones
- **Technical components**: Sensors, electronics, and 3D-printed structural parts
- **Design focus**: Capability to operate in severe turbulence while collecting meteorological data

## Products & Capabilities Described

### S0 UAS
- **What it is**: An air-deployed, expendable or recoverable UAS platform
- **Proposed use**: In-situ measurements in tropical cyclones and other turbulent boundary layer environments
- **Key capability**: Designed to penetrate and survive severe atmospheric turbulence where manned aircraft operations are limited or dangerous
- **Target application**: Improving tropical cyclone intensity forecasting through direct atmospheric measurements

## Use Cases & Applications
- Tropical cyclone (hurricane) intensity forecasting
- Boundary layer observations in severe weather
- In-situ atmospheric measurements in high-turbulence environments
- Enhanced data collection for NOAA meteorological research
