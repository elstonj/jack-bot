## Executive Summary
This document outlines a three-year budget proposal for integrating N20 (nitrous oxide) sensors onto Black Swift's S2 unmanned aircraft system. The project involves developing custom payloads, conducting flight missions, and supporting data processing, with total project cost of $93,980 across non-recurring engineering, mission operations, and payload integration.
