## Document Purpose & Content
This is a **contractual template/instruction document**, not a technical proposal or report. It specifies requirements for BST to submit milestone-based deliverables for their Air Force SBIR Phase II contract on soil moisture mapping. The document provides:

- **Budget Cap**: Maximum $1.25M SBIR funds (additional non-SBIR Federal funds permitted with no maximum)
- **Milestone Limit**: Maximum 15 milestones
- **Milestone Structure**: Each milestone must include:
  - Task description
  - Expected delivery date (relative to contract award)
  - Deliverable acceptance criteria (measurable/demonstrable completion)
  - Payment amount
- **Constraint**: Status reports explicitly excluded as milestones; only deliverable completions qualify
