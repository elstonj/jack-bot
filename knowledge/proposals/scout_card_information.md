## Product Description: SØ VTOL UAS

### Core Capabilities
- **Type:** Intelligent unmanned aircraft with autonomous flight capability
- **Altitude Ceiling:** Up to 15,000 ft (4,570 m) AGL
- **Operating Conditions:** Harsh conditions including extreme temperatures and high winds
- **Flight Duration:** Up to 60 minutes endurance
- **Takeoff/Landing:** Vertical Takeoff and Landing (VTOL) from confined locations
- **Primary Configuration:** Atmospheric data collection with modular architecture for payload integration

### Key Technical Features
- **VTOL Architecture:** Pivoting motors enable VTOL functionality while maintaining fixed-wing aircraft range and endurance
- **Control Interface:** Android-based tablet operation, accessible to operators of varying experience levels
- **Mission Planning:** Waypoint-based planning with fully autonomous execution
- **Autonomous Flight Phases:** Takeoff, climbout, landing, and transit all autonomous (no operator intervention required)
- **Sensors:** Comprehensive sensor suite for atmospheric profiling including:
  - Air temperature
  - Wind speed and direction
  - Dewpoint
  - Atmospheric pressure

### Design Advantages
- **Payload Modularity:** Rapid integration of passive or active payloads
- **Logistics:** Small logistics footprint, simplified deployment
- **Cost:** Significantly less expensive than comparable UAS platforms
- **Operations:** Low operational expenses over platform lifetime
- **Versatility:** Can be configured for multiple missions beyond meteorology (resilient mesh communications, intelligence, surveillance, reconnaissance)

## Differentiators vs. Competitors

1. **Intuitive Operation:** Android tablet interface accessible to varying experience levels
2. **Autonomous Mission Execution:** Waypoint planning with fully autonomous flight from launch through recovery
3. **VTOL + Fixed-Wing Hybrid:** Combines takeoff/landing flexibility of VTOL with range and endurance of fixed-wing aircraft; eliminates need for special launch/recovery equipment
4. **Proven Track Record:** Black Swift recognized as leading manufacturer of robust unmanned aircraft systems for atmospheric research in extreme environments (high-altitude, arctic, desert, corrosive particulates, strong turbulence)
5. **Cost-Effective:** Lower acquisition and operational costs without capability compromise
6. **Mission Flexibility:** Modular design enables rapid expansion of capabilities
