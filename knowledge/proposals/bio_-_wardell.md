## Executive Summary
Dr. Lois Wardell is a multidisciplinary scientist and technology development manager with 15+ years of research experience and nearly a decade in environmental engineering. Her expertise spans volcanic and atmospheric monitoring, UAS program management, sensor development, and environmental compliance, with field experience across all seven continents.
