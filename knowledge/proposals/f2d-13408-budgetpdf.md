## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies submitted a $1,249,896.88 SBIR Direct to Phase II proposal to develop an all-weather UAS for persistent base security and rapid response under Air Force topic AFX246-DPCSO1. The base year budget covers direct labor for engineering and software development, procurement of a BST UAS system and sensor gimbal, and on-site testing in Alaska.

## Technical Approach
Not detailed in budget document. Budget supports:
- Development team of 4 primary roles: Principal Investigator (Aerospace Engineer, PhD), Lead Engineer (Aerospace Engineer, PhD), Software Developer (Master's), and Program Manager (Master's)
- Integration and testing of BST UAS platform with external sensor gimbal (Trillium)
- Field validation testing in Alaska to verify all-weather operational capability
