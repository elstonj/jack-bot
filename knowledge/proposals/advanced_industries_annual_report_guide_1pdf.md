## Executive Summary
This is an instructional document from Colorado OEDIT detailing the process for grant recipients to submit required annual reports for the Advanced Industries Program. The report is a contractual and statutory requirement that must be submitted annually for five years, with a September 1st deadline each year.

## Notable Details
- **Reporting Requirement**: Annual reports are mandatory for five years from grant award, even if a final report has already been submitted
- **Portal System**: OEDIT transitioned to a new online portal system in 2024; grantees should have received migration emails
- **Access**: Username format is email address with ".oeditcc" suffix (e.g., juliek@gmail.com.oeditcc)
- **Support Contact**: oedit_aigrantmanager@state.co.us for questions about reporting requirements
- **Data Preservation**: Users must save links to draft reports for later recovery, as the portal system does not automatically retain access paths
