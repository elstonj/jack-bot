## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies LLC has been selected as an apparently successful offeror for Navy SBIR Phase I funding under Topic N251-016 for development of an expendable sonobuoy-launched unmanned aerial vehicle (UAV) system designed to support Anti-Submarine Warfare (ASW) operations including cued search, detection, tracking, and classification of submarine contacts.
