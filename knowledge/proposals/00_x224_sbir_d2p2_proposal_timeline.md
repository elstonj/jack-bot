## Program Requirements (X22.4-ODCSO1 / X22.4-ODCSO2)

**Topic Options:**
- X22.4-ODCSO1: Direct-to-Phase-II Call for Innovative Defense-Related Dual-Purpose Technologies/Solutions with a Clear Air Force Stakeholder Need
- X22.4-ODCSO2: Direct-to-Phase-II Call for Innovative Defense-Related Dual-Purpose Technologies/Solutions with a Clear Space Force Stakeholder Need

**Award Parameters:**
- Maximum award amount: $1.25M
- Maximum period of performance: 18 months technical + 3 months reporting
- Type: Open Topic (no predefined technical areas)
- Requirement: Signed MOU/Customer Memorandum from DAF sponsor/partner

## Evaluation Criteria (Descending Order of Importance)

1. **Defense Need** (Primary)
   - Appropriateness and relevance in fulfilling DAF need
   - Account of how proposal meets Air Force technical area need
   - Signed customer memorandum and/or letters of support indicate appropriateness

2. **Technical Approach & Team** (Secondary)
   - Soundness, technical merit, and innovation of proposed approach
   - Differentiation from current customer alternatives
   - Qualifications of Principal Investigators/Project Managers and supporting staff

3. **Commercialization Potential** (Tertiary)
   - Government and private sector commercialization potential
   - BST's record of commercializing SBIR or other research
   - Phase II follow-on commitments
   - Matching funding (Government or private sources)
