## Products & Capabilities Described

**Iridium SBD (Short Burst Data) Service:**
- Global satellite communication service for IoT and field devices
- Four tier options available with monthly fees ranging from $21.99–$44.99/month
- Data allowances: 0 bytes (STD plan), 12KB, 17KB, or 30KB per month
- Overage rates: $1.40–$1.80 per KB
- Minimum billable message: 10–30 bytes (depending on plan)
- Mailbox check: $0.05 (charged only if mailbox empty)
- Registration fee: $0.05 per additional monthly registration (first registration free)
- Minimum contract: 3 months
- Activation fees: Free for STD plan; $50 for other plans
- Optional tracking services: $14.00/month

## Use Cases & Applications
Iridium SBD is being evaluated as a communication method for remote field operations, likely for transmitting sensor data from wildfire detection UAVs or ground stations in areas without cellular coverage.
