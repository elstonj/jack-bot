- Date: 16 January 2025

## Executive Summary
EKase Consulting, LLC proposes to provide Technical and Business Assistance services to Black Swift Technologies in support of a Navy SBIR Phase I award for developing a small logistics UAS with magnetic anomaly detection (MAD) and acoustic sensing capabilities. Services focus on market research, commercialization strategy, and relationship building with industry stakeholders, valued at $6,500 over 43.33 hours.
