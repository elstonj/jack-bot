## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies is a specialized engineering firm founded in 2001 that designs and operates small Unmanned Aircraft Systems (sUAS) for extreme environments. The company provides custom UAS solutions centered on the modular SwiftCore™ Flight Management System and has a legacy of serving government agencies (NASA, NOAA, NPS, DOI) and leading research institutions with over 100 publications in the field.
