## Executive Summary
This document presents design modifications and hardware developments for the S2 XT aircraft, focusing on payload integration improvements, environmental sealing, and contamination mitigation for volcanic ash sampling and monitoring operations.

## Technical Approach
The S2 XT underwent several design refinements:
- **Payload sealing system**: New toolless, rapid-swap mechanism for field payload changes
- **Camera mounting**: IP63-rated camera mount with 3D printed plastic components (orange fuselage-mounted piece and blue payload tray-attached piece)
- **Motor cowling**: New protective cover for the aircraft motor
- **Wing pod integration**: Video camera/transmitter pod designed for underwing installation
- **Contamination testing**: Simulation testing of volcanic ash and atmospheric particulate interactions with propwash, motor cowl, and servo covers to evaluate durability and protection

## Products & Capabilities Described

### S2 XT (SuperSwift XT)
- Small unmanned aircraft platform
- Payload tray with integrated IP63 camera mounting capability
- Rapid toolless payload swap capability for field operations
- Motor cowling for environmental protection
- Underwing pod attachment points for sensors/transmitters
- Designed to withstand volcanic ash contamination exposure

## Use Cases & Applications
- **Volcano monitoring**: Primary application; aircraft designed to sample and monitor volcanic ash in atmospheric conditions
- **Volcanic ash interaction study**: Testing focused on how volcanic ash and particulates interact with aircraft propulsion and control surfaces

## Notable Details
- **3D printed components**: Camera mount utilizes 3D-printed plastic for rapid prototyping and field serviceability
- **Environmental rating**: IP63 rating indicates dust and moisture protection suitable for harsh volcanic environments
- **Field operability**: Emphasis on toolless design and rapid reconfiguration for remote volcanic monitoring campaigns
- **Contamination mitigation**: Motor cowl and servo cover protection indicates design consideration for abrasive ash particles
- **Integrated video transmission**: Wing pod houses both camera and data transmitter for real-time monitoring capability
