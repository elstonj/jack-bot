## Executive Summary
This document describes Black Swift Technologies' physical facilities and equipment capabilities in Boulder, Colorado. BST maintains a 3,000 sq. ft. facility equipped for prototyping, testing, and manufacturing of unmanned aircraft systems, electrical subsystems, and avionics, with both simulation and validation resources.

## Facilities Overview

**Location & Size:**
- 3,000 sq. ft. facility in Boulder, CO

**Computing & Simulation Resources:**
- Full suite of atmospheric and aircraft simulation tools
- Capability for full mission scenario analysis
- Multiple full autopilot systems available for testing

## Equipment & Capabilities

**Prototyping & Manufacturing:**
- FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) 3D printers
- SLA (Stereolithography) 3D printers
- Rapid prototyping and iteration capability
- Electronics benches and workspace
- On-site integration of prototype and production unmanned aircraft
- Sensor and avionics integration capability
- Tools for both prototype and production assembly

**Testing & Validation:**
- Mixed-condition wind/rain tunnel for environmental resistance evaluation of subcomponents
- Electrical subsystem testing capability
- Software interface testing capability

**Facilities Infrastructure:**
- Meeting space for stakeholder coordination
- Workshop/shop facilities for prototyping and final QC

## Production Approach
BST performs prototyping and final QC in-house while partnering with local manufacturers for component delivery. This hybrid approach is noted as a strategy to simplify scaling production without maintaining full manufacturing in-house.

## Notable Details
- Document emphasizes in-house capability for systems integration and testing while leveraging external manufacturers for scaled production
- Facility supports complete development cycle from simulation through validation to manufacturing
