## SBIR Topics Reviewed

### 1. AF25D-T008: Detection of Uncrewed Aircraft Systems in Cluttered Environments
- **Type**: STTR (Small Business Technology Transfer)
- **Objectives**: Develop sensor technology to autonomously detect small UAS flying low through cluttered environments (obscured by trees/buildings)
- **BST Assessment**: Possible fit; references "Kara Dag tech"
- **Status**: Under consideration

### 2. AFX256-CSO1: Low-Cost Payloads for Electronic Warfare
- **Objectives**: 
  - Develop low-cost EW payloads for Group 3 and below sUAS capable of coordinated, scalable effects (geolocation, jamming, spoofing)
  - Develop system of 3+ sUAS
  - Price target: ≤$10K per system
  - Rapid deployable, scalable with mesh networking
  - Use COTS hardware and open-source software
  - Platform-agnostic payloads
- **BST Assessment**: Potentially fits if partnership with Kara Dag develops, but "not for this round probably"
- **Status**: Deferred

### 3. AFX256-DCSO3: HEIGHT (High-altitude Enhanced Information GatHering Technology)
- **Objectives**: Investigate innovative concepts leveraging high altitudes to advance DoD mission capabilities
- **BST Assessment**: "Not for us now" but notes interest in delivering ISR from very high altitude balloon platforms; mentions possible future concept of deploying S0's from balloon platforms ("probably doesn't match the spirit of this one")
- **Status**: Future consideration only

### 4. AF254-0807: Autonomous UAS Intelligence, Surveillance, Reconnaissance and Targeting (ISR-T)
- **Objectives**: Develop low SWaP solution for Group 2 or 3 UAS for autonomous ISR-T in congested/contested environments
  - Autonomous target acquisition and tracking using EO/IR video and RF sensors
  - Operate with limited/no communications while jammed or spoofed
  - Handle targets employing camouflage, concealment, deception (CCD)
  - Track targets in complex environments (parking lots, etc.)
  - Handle evasive motion and high-speed maneuvers
- **BST Assessment**: "Probably a bit of a stretch for us, seems just focused on camera + RF payloads in GPS-denied environments"
- **Status**: Not pursued

### 5. AF254-D0815: Visual Position and Navigation Capability Using Computer Vision for SUAS in GPS-Denied Environments
- **Objectives**: Develop robust visual navigation system for drone operations without GPS (unavailable, degraded, or jammed)
  - Use advanced computer vision algorithms
  - Detect/recognize skylines and terrain features
  - Match features against preprocessed satellite data repository
  - Achieve geolocation precision within 5 meters
- **BST Assessment**: No notes provided
- **Status**: Under review
