## Executive Summary
This is a template document for Black Swift Technologies' data management plans in federal proposals. It establishes BST's standardized approach to handling research data, including definitions, storage, sharing, preservation, and protection policies compliant with federal regulations (2 CFR 200.315).

## Data Types Covered
The template identifies research data categories to be generated in BST projects:
- Input, configuration, and problem definition files
- Simulation results (raw and processed)
- Images and numerical values used to generate them
- Papers, presentations, and reports
- Software (source code and executable)
- Video and audio files
- Associated metadata

## Data Sharing & Accessibility Policy
BST commits to making research data publicly available, open, machine-readable, and digitally accessible at publication. Methods include:
- Supplementary information attached to publications
- Public version control repositories
- Application containers on public repositories
- Black Swift Technologies website (www.bst.aero.com)
- Public long-lived data archives
- Publications indicate how data can be accessed

## Data Preservation & Storage Plan
- Operational codes: Stored in NISAR's cloud
- Analysis codes: NASA repository (https://data.nas.nasa.gov/) and PI's GitHub
- Software: Posted at project end
- Input/configuration files and images: Stored in publication repository
- Papers/presentations/reports: [Database/location to be specified]
- Video/audio files: [Storage method to be specified]; may appear on Black Swift website or public platforms (YouTube, etc.)
- Backup schedule: Regular backups with permanent monthly archive to secure, offline locations

## Compliance & Protection
- Compliant with federal law regarding confidentiality, personal privacy, PII, and national/homeland/economic security
- Respects proprietary interests, business confidential information, and IP rights
- Commitment not to share proprietary or PII data
- Projects will neither generate nor use PII, export-controlled data, or confidential data

## Rationale
BST's data management policies emphasize computational reproducibility and publication of non-proprietary research results to benefit the scientific community and US economy.
