## Executive Summary
This is a draft annual report for a Colorado Advanced Industries ESCR grant supporting Black Swift Technologies' methane leak detection project. The grant funds development of a methane detection sensor integrated with the S2 platform, pilot testing with an anchor customer (PDCE), and commercialization activities targeting oil & gas and pipeline monitoring markets.
