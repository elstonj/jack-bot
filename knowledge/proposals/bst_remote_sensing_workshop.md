## Executive Summary
BST proposed a four-day remote sensing workshop in partnership with the University of Maryland Eastern Shore to train students and faculty across aviation, engineering, natural sciences, and computer science disciplines. The workshop would cover the complete workflow of airborne remote sensing data collection, processing, and analysis with hands-on instruction and real-world datasets.

## Technical Approach
The workshop curriculum covers:
- Overview of airborne systems and sensors
- Post-processing techniques for remote sensing data
- Conversion of raw data to actionable information
- Hands-on practical work with real-world datasets
- Integration across multiple disciplines (aviation, engineering, natural sciences, computer science)

## Products & Capabilities Described
**Unmanned Aircraft Systems:**
- Used for soil moisture mapping (demonstrated in workshop materials)
- Capable of generating 3D maps
- Platforms for remote sensing data collection

## Use Cases & Applications
- Soil moisture mapping and monitoring
- 3D mapping applications
- General remote sensing data collection and analysis
