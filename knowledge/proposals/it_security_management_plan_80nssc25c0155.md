## Key IT Security Requirements Established

**NASA IT System Access:**
- Remote access limited to NASA SBIR/STTR Awardee Firm ProSAMS (https://prosams.nasa.gov/) for submitting contract deliverables, invoices, and technical reports
- Access managed through NASA Account Management System (NAMS) and Identity and Access Management System (IdMAX)
- Registration restricted to personnel involved in contract negotiation and administration
- All registered personnel required to complete NASA Online Annual IT Security Training

**Data Protection Responsibilities:**
- Protect confidentiality, integrity, and availability of NASA Electronic Information and IT resources
- Comply with NIST and NASA IT Security requirements, regulations, policies, and guidelines
- Handle Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) according to federal safeguarding standards

**General Rules of Behavior (15 rules)**
Including: lawful use only, PII/CUI protection, incident reporting, encryption of mobile data, screen-locking procedures, access authorization verification, minimal data during travel, proper document labeling, separation of duties, password protection, prohibition on anonymizer sites.

**Sensitive Information Protection (per 1852.237-72):**
- Use sensitive information only for contract performance, not competitive advantage
- Restrict access to employees with need-to-know
- Preclude external disclosure
- Train employees with written affirmation of compliance
- Implement monitoring and breach reporting

**Incident Response:**
- Immediate notification to NASA Security Operations Center upon any IT security incident affecting confidentiality, integrity, or availability
- Notification to Contracting Officer (CO) and Contracting Officer's Representative (COR) required for any compromise, misuse, distortion, loss, or destruction of NASA IT systems, data, or contract deliverables
