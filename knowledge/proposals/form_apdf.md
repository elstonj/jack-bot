## Executive Summary
Black Swift Technologies submitted a Phase II SBIR application to NASA's Climate, Cryosphere, and Resilient Polar Projects program for a soil moisture mapping unmanned aerial system (sUAS). The 15-month project proposed to develop or advance UAS-based soil moisture sensing capabilities, building on prior Phase I work.
