# Broad Area Announcement: Demonstration of Uncrewed Aircraft Systems (UAS) Payloads/Technology/Applications/Equipment

## Document Metadata
- **Type:** Broad Area Announcement (solicitation for vendor demonstrations)
- **Client/Agency:** U.S. Bureau of Reclamation, Preparedness Division
- **Program/Solicitation:** UAS Payloads/Technology/Applications/Equipment Demonstration
- **Date:** Not explicitly stated; event scheduled for early November 2024
- **BST Products/Systems Referenced:** None identified in this document
- **Key Personnel:** Mary Couture, Preparedness Division Program Analyst (contact)

## Executive Summary
The Bureau of Reclamation's Preparedness Division is seeking qualified vendors to demonstrate innovative UAS payloads, technology, applications, and equipment in real-world settings. The event will take place in early November 2024 at the Denver Federal Center in Lakewood, Colorado, targeting the broader Reclamation UAS community to showcase practical applications and novel use cases that address specific challenges within Reclamation operations.

## Technical Approach
This is a solicitation document, not a technical proposal from BST. It outlines the Bureau of Reclamation's requirements for vendor demonstrations rather than describing a specific technical approach by Black Swift Technologies.

## Products & Capabilities Described
The announcement lists 28 categories of potential demonstration topics that vendors may showcase. Key areas include:

- **Sensor Systems:** LiDAR, thermal imaging (including high-resolution), environmental monitoring sensors
- **Data Processing:** Point cloud processing, image stitching, feature extraction, ortho imagery for change detection
- **Positioning:** PPK/RTK positioning systems, ground control minimization solutions
- **Flight Control:** Autonomous flight systems, obstacle avoidance, sense and avoid systems, night flight capabilities
- **Communications:** Beyond-line-of-sight (BVLOS) communication systems, robust ground control station connectivity
- **Advanced Capabilities:** Swarm intelligence and cooperative control, subject-based navigation systems
- **Specialized Applications:** Corridor & channel bed mapping, 3D modeling, digital twin creation
- **Infrastructure:** Payload integration platforms, battery and power management systems, cybersecurity solutions

## Use Cases & Applications
While this is a solicitation rather than a proposal, it indicates Reclamation's interest in UAS applications for:
- Infrastructure inspection and assessment
- Surveying and topographic mapping
- Environmental monitoring and disaster response
- Search and rescue operations
- Precision agriculture and reforestation
- Building inspections and structural assessments
- Wildlife monitoring
- Urban planning and geospatial data collection
- Change detection for land-use planning

## Event Logistics
- **Date:** Thursday, November 7th or Thursday, November 14th, 2024
- **Location:** Denver Federal Center, Lakewood, Colorado
- **Venue:** Both indoor and outdoor demonstration spaces available
- **Vendor Responsibility:** All equipment, personnel, resources, and costs are vendor-supplied; Reclamation provides no compensation or reimbursement
- **Weather Contingency:** Vendors must prepare backup plans for inclement weather

## Compliance Requirements
Vendors must attest in writing that demonstrated products comply with:
- National Defense Authorization Act (NDAA) FY 2020, 2023, and 2024 requirements
- American Security Drone Act of 2023
- Other relevant federal regulations
- **Critical Note:** Submission without compliance attestation on page one will be rejected, not reviewed, and/or disqualified

## Response Requirements
Vendor responses must include:
1. Written compliance attestation (page one)
2. Detailed technical specifications (dimensions, weight, power requirements, system compatibility)
3. Description of UAS payloads, technology, and how they address Reclamation's specific needs
4. Explanation of innovation and uniqueness relative to standard industry solutions
5. Detailed demonstration plan including scenarios, environments, and use cases

## Evaluation Criteria
- Innovation and uniqueness of payloads, technology, and equipment
- Practicality and applicability to real-world scenarios
- Technical capabilities and performance metrics
- Vendor experience and track record in UAS industry
- Compatibility and integration potential with existing Reclamation UAS platforms and workflows
