## Notable Details

### Grant Program Requirements:
- **Matching Funds:** Applicants must secure 2X the grant amount as cash match (required within 30 days of award, or conditionally within 6 months)
- **Prior Capital Limit:** Recipients cannot have received more than $20M from third-party sources
- **Project Timeline:** 12, 24 (max for non-bioscience), or 36 months (max for bioscience)
- **Commercialization Milestones Required:**
  - Proof of Principle
  - Intellectual Property Protection
  - Prototype & Technical Validation
  - Market Assessment
  - Startup Functions

### Application Sections:
1. **Executive Summary & Company Info** (Questions 1-14)
2. **Project & Grant Amount** (Questions 15-32) - covers funding request, matching funds, future capital needs, revenue projections
3. **Stakeholder Collaboration** (Questions 33-36) - research institution partnerships, incubators/accelerators, investor referrals
4. **Product & Technology** (Questions 37-39) - product description, innovation claims, IP strategy
5. **Business Strategy & Financials** (Questions 40-51) - elevator pitch, business history, value proposition, revenue streams, target market, competition, scalability, manufacturing/distribution, regulatory pathway, exit strategy, market traction
6. **Company Management** (Questions 52-53) - team bios and advisor information
7. **Benefits to Colorado** (Questions 54-62) - FTE creation/retention, capital investment estimates, economic growth impact
8. **Documentation Requirements** (Questions 63-71) - matching fund proof, capitalization table, milestones, pitch deck, budget, financials, CVs, letters of support
9. **Compliance** (Questions 72-74) - legal structure, certificate of good standing, statutory certification

### Response Type Variety:
The application uses multiple formats: narratives (with character limits ranging 500-5,000), numeric fields, text entries, yes/no, dropdown selections, multiple choice, conditional questions, checkboxes, and file uploads (max 5 MB per file, 50-character file names).
