## Executive Summary
This is a standard Small Business Innovation Research (SBIR) Phase II funding agreement certification form completed by Black Swift Technologies as a requirement for receiving federal SBIR award funding. The document requires the awardee to certify compliance with SBA eligibility requirements, ownership and control standards, employee count limits, work performance requirements, and other program compliance conditions.

## Document Content
This is a **boilerplate federal compliance form** rather than a substantive proposal or technical document. It contains 18 certification checkboxes requiring the awardee to affirm:

### Key Compliance Requirements Certified:
1. **Ownership & Control**: Business meets SBA requirements per 13 CFR 121.702
2. **Size**: No more than 500 employees (including affiliates)
3. **Principal Investigator/Project Manager**: Will spend >50% time on award
4. **Work Performance**: At least 50% of research performed by BST (Phase II requirement)
5. **Location**: R&D performed in the United States
6. **Facilities**: Work performed at Awardee's facilities by Awardee's employees (unless otherwise approved)
7. **Venture Capital Disclosure**: Declaration of VC ownership status
8. **Conflict Notification**: Will notify if work is subsequently funded elsewhere
9. **Authority**: Signatory is authorized officer
