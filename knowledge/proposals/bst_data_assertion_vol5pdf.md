## Executive Summary
This is a supporting document for BST's DTRA PLUMES proposal that formally asserts restrictions on the Government's rights to use, release, or disclose specified technical data and/or computer software developed or delivered under the program. The document establishes the legal basis for protecting BST's proprietary information and defines what data will be furnished with restrictions.

4. **Name of Person Asserting Restrictions** – BST representative(s) making the assertion
