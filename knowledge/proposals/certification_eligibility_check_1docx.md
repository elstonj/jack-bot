## Executive Summary
This is a compliance checklist document for a DOD/Air Force SBIR Phase I proposal submission. It outlines mandatory eligibility requirements and certification questions that must be answered correctly to avoid proposal disqualification, covering small business status, ownership structures, fraud history, principal investigator requirements, and various regulatory compliance matters.

## Document Content
This is a **supporting reference document, not a substantive proposal**. It contains the eligibility questions and compliance requirements from an Air Force SBIR solicitation, presented as a checklist with pass/fail criteria.

### Key Disqualification Triggers (Critical Eligibility Requirements)
1. **Small Business Status**: Firm must have ≤500 employees including affiliates; must meet ownership/control requirements (13 CFR 121.702)
2. **Ownership Structure**: Cannot be >50% owned by venture capital firms, hedge funds, or private equity firms (with limited exceptions for small business VCs/PE)
3. **Work Performance**: Minimum 2/3 of R&D/analytical work must be performed by the small business itself
4. **Principal Investigator**: Must have primary employment with the proposing firm
5. **Fraud/Debarment**: Firm, PI, or Corporate Official cannot have fraud convictions or civil liability related to SBIR/STTR or Federal funds
6. **Equivalent Work**: Cannot have received prior government contract award for essentially equivalent work
7. **TABA**: Air Force does not allow Technical and Business Assistance (TABA) costs
8. **Compliance Documentation**: Human subjects, animal subjects, or recombinant DNA research requires specific institutional approvals and documentation

### Non-Disqualifying Items
- Release of contact information to EDOs
- DCAA audit review history
- Socially/economically disadvantaged status
- Woman-owned small business status
- HUBZone certification
- Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned status
- PI gender or disadvantaged status
- PI time commitment (any non-zero percentage acceptable)

### Special Provisions Requiring Disclosure
- Corporate entity ownership (if ≥50% owned by corporate entity, describe relationship)
- Venture capital/hedge fund/PE firm ownership (explain structure if applicable)
- Affiliate relationships (per 13 CFR 121.103)
- Foreign national involvement (require country, visa/work permit, specific task assignments)
- Teaming partners or subcontractors (must appear in Volumes 1, 3, and 5 of proposal)
- Prior proposals for equivalent work at other agencies (must disclose in Coversheet Supplement)
- Data rights assertions under DFARS 252.227-7017 (requires Data Rights Assertions Table in Volume 5)
