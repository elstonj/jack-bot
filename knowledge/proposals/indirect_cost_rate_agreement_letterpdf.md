# Indirect Cost Rate Agreement Letter

## Document Metadata
- Type: Official correspondence / Rate approval letter
- Client/Agency: NOAA (National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration) - Grants Management Division
- Program/Solicitation: NOAA indirect cost rate approval for FY 2021
- Date: February 18, 2021
- BST Products/Systems Referenced: None
- Key Personnel: Melissa Phillips (BST contact); Lamar Dwayne Revis (NOAA Grants Officer)

## Executive Summary
NOAA approved Black Swift Technologies to use provisional indirect and fringe cost rates for fiscal year 2021 based on BST's submitted Indirect Cost Rate Proposal, allowing BST to proceed with NOAA-funded work without waiting for final signed agreement due to NOAA's delayed CPA firm contracting process.

## Technical Approach
Not applicable - administrative/financial document.

## Products & Capabilities Described
None referenced.

## Use Cases & Applications
Not applicable - this is a rate authorization letter for NOAA grant administration purposes.

## Key Results
- **Provisional Indirect Cost Rate:** 18.32%
- **Fringe Rate:** 29.28%
- **Effective Date:** FY 2021
- **Submission Date:** November 16, 2020
- **Authorization Date:** February 18, 2021

## Notable Details
- BST had already completed and submitted its Indirect Cost Rate Proposal to NOAA GMD before this letter
- The provisional rates were approved for immediate use despite the absence of a final signed agreement
- NOAA was experiencing delays in securing a CPA firm to conduct the standard audit review process
- The approval allowed BST to proceed with NOAA-funded operations without operational interruption
