## Summary
Cory Dixon is Director of Engineering and Operations at Black Swift Technologies as of July 2014. He holds a Ph.D. in Aerospace Engineering from CU Boulder and brings extensive experience in unmanned aircraft systems, embedded software, and cooperative robotic systems. His background spans both academic research and industry R&D roles.

## Education
