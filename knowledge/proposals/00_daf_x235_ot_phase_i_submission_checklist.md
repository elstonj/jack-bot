## Executive Summary
This is an administrative checklist and submission guidance document for Phase I proposals under the Air Force SBIR/STTR program, Topic X23.5 (CSO1). It outlines all required proposal components, registration requirements, submission volumes, and compliance documentation needed for a Phase I proposal with a maximum 3-month period of performance and $75,000 award.

## Technical Approach
Not applicable—this is a compliance and submission checklist, not a technical proposal.

## Proposal Structure Required
The submission consists of six volumes:

**Volume 1 - Proposal Coversheet**
- Certification questions, technical abstract, anticipated benefits, contact information

**Volume 2 - Technical Volume**
- Maximum 25 slides (excess slides not reviewed)
- Required content: Overall summary, technical merit summary, concept/significance, commercialization potential, team capability, defense need, objectives, plan outline, transition plan, related work (dual-use focus), clearances/certifications

**Volume 3 - Cost Volume**
- Direct labor, direct material, other direct costs
- Requirement: Minimum two-thirds of research/analytical work must be performed by the proposing firm (measured by direct and indirect costs, excluding profit)

**Volume 4 - Company Commercialization Report (CCR)**

**Volume 5 - Supporting Documents (Required)**
- Coversheet supplement, supplemental cost information, key personnel resumes, non-proprietary work plan, SBIR/STTR lifecycle certification, ESOH questionnaire, contractor certification (video surveillance/telecom), AF security program questionnaire, senior/key person profiles, funding agreement certification

**Volume 5 - As Applicable**
- DD Form 2345 (military critical tech data agreement)
- Letters of support
- Foreign government ownership disclosure
- Joint venture eligibility certification
- Data rights assertion and allocation tables

**Volume 6 - Fraud, Waste, and Abuse Training**
- Certification of FWA training completion in DSIP

## Key Requirements
- **Award Amount:** Maximum $75,000
- **Period of Performance:** Maximum 3 months
- **Submission Deadline:** 23 February 2023, NLT 12:00 PM ET
- **Work Share:** Minimum 2/3 of research/analytical work by prime contractor
- **Slide Limit:** 25 slides maximum (hard limit)
- **Contact:** USAF SBIR/STTR One Help Desk (1-855-855-5360 or usaf.team@afsbirsttr.us)

## Notable Details
- Proposal titles and numbers must match across all documents and DSIP entry
- Digital signatures must not be stripped after upload
- Direct material costs require justification (vendor quotes, historical costs) if proposed
- Dual-use research focus implied by "Related Work (Dual-Use)" requirement
- Lifecycle certification and various security/compliance certifications required
- Data rights and allocation of rights assertions required
