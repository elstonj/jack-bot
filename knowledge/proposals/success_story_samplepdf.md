## Executive Summary
Coney Island, Inc. developed Electronic Handbooks (EHBs)—internet-based tools for paperless documentation and management of complex distributed processes—under a NASA SBIR Phase II contract. The company successfully commercialized the EHB methodology across both private sector (automotive, financial services, construction) and public sector (healthcare, education) applications.

## Technical Approach
Coney Island developed a four-part methodology for Electronic Handbooks consisting of:
1. User interface design
2. Backend architecture
3. Requirements capture
4. Demonstration/deployment process

This methodology enables the creation, deployment, and marketing of web-based paperless documentation systems for managing complex workflows.
