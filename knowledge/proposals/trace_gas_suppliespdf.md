## Executive Summary
This document provides a detailed line-item budget justification for a gas payload used in a NASA SBIR Phase III volcano monitoring project. It itemizes components for a trace gas sensor system, totaling $3,138.00, with notes clarifying which items are BST-manufactured components versus commercial off-the-shelf (COTS) parts.

## Technical Approach
The payload integrates multiple sensor and support systems for trace gas measurement:
- **Sensor suite**: City Tech T3STF (0-100 range) and City Tech T3H (0-100 range) gas sensors for atmospheric monitoring
- **Avionics/Communications**: BST GNSS board for positioning, BST Actuator Board for control
- **Structure**: Kevlar nose cone, payload scaffold, forward bulkhead, carbon mounting plates, and 3D-printed supports
- **Power Management**: 5V and 12V voltage regulators
- **Data Acquisition**: NDC current loop reader for sensor signal conditioning
- **Integration**: S-mount fittings, connectors, and mounting hardware
