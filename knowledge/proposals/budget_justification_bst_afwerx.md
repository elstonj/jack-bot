## Executive Summary
This budget justification supports a Phase I AFWERX proposal for field-deployable composite manufacturing enabling rapid, automated production of modular UAS. The total subcontract value is $24,556.00 covering a 3-month performance period (2/3/2025 - 5/2/2025), with primary costs allocated to senior personnel and indirect rates.

## Budget Summary

### Labor Costs
- **Senior Personnel (Dr. Jack Elston - PI): $14,156.00**
  - 73 hours of work at $150.00/hr
  - Represents 0.53 calendar months over 3-month project duration
  - Role: Technical advisor, lightweight modular fuselage design leadership, full aircraft design responsibility, project planning and monitoring

### Fringe Benefits
- Rate: 29.280% of labor
- Includes: Health insurance, Life and AD&D insurance, PTO, sick time, 401(k) matching, state/Federal workers' compensation and unemployment insurance

- Overhead: 46.61% on TDL + FR base ($14,156.00) = $6,598.00
- G&A: 18.32% on Total Direct Costs base ($20,754.00) = $3,802.00

## Technical Approach
Design and development of lightweight, modular fuselage for rapid, automated production using field-deployable composite manufacturing techniques. BST will lead full aircraft design under Dr. Elston's technical oversight.
