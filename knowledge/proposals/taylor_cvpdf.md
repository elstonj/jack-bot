## Executive Summary
Joey Taylor is an atmospheric scientist and UAS engineer specializing in wildfire remote sensing and dynamics. Currently an Associate Scientist at CIRES/CU Boulder, he is developing the NightFOX Unmanned Aerial System to deliver high-resolution measurements of wildfire environments using remote sensing and in-situ instrumentation. His background combines computational physics, climate modeling, and field-based research.

## Current Research Focus
- Wildfire spread dynamics and rapid flareup processes
- Development of NightFOX UAS for high-resolution wildfire environment measurements
- Remote sensing and in-situ instrumentation for wildfires
- Fire weather forecasting and incident response support
