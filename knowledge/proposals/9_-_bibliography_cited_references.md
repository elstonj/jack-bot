## Executive Summary
This is a reference bibliography from a 2018 USDA proposal for a high-resolution soil moisture mapping unmanned aircraft system. The citations document foundational research in microwave radiometry, soil moisture remote sensing, and UAS technology, with significant involvement from BST personnel.

## Technical Approach & Key Research Areas
The bibliography reflects research in:
- **Microwave radiometry** for soil moisture estimation using polarimetric scanning radiometers (PSR)
- **C-band and X-band** microwave observations
- **Microstrip colinear antenna arrays** for small UAS lobe differencing correlation radiometry
- **BRDF/Albedo validation** using multi-angle imaging UAS systems
- **Agricultural surveillance** using fixed-wing UAS platforms

## Products & Capabilities Described

### Polarimetric Scanning Radiometer (PSR)
- Used for soil moisture mapping and retrieval
- Operates at C-band and X-band frequencies
- Validated against AMSR-E satellite data
- Demonstrated capability in high vegetation and rough topography environments
- Multiple field experiments: SMEX02, SMEX03, Southern Great Plains 1999

### Microstrip Colinear Antenna Array
- Designed for small unmanned aerial systems
- Application: Lobe differencing correlation radiometry
- Published in IEEE Transactions on Antennas and Propagation (April 2018)

### MALIBU System
- High spatial resolution multi-angle imaging unmanned airborne system
- Purpose: Validate satellite-derived BRDF/Albedo products
- Demonstrated at AGU Fall Meeting 2016

## Use Cases & Applications
- **Soil moisture mapping** and estimation in agricultural areas
- **Satellite data validation** (AMSR-E, BRDF/Albedo products)
- **Agricultural surveillance** of agricultural systems
- **High vegetation and rough topography** environments
