## Project Notes
    Dr. Masri reached out to BST in May 2024. He has a research grant studying Methane emissions from wetlands and bottomland hardwood forests. Part of the project is to upscale the site measurement to a larger scale using remote sensing. The location is Murphy's Pond (Kentucky).
    BST sent multiple estimates to Dr. Masri for budgeting purposes, so it was not clear if the project would move forward. He informed them that since the estimate is more than $10k requires a review and open competition.
    In December 2024 BST submitted a proposal, which included estimate #1343 ($86,862.94). In January 2025 BST received a request to sign up as a vendor with Murray State for estimate #1343 via their site Payment Works. BST was approved as a vendor on January 22, 2025.
    Meeting on January 29, 2025 confirming that the project has been approved. Travel dates will be between May 2025 to October 2025.
