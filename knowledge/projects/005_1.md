From Barbados Prime Minister's Office:  Please be informed that the frequency range 902 MHz to 928 MHz is the unregulated ISM band and your drone may be prone to interference when it operates in this band.  Users in the ISM bands generally operate at their own risk and Digital Infrastructure does not offer assistance with interference issues in these bands.  Digital Infrastructure only becomes involved when a user is dominating the band, creating excessive interference that dominates  a large portion of the band, or is a commercial entity that should not be operating in the band.
