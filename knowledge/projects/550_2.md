Through targeted research and development, this project will focus on refining the S0 platform and demonstrating its utility for operational Navy use.
The proposed technology has the potential to revolutionize atmospheric profiling in hazardous environments. Improved operational readiness will provide the Navy with a reliable tool for real-time decision-making, ensuring the safety of personnel and assets during severe weather events. Detailed measurements of air-sea interactions will provide insights into the physical processes driving weather systems, contributing to the broader scientific community’s efforts to understand and mitigate climate impacts. Additionally, the development of scalable, cost-effective UAS platforms will have applications beyond military use, including civilian weather monitoring, environmental research, and disaster response.
