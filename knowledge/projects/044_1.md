Overview
Black Swift Technologies (BST) proposes to partner with EMASS to integrate and validate the ECS-DoT ultra-low-power Edge A.I. System-on-Chip (SoC) on a UAS (uncrewed aircraft system). BST will leverage its proven expertise in developing high-performance, field-hardened UAS systems for NASA, NOAA, and other federal research programs.

The objective of this project is to bridge simulation results with operational testing, quantifying the ECS-DoT’s impact on flight endurance, onboard autonomy, and energy efficiency. Upon completion, BST will have conducted flight testing and analysis of a BST E2 Quadcopter platform with integrated ECS-DoT SoC to demonstrate improvements in flight efficiency and endurance.
