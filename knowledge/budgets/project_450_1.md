### Key Observations

1. **Year One slightly under budget** by $659.36 despite labor and rental overruns offset by travel savings ($3,167.36 under).

2. **Year Two finished slightly over budget** by $641.99, primarily due to materials overrun ($3,000.00) partially offset by travel savings ($3,022.01).

3. **Final invoice (1595)** dated 11/28/2023 is minimal ($652.50) and labeled "Final," indicating project completion. Covers:
   - Project Management Meetings: 1 × $174.00
   - Data Processing (NRE): 1 × $174.00
   - Final Report Preparation: 1.75 hrs × $174.00 = $304.50

4. **Total project underspend across both years**: Approximately $17.37 remaining, indicating excellent budget management and near-perfect execution to contract value.

5. **No cost overruns** on the overall contract; Year One and Year Two variances were internal to each year and resolved by project close-out.

6. **Labor and rental costs** were primary drivers (~85% of total budget), consistent with UAS operations and personnel-intensive field deployments.
