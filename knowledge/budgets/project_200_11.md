### Budget vs. Actual Observations
1. **Overall Project Status:** Contract fully invoiced ($74,428.00) by 09/15/2025, 15 days before period end (09/30/2025).
2. **Direct Cost Tracking:** Only $25,186.18 of tracked direct costs have been expended against $39,428.00 budgeted, leaving ~$14,242 unspent on tracked items. This suggests:
   - The $74,428 contract value includes fixed deliverable payments ($35,000 + $35,000 + $4,428 = $74,428) that are not fully reflected in the line-item labor/materials tracking.
   - Much of the work is bundled into deliverable milestones rather than hourly/material tracking.
3. **Labor Utilization:** 
   - Actual labor hours logged: 234:04:26 hours total (approximately 234 hours)
   - Estimated 280 hours (60+60+80+60) were budgeted for employees
   - Significant variance on PI (Elston/Stachura): logged -48.5 fewer hours than estimated but charged full $11,635 (likely due to deliverable-based compensation model).
4. **Equipment/Materials:**

   - $3,440.13 spent (57% of allocation)
   - $2,558.87 remaining
   - SkyGuard equipment purchase ($3,120) was covered within budget allocation.
