## Summary

**Project 400-6** is a 3-month Phase I SBIR subcontract between BST (as technical advisor) and Curable Composites under AFWERX funding. The $24,556 award focuses on demonstrating field-deployable composite manufacturing for UAS airframe components. Labor costs dominate the budget ($14,156 base + $3,206 fringe), with Dr. Jack Elston providing technical leadership at $150/hr. Actual spending through August 2025 was minimal (~$970), with a $12,500 interim payment in September upon final report delivery. The project appears on-track financially with significant remaining budget capacity as of October 2025.
