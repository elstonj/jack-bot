4. **Travel Justified:** Two recurring test events (Camp LeJeune & Camp Pendleton) with associated TDY costs; baggage fees noted for UAS equipment transportation.
5. **Fee Structure:** 7% fixed fee on CPFF subcontract; profit embedded in Phase II proposal at 6.5%–7% markup on total costs (typical for SBIR).
