5. **Multi-Year Commitment**: Weather Stream engagement extends 3 years (April 2025–2028) at consistent annual spend of ~$80K, while Pepperwood budget appears to be annual or single-phase.

6. **No Equipment Purchases**: Unlike typical research projects, minimal equipment costs beyond field supplies (bench scale $1,000, tools). Suggests reliance on existing BST and partner infrastructure.
